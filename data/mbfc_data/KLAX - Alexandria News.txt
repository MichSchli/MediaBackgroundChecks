History
Founded in 1979, KLAX – Alexandria News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Alexandria, Louisiana, United States. The associated website klax-tv.com covers news, sports, crime, and weather. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Cox Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cox_Enterprises) owns KLAX – Alexandria News through their subsidiary Cox Media Group. They are a company that owns numerous newspapers, television stations, and radio stations, such as the [Atlanta Journal-Constitution](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/atlanta-journal-constitution/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KLAX – Alexandria News covers news, sports, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Alexandria, Louisiana area. Local news is covered with minimally loaded language, such as this
[Former Rapides Parish Teacher, Fugitive Sex Offender caught in Florida](https://klax-tv.com/former-rapides-parish-teacher-fugitive-sex-offender-caught-in-florida/). Many local news stories contain videos from the TV station. The national news is primarily republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, KLAX – Alexandria News does not publish op-eds; however, when covering politics, they present info with minimal bias, such as this
[Exclusive Interview with Mayoral Candidate Lorenzo Davis, Sr.](https://klax-tv.com/exclusive-interview-with-mayoral-candidate-lorenzo-davis-sr/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
