History
American Intelligence Media (Aim4Truth) is an alternative news and opinion website that favors a Christian perspective. According to their
[about page](https://aim4truth.org/about/), “They researched the deep truths of the way the world works and rewrote history–real-time, based on truth, not on the lies of the mainstream media and government-controlled propaganda and indoctrination.”
The website lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership or editors. It features the tagline “Christians addicted to Truth.” The domain is
[registered](https://whois.domaintools.com/aim4truth.org) in the United States. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
American Intelligence Media lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership. There is also no disclosure of funding or revenue generation.
Analysis / Bias
Essentially, this is a conspiracy website that focuses heavily on
[false flag operations](https://aim4truth.org/2017/10/03/breaking-false-flag-in-las-vegas/), [9-11 conspiracies](http://newmediacentral.net/mysterious-911-witness-deaths/), [Chemtrails/Geoengineering](https://aim4truth.org/2017/09/05/global-climate-change-how-geoengineering-is-causing-a-worldwide-weather-apocalypse/), and the [New World Order](https://aim4truth.org/2017/04/14/exposing-the-lineage-of-the-new-world-order/). Frequently, American Intelligence Media links to stories that will carry you to other conspiracy/questionable sources.
The website also publishes memes that are right-wing and tin-foil hat conspiracy such as this
[THE ‘VACCINE’ IS A BIOWEAPON OF WAR.](https://aim4truth.org/2021/03/11/cat-report-698/) In general, most content is either false or misleading.
