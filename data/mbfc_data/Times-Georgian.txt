History
The Times-Georgian is a daily newspaper published in Carrollton, Georgia. times-georgian.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Paxton Media Group,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paxton_Media_Group) which owns several right-leaning newspapers across the USA, such as the [Elkhart Truth](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-elkhart-truth/). Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Times-Georgian reports national news by republishing articles from credible newsagents such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). They report local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias using neutral headlines such as [Carroll County school bus backs into vehicle](https://www.times-georgian.com/times_georgian/carroll-county-school-bus-backs-into-vehicle/article_1c7fb30a-20cd-5363-9004-f939cce004e7.html).
