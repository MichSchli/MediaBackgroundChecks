History
The Lebanon Valley News, based in Lebanon, New Hampshire, is a news website that focuses on arts, entertainment, and culture in the Lebanon Valley region. The website covers a variety of topics, including local news, music, theater, art exhibits, and social issues.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Lebanon Valley News is owned by
[Newspapers of New England, Inc. (NNE)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newspapers_of_New_England,_Inc.), which is a privately owned publisher of nine daily and weekly newspapers in New Hampshire and Massachusetts. The paper is funded through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
The Lebanon Valley News publishes local news through staff journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Rutland jury finds former child care provider guilty of fatally medicating infant](https://articles.vnews.com/Rutland-jury-finds-former-child-care-provider-guilty-of-fatally-medicating-infant-53262614). The paper and website do not cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of articles classified as “opinion” indicates more favor a left-leaning perspective, such as
[Editorial: Trump’s Claremont speech crossed a dangerous line](https://www.vnews.com/trump-rhetoric-editorial-53065113). In general, the news is reported factually with a left-leaning editorial bias.
