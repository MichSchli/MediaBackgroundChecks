History
Williams Lake Tribune is a newspaper published in Williams Lake, British Columbia, Canada. The website wltribune.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Williams Lake, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Williams Lake Tribune. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Williams Lake Tribune covers news for Williams Lake and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Williams Lake city councillor is sharing positivity for National Indigenous History Month](https://www.wltribune.com/news/williams-lake-city-councillor-is-sharing-positivity-for-national-indigenous-history-month/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Williams Lake Tribune does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
