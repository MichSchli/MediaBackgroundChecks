History
The Sooke News Mirror is a newspaper published in Sooke, British Columbia, Canada. The website sookenewsmirror.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Sooke area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Sooke News Mirror. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Sooke News Mirror covers news for Sooke and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Man on provincewide warrant sought by Victoria police](https://www.sookenewsmirror.com/news/man-on-provincewide-warrant-sought-by-victoria-police/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Sooke News Mirror does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
