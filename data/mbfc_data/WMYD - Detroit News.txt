History
Founded in 1968, WMYD – Detroit News is an independent station licensed to Detroit, Michigan, United States. tv20detroit.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WMYD – Detroit News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WMYD – Detroit News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Detroit, Michigan area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Man arrested after fatal assault at GM Orion Assembly Plant, police say](https://www.tv20detroit.com/news/gm-orion-assembly-plant-closed-after-police-incident-early-thursday-morning). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[How have Afghan refugees adjusted to the United States?](https://www.tv20detroit.com/news/national-politics/how-have-afghan-refugees-adjusted-to-the-united-states) In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
