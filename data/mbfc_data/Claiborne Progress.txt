History
The Claiborne Progress is a newspaper published in Tazwell, Tennessee. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Claiborne Progress is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Claiborne Progress primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Tennessee board to approve $16 million in incentives](https://www.claiborneprogress.net/2023/02/20/tennessee-board-to-approve-16-million-in-incentives/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Claiborne Progress labels its opinion section appropriately, with more op-eds favoring the right. In general, the Claiborne Progress is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
