History
Founded in 1961, WLKY – Louisville News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Louisville, Kentucky, United States. WLKY.com is the associated website that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Louisville, KY area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WLKY – Louisville News is owned by Hearst Communications, which owns newspapers, magazines, television channels, and television stations, including the
[San Francisco Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-francisco-chronicle/), the [Houston Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/houston-chronicle/), and [Esquire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/esquire-magazine/). According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Hearst donated [70% to Republican candidates](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/recipients?id=D000034391) and causes in 2020. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WLKY – Louisville News reports on news, crime, sports, weather, and entertainment for the greater Louisville, KY area. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[LMPD: Bicyclist dies after being struck twice in PRP, neither vehicle stopped](https://www.wlky.com/article/lmpd-bicyclist-killed-prp-hit-and-run-louisville-bicycle-death/40634039). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other Hearst affiliates typically cover the national news.
Editorially, WLKY – Louisville News does not publish editorial pieces, and when reporting on national politics, there is minimal bias as the primary source is usually the Associated Press. In general, they present fact-based, low-biased news.
