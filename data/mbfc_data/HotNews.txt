History
Founded in 1999,
[HotNews](https://books.google.com/books?id=z_hVDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA50&lpg=PA50&dq=Romania%27s+hotnews+established+owned&source=bl&ots=P6mG930SSQ&sig=ACfU3U0GYHcrzt1zqQAgLf8VEZO43zcOkg&hl=en&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwijgZrL5NjxAhVCXM0KHU23A3EQ6AEwCXoECBwQAw#v=onepage&q=Romania's%20hotnews%20established%20owned&f=false) is a news and analysis website headquartered in Bucharest, Romania. HotNews is published in Romanian, English, and Spanish. The website covers various topics such as technology, finance, national and international politics, and current affairs. [Clarice Dinu](https://www.hotnews.ro/redactia_hotnews) serves as editor-in-chief. [Read our profile on Romanian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/romania-media-profile/)
Funded by Ownership
HotNews.ro was founded by
[Ioan Margarit](https://www.romaniajournal.ro/society-people/editor-in-chief-and-coordinating-editor-announce-leaving-hotnews-a-blow-from-liviu-dragnea/), and the former editor and director were Cristian Pantazi; however, HotNews does not clearly disclose its ownership. For a history of HotNews, please see [Here](https://books.google.com/books?id=z_hVDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA50&lpg=PA50&dq=Romania%27s+hotnews+established+owned&source=bl&ots=P6mG930SSQ&sig=ACfU3U0GYHcrzt1zqQAgLf8VEZO43zcOkg&hl=en&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwijgZrL5NjxAhVCXM0KHU23A3EQ6AEwCXoECBwQAw#v=onepage&q=Romania's%20hotnews%20established%20owned&f=false).
Analysis / Bias
HotNews list their Media Partners as
[Euractiv](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/euractiv/), [RFI](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/rfi-radio-france-internationale/), [Deutsche Welle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/dw-news/), and the Rise Project. They also collaborate with News agencies such as [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/), [AFP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/afp-agence-france-presse/), and [Agerpres](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/agerpres/). They list their partners as GTS, Medlife, Tremendous, Esri Romania, and Nikon.
On issues such as LGBT rights, HotNews reports with a positive tone, such as in this article
[“European Parliament condemns Hungarian anti-pedophilia law “in the strongest possible terms” and attacks on LGBT rights.”](https://www.hotnews.ro/stiri-meetyourmep-24907604-parlamentul-european-condamna-termenii-cei-mai-fermi-putinta-legea-anti-lgbt-din-ungaria.htm) At the end of the article, they also summarize the context, “On 15 June 2021, the Hungarian Parliament voted almost unanimously (157 votes to 1) in favor of rules that, under the pretext of combating pedophilia, restrict the freedom of expression and rights of children by banning the presence of LGBTIQ content in educational materials or TV shows for people under the age of 18. The new law entered into force on July 8, 2021.”
When it comes to sourcing
[articles](https://www.hotnews.ro/stiri-international-24908230-presedintele-haitian-fost-asasinat-comando-strain.htm), they use official sources such as [europarl.europa.eu](https://www.europarl.europa.eu/news/ro/press-room/20201002IPR88432/pe-cere-un-mecanism-eficient-si-obligatoriu-pentru-a-proteja-valorile-uniunii), [ec.europa.eu,](https://ec.europa.eu/info/aid-development-cooperation-fundamental-rights/your-rights-eu/eu-charter-fundamental-rights_ro) [BBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bbc/) and news.ro.(Romanian News Agency). They also publish Technology articles such as” [Starlink: Everything you need to know about the constellation through which Elon Musk wants to connect the entire planet to the internet – speed, price, problems and more](https://science.hotnews.ro/stiri-tehnologie-24910209-starlink-tot-trebuie-stii-despre-constelatia-prin-care-elon-musk-vrea-conecteze-internet-intreaga-planeta-viteza-pret-probleme-altele.htm).” This article is sourced through [CNET](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnet/); however, they do not provide a hyperlink. Finally, they republish articles from the [Financial Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/financial-times/), such as [ “Financial Times: European democracy is adapting to the far right.”](https://www.hotnews.ro/stiri-international-24887542-financial-times-democratia-europeana-adapteaza-extrema-dreapta.htm) In general, HotNews reports the news factually and with a moderate left-leaning bias.
