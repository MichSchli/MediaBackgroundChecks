History
The Bainbridge Post-Searchlight is a newspaper published in Bainbridge, Georgia. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.

Funded by / Ownership
The Bainbridge Post-Searchlight is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Bainbridge Post-Searchlight primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Grimsley Pharmacy celebrates 100th anniversary in business](https://www.thepostsearchlight.com/2022/12/14/grimsley-pharmacy-celebrates-100th-anniversary-in-business/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
