History
Founded in 2004, American Public Media (APM) is the second-largest producer and distributor of public radio programs in the United States after
[NPR](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/npr/). APR produces content for NPR. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
American Public Media Group is the parent company of American Public Media,
[Minnesota Public Radio](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/minnesota-public-radio-mpr/), and Southern California Public Radio. APMG operates Public Radio Market under license from American Public Media.
Analysis / Bias
In review, like NPR, American Public Media does not use loaded words, and all information is sourced correctly. Specifically, they use
[BBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bbc/) as their primary news source, which is highly credible with a slight leftward bias in story selection.
