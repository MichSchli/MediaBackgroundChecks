History
Founded in 2018, Brighteon is a right-wing conspiracy and pseudoscience video-sharing website that publishes false information. The founder is Mike Adams, who owns several Questionable websites such as
[Natural News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/natural-news/) and [News Target](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/news-target/). Although not disclosed, Brighteon is a part of the Natural News Network. This website lacks transparency and does not disclose ownership. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Mike Adams owns Brighteon. He owns many other fake and/or controversial right-wing conspiracy websites. Like all Mike Adams’s properties, the website generates revenue through online advertising, donations, and an online store that sells supplements and preparedness supplies.
