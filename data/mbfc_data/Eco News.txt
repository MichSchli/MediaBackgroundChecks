Factual Reporting: MIXED
Country: Unknown
History
Eco News claims via its tagline to be “on the Frontline for Earth Liberation.” In reality, this is a moderate pseudoscience website that does not have an about page nor contact information.
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not disclose ownership and it is funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
Eco News is a news and opinion website that focuses on the environment and natural health. Eco News has a strong left-wing bias politically and uses loaded emotional words in headlines and articles. For the most part, they source properly, however they do use other pseudoscience sources such as the
[Health Sciences Institute](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/health-sciences-institute/). While many articles are factual on this site, there is a moderate [promotion](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/) of [vaccination propaganda](http://econewsmedia.org/2017/10/21/9-year-old-girl-paralyzed-blinded-two-days-receiving-flu-shot/) and [anti-Gmo ](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-gmo-dangers/)positions that is not based on scientific evidence.
Eco News has also
[published](http://ecomedia.news/2018/02/01/nfl-lawyer-claimed-super-bowl-rigged-found-dead/) a [fake news story](https://www.politifact.com/punditfact/statements/2018/feb/02/eco-news/story-nfl-lawyer-said-super-bowl-rigged-and-was-mu/).
