History
Founded in 1985, KLJB – Quad Cities News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station licensed to Davenport, Iowa. The associated website is ourquadcities.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KLJB – Quad Cities News is owned by
[Nexstar Media Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nexstar_Media_Group), which owns around 200 TV stations nationwide. The founder, chairman, president, and CEO of Nexstar is Perry A. Sook. [According](https://www.opensecrets.org/pacs/pacgot.php?cmte=C00567388&cycle=2018) to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Nexstar Media group gave more money to Republicans in the 2018 election cycle (45% to Democrats, 55% to Republicans). However, in 2020 they gave more to Democrats (55% Democrats, 45% to Republicans). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KLJB – Quad Cities News website publishes news for the greater Davenport, Iowa area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:
[Man arrested in connection with 2021 Rock Island murder](https://www.ourquadcities.com/news/local-news/man-arrested-in-connection-with-2021-rock-island-murder/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through Nexstar Media Wire, the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[President and lawmakers push for new gun legislation](https://www.ourquadcities.com/washington/washington-dc/president-and-lawmakers-push-for-new-gun-legislation/). Reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides, and avoids using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
