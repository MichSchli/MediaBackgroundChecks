History
[Founded in 2006](https://whois.domaintools.com/fitsnews.com), FitsNews is a news and opinion blog with the tagline “Independent, Unapologetic.” According to their [contact page](https://www.fitsnews.com/make-contact/), they are based in South Carolina. The website lacks transparency as they do not provide an about page or properly disclose ownership.
The website was founded by
[Will Folks](https://ballotpedia.org/Will_Folks), who is the president of Viewpolitik, LLC, a political public relations firm. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not clearly indicate ownership; however, at the bottom of each page, it states they are copyrighted through Fitsnews LLC. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Fitsnews publishes news stories written by Fitsnews staff. Articles and headlines typically utilize low to moderate loaded emotional language such as this
[Why Is Liberal Charleston Mayor Paying South Carolina’s Top Republican Election Attorney?](https://www.fitsnews.com/2020/09/03/why-is-liberal-charleston-mayor-paying-south-carolinas-top-republican-election-attorney/) This story is primarily sourced back to previous Fits articles. The website primarily covers South Carolina politics with more stories favoring the right such as this [University Of South Carolina-Aiken’s Campus Rag Is Racy, Revealing And Uber-Liberal](https://www.fitsnews.com/2020/02/17/university-of-south-carolina-aikens-campus-rag-is-racy-revealing-and-uber-liberal/).
Fitsnews also covers DC politics from a conservative/libertarian perspective with stories like this
[First Amendment Watch: Another Successful Attack On Religious Liberty In America](https://www.fitsnews.com/2020/07/25/first-amendment-watch-another-successful-attack-on-religious-liberty-in-america/). Editorially more stories favor the right; however, they do not hesitate to criticize conservatives, such as this [Is Lindsey Graham About To Have A Problem?](https://www.fitsnews.com/2020/06/05/is-lindsey-graham-about-to-have-a-problem/) and this [Donald Trump Touts Infrastructure Project Benefiting Liberal Billionaire](https://www.fitsnews.com/2020/06/18/donald-trump-touts-infrastructure-project-benefiting-liberal-billionaire/). Regarding science, they [ support the consensus](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/) on [climate change](https://www.fitsnews.com/2018/11/23/climate-change-report-a-challenge-to-donald-trump/) and report accurately on [Coronavirus](https://www.fitsnews.com/2020/09/02/about-those-covid-19-comorbidities/); however, they take the political view that the economy and businesses should be open.
