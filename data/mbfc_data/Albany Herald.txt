History
The Albany Herald is a newspaper published in Albany, Georgia. albanyherald.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports for the Albany area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Southern Communities Newspapers](https://www.albanyherald.com/site/contact.html). Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Albany Herald reports national news by republishing articles from credible newsagents such as CNN. They report local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias using neutral headlines, such as
[Motorcycle crash victims identified by police](https://www.albanyherald.com/news/motorcycle-crash-victims-identified-by-police/article_ef2405b4-bdf7-11ee-918c-1bc11e909de1.html). All information is properly sourced.
Editorially, there is a reasonable balance between left and right-leaning opinions, with slightly more favoring the right such as this from Ann Coulter
[ANN COULTER: The never-ending media hunt for right-wing violence](https://www.albanyherald.com/opinion/). In general, the paper is fact-based and holds a slight right-leaning editorial bias.
