History
[Founded in 1940](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WBBM-TV), WBBM – CBS Chicago is a CBS-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Chicago, Illinois, United States. The associated website is [chicago.cbslocal.com](https://chicago.cbslocal.com/) that features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Chicago area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WBBM – CBS Chicago is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the WBBM – CBS Chicago website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater Chicago region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording: [Man Shot In Leg While Sitting Inside Grand Crossing Home](https://chicago.cbslocal.com/2021/06/29/man-shot-in-leg-while-sitting-inside-grand-crossing-home/). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published from other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WBBM – CBS Chicago does not publish op-eds; however, they do report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[Local Pastor Calls On President Biden To Declare Violence A Public Mental Health Crisis, As Gov. Pritzker Has Done](https://chicago.cbslocal.com/2021/05/02/local-pastor-president-biden-violence-public-mental-health-crisis/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
