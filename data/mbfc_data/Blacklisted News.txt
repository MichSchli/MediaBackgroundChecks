History
[Founded in 2006](http://whois.domaintools.com/blacklistednews.com), Blacklisted News is an alternative news and opinion source that occasionally promotes conspiracy theories. A domain search reveals the website is registered to Doug Owen. According to their [Patreon page](https://www.patreon.com/blacklistednews), “Doug Owen is the current Senior Editor of BlacklistedNews.com, political analyst, and host of the Blacklisted Radio Podcast.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Blacklisted News appears to be owned by Doug Owen; however, this is not explicitly indicated on the website. Revenue is generated through advertising and donations.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Blacklisted News publishes a podcast by Doug Owen and news on the website. News articles on the website often are republished from other sources such as the Questionable
[Quillette](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/quillette/) and the conspiracy website [Zero Hedge](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/zero-hedge/). Headlines often use emotionally loaded wording such as this: [PRINCETON STUDY: THE U.S. IS NOT ‘LOSING’ ITS DEMOCRACY, IT’S ALREADY LONG GONE](https://www.blacklistednews.com/article/75510/princeton-study-the-us-is-not-losing-its-democracy-its-already-long.html). This story comes from the Pseudoscience website [Collective-Evolution](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/collective-evolution/).
Editorially, Blacklisted News tends to lean left with frequent negative reporting regarding the former
[Trump administration](https://www.blacklistednews.com/article/75520/rudy-giulianis-son-makes-95000-working-as-sports-liaison-for-the-white.html). They also frequently publish conspiracies such as [Chemtrails](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-chemtrails/), [New World Order](https://www.blacklistednews.com/article/75270/the-new-world-is-emerging-before.html), and Clinton murder conspiracies: [COMPLETE LIST OF CLINTON ASSOCIATES WHO ALLEGEDLY DIED MYSTERIOUSLY OR COMMITTED SUICIDE BEFORE TESTIMONY](https://www.blacklistednews.com/article/74251/complete-list-of-clinton-associates-who-allegedly-died-mysteriously-or-committed-suicide-before.html). This story comes from the highly disreputable [Gateway Pundit](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-gateway-pundit/). In general, Blacklisted News is a website that promotes conspiracies and pseudoscience; however, some stories are factual and derived from credible sources.
