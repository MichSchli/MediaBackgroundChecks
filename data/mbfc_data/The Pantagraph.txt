History
[The Pantagraph](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Pantagraph) is a daily newspaper that serves Bloomington-Normal, Illinois, along with 60 communities and eight counties in the Central Illinois area. Its headquarters are in Bloomington, Illinois. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lee Enterprises owns the Pantagraph. According to
[Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Lee Enterprises has donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) since 1990. An online advertising and subscription model generates revenue for the paper.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Pantagraph reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and entertainment for the greater Bloomington, Illinois, region. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Gunman sentenced for Bloomington double murder](https://pantagraph.com/news/local/crime-and-courts/gunman-sentenced-for-bloomington-double-murder/article_16ffdea4-5881-523e-940f-b6390c303120.html#tracking-source=featured-homepage). The website publishes national and international news from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://www.kenoshanews.com/news/local/kenosha-man-wanted-in-officer-shooting/article_59c674e8-f6d8-51d8-b194-c1ef3c54b983.html#tracking-source=home-breaking).
Editorially, the Pantagraph endorsed Democratic Presidential candidate
[Hillary Clinton in 2016](https://pantagraph.com/news/opinion/editorial/editorial-clinton-is-the-better-choice-for-president/article_deacd4e0-7f40-5910-bfef-f25696c67920.html); however, in 2020, they did not endorse a candidate for President. A review of editorial articles demonstrates [slightly more](https://pantagraph.com/opinion/editorial/our-view-democrats-methods-maddening/article_398734d9-2942-54cc-bf9f-92151bc9ed81.html) favor a conservative perspective. Generally, the news is reported factually and with a slight right-leaning editorial bias.
