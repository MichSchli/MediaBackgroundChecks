History
Founded in 2012, Understand Reality Through Science is the personal blog of author Tom Rafferty, who wrote the book
[“Making Stuff Up is Unwise: An Introduction to Reason, Skepticism and Science.](https://www.amazon.com/dp/1478106123)” The blog writes original content and curated and hand-selected science news from a diverse range of scientific topics. They specialize in debunking pseudoscience and magical thinking. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Understand Reality Through Science is owned by Tom Rafferty and does not solicit revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Understand Reality Through Science is a blog that serves as an encyclopedia of topics such as explanations of
[pseudoscience](https://understandrealitythroughscience.blogspot.com/p/blog-page_30.html), [logical fallacies](https://understandrealitythroughscience.blogspot.com/p/search-logical-fallacies.html), as well as politics, and [Math And Logic as Formal Sciences](https://understandrealitythroughscience.blogspot.com/2019/11/math-and-logic-are-formal-sciences.html). Information on this blog is well-researched and offers a pro-science approach to various topics.
