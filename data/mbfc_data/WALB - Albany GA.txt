History
Founded in 1954 and an NBC/ABC-affiliated television station, WALB – Albany GA serves Albany, Georgia, and the surrounding area. Walb.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WALB – Albany GA is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WALB – Albany GA provides news coverage for Albany, Georgia, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Broken Heart Bakery to stay open thanks to private investment](https://www.walb.com/2021/11/13/broken-heart-bakery-stay-open-thanks-private-investment/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WALB – Albany GA does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
