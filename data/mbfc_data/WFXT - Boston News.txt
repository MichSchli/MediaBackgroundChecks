History
Founded in 1972, WFXT – Boston News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station licensed to Boston, Massachusetts, United States. The associated website boston25news.com covers news, sports, crime, and weather. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Cox Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cox_Enterprises) owns WFXT – Boston News through their subsidiary Cox Media Group. They are a company that owns numerous newspapers, television stations, and radio stations, such as the [Atlanta Journal-Constitution](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/atlanta-journal-constitution/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WFXT – Boston News covers news, sports, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Boston, Massachusetts area. Local news is covered with minimally loaded language, such as this
[Two men with life-threatening injuries, three arrested after Chinatown shooting](https://www.boston25news.com/news/local/two-men-with-life-threatening-injuries-three-arrested-after-chinatown-shooting/6XT5PME2NNFCRNBQ6ICTROM5SI/). Many local news stories contain videos from the TV station. The national news is primarily republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WFXT – Boston News does not publish op-eds; however, when covering politics, they present info with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden bans Russian aircraft from US airspace](https://www.boston25news.com/news/trending/biden-bans-russian-aircraft-us-airspace/AHVFSSA6IJFJJMSZH5NIRSVVFU/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
