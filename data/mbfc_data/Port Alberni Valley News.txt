History
The Port Alberni Valley News is a newspaper published in Port Alberni, British Columbia, Canada. The website albernivalleynews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Port Alberni area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Port Alberni Valley News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
