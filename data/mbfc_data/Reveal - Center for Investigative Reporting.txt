History
The Center for Investigative Reporting (CIR) is a nonprofit news organization based in Emeryville, California, and has conducted
[investigative journalism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Center_for_Investigative_Reporting) since 1977. It is known for producing stories that reveal scandals or corruption in government agencies and corporations. CIR has won numerous high-standard journalism awards.
According to their
[about page](https://www.revealnews.org/about-us/), “We engage and empower the public through investigative journalism and groundbreaking storytelling that sparks action improves lives and protects our democracy.” [Over their history](https://www.revealnews.org/our-history/) CIR has partnered with major news organizations such as [Mother Jones](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mother-jones/), [Los Angeles Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/la-times-los-angeles-times/), [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/), 60 Minutes, and [NPR](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/npr/). [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
In review, revealnews.org is a website that features articles, videos, and podcasts by the Center for Investigative Journalism. Headlines typically utilize minimally loaded language and accurately reflect the contents of the article, such as this:
[Leaked immigration court official’s directive could violate rules that protect families from deportation](https://www.revealnews.org/article/leaked-immigration-court-officials-directive-could-violate-rules-that-protect-families-from-deportation/). This story is appropriately sourced to [NPR](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/npr/), [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/) and Justice.gov. Story selection often is related to investigations, however, the investigations usually favor the left such as this [Inside hate groups on Facebook, police officers trade racist memes, conspiracy theories and Islamophobia](https://www.revealnews.org/article/inside-hate-groups-on-facebook-police-officers-trade-racist-memes-conspiracy-theories-and-islamophobia/), and this [See how climate change could bring disaster to some popular coastal national parks](https://www.revealnews.org/article/see-how-climate-change-could-bring-disaster-to-some-popular-coastal-national-parks/). In general, Revealnews.org produces high-quality journalism that is factual and moderately left-biased.
