Analysis / Bias
In review, Capital & Main is a progressive liberal publication. Capital & Main publishes news using moderately loaded words such as this:
[Big Pharma Bankrolled Pro-Trump Group As Trump Pushed Pharma Tax Cut](https://capitalandmain.com/big-pharma-bankrolled-pro-trump-group-as-trump-pushed-pharma-tax-cut-1115). This article and all others reviewed were very well sourced to credible media outlets such as the [Associated Press](https://capitalandmain.com/big-pharma-bankrolled-pro-trump-group-as-trump-pushed-pharma-tax-cut-1115) and the [Sacramento Bee](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/sacramento-bee/).

[Climate Change and Green Energy](https://capitalandmain.com/michael-mann-says-this-summer-is-a-big-warning-about-climate-change-0824). The article goes on to “ay: “A rising Democratic star has voted for GOP bills that Trump critics say have aided big banks, undercut the fight against climate change, and supported the president’s anti-immigrant agenda.” Capital & Main does not have a favorable view of former President Trump, as evidenced by [Trump’s Treasury Department Hands Banks a Windfall.](https://capitalandmain.com/trumps-treasury-department-hands-banks-a-windfall-0808)
