History
The Didsbury Review is an English-language newspaper in Didsbury, Alberta, Canada. mountainviewtoday.ca/didsbury-news is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Didsbury Review. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Didsbury Review publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Didsbury region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Didsbury’s 2023 policing priorities include targeting major crime](https://www.mountainviewtoday.ca/didsbury-news/didsburys-2023-policing-priorities-include-targeting-major-crime-6776529). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Didsbury Review’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Seven reasons we should care about caregivers](https://www.mountainviewtoday.ca/beyond-local/seven-reasons-we-should-care-about-caregivers-6851973). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
