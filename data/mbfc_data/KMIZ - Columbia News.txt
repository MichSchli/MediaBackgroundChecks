History
Founded in 1962, KMIZ – Columbia News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Columbia, Missouri. The associated website is abc17news.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns KMIZ – Columbia News and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KMIZ – Columbia News website publishes news for the greater Columbia-Jefferson City, Missouri area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this: .
[New Missouri congressional district map signed into law](https://abc17news.com/politics/missouri-politics/2022/05/18/new-missouri-congressional-district-map-signed-into-law/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [CBS News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/), [CNN Newswire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[Walsh drops out of race due to new congressional map](https://abc17news.com/politics/missouri-politics/2022/05/16/walsh-drops-out-of-congressional-race/). Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoiding using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
