History
The Moore American is a newspaper published in Moore, Oklahoma. mooreamerican.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Moore American. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Moore American covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Moore, Oklahoma, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Suspects charged in BB gun vandalism case](https://www.mooreamerican.com/news/suspects-charged-in-bb-gun-vandalism-case/article_062fcb27-ed4e-5013-a94b-0dc7a6cf2569.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
