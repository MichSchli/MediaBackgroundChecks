History
Founded in 1953, KXLF – Butte News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Butte, Montana United States. KXLF.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KXLF – Butte News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KXLF – Butte News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Butte, Montana area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Butte Youth Court needs more money for more juvenile detention expenses](https://www.kxlf.com/news/local-news/butte-youth-court-needs-more-money-for-more-juvenile-detention-expenses). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Country continues to wait for student debt forgiveness decision by President Biden](https://www.kxlf.com/news/national-politics/country-continues-to-wait-for-student-debt-forgiveness-decision-by-president-biden). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
