History
Established in 1990,
[The Death Penalty Information Center (DPIC)](https://deathpenaltyinfo.org/about/about-us) is a non-profit organization headquartered in Washington, D.C. Their primary mission is to serve as an informational resource on the death penalty for the public and media. In addition, under the section labeled ‘For Educators,’ you can find high school and college curricula and a teacher’s guide. For a comprehensive guide on navigating DPIC’s website and accessing its resources, you can visit their [Welcome to DPIC’s New Website](https://deathpenaltyinfo.org/stories/welcome-to-dpics-new-website) page. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The DPIC is a non-profit organization funded mainly through grants, donations, and other philanthropic support. They disclose major funding sources on their website and adhere to transparency guidelines. Some funders include M. Quinn Delaney and the Tides Foundation,
[Open Society Foundation](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/open-society-foundations/)s, and the Roderick MacArthur Foundation. Here is the list of [staff and board of directors.](https://deathpenaltyinfo.org/about/staff-and-board-of-directors)
Analysis / Bias
The Death Penalty Information Center provides data and insights on capital punishment to the media, public, and stakeholders. It also publishes news stories and creates reports, press releases, and briefings. For instance, an article titled
[“Trump Administration Presses Forward with Death Penalty Despite Election Defeat”](https://deathpenaltyinfo.org/news/trump-administration-presses-forward-with-death-penalty-despite-election-defeat-announcing-3-more-death-warrants-and-more-capital-prosecutions) frames the continuation of the death penalty under the [Trump administration](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/donald-trump-republican-presidential-candidate-bias-profile/) as a controversial action “despite” electoral defeat. They use terms such as “historically unprecedented” and “lame duck.” The article includes quotes from the incoming [Biden administration](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/joe-biden-democratic-presidential-candidate-bias-profile/).
Another article,
[“Democratic Legislators Introduce Death Penalty Repeal Bills, Urge President Biden to Commute Federal Death Sentences,”](https://deathpenaltyinfo.org/news/democratic-legislators-introduce-death-penalty-repeal-bills-urge-president-biden-to-commute-federal-death-sentences) discusses Democrats’ efforts to abolish the federal death penalty, aligning with President Biden’s position and a progressive stance.
While DPIC maintains a stated position of neutrality on the death penalty, its focus on issues like
[racial](https://deathpenaltyinfo.org/policy-issues/race) and [economic](https://deathpenaltyinfo.org/news/representation-why-poor-people-in-texas-end-up-on-death-row-and-face-execution) disparities, [wrongful convictions](https://deathpenaltyinfo.org/policy-issues/innocence/executed-but-possibly-innocent), and ethical questions related to the application of the death penalty aligns closely with left-leaning viewpoints on criminal justice and social justice reform.
