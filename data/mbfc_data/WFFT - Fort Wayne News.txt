History
Founded in 1977, WFFT – Fort Wayne News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station licensed to Fort Wayne, Indiana, United States. wlfi.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Lafayette area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WFFT – Fort Wayne News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WFFT – Fort Wayne News publishes news for Fort Wayne and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Woman accused of shooting ex and his friend after they left Fort Wayne movie theater](https://www.wfft.com/news/crime/woman-accused-of-shooting-ex-and-his-friend-after-they-left-fort-wayne-movie-theater/article_26f3b704-f749-11ec-805c-0f94a8a04910.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
