History
[Founded in 2015](http://moronmajority.com/about/), the MoronMajority is a website/blog that publishes satirical content. The website is appropriately labeled as satire with the following [disclaimer](http://moronmajority.com/disclaimer/) “This site is devoted to satire. If you believe any of the stuff you read here, don’t look now, but you probably belong to the group this site is named for.” [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website is owned by John Deprospo and does not appear to be monetized.
Analysis / Bias
In review, MoronMajority is a clearly labeled satire website that pokes fun at conservatives and, in particular, Donald Trump, such as this:
[Breaking News … Trump Presidential Library To Be Located In Moscow](http://moronmajority.com/breaking-news-trump-presidential-library-to-be-located-in-moscow/). The website also published an article that was [fact-checked](https://www.snopes.com/fact-check/trump-manson-pardon/?collection-id=226023) by [Snopes](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/snopes/) after it was republished by the [Daily Kos](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-kos/) and Presidential candidate Marianne Williamson: [Trump Pardons Charles Manson](http://moronmajority.com/trump-pardons-charles-manson/).
