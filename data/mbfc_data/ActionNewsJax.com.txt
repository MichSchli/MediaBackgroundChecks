History
ActionNewsJax.com is the news and information
[website](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Action_News_Jax) for CBS affiliate WJAX-TV and Fox affiliate WFOX-TV. The website provides news, sports, weather, and traffic for the Jacksonville, Florida, area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
ActionNewsJax.com, WJAX, and WFOX are owned by
[Cox Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cox_Enterprises), a company that owns numerous newspapers, television stations, and radio stations. ActionNewsJax.com generates revenue through advertising.
