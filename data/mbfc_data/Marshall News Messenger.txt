History
[Founded in 1877](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Marshall_News_Messenger), The Marshall News Messenger is a daily broadsheet newspaper independently published in Marshall, Texas. The newspaper serves Marshall and the surrounding area. [Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The newspaper is owned by
[M. Roberts Media](https://mrobertsmedia.com/), which publishes 6 newspapers in the eastern Texas region. The paper is funded through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Marshall News Messenger reports local news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Marion County district judge arrested on DWI charge](https://www.marshallnewsmessenger.com/news/marion-county-district-judge-arrested-on-dwi-charge/article_02476391-4886-50df-997b-da9078aa89b5.html). National and world news comes almost exclusively from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Marshall News Messenger tends to lean left with op-eds such as this
[Another View: We froze and Abbott got paid – $1 million from the billionaire profiteer of Texas’ deadly storm](https://www.marshallnewsmessenger.com/opinion/another-view-we-froze-and-abbott-got-paid---1-million-from-the-billionaire/article_fb1f564a-f7c8-11eb-b717-8767b49a0c5d.html). In general, the Marshall News Messenger is factual, uses credible sources, and holds a slightly Left-leaning editorial bias.
