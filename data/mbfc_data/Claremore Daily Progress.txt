History
The Claremore Daily Progress is a newspaper published in the city of Claremore, Oklahoma. claremoreprogress.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Claremore Daily Progress. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Claremore Daily Progress covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Claremore, Oklahoma region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Chick-fil-A donates over $1,300 to CPSF](https://www.claremoreprogress.com/news/local_news/chick-fil-a-donates-over-1-300-to-cpsf/article_9070e406-7d98-11ed-8a79-1be3f64075b0.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
