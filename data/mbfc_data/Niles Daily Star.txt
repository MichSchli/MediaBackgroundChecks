History
The Niles Daily Star is a newspaper published in Niles, Michigan. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.

Funded by / Ownership
The Niles Daily Star is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Niles Daily Star primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Niles martial arts school relocates](https://www.leaderpub.com/2023/04/05/niles-martial-arts-school-relocates/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Niles Daily Star labels its opinion section appropriately, with more op-eds favoring the right. In general, the Niles Daily Star is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
