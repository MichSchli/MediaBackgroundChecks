History
Founded in 1954 and a CBS affiliated television station, KGWN – Wyoming News Now serves Cheyanne and Scottsbluff, Wyoming, and the surrounding area. Wyomingnewsnow.tv is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.

Funded by / Ownership
KGWN – Wyoming News Now is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KGWN – Wyoming News Now provides news coverage for Dothan, Alabama, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[WYDOT urges drivers to safely view wildlife](https://www.wyomingnewsnow.tv/2021/05/10/wydot-urges-drivers-to-safely-view-wildlife/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KGWN – Wyoming News Now does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
