History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, ConstructionDive is an online news platform that focuses on the construction industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.constructiondive.com/about/), “Construction Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping the construction and building industry.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates ConstructionDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and CybersecurityDive. ConstructionDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
ConstructionDive publishes news and information related to the construction industry. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[Construction’s manufacturing boom: Mapping the biggest facilities underway in the US](https://www.constructiondive.com/news/manufacturing-construction-boom-tracker-map/688140/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like this
[US manufacturing boom unlocks ‘once-in-a-lifetime’ opportunity for contractors](https://www.constructiondive.com/news/manufacturing-construction-onshoring-ancillary-projects/699500/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
