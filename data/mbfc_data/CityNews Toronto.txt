History
Launched on August 8, 1962, CityNews Toronto (formerly 680News) is a Canadian radio station serving the Greater Toronto area. Its radio station and website are in all-news format.

Funded by / Ownership
Rogers Media owns CityNews Toronto. According to
[The Canada Guide](http://www.thecanadaguide.com/basics/news-and-media/), more than 80 percent of Canadian media is owned by a cartel of just five corporations: Bell Media, Rogers Media, Postmedia, Corus, and Torstar, each of which owns dozens of different print publications and television networks under various subsidiaries and affiliates. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, CityNews Toronto provides national coverage, feature stories, and analysis of issues in Canada and abroad. They utilize minimal loaded words in their articles, such as this
[Man shot multiple times outside Ryerson student center](https://www.680news.com/2019/06/14/shooting-ryerson-student-centre/). They also present low-biased coverage of USA news and utilize credible sources such as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, they do not publish opinion pieces, while political news is delivered with low bias. Finally, a
[Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/) Institute survey found that 66% of respondents [trust CityNews coverage](https://reutersinstitute.politics.ox.ac.uk/digital-news-report/2021/canada) and 12% do not, ranking them #5 in trust among the major English-language Canadian news providers.
