History
The Clinton Herald is a newspaper published in the city of Clinton, Iowa. clintonherald.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Clinton Herald. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Clinton Herald covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Clinton, Iowa, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Goebel-Bly joins First Central State Bank](https://www.clintonherald.com/news/local_news/goebel-bly-joins-first-central-state-bank/article_52424928-19e7-11ed-8050-13c00c0f7fb2.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
