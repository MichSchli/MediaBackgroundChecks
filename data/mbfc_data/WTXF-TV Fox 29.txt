History
[Founded in 1962](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WTXF-TV), WTXF-TV is a Fox-affiliated television station licensed in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. It serves the Philadelphia, Southern New Jersey, and Delaware regions. Fox29.com is a news and information website for WTXF-TV that primarily covers news, politics, entertainment, and sports. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTXF-TV Fox 29 is owned by the
[Fox News Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fox_News) division of [Fox Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fox_Corporation). The Fox Corporation was formed from the 2019 acquisition of [21st Century Fox](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/21st_Century_Fox) by [The Walt Disney Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company). Fox Corp. was spun off from 21st Century Fox, and its stock began trading on March 19, 2019. The Murdoch family owns it via a family trust with 39% interest; [Rupert Murdoch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rupert_Murdoch) is co-executive chairman, while his son [Lachlan Murdoch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lachlan_Murdoch) is chairman and CEO. Revenue is generated through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Fox 29 website publishes politics, entertainment, crime, and local news. Reporters write local news with minimal bias, such as this
[Pennsylvania residents told to wear masks in public](https://www.fox29.com/news/pennsylvania-residents-told-to-wear-masks-in-public). Many stories feature videos from WTXF news programming. The Associated Press serves national and international news.
Editorially, Fox 29 does not publish op-eds; however, they frequently report on politics and utilize the Associated Press:
[Pennsylvania lawmakers vote to delay the primary election to June 2](https://www.fox29.com/news/pennsylvania-lawmakers-vote-to-delay-primary-election-to-june-2). In general, Fox 29 reports news factually and with little bias.
