History
Founded in 1996, Alaska Survey Research (ASR) is based in Anchorage, Alaska, and specializes in market research.
[Ivan Moore](https://alaskasurveyresearch.com/), the founder, has a background in mathematics and statistics and has been working in the market research field in Alaska since 1990. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Alaska Survey Research is owned by Ivan Moore. The sale of surveys and market research generates revenue. ASR often allows survey participants to donate their payments to charity, indicating a degree of community involvement and participant engagement in their funding model.
Analysis / Bias
Alaska Survey Research conducts market research and polling via telephone and text messaging in the state of Alaska. The website does not generally report on politics.
[FiveThirtyEight](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fivethirtyeight/), an expert on measuring and rating pollster performance, [has evaluated 15 polls by ASR](https://projects.fivethirtyeight.com/pollster-ratings/alaska-survey-research/), indicating they are accurate 67% of the time or Mostly Factual. They also conclude that their polling favors the left with a score of +1.8, which equates to a Left-Center bias in polling. In general, ASR is a reasonably accurate pollster that slightly favors the left (Democratic Candidates).
