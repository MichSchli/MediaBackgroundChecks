History
Founded in 1952 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station, KGMB – Hawaii News Now serves Honolulu, Hawaii, and the surrounding area. hawaiinewsnow.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KGMB – Hawaii News Now is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KGMB – Hawaii News Now provides news coverage for Honolulu, Hawaii, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Man sentenced in 2021 Waikiki stabbing that left visitor dead](https://www.hawaiinewsnow.com/2022/12/14/murder-suspect-2021-waikiki-stabbing-sentenced-life/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KGMB – Hawaii News Now does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
