History
[Showbiz411](https://www.showbiz411.com/about) is a Hollywood news, scoops, and film review blog run by gossip blogger Roger Friedman. The website provides extensive coverage on various topics, from current events and popular culture to movie critiques and celebrity updates, among others. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Showbiz411 is owned and operated by Roger Friedman. The website generates revenue through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In reviewing the content, Showbiz411 publishes news about the entertainment industry with a focus on celebrity gossip and film reviews. The news reporting tends to exhibit tabloid-style journalism. For example, the article
[“Barron Trump, 17, Used by Father in New Anti-Biden Post on Social Media: All Bets are Off Now on What’s Exempt in Political Coverage”](https://www.showbiz411.com/2023/07/21/barron-trump-17-used-by-father-in-new-anti-biden-post-on-social-media-all-bets-are-off-now-on-whats-exempt-in-political-coverage) takes a negative view of Donald Trump’s social media post about his son Barron, using phrases like “thrown Barron into the mix,” and “he thinks it’s funny,” which have a critical and mocking tone. It also speculates about Barron’s height, voice, and English proficiency in a way that may be intended to ridicule him, contributing to an overall negative portrayal of Trump and his actions. The website also uses loaded language in its headlines and articles, particularly when discussing controversial topics or individuals. This is evident in the aforementioned article, where the headline suggests a critical stance toward Donald Trump’s use of his son in political discourse.
Another news article titled
[“Caroline Kennedy’s Son Backs Biden, Knocks Cousin Robert Kennedy: “It’s about a lot more than Camelot and conspiracy theories’”](https://www.showbiz411.com/2023/07/21/caroline-kennedys-son-backs-biden-knocks-cousin-robert-kennedy-its-about-a-lot-more-than-camelot-and-conspiracy-theories) covers Caroline Kennedy’s son, Jack Schlossberg, expressing his support for Joe Biden and distancing himself from his cousin Robert Kennedy Jr. The article has a pro-Biden tone, as it highlights the positive aspects of Joe Biden’s presidency and Jack Schlossberg’s endorsement of him and is critical of his cousin Robert Kennedy Jr.’s candidacy. As a result, the article presents a biased perspective in favor of Joe Biden and against Robert Kennedy Jr. There are no sources provided other than quotations.
In general, Showbiz411 holds a moderate left-leaning editorial bias and is mostly factual in reporting due to poor sourcing techniques.
