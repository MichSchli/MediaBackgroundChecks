History
Founded in 1979, the Lompoc Record (LR) is a broadsheet newspaper based in Lompoc, CA. LR’s focus is on local news, information, and entertainment, with the articles supplied by staff writers.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Santa Maria California News Media Inc. owns and publishes the Lompoc Record. They also own and publish the
[Santa Maria Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/santa-maria-times/). Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Lompoc Record publishes news for Lompoc, California, and the surrounding region. Articles and headlines use minimally loaded language such as this
[COVID-19 in Santa Barbara County | By The Numbers](https://lompocrecord.com/news/local/covid-19-in-santa-barbara-county-by-the-numbers/article_dce7b80c-939c-5102-b0db-3760df10270a.html). It’s national and political news articles are from the [Associated Press (AP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)).
Editorially, the Lompoc Record provides a balance of left and right opinions. In general, they are factual and low-biased.
