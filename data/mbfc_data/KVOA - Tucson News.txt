History
Founded in 1953, KVOA – Tucson News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Tucson, Arizona, United States. kvoa.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Tucson area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns KVOA – Tucson News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KVOA – Tucson News publishes news for Tucson and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Rio Rico High School students sent home over rifle incident](https://www.kvoa.com/news/rio-rico-high-school-students-sent-home-over-rifle-incident/article_b7ccc08e-2ae0-11ed-9208-7b5a4aba3c48.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
