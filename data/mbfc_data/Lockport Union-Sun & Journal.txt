History
The Lockport Union-Sun & Journal is a newspaper published in Lockport, New York. lockportjournal.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Lockport Union-Sun & Journal. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Lockport Union-Sun & Journal covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the lockport, New York region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Reenactments exemption in state gun law delayed](https://www.lockportjournal.com/news/local_news/reenactments-exemption-in-state-gun-law-delayed/article_a7b85084-9a00-567e-aacc-846a66ff5906.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
