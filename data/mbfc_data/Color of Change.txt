History
Color of Change is a nonprofit civil rights advocacy organization based in the United States. The organization aims to bring about social and political change by mobilizing and amplifying the voices of Black people and communities. The website serves as a platform to promote campaigns and initiatives related to racial justice and equality.
According to its
[About page](https://colorofchange.org/about/) “Color Of Change is the nation’s largest online racial justice organization. We help people respond effectively to injustice in the world around us. As a national online force driven by 7 million members, we move decision-makers in corporations and government to create a more human and less hostile world for Black people in America. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
Color of Change is primarily an advocacy website focused on issues related to racial justice and equality. The organization campaigns for policy reforms, corporate accountability, and social change to address systemic racism and promote the well-being of Black communities.
As an advocacy organization, colorofchange.org takes a clear stance on social and political issues, advocating for change from a progressive perspective such as this
[Demand Applebee’s Increase Wages Equitably](https://act.colorofchange.org/sign/demand-applebees-increase-wages-equitably?source=coc_main_website&_gl=1*1pexaiv*_ga*MjIzNDkyNDI2LjE2ODg5MjQxNTE.*_ga_81WFRBRG4C*MTY4ODkyNDE1MS4xLjEuMTY4ODkyNDYyOS4wLjAuMA..) and this [Stop Enabling DeSantis & Let AP African American Studies Be Taught in Schools!](https://campaigns.organizefor.org/petitions/stop-enabling-fl-gov-desantis-and-allow-ap-african-american-studies-to-be-taught-in-high-schools?_gl=1%2A4kim8j%2A_ga%2AMjIzNDkyNDI2LjE2ODg5MjQxNTE.%2A_ga_81WFRBRG4C%2AMTY4ODkyNDE1MS4xLjEuMTY4ODkyNDc2Mi4wLjAuMA..) Both campaigns are sourced directly from the petitions. In general, Color of Change is mostly fact-based and holds progressive left biases.
