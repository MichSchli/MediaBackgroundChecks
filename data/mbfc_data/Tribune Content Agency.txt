History
The Tribune Content Agency produces up to 250 news stories and images per day for distribution to Newspapers and media outlets. The Tribune News Service was
[founded in 1918.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribune_Media) [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Tribune Publishing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribune_Publishing) owns the Tribune Content Agency.
Analysis / Bias
In review, they deliver news stories to hundreds of newspapers in the United States and internationally. The majority of these papers fall within the Left-Center to Least Biased categories. The news reporting is generally free of loaded words and pretty straightforward, such as you would find with
[Reuters](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tribune_Media).
