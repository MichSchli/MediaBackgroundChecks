History
The Bull – Tulsa is a radio station based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. thebulltulsa.com is the website for the radio station and its news programming.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Griffin Communications, a media company based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, owns The Bull – Tulsa. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Bull – Tulsa primarily covers local news, crime, entertainment, and sports for the Tulsa, Oklahoma, area. Local news is covered by the Least Biased
[KOTV – News On 6](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/kotv-newson6-bias/) TV station with minimal bias through reporters like this [Overnight Stabbing At Tulsa Shopping Center Leaves Man Injured](https://www.thebulltulsa.com/story/63299ad230e09307291464f7/overnight-stabbing-at-tulsa-shopping-center-leaves-man-injured). The Associated Press provides national and international news.
The Bull – Tulsa does not offer editorials and rarely covers political news. Most political news is derived from third-party sources such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). In general, news reporting is factual and displays minimal bias.
