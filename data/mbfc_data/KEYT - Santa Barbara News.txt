History
Founded in 1953, KEYT – Santa Barbara News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)/ [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Santa Barbara, California. The associated website is kevt.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns KEYT – Santa Barbara News and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KEYT – Santa Barbara News website publishes news for the greater Santa Barbara, California area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:
[Bird’s-eye view of tile damage on Santa Barbara County’s historic courthouse](https://keyt.com/news/santa-barbara-s-county/2022/06/10/birds-eye-view-of-tile-damage-on-santa-barbara-countys-historic-courthouse/) Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [CBS News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/), [CNN Newswire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[House approves Carbajal sponsored ‘red flag’ gun bill unlikely to pass Senate](https://keyt.com/politics/2022/06/09/house-approves-carbajal-sponsored-red-flag-gun-bill-unlikely-to-pass-senate/) Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoiding using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
