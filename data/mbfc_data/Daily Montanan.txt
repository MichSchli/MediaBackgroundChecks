History
The Daily Montanan is an online news website from Montana. Their
[about](https://dailymontanan.com/about/) statement reads, “The Daily Montanan is a nonprofit, nonpartisan source for trusted news, commentary and insight into statewide policy and politics beneath the Big Sky.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
According to their about page, the Daily Montanan is part of
[States Newsroom](https://statesnewsroom.com/about/), a national 501(c)(3) nonprofit [supported by grants and a coalition of donors and readers](https://statesnewsroom.com/support/). States Newsroom publishes 28 other [publications](https://statesnewsroom.com/newsrooms/). The Daily Montanan is transparent as they link to the States Newsroom donors page. Initially, when they launched, they got fiscal sponsorship from the Hopewell Foundation; however, once the IRS approved their application for 501(c)(3) charity certification, they severed their ties with Hopewell Foundation. There has been criticism of their involvement with [the Hopewell Foundation,](https://www.phoenixnewtimes.com/news/mysterious-funders-behind-new-arizona-media-outlet-10870282) and they have responded to the criticism [here](https://www.azmirror.com/blog/a-few-friday-thoughts-on-what-the-arizona-mirror-is-and-isnt/).
Analysis / Bias
The Daily Montanan publishes original news reporting on politics and policy from Montana. Coverage focuses on the legislature, the environment, and how national policy affects the people of Montana. Articles and headlines promote liberal perspectives through moderate, loaded language
[Montana Seeks to Insulate Nursing Homes From Future Financial Crises](https://dailymontanan.com/2023/02/25/montana-seeks-to-insulate-nursing-homes-from-future-financial-crises/).
They properly source, linking to credible sources such as
[whitehouse.gov](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/whitehouse-gov/), [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/), [Politico](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/politico/), [Center for Responsive Politics (Open Secrets)](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), and bls.gov (U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics).
Editorially, opinion pieces favor the left, such as this
[Montana’s Public Service Commission is broken. Let’s fix it.](https://dailymontanan.com/2023/02/27/montanas-public-service-commission-is-broken-lets-fix-it/). On the issues, the Daily Montanan promotes LGBT equality and has concerns about climate change. Finally, they frequently report negatively on Republican politicians, such as this [Conservative contradictions stacking up in Montana Legislature](https://dailymontanan.com/2023/02/17/conservative-contradictions-stacking-up-in-montana-legislature/). The Daily Montanan generally reports news factually; however, story selection and op-eds strongly favor a liberal perspective.
