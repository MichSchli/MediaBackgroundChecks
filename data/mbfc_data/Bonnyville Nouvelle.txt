History
The Bonnyville Nouvelle is an English-language newspaper in Bonnyville, Alberta, Canada. lakelandtoday.ca is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Bonnyville Nouvelle. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Bonnyville Nouvelle publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Bonnyville, Alberta region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[St. Paul man faces numerous charges after an alleged a break and enter](https://www.lakelandtoday.ca/local-news/st-paul-man-faces-numerous-charges-after-an-alleged-a-break-and-enter-6484278). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Bonnyville Nouvelle’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[OPINION: Food shortages need local discussions](https://www.lakelandtoday.ca/opinion/opinion-food-shortages-need-local-discussions-6132357). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
