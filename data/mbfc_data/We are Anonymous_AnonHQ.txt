History
[Founded in 2014](https://whois.domaintools.com/anonhq.com), We Are Anonymous/AnonHQ publishes mostly fake news stories and conspiracies. The website lacks transparency as they do not name authors (anonymous), owners, or provide an about page.
Funded by / Ownership
We Are Anonymous, not surprisingly, does not disclose ownership as they are supposedly anonymous. Revenue is derived through advertising, donations, and an online store that sells branded merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
In review. We are Anonymous/AnonHQ frequently publish articles considered conspiracy theories or pseudoscience. For example, they report on
[chemtrails](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-chemtrails/) [Humans Are Being Poisoned By The Aluminum Found In Chemtrails](https://anonhq.com/humans-poisoned-aluminum-found-chemtrails/), Coronavirus conspiracies [China Begins Construction Of New Biolab With Extremely Deadly SARS & MERS Viruses](https://anonhq.com/china-begins-construction-of-new-biolab-with-extremely-deadly-sars-mers-viruses/). They also report on false flags [Anonymous Urgent Message to the World: Massive False Flag Warning in Effect](https://anonhq.com/anonymous-urgent-message-world-massive-false-flag-warning-effect/) and pseudoscience such as [miracle cancer cures](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-alternative-cancer-treatments/) such as this [There are Now 100 Scientific Studies Proving Cannabis, Cures Cancer](https://anonhq.com/now-100-scientific-studies-proving-cannabis-cures-cancer/). More or less, everything on this website is either misleading or false.
