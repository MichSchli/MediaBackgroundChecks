History
Founded in 1948, NBC 5 Dallas-Fort Worth – KXAS is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Fort Worth, Texas, United States. NBCdwf.com is the associated website covering news, weather, sports, crime, and lifestyle. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
NBC 5 Dallas-Fort Worth – KXAS is owned by NBCUniversal a subsidiary of Comcast, which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, NBC 5 Dallas-Fort Worth – KXAS covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Police Fatally Shoot Armed Man at Duncanville Field House; Campers Unharmed](https://www.nbcdfw.com/news/local/watch-gunfire-reported-outside-duncanville-field-house-campers-moved-to-rec-center/2990942/). Most stories are accompanied by videos from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other NBC-owned stations provide national and world news. All information is sourced from first-hand accounts and other credible media sources.
Editorially, NBC 5 Dallas-Fort Worth – KXAS typically does not produce opinion content; however, they report on national politics with a slight left-leaning bias in wording and story selection
[Biden to Visit Israel and ‘Pariah’ Saudi Arabia Next Month](https://www.nbcdfw.com/news/national-international/biden-to-visit-israel-and-pariah-saudi-arabia-next-month/2991752/). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering political issues.
