History
Founded in 2015, the Burrard Street Journal is a Canadian satire website.
[Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not disclose ownership, and revenue is derived through donations and advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Burrard Street Journal disclaims they are a satire on their
[about page](https://www.burrardstreetjournal.com/about-burrard-street-journal-canadian-satire-football-satire-website/). They state, “The Burrard Street Journal is an occasionally funny Canadian satire, parody and comedy news website based in Vancouver, BC.” While the satire found on this website covers many topics more mock the right such as this [Trump Declares Hillary Clinton Is Coronavirus ‘Patient Zero’](https://www.burrardstreetjournal.com/trump-declares-hillary-clinton-is-coronavirus-patient-zero/) and this [Donald Trump Jr. Tests Positive For Being A Dipshit](https://www.burrardstreetjournal.com/donald-trump-jr-tests-positive-for-being-a-dipshit/). In general, this is a left-leaning satire website.
