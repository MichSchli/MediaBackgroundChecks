History
The Grunion Gazette is a weekly newspaper published in Long Beach, California, United States. presstelegram.com/the-grunion/ is the website for the paper covering local news, sports, crime, lifestyle, and opinion.

Funded by / Ownership
The Grunion Gazette is owned by
[MediaNews Group](https://www.medianewsgroup.com/our-brands/) and published by Digital First Media, which publishes 190 newspapers across the USA. Digital First Media is owned by [Alden Global Capital LLC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alden_Global_Capital), a New York-Based Hedge Fund, described as “ [Vulture Capitalists](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/04/07/business/media/denver-post-opinion-owner.html)” by former [Denver Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/denver-post/) writers for the cuts made to newsrooms. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Grunion Gazette publishes local and regional news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Long Beach receives more than $30 million for interim housing projects](https://www.presstelegram.com/2022/08/24/long-beach-receives-more-than-30-million-for-interim-housing-projects/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/).
Editorially, the Grunion Gazette publishes an appropriate mix of right and left-leaning opinions. In general, they report factually and with credible sources, while op-eds are mostly balanced with differing viewpoints.
