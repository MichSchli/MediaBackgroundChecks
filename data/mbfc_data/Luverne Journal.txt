History
The Luverne Journal is a newspaper published in Luverne, Alabama. luvernejournal.com covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Luverne Journal is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias

[Representative Chris Sells makes monetary donations to Crenshaw County Public Schools](https://www.luvernejournal.com/2022/09/02/representative-chris-sells-makes-monetary-donations-to-crenshaw-county-public-schools/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
