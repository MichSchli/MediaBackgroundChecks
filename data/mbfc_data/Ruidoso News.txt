History
The Ruidoso News is a newspaper that serves Ruidoso, New Mexico, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. ruidoso.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Ruidoso News. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Ruidoso News covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Ruidoso, New Mexico area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[El Paso shoppers tell of terror as shots rang out at Cielo Vista Mall](https://www.alamogordonews.com/story/news/crime/2023/02/16/shooting-el-paso-cielo-vista-mall-survivors-share-their-stories/69910435007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Ruidoso News does not publish op-eds; therefore, editorial bias is determined by story selection, which minimally leans left. In general, they are factual and least biased.
