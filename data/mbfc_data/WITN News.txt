History
Founded in 1955 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, WITN News serves Greenville – New Bern – Washington, NC, and the surrounding area. Witn.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WITN is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WITN News provides news coverage for Greenville – New Bern – Washington, North Carolina, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Police release tentative ID of dismembered woman found in Goldsboro](https://www.witn.com/2021/06/02/police-release-tentative-id-of-dismembered-woman-found-in-goldsboro/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WITN News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
