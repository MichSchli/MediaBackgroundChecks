History
According to
[Whois,](http://whois.domaintools.com/hillreporter.com) the domain was purchased in May of 2018, and the website launched shortly thereafter. The Hill Reporter [described themselves](https://hillreporter.com/about) as “Our mission is to deliver factually correct news stories. We report on breaking political stories that speak to the current political climate in the United States.” However, at the time of this new review in December 2022, they no longer have an about page.
The Hill Reporter was founded by three individuals, James Kosur, formerly an editor at
[Business Insider](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/business-insider/), and brothers Ed and Brian Krassenstein, members of the “ [#Resistance](https://thinkprogress.org/who-are-the-krassenstein-brothers-251f68acf674/)” to President Donald Trump.
The current managing editor is
[Brett D. Gilman](https://hillreporter.com/about).
In 2019, the website was
[purchased](https://hillreporter.com/meet-hillreporter-coms-new-owner-roman-romanuk-47897) by Roman Romanuk, who is the CEO of [Prezna](https://www.linkedin.com/in/romanromanuk/), an internet marketing company based in Seattle, Washington. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website is owned by Roman Romanuk through its company Prezna. Revenue is derived through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Hill Reporter rarely publishes original news reporting but rather reports on existing news stories through staff writers. Story selection is strongly left-leaning, featuring moderately loaded emotional language such as this
[Today Trump Slammed China Over COVID-19 Transparency, In January, he Lauded Them](https://hillreporter.com/today-trump-slammed-china-over-covid-19-transparency-in-january-he-lauded-them-64446).
The Hill Reporter frequently reports negatively on former President Trump, such as this
[Trump Explodes At Reporter When She Calls Out His Propagandist Coronavirus Video That Ignores His February Downplaying Of The Disease](https://hillreporter.com/trump-explodes-at-reporter-when-she-calls-out-his-propagandist-coronavirus-video-that-ignores-his-february-downplaying-of-the-disease-64348) while reporting favorably on those who have a liberal perspective [Pelosi Says Trump’s Initial Coronavirus Denials Will Result In Deaths — ‘As The President Fiddles, People Are Dying](https://hillreporter.com/pelosi-says-trumps-initial-coronavirus-denials-will-result-in-deaths-as-the-president-fiddles-people-are-dying-62918).’ A review of numerous articles demonstrates consistent proper sourcing of information to such sources as the [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/), [The Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/), and [FactCheck.org](http://Factcheck.org).
Editorially, the Hill Reporter offers an
[opinion page](https://hillreporter.com/category/opinion) that strongly favors a liberal perspective, such as this [Former RNC Boss Blasts His Own Party’s Stance Over Their Obvious Voter Suppression Tactics](https://hillreporter.com/former-rnc-boss-blasts-his-own-partys-stance-over-their-obvious-voter-suppression-tactics-64122). In general, the Hill Reporter is factual in reporting while exhibiting a strong left-leaning bias through story selection, editorial positions, and wording.
