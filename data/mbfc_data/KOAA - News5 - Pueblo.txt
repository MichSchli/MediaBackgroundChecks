History
Founded in 1953, KOAA – News5 – Pueblo is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Pueblo, Colorado, United States. koaa.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KOAA – News5 – Pueblo is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KOAA – News5 – Pueblo covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Pueblo, Colorado area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Westcliffe man killed in crash with semi](https://www.koaa.com/news/covering-colorado/westcliffe-man-killed-in-crash-with-semi). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Former Atlanta mayor joins Biden administration](https://www.koaa.com/news/national/former-atlanta-mayor-joins-biden-administration). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
