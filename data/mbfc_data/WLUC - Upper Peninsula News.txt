History
Founded in 1956 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)/ [FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station, WLUC – Upper Peninsula News serves Marquette, Michigan, and the surrounding area. uppermichiganssource.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WLUC – Upper Peninsula News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WLUC – Upper Peninsula News provides news coverage for Marquette, Michigan, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Suspect in custody after barricading himself from police](https://www.uppermichiganssource.com/2022/07/17/suspect-custody-after-barricading-himself-police/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WLUC – Upper Peninsula News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
