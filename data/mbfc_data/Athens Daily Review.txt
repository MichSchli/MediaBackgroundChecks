History
The Athens Daily Review is a newspaper published in the city of Athens, Texas. athensreview.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Athens Daily Review. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Athens Daily Review covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Athens, Texas region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Paxton seeks to throw out Harris County late-cast ballots](https://www.athensreview.com/news/paxton-seeks-to-throw-out-harris-county-late-cast-ballots/article_cfd35780-c895-5b94-a867-04ba574d0dc7.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
