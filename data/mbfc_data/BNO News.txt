History
[Founded in 2007](https://bnonews.com/index.php/about-us/), BNO News is a news agency based in Tilburg, the Netherlands. It provides news wire services and breaking news to companies and media outlets. According to their [about page](https://bnonews.com/index.php/bno-news-wire-service/), “BNO News is a news organization that provides live coverage of breaking and developing stories, with impartiality, responsibility, and fairness among its core values. We strive to provide fast, accurate, and unbiased reporting.” [Read our profile on Netherlands media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/netherlands-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[According](https://bnonews.com/index.php/contact-us/) to their website, “BNO News B.V. is incorporated in the Netherlands and registered by the Chamber of Commerce under nr. 17264724.” Advertising generates revenue for the company. They do not specify if they license their content for a fee, but it is assumed they do.
Analysis / Bias
Like most news wire companies, it provides straightforward news reporting with minimal bias, such as this
[At least 9 people shot in Savannah, Georgia, neighborhood](https://bnonews.com/index.php/2021/06/at-least-9-shot-in-savannah-georgia/). This story is properly sourced to local media. As a news service that delivers breaking news, there is always a chance they will need to correct stories as more information becomes available. The website does not produce editorial/opinion content.
During the Covid-19 pandemic, BNO News provided stats and
[detailed information](https://bnonews.com/index.php/2020/08/covid-19-reinfection-tracker/) that was relied upon by major news services. Therefore, they support the consensus of science on this issue. In general, BNO News is high factual and low in bias.
