History
Founded in 2013, The Florida Squeeze is a left-leaning blog covering state and local politics from a Progressive Perspective. The Florida Squeeze was founded by Kartik Krishnaiyer.
The website does not have an about page or a clear description of ownership.

The Florida Squeeze is a news and opinion blog that reports from a progressive left perspective. The content of headlines and articles use strong loaded words such as this
[THE FLORIDA BOARD OF EDUCATION’S THREE HOURS OF MISOGYNY AND DEMAGOGUERY SHOULD AWAKEN THE MASSES TO THE CREEPING AUTOCRACY WE LIVE IN (BUT IT PROBABLY WON’T)](https://thefloridasqueeze.com/2021/08/18/the-florida-board-of-educations-three-hours-of-misogyny-and-demagoguery-should-awaken-the-masses-to-the-creeping-autocracy-we-live-in-but-it-probably-wont/). The Florida Squeeze typically sources its information from left-leaning sources.
Editorially, all stories reviewed favor a liberal perspective with many denigrating the right such as this
[TOURISM TUMBLING AS DESANTIS COVID GAMBLE BLOWS UP SPECTACULARLY](https://thefloridasqueeze.com/2021/08/27/tourism-tumbling-as-desantis-covid-gamble-blows-up-spectacularly/). In general, the news is reasonably factual but holds a one-sided progressive opinion left bias.
