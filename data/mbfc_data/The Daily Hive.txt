History
Founded in 2008, Daily Hive (formerly Vancity Buzz) is a local news and lifestyle media outlet serving Canada and the Pacific Northwest of the United States. According to their
[about page](https://dailyhive.com/page/about-us), “Daily Hive embraces the unique qualities that exist in a city and reports it to produce genuine conversation among communities. With unique thematic channels, such as Dished, Mapped, and Urbanized, Daily Hive delivers top-tier information to its local audience and advertisers in Vancouver, Toronto, Calgary, Montreal, Edmonton, Seattle, and Portland.” [Read our profile on Canadian Media and Government](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Daily Hive is owned by its founders Manny Bahia and Karm Sumal. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Daily Hive Covers news and events from Vancouver, Toronto, Calgary, Montreal, Edmonton, Seattle, and Portland. They also feature subchannels (Brands) such as DH News – News, Dished – Food, Urbanized – Real estate, architecture, urban design, transportation, and urban issues, Venture – Technology and business, Offside – Sports, Listed – Events, Mapped – Travel, Grow – Cannabis, Colony Digital – Digital marketing agency, and ID Agency – Influencer agency.
News articles contain moderately loaded emotional language, such as this
[Vancouver restaurant posts savage signs shooing away anti-maskers](https://dailyhive.com/vancouver/6-degrees-eatery-signs-anti-maskers). The articles reviewed were properly sourced to credible outlets and information.
Editorially, the Daily Hive tends to lean slightly left with stories promoting action on climate change, such as this
[Unprecedented heat dome fails to spark climate change discussion in BC politics](https://dailyhive.com/vancouver/heat-wave-bc-politics). They also report accurately on Covid-19 through the promotion of social distancing and vaccinations [Higher vaccine coverage needed to relax mask mandates: Tam](https://dailyhive.com/vancouver/mask-mandates-delta-vaccine). In general, the Daily Hive reports news factually and holds a left-leaning editorial bias.
