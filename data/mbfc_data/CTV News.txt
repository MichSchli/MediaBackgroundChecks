History
Established in
[1961](https://www.broadcasting-history.ca/listing_and_histories/ctv-television-network) and based in Canada, CTV News is the news division of CTV Television Network. The CTV News [website](https://www.ctvnews.ca/) features Canada and world news, politics, sports, entertainment, and more. CTV’s parent company BCE Inc. (Bell Canada Enterprises), was founded by [Charles Fleetford Sise](https://www.thecanadianencyclopedia.ca/en/article/charles-fleetford-sise) in 1880. He was the second president of Bell Canada (The Bell Telephone Company of Canada).

Funded by / Ownership
[CTV Inc](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CTV_Television_Network). owns CTV, a subdivision of Bell Media Inc., a subsidiary of [BCE Inc](http://www.bce.ca/). (Bell Canada Enterprises), a holding company for [Bell Canada](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BCE_Inc.). Bell Media is headquartered in East Toronto, Ontario. [Gordon M. Nixon](http://www.bce.ca/governance/boardcommittee/boardmembers/gordon-nixon) is Chair of the Board of both BCE and Bell Canada. [George Cope](http://www.bce.ca/aboutbce/executiveteams) is the President & Chief Executive Officer of BCE and Bell Canada.
CTV News revenue is based on advertising, and you can find financial reports
[here](http://www.bce.ca/investors/financialperformance/quarterly). [CBC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbc-news-canadian-broadcasting/) (Canadian Broadcasting) reports a Government plan titled “ [Journalists question Liberal government’s $600M media bailout plan’](https://www.cbc.ca/news/politics/journalists-question-media-bailout-1.5147761). CTV reports, [“Minister vows transparency over $600M media fund, defends inclusion of Unifor”. ](https://www.ctvnews.ca/politics/minister-vows-transparency-over-600m-media-fund-defends-inclusion-of-unifor-1.4436796) It is not clear how much funding they received.
Analysis / Bias
In review, CTV News republishes articles from the
[Associated Press,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) [AFP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/afp-agence-france-presse/), and their national news agency, [The Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Further, CTV News publishes articles written by journalists such as
[“Truth Tracker: No, Justin Trudeau isn’t converting from Christianity to Islam](https://www.ctvnews.ca/politics/truth-tracker-no-justin-trudeau-isn-t-converting-from-christianity-to-islam-1.4477485).” [Justin Trudeau](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Justin_Trudeau) is the current Prime Minister of Canada. He is also the leader of the Liberal Party of Canada (Center-Left). When publishing articles regarding conservative Doug Ford, there is slightly loaded language such as this: [“Doug Ford’s chief of staff resigns one day after appointment controversy](https://www.ctvnews.ca/politics/doug-ford-s-chief-of-staff-resigns-one-day-after-appointment-controversy-1.4477766).” This story is sourced from the right-leaning [Globe and Mail](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-globe-and-mail/).
Editorially, CTV News does not endorse political candidates. Further, they do not publish opinion content; however, the website does publish the
[political blog](https://www.ctvnews.ca/politics/don-martin-s-blog) of Don Martin, who tends to [lean right](https://www.ctvnews.ca/politics/don-martin-s-blog/don-martin-kenney-is-a-dangerous-political-animal-unleashed-against-all-things-liberal-1.4405712) with his posts. Finally, a [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/) institute survey found that 71% of respondents [trust their news coverage](https://reutersinstitute.politics.ox.ac.uk/digital-news-report/2021/canada) and 12% do not, ranking them #2 in trust among the major English language Canadian news providers.
