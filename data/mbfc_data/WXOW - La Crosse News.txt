History
Founded in 1970, WXOW – La Crosse News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to La Crosse, Wisconsin, United States. wxow.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater La Crosse area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WXOW – La Crosse News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WXOW – La Crosse News publishes news for La Crosse and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[One dead in Trempealeau County ATV accident](https://www.wxow.com/news/top-stories/one-dead-in-trempealeau-county-atv-accident/article_9eebcf65-28e5-5856-b8ae-aebb1ca75399.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
