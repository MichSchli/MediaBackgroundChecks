History
The Bowen Island Undercurrent is an English-language newspaper in Bowen Island, British Columbia, Canada. bowenislandundercurrent.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Bowen Island Undercurrent. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Bowen Island Undercurrent publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Bown Island, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Amendments to Bowen’s development bylaws pass first reading.](https://www.bowenislandundercurrent.com/local-news/amendments-to-bowens-development-bylaws-pass-first-reading-6554785) The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Bowen Island Undercurrent’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Opinion: We have yet to see a real remedy for our broken health-care system](https://www.bowenislandundercurrent.com/economy-law-politics/kirk-lapointe-we-have-yet-to-see-a-real-remedy-for-our-broken-health-care-system-6555324). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
