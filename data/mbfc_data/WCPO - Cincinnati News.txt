History
Founded in 1948, WCPO – Cincinnati News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Cincinnati, Ohio, United States. WCPO.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WCPO – Cincinnati News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WCPO – Cincinnati News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Cincinnati, Ohio area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[2 men shot in Roselawn](https://www.wcpo.com/news/local-news/hamilton-county/cincinnati/roselawn/2-men-shot-in-roselawn). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Bidens to visit Uvalde, Texas on Sunday](https://www.wcpo.com/news/national/bidens-to-visit-uvalde-texas-on-sunday). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
