History
The Anderson Herald Bulletin is a newspaper published in the city of Anderson, Indiana. heraldbulletin.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Anderson Herald Bulletin. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Anderson Herald Bulletin covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Anderson, Indiana, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Alexandria woman sentenced to prison for sexual battery](https://www.heraldbulletin.com/news/local_news/alexandria-woman-sentenced-to-prison-for-sexual-battery/article_cd244b52-35ce-11ed-9f43-df292aa12179.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
