History
Founded in 1887, The Demopolis Times is a broadsheet newspaper published in Demopolis, Alabama. demopolistimes.com covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Demopolis Times is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Demopolis Times primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Marengo County Commission swears in new member.](https://www.demopolistimes.com/2022/07/14/marengo-county-commission-swears-in-new-member/) The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Demopolis Times labels their opinion section appropriately with more op-eds favoring the right. In general, the Demopolis Times is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
