History
Launched in 1921, by
[Ismael Pérez Pazmiño](https://corporativo.eluniverso.com/quienessomos/directores.htm), [El Universo](https://www.eluniverso.com/quienes-somos/) is an Ecuadorian national daily newspaper based in Guayaquil, Ecuador. The newspaper is family-owned, and Carlos Pérez Barriga currently leads El Universo. In 2010, former president Rafael Correa filed [a suit](https://cpj.org/2012/02/el-universo-sentence-a-dark-precedent-for-free-pre/) against the owners of El Universo (Carlos, César, Nicolas Pérez Barriga, and Editor Emilio Palacio) due to “Palacio’s column titled “NO to lies.” Defamation, slander, and libel fall under the [criminal code in Ecuador](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/ecuador-media-profile/).
El Universo covers national and international news and politics, Guayaquil news, sports, business, and entertainment. They also have
[supplements](https://corporativo.eluniverso.com/quienessomos/producto.htm#4) such as Mi Mundo (Children’s Magazine), La Revista (Fashion), DxT (Sports).

Analysis / Bias
In 2021 Reporters Without Borders ranked
[Ecuador](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/ecuador-media-profile/) 96/180 in their Press Freedom Index, citing a “Worrying increase in violence.” In Ecuador, defamation law is a criminal offense. The President of Ecuador is [ Guillermo Lasso,](https://guillermolasso.ec/) who leans [center-right.](https://www.france24.com/en/live-news/20210524-lasso-inaugurated-as-first-right-wing-ecuador-president-in-14-years)
El Universo is known for supporting conservative political views and being center-right on the political spectrum.
In review, El Universo publishes articles with emotionally loaded language such as
[“This is the foundation to which Guillermo Lasso will donate his salary](https://www.eluniverso.com/noticias/politica/esta-es-la-fundacion-a-la-que-guillermo-lasso-donara-su-sueldo-nota/)” and [“Guillermo Lasso announces that he will veto abortion bill for rape](https://www.eluniverso.com/noticias/politica/guillermo-lasso-anuncia-que-vetara-proyecto-de-aborto-por-violacion-nota/).” News reports related to the opposition have neutral headlines such as [“Pachakutik decides to revoke the expulsion of Assemblywoman Mireya Pazmiño.”](https://www.eluniverso.com/noticias/politica/pachakutik-decide-revocar-la-expulsion-de-la-asambleista-mireya-pazmino-nota/) The Pachakutik is an indigenous left-wing party in Ecuador. El Universo utilizes national sources such as factually mixed right-center biased [Ecuavisa](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/ecuavisa/) (Ecuadorian free-to-air television network). When it comes to sourcing, El Universo covers foreign politics through credible sources such as Agencia EFE, S.A. (Spanish international news agency from Spain), and [AFP (Agence France Presse](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/afp-agence-france-presse/)). In general, El Universo reports news factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
