History
Neon Nettle is an alternative and independent news source. According to their
[about page](http://www.neonnettle.com/about), “Neon Nettle was launched as an educational and thought-provoking publication. At Neon Nettle, we believe the mainstream has become less valid as it continues its ongoing practices of censorship and engineered narratives.” Neon Nettle is located in London, England, and the senior editor is [Jack Murphy](http://www.neonnettle.com/contact). [Read our profile on the UK government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not list who owns it. Neon Nettle generates revenue through donations and advertising.
Analysis / Bias
Neon Nettle is a right-wing conspiracy and pseudoscience website. There are many articles about
[Aliens](https://web.archive.org/web/20170301044711/https://neonnettle.com/videos/607-4chan-first-leaked-image-of-reptile-alien-analysed-by-researchers), [New World Order](https://web.archive.org/web/20180115022723/https://neonnettle.com/news/1839-george-soros-i-will-take-down-president-trump-for-the-nwo), etc. They have a [Pants on Fire claim](http://www.politifact.com/punditfact/statements/2017/apr/04/blog-posting/doctors-deaths-were-not-connected-fake-news-websit/) with [Politifact](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/politifact/) and are listed on their [fake news list](http://www.politifact.com/punditfact/article/2017/apr/20/politifacts-guide-fake-news-websites-and-what-they/?platform=hootsuite). They also have a [Mostly False claim](https://www.snopes.com/fact-check/pedophile-prison-death/) via [Snopes](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/snopes/) and another [here](https://www.snopes.com/fact-check/law-bans-antifa/). Really the best way to discover this site is actually to visit it and see it for yourself. I suggest a tinfoil hat.
