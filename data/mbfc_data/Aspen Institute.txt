History
Founded in 1949, the Aspen Institute is a research institute and think tank that promotes the creation of a free, just, and equitable society. According to their
[about page](https://www.aspeninstitute.org/what-we-do/), “the Institute drives change through dialogue, leadership, and action to help solve the most important challenges facing the United States and the world.” The current President and CEO are [Daniel R. Porterfield](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniel_R._Porterfield). [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Aspen Institute is a nonprofit organization that is primarily funded through donations. Large donors include the Gates Foundation, Carnegie Corporation, and the Ford Foundation.
[According](https://web.archive.org/web/20200901175135/https://static.wixstatic.com/ugd/3ba8a1_4f06e99f35d4485b801f8dbfe33b6a3f.pdf) to the [Center for International Policy](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-international-policy/), they have received “sizeable donations from undemocratic regimes in Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates.”
Analysis / Bias
The Aspen Institute produces seminars, policy programs, conferences, and leadership development initiatives. The website features a blog that promotes news and information with left-leaning bias such as this
[Environmental Change, and Global Health Are Two Sides of the Same Coin](https://www.aspeninstitute.org/blog-posts/environmental-change-and-global-health-are-two-sides-of-the-same-coin/). This story is properly sourced to [NASA](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nasa/), [ProPublica](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/propublica/), and [Science Direct](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/sciencedirect/).
Editorially, they report favorably on
[Joe Biden](https://www.aspenideas.org/index.php/sessions/metoo-times-up-what-now-ensuring-opportunity-for-all-women-and-girls) and negatively on former President Trump [Anti-Muslim, Anti-Refugee Rhetoric Poses a Big Threat to Our Communities](https://newvoicesfellows.aspeninstitute.org/anti-muslim-anti-refugee-rhetoric-poses-a-big-threat-to-our-communities/?_ga=2.220956444.566534003.1621771866-1431209553.1621771866). In general, they report news factually and with a left-leaning editorial bias.
