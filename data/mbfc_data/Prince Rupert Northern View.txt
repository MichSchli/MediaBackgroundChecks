History
Prince Rupert Northern View is a newspaper published in Prince Rupert, British Columbia, Canada. The website thenorthernview.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Prince Rupert, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Prince Rupert Northern View. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Prince Rupert Northern View covers news for Prince Rupert and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[B.C. expands green innovation initiative to Prince Rupert port](https://www.thenorthernview.com/news/b-c-expands-green-expansion-initiative-to-prince-rupert-port/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Prince Rupert Northern View does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
