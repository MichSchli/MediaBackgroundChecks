History
[Founded in 1947](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Bay_Times), The East Bay Times, formerly the Contra Costa Times, is a daily newspaper based in Walnut Creek, California, U.S. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The East Bay Times is owned by MediaNews Group, formerly
[Digital First Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_First_Media), which owns nearly 100 newspapers across the USA. Digital First Media is, in turn, owned by [Alden Global Capital LLC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alden_Global_Capital), a New York Based Hedge Fund, which has been described as “ [Vulture Capitalists](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/04/07/business/media/denver-post-opinion-owner.html)” by former Denver Post writers for the cuts made to newsrooms. Revenue is derived through subscription fees and advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the East Bay Times reports local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[East Bay man, arrested while allegedly ‘shedding clothes’ after Oakland shooting, charged with murdering teen](https://www.eastbaytimes.com/2021/03/19/east-bay-man-arrested-while-allegedly-shedding-clothes-after-oakland-shooting-charged-with-murdering-teen/). National and international news is usually covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the website features an area called the Canniforian, which covers the California Marijuana industry. The East Bay Times also usually endorses Democratic candidates such as Joe Biden in the 2020 Presidential election. In general, the news is reported factually and with a left-leaning editorial bias.
