History
The Santa Barbara Independent was created from the merger of the Santa Barbara News & Review (established 1973) And The Santa Barbara Weekly (established 1984). The Santa Barbara Independent then implemented Web-based columns and news articles at
[independent.com](http://www.independent.com/). Independent is an online community newspaper that includes news stories, arts, local politics, reviews of restaurants, and culture. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Brandi Rivera is the owner and publisher of the Santa Barbara Independent. Revenue is derived through subscription fees and classifieds.
Analysis / Bias
The Santa Barbara Independent is an alternative newspaper serving the city of Santa Barbara and the surrounding communities. The paper covers local news, entertainment, lifestyle, politics, sports, and business. Articles and headlines use moderately loaded emotional language such as this
[Water War Breaks Out as Drought Descends](https://www.independent.com/2021/06/23/water-war-breaks-out-as-drought-descends/).
Editorially, on opinion pieces, the paper leans towards the left with the choice of words such as this referrng to former President Trump:
[Our Disloyal President White Supremacist Violence Is Not an Issue with ‘Many Sides’](http://www.independent.com/news/2017/aug/14/our-disloyal-president/) and this supporting women’s right’s [Respect for Women in Politics Remains in Short Supply](https://www.independent.com/2021/08/20/respect-for-women-in-politics-remains-in-short-supply/). Further, in 2024, they [endorsed](https://www.independent.com/2024/02/15/endorsements-for-the-march-5-2024-presidential-primary-election/) current Democratic President Joe Biden. In general, they report news factually and with moderate liberal bias.
