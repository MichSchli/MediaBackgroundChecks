History
Press for Truth does not have an about page; however, according to their
[Facebook page](https://www.facebook.com/PressForTruth), they are founded by Dan Dicks, who is an “investigative journalist and an award-winning documentary filmmaker.” Press for Truth is an “independent media outlet which produces videos and documentary films about issues that the mainstream media often fails to report on.” [Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Press for Truth lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership; however, the primary contact is Dan Hicks. Donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Press for Truth publishes news stories and investigations that are generally credible and properly sourced. However, they also explore conspiracy theories such as the
[Bilderbergs](https://pressfortruth.ca/video-reports/bilderberg-extensive-coverage/) and [911 Truth](https://pressfortruth.ca/video-reports/911-extensive-coverage/). They sometimes use Russian propaganda sources such as [RT](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/rt-news/) and [Sputnik](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/sputnik-news/) and legendary conspiracy website [Infowars](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/infowars-alex-jones/).
At the time of this review, they promote Covid-19 misinformation and often rail against the Trudeau government
[Theo Fleury Opens Up About Sexual Abuse Comparing Former Abuser To Current Canadian Government!!!](https://pressfortruth.ca/theo-fleury-opens-up-about-sexual-abuse-comparing-former-abuser-to-current-canadian-government/) In general, they promote conspiracy theories and pseudoscience favored by the Right.
