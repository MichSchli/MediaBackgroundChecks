History
Founded in 1953, WKRN – Nashville News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Nashville, Tennessee. The associated website is wkrn.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WKRN – Nashville News is owned by
[Nexstar Media Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nexstar_Media_Group), which owns around 200 TV stations nationwide. The founder, chairman, president, and CEO of Nexstar is Perry A. Sook. [According](https://www.opensecrets.org/pacs/pacgot.php?cmte=C00567388&cycle=2018) to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Nexstar Media group gave more money to Republicans in the 2018 election cycle (45% to Democrats, 55% to Republicans). However, in 2020 they gave more to Democrats (55% Democrats, 45% to Republicans). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WKRN – Nashville News website publishes news for the greater Nashville, Tennessee area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias such as this:
[Manhunt underway for suspect who dragged Perry County officer with vehicle](https://www.wkrn.com/news/local-news/manhunt-underway-for-suspect-who-dragged-perry-county-officer-with-vehicle/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through Nexstar Media Wire, the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[House sends debt limit hike to Biden, staving off default](https://fox5sandiego.com/news/politics/house-sends-debt-limit-hike-to-biden-staving-off-default/). Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoids using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
