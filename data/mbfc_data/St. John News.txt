History
The St. John News is a newspaper published in St. John, Kansas. sjnewsonline.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the St. John Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The St. John News is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The St. John News covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater St. John, Kansas, area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Highly pathogenic Avian Influenza (HPAI) returns to Kansas](https://www.sjnewsonline.com/2022/10/12/highly-pathogenic-avian-influenza-hpai-returns-to-kansas/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
