History
[Founded in 2020](https://whois.domaintools.com/patriotuproar.com), Patriot Uproar is an anonymously published conservative news and opinion blog. According to its [about page](https://patriotuproar.com/about-the-patriot-uproar/), “Our goal is to give a voice to the unheard patriots across America – the silent majority shall be silent no longer!”
The website lacks transparency as they do not name authors or ownership.
