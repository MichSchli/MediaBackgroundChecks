History
Founded in 2006, Black Agenda Report is a news and opinion website and radio program. Glen Ford, Bruce Dixon, Margaret Kimberley, and Leutisha Stills are the founders. Black Agenda Report covers news related to racism, labor, and socialist left-leaning policy.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Black Agenda Report does not clearly disclose ownership on the website. Donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the website features the tagline “News, Commentary and Analysis from the Black Left.” Upon review, this is a very fair description as most articles favor the left and are concerned with black issues. Headlines and articles feature emotionally loaded wording such as this
[Trump is Wrong, Joe Biden is not the “Radical Left.” He is an Enemy of Humanity.](https://blackagendareport.com/index.php/trump-wrong-joe-biden-not-radical-left-he-enemy-humanity) This story is correctly sourced from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/), [Business Insider](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/business-insider/), and [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/). As can be seen from this article, they do not have a favorable opinion of establishment Democrats.
Here is another example:
[Biden Offers Nothing But More War, Austerity, and White Supremacy – Without Trump](https://blackagendareport.com/index.php/biden-offers-nothing-more-war-austerity-and-white-supremacy-without-trump). While they do report negatively on former President Trump, they usually do this in the context of exposing corporate centrist Democrats [Why a United Front Against Trump is Dangerous Territory for the Left](https://blackagendareport.com/danger_of_front_against_trump). The website also frequently promotes socialism: socialism [ is Still the Way Forward: Combating Neo-Liberal Imperialist Lies.](https://blackagendareport.com/thewayforward) Editorially, Black Agenda Report aligns with the socialist left and reports news Mostly Factually, but lacks transparency.
