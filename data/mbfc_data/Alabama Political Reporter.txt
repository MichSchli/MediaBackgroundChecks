History
The Alabama Political Reporter is a
[website](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alabama_Political_Reporter) that covers Alabama politics. It is published five days each week. According to their [about page](https://www.alreporter.com/about/), “APR is an independent, non-partisan media company that has positioned itself — via its robust, interactive website — as the most trusted source for political news, analysis, and commentary in the state of Alabama.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Bill Britt owns the Alabama Political Reporter. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Alabama Political Reporter reports political news related to Alabama. News is reported with minimal loaded words and typically sourced through direct quoting versus linking. Stories cover both sides of the political spectrum with good balance. Alabama Political Reporter also features opinion pieces that both favor the right and left. Again, there is a reasonable balance with opinion pieces with slightly more favoring of the right.
