Country: USA

History
Founded under a new domain in 2019 The Daily Buzz is a multimedia and news website that covers a variety of topics, including politics. According to their
[about page](https://the-daily.buzz/about/) “The Daily Buzz is a multimedia digital platform, striving to deliver the best the internet has to offer: from the uplifting to the sidesplitting. Our Meme Masters and Video Virtuosos serve up buzzworthy content and news on a daily basis that’s sure to make even your most hipster friend crack an unironic smile.”
The website lacks transparency as they do not disclose editors or owners.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Daily Buzz does not disclaim ownership and there does not openly appear to be a means of revenue. However, they state they are a “participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking the-daily.buzz to Amazon properties.”
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Daily Buzz generally publishes news that is summarized from other sources that use moderately loaded words such as this
[US President Expected to Trump Competition and Seal Re-Election](https://the-daily.buzz/us-president-expected-to-trump-competition-and-seal-re-election/). This story is properly sourced to [Investopedia](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/investopedia/). Many stories appear to favor the left such as this [Senate Dems Trolled Trump With Red Ink Revisions, Like Your Last Teacher.](https://the-daily.buzz/a/senate-dems-decided-to-troll-trump-with-red-ink-revisions-like-your-last-teache) This story is also appropriately sourced to [NBC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/), [USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), and [The Wall Street Journal](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/wall-street-journal/).
Editorially, most stories favor the left, but are sourced to credible media such as this
[Good Christian Family Man Bill O’Reilly Loses Custody Of His Kids, Was Accused Of Domestic Violence](https://the-daily.buzz/good-christian-family-man-bill-oreilly-loses-custody-of-his-kids-was-accused-of-domestic-violence/). In general, the news reporting on this site is factual, unfortunately, they are not transparent and lack credibility.
