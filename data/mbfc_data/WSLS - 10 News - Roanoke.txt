History
Founded in 1952, WSLS – 10 News – Roanoke is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Roanoke, Virginia, United States. Wsls.com is the associated website that reports news, crime, sports, weather, and lifestyle for the greater Roanoke area. [Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WSLS – 10 News – Roanoke is owned by
[Graham Holdings](https://www.ghco.com/), which owns seven TV stations and the website [Slate](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/slate/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WSLS – 10 News – Roanoke provides news, sports, weather, crime reporting, and lifestyle for the greater Roanoke metro area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this
[Clifton Forge man pleads guilty to abducting Giles County boy from church](https://www.wsls.com/news/local/2022/07/15/clifton-forge-man-pleads-guilty-to-abducting-giles-county-boy-from-church/). Many stories provide videos from the WJXT TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally offers national and world news.
Editorially, they do not produce Op-Eds; however, they do report on politics through the Associated Press and original reporting. Political news is generally balanced and presented with minimal bias, such as this
[Democrat Beto O’Rourke adds $27M to race for Texas governor](https://www.wsls.com/news/politics/2022/07/15/democrat-beto-orourke-adds-27m-to-race-for-texas-governor/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
