History
KCBX – NPR – San Luis Obispo is an NPR FM radio station (90.1 FM) based in San Luis Obispo, California, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KCBX – NPR – San Luis Obispo is owned and broadcast by KCBX Public Radio. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.

[NPR](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/npr/) and often utilizes minimally loaded language, such as this [Rep.
