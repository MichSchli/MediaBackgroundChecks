History
[BDnews24](https://aboutus.bdnews24.com/about-us-2/) is a free-to-access 24/7 English-language news portal based in Bangladesh. According to its about page, the news site was initially founded as a news agency owned by Bangladesh News 24 Hours Ltd. In 2016, Toufique Imrose Khalidi and Asif Mahmood became the new owners of BDnews24. Toufique Imrose Khalidi is also the editor-in-chief of BDnews24. [Read our profile on Bangladesh media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bangladesh-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
In 2023,
[Reporters Without Borders](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reporters-without-borders/) ranked Bangladesh 161/180 in their [Press Freedom Index,](https://rsf.org/en/bangladesh) stating there is a “disturbing increase in press freedom violations.” Sheikh Hasina has been the country’s Prime Minister since 2009. She is the leader of the Center-Left Awami League. [Read our profile on Bangladesh media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bangladesh-media-profile/)
In review, BDnews24 publishes news with a pro-government tone, such as this
[“Hasina hooked on fishing as she goes out for a breath of fresh air amid virus blow”](https://bdnews24.com/people/2020/09/09/hasina-hooked-on-fishing-as-she-goes-out-for-a-breath-of-fresh-air-amid-virus-blow) and [“The new cabinet: Full of surprises, and a first in Bangladesh](https://opinion.bdnews24.com/2019/01/07/the-new-cabinet-full-of-surprises-and-a-first-in-bangladesh/).” They also report news related to the opposition with a neutral tone [“RAB arrests cartoonist Kishore, writer Mushtaq Ahmed in digital security case.”](https://bdnews24.com/bangladesh/2020/05/06/rab-arrests-cartoonist-kishore-writer-mushtaq-ahmed-in-digital-security-case) When covering world news pertaining to the USA, they cover the Tformer rump administration with a critical tone by republishing [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/) articles such as [“What Trump and toxic cops have in common.”](https://bdnews24.com/world/americas/2020/06/01/what-trump-and-toxic-cops-have-in-common) They generally source information poorly and report one-sided government propaganda through bias by omission.
