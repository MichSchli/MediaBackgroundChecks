History
The Beavercreek News-Current is a newspaper based in Beavercreek, Ohio, serving Beavercreek and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at beavercreeknewscurrent.net.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Beavercreek News-Current and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
