History
The Pharos Tribune is a newspaper published in the city of Logansport, Indiana. pharostribune.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Pharos Tribune. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Pharos Tribune covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Logansport, Indiana, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Police announce arrest in Delphi double murder case](https://www.pharostribune.com/news/article_446a3cea-5923-11ed-90e2-87658270b9f5.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
