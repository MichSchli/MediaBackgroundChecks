History
The Midweek is a newspaper published in Dekalb, Illinois. shawlocal.com/midweek-news/ is the website covering news, crime, sports, and business for the Dekalb area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Midweek is owned by
[Shaw Media](https://shawmedia.com/our-markets/), which is an American, Dixon, Illinois-based publisher of about 80 newspapers and news websites in Illinois and Iowa. Revenue is derived from sponsored content, advertising, and subscription fees.
