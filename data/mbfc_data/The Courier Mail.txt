History
[Founded in 1846](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Courier-Mail), The Courier-Mail is a daily tabloid newspaper published in Brisbane, Australia. The current editor is Sam Weir. [Read our profile on Australia media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/australia-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Courier-Mail is owned by
[News Corp Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/News_Corp_Australia) and is funded through subscriptions and advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Courier-Mail is a newspaper that utilizes journalists for local news, and for international news, they source the
[AP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) as well as News Corp writers. There is the moderate use of loaded words in headlines such as this: [Election timetable sharpens MPsâ€™ minds](https://www.couriermail.com.au/news/breaking-news/election-timetable-sharpens-mps-minds/news-story/85326010c48da6e3a8b52697457316dd). Sourcing for this article comes from the least biased AP. When it comes to USA news The Courier-Mail [does not have a favorable opinion](https://www.couriermail.com.au/business/work/saudi-first-republicans-blast-trump-over-support-for-middle-eastern-nation/news-story/834e7c482a36d38645c914e37471f764) of USA President Donald Trump, but this does not convey a right or left bias as many affiliated with the right-center is opposed to former President Trump. However, when it comes to Australian politics, they tend to favor a more [free market right leaning policy](https://www.couriermail.com.au/business/economy/six-path-ways-to-australias-economic-armageddon/news-story/5f11849237d1621569e85a9f2c2a1948).
