History
WSHU – NPR – Fairfield is an NPR FM radio station (91.1 FM) based in Fairfield, Connecticut, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WSHU – NPR – Fairfield is owned and broadcast by Sacred Heart University. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
