History
Radio Free Europe / Radio Liberty (RFE/RL) is a private, nonprofit, multimedia broadcasting corporation founded and funded by the US government during the Cold War to counter Soviet propaganda. According to their
[about page](https://pressroom.rferl.org/p/6092.html), their mission is “RFE/RL journalists report the news in 20 countries where a free press is banned by the government or not fully established. We provide what many people cannot get locally: uncensored news, responsible discussion, and open debate.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The U.S. Congress currently funds RFE/RL through the Broadcasting Board of Governors (BBG). The BBG is a bipartisan federal agency overseeing all U.S international broadcasting services. The RFE/RL president is
[Jamie Fly](https://pressroom.rferl.org/management-and-governance), and the Vice President/Editor-in-Chief of programming is [Daisy Sindelar](https://pressroom.rferl.org/management-and-governance).
Analysis / Bias
In review, Radio Free Europe / Radio Liberty (RFE/RL) publishes some articles without hyperlinked sourcing. However, at the end of each piece, they source their information by writing, “With reporting by
[AP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [AFP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/afp-agence-france-presse/), and [Reuters.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/)” They also use other news service agencies, such as in this [article](https://www.rferl.org/a/putin-inauguration-russia-navalny-protests/29211663.html) where they list “With reporting by RFE/RL’s Russian Service, AP, [Bloomberg](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bloomberg/), Interfax, Gazeta.ru, Dozhd TV, [TASS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/russian-news-agency-tass/), and [The Moscow Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/moscow-times/).”
Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty (RFE/RL) utilizes headlines that are written in a neutral tone, without bias favoring one side or another:
[“Russian, Tehran Concerned Ahead Of Trump Decision On Iran Deal”](https://www.rferl.org/a/oil-prices-hit-highest-levels-november-2014-ahead-trump-decision-iran-nuclear-deal/29214363.html) and “ [Armenian Protest Leader Holds Rally Ahead Of May 8 Vote.”](https://www.rferl.org/a/armenian-protest-leader-holds-rally-ahead-of-may-8-vote/29213024.html) They also conduct interviews and [produce reports](https://www.rferl.org/a/our-children-aren-t-lab-rats-russian-transplant-patients-warn-of-dangers-of-generic-drugs/29211086.html) through their correspondents in respective countries; however, it is difficult to determine their accuracy or if the interviews occurred. From a pure news reporting aspect, RFE/RL is balanced and sourced from credible media outlets.
Radio Free Europe was initially started as a Government-run propaganda outlet to influence Europe. Over time, they moved away from the propaganda; however, in 2016, they were
[criticized](https://www.huffpost.com/entry/radio-free-europe-posted-stealth-us-facebook-ads_n_5b5538e8e4b0fd5c73c6ae4e) for running pro-Government Facebook ads.
