History
AlternativeNews.com is a news aggregation website that pulls news from conspiracy, pseudoscience, and far-right-leaning sources. The website has the tagline “The Independent News Source for Free-Thinking People.” AlternativeNews.com does not have an about page, disclaimer, or ownership disclosure.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website is owned by Mike Adams, who owns
[several](https://www.mcgill.ca/oss/article/quackery/mike-adams-building-alternate-reality-online) so-called alternative news sites such as [Natural News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/natural-news/). AlternativeNews.com does not have advertising but serves as a portal to websites owned by Mike Adams, increasing traffic on the websites he owns.
Analysis / Bias
In review, AlternativeNews.com is a news aggregation website that links to many of Mike Adams’s Alternative News sites, such as Natural News,
[News Target](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/news-target/), [Science.news](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/science-news-2/), [Climate.news](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/climate-news/), [Trump.news](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/trump-news/), and [Liberty.news](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/liberty-news-2/). AlternativeNews.com also features the [Good Gopher](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/goodgopher/) browser that links to Mike Adams’s websites. For background Natural News and News Target both promote right-wing conspiracies and quackery-level pseudoscience. See their hyperlinked reviews above. This source’s purpose is to promote Mike Adams’ websites and promote junk science, conspiracies, and quackery-level pseudoscience. Mike Adam’s websites have been called an “ [empire of misinformation.](https://www.mcgill.ca/oss/article/quackery/mike-adams-building-alternate-reality-online)“
