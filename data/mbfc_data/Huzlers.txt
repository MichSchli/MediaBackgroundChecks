History
Founded in 2013, Huzlers is a satire website that disclaims on their
[about page](https://www.huzlers.com/about-us/) “Huzlers is a satirical and fictional entertainment blog producing content in such a complex way that it could not conveniently be presented or represented anywhere else but Huzlers!” [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Huzlers is owned by
[Pablo Reyes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pablo_Reyes_Jr) and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Huzler’s brand of satire is often controversial and widely shared such as this
[Scientists Discover New HIV Strain, That Explodes Your P*nis](https://www.huzlers.com/scientists-discover-new-hiv-strain-that-explodes-your-pnis/). Another story reads [Tekashi 6ix9ine Reportedly Reveals Identity of Infamous Zodiac Killer To Judge As Bonus](https://www.huzlers.com/tekashi-6ix9ine-reportedly-reveals-identity-of-infamous-zodiac-killer-to-judge-as-bonus/). Most stories poke fun at celebrities. While other rating sources have given a red shield to this site, we give them the satire rating as they fully disclaim this is a satire website. Not sure how anyone can be confused.
