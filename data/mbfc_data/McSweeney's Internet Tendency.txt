History
[Founded in 1998](https://www.mcsweeneys.net/pages/about-us) by [Dave Eggers](https://www.charlestoncitypaper.com/charleston/tips-for-getting-published-on-mcsweeneys-internet-tendency/Content?oid=4894593), McSweeney’s Internet Tendency is a humor website that openly disclaims satire. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
McSweeney’s, a nonprofit publishing company based in San Francisco, owns McSweeney’s Internet Tendency. The website is funded through donations and a
[store](https://store.mcsweeneys.net/) that sells its books.
Analysis / Bias
In review, McSweeney’s Internet Tendency publishes satire and humorous stories such as this:
[PUMPKIN SPICE: NOW FOR MEN](https://www.mcsweeneys.net/articles/pumpkin-spice-now-for-men). They also frequently publish political satire, with President Donald Trump being a frequent topic [I AM GODZILLA, KING OF MONSTERS, AND I TOO WAS CONTACTED BY THE TRUMP ADMINISTRATION TO INVESTIGATE HUNTER BIDEN](https://www.mcsweeneys.net/articles/i-am-godzilla-king-of-monsters-and-i-too-was-contacted-by-the-trump-administration-to-investigate-hunter-biden). In general, this a longstanding, well-respected humor website that fully disclaims its satirical nature.
