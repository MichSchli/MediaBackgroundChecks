History
Opposition Report is a liberal news and opinion website that does not have an about page. A
[Whois search](http://whois.domaintools.com/oppositionreport.com) reveals the domain was purchased in February 2017.

[Addicting Info,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/addicting-info/) a popular left-biased news and information website. The website appears to be funded primarily through online advertising.
