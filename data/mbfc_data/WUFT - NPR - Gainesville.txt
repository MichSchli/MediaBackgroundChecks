History
WUFT – NPR – Gainesville is an NPR FM radio station (89.1 FM) based in Gainesville, Florida, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WUFT – NPR – Gainesville is owned and broadcast by the University of Florida. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
