History
Founded in 2005 and a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station, KIIT – North Platte News serves North Platte, Nebraska, and the surrounding area. knopnews2.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KIIT – North Platte News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KIIT – North Platte News provides news coverage for North Platte, Nebraska, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Escaped inmate arrested after pursuit hits 110 mph in Norfolk, NE](https://www.knopnews2.com/2022/12/06/escaped-inmate-arrested-after-pursuit-hits-110-mph-norfolk-ne/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KIIT – North Platte News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
