History
The Crossville Chronicle is a newspaper published in Crossville, Tennessee. crossville-chronicle.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Crossville Chronicle. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Crossville Chronicle covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Crossville, Tennessee region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Sixth Memphis officer disciplined in Nichols beating, death](https://www.crossville-chronicle.com/news/tennessee_news/sixth-memphis-officer-disciplined-in-nichols-beating-death/article_7c648c98-eeb9-5cb0-96f0-bc186570d4c8.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
