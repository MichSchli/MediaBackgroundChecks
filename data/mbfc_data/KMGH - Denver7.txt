History
Founded in 1953, KMGH – Denver7 is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Denver, Colorado, United States. thedenverchannel.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KMGH – Denver7 is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KMGH – Denver7 covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Denver, Colorado area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Denver police chief says investigation into how LoDo bystanders were hurt could take days](https://www.thedenverchannel.com/news/local-news/denver-police-chief-says-investigation-into-how-lodo-bystanders-were-hurt-could-take-days). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Lawmakers, survivors laud gun violence prevention headway on anniversary of theater shooting](https://www.thedenverchannel.com/news/politics/lawmakers-survivors-laud-gun-violence-prevention-headway-on-anniversary-of-theater-shooting). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
