History
Founded in 1863, The Journal Gazette is a daily newspaper from Fort Wayne, Indiana.
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by The Journal Gazette Co., and revenue is generated through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Journal Gazette covers local news with journalists and reporters throughout the Fort Wayne region. For national news, they use the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). All news is reported with minimal loaded words and bias. The Journal Gazette always sources its information properly. [Editorially](https://herald-review.com/opinion/#tracking-source=menu-nav), there is a balance between right and left-leaning opinions with just a slight edge to the left.
