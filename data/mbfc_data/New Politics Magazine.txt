History
Founded in 1961, New Politics is an independent socialist journal that publishes news and commentary from a Democratic Socialist perspective. According to their
[about page](https://newpol.org/about/), New Politics is an “independent socialist forum for dialogue and debate on the left. It is committed to the advancement of the peace and anti-intervention movements. It stands in opposition to all forms of imperialism and is uncompromising in its defense of feminism and affirmative action.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
New Politics lacks transparency as they do openly disclose ownership. Revenue is derived through donations, subscription fees, and advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, New Politics reports news from a progressive democratic perspective with the use of loaded emotional language such as this
[The Border’s COVID-19 Pandemic: Crisis Upon Crisis](https://newpol.org/4430-2/). This story is republished from The American Program. Story selection always favors the left such as this [Protests over George Floyd’s Murder Open National Debates on Racism](https://newpol.org/protests-over-george-floyds-murder-open-national-debates-on-racism/), while frequently denigrating the right such as this [Protests Against Racism; LBGTQ Pride; Continuing Crisis; Trump’s Decline](https://newpol.org/protests-against-racism-lbgtq-pride-continuing-crisis-trumps-decline/). This story is not sourced at all.
Editorially, all stories favor the left and some are not sourced properly.
