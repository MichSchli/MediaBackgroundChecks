History
Hiawatha World is a newspaper published in Hiawatha, Kansas. The associated website is hiawathaworldonline.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns Hiawatha World and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Hiawatha World website publishes news for the greater Hiawatha, Kansas area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:

The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other large, credible media outlets cover national and international news.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds while political news coverage is balanced. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
