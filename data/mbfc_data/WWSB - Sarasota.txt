History
Founded in 1971 and an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station, WWSB – Sarasota serves Sarasota, Florida, and the surrounding area. Mysuncoast.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WWSB – Sarasota is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WWSB – Sarasota provides news coverage for Gainesville, Florida, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Bicyclist dies in crash on U.S 41 in Bradenton](https://www.mysuncoast.com/2021/11/03/bicyclist-dies-crash-us-41-bradenton/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WWSB – Sarasota does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
