History
The Bureau County Republican is a newspaper that covers Bureau County in Illinois. shawlocal.com/bureau-county-republican/ is the website covering news, crime, sports, and business for the Bureau County area.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Bureau County Republican is owned by
[Shaw Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shaw_Media_(United_States)), which is an American, Dixon, Illinois-based publisher of about 80 newspapers and news websites in Illinois and Iowa. Revenue is derived from sponsored content, advertising, and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, local news is published through journalists and with minimal bias, such as this:
[Illinois takes Equal Rights Amendment to appeals court](https://www.shawlocal.com/news/2022/09/28/illinois-takes-equal-rights-amendment-to-appeals-court/). National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other wire services.
Editorially, they have a page dedicated to op-eds that are basically balanced in content. Generally, news reporting is factual and low-biased, while op-eds are reasonably balanced.
