History
Vaxxter is a website that claims to be “the leading authority on alternative health news.” The founder of Vaxxter is Dr. Sherri Tenpenny who was formerly a practicing Osteopath. The website presents her as “one of the country’s most knowledgeable and outspoken physicians regarding the impact of vaccines on health,” however Tenpenny does not have any
[educational background](http://americanloons.blogspot.com/2014/11/1198-sherri-tenpenny.html) or expertise on infectious disease or immunology. Dr. Tenpenny has been featured on both conspiracy theorist [Alex Jones](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/infowars-alex-jones/) show and quack pseudoscience purveyor the [Dr. Oz Show](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/doctoroz-com/).
