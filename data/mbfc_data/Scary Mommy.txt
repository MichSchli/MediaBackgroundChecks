History
[Founded in 2008](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scary_Mommy), Scary Mommy is a news and opinion website/blog geared toward parenting. They also cover culture and politics as it relates to women and parenting. According to their [about page](https://www.scarymommy.com/about-scary-mommy/), “Scary Mommy is one of the largest, most influential and trusted sources of entertainment and information for millennial moms online.” Jill Smokler created the blog and website. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Some Spider Studios](https://somespider.com/) based in New York City owns Scary Mommy. Some Spider Studios operates other brands such as cafe.com and dad.com. The company is owned by Vinit Bahara, the brother of U.S. Attorney Preet Bahara. Scary Mommy generates revenue through advertising and the sale of merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Scary Mommy publishes original articles on pregnancy, parenting, and child care. Articles and headlines use moderately loaded language such as this
[Teacher Resigns After Racist Rant About Black Families Caught On Zoom Call](https://www.scarymommy.com/california-school-sued-racist-teacher-zoom/). This story is properly sourced to the [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/). In general, stories tend to favor the left: [We Have To Keep That Anti-Trump Momentum Going](https://www.scarymommy.com/now-isnt-time-stop-paying-attention-politics/). A review of their source usage shows they source properly from credible outlets such [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/), [NPR](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/npr/), and the [Mayo Clinic](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mayo-clinic/).
Editorially, they favor the left through concern for
[climate change](https://www.scarymommy.com/greta-thunberg-trump-election-tweets/), support for [LGBTQ](https://www.scarymommy.com/15-things-lgbtq-moms-wish-straight-moms-knew/), and support for [Democratic candidates](https://www.scarymommy.com/biden-man-of-faith/). In general, Scary Mommy reports news that is factual and with a left-leaning editorial bias. However, they have failed three fact checks, which renders them mixed for factual reporting.
