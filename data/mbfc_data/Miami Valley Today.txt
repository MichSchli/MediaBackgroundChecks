History
Miami Valley Today is a newspaper based in Miami Valley, Ohio, serving the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at miamivalleytoday.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns Miami Valley Today and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Miami Valley Today publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Troy woman appreciates holistic, supportive approach to health care](https://miamivalleytoday.com/troy-woman-appreciates-holistic-supportive-approach-to-health-care/). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, Miami Valley Today leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
