History
Assiniboia Times is an English-language newspaper in Assiniboia, Saskatchewan, Canada.
[sasktoday.ca/south/assiniboia-times](https://www.sasktoday.ca/south/assiniboia-times) is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle. [Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Assiniboia Times. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Assiniboia Times publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Assiniboia, Saskatchewan region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Health clinic returns to Lafleche](https://www.sasktoday.ca/south/local-news/health-clinic-returns-to-lafleche-6340289). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Assiniboia Times’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Column: Holidays don’t have to hurt the environment](https://www.sasktoday.ca/south/opinion/column-holidays-dont-have-to-hurt-the-environment-6275752). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
