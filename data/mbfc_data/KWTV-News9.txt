History
Founded in 1953, KWTV is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. News9.com is the website for the TV station and its news programming. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Griffin Communications, a media company based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma owns KWTV-News9. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, News9.com primarily covers local news, crime, entertainment, and sports for the Oklahoma City area. Local news is covered with minimal bias through reporters like this
[Oklahoma City Attorney Infected With Coronavirus Released From Hospital, Recovering At Home](https://www.news9.com/story/41979196/oklahoma-city-attorney-infected-with-coronavirus-released-from-hospital-recovering-at-home). The Associated Press provides national and international news.
KWTV-News9 does not offer editorials but does cover politics with minimal bias, such as this
[British Prime Minister Boris Johnson Moved To Intensive Care](https://www.news9.com/story/41978619/british-prime-minister-boris-johnson-moved-to-intensive-care). Most political news is derived from third-party sources such as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). In general, news reporting is factual and displays minimal bias.
