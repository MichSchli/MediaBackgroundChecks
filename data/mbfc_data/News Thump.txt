History
[Founded in 2010](http://whois.domaintools.com/newsthump.com), News Thump is a satirical website from the United Kingdom. According to their [about page](https://newsthump.com/about-us/) “NewsThump is a satirical and spoof news website, taking a daily swipe at current affairs from the UK and around the world.” [Read our profile in UK Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
News Thump is owned by Thump Publishing LTD. and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, News Thump is a satire website based on clearly disclaiming “NewsThump is a satirical and spoof news website.” A sample story reads
