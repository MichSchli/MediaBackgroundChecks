History
North Shore News is an English-language newspaper in North Vancouver, BC, Canada. nsnews.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the North Shore News. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, North Shore News publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater North Vancouver, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[B.C. court upholds three-year drug trafficking sentence](https://www.nsnews.com/highlights/bc-court-upholds-three-year-drug-trafficking-sentence-7118834). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, North Shore News’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Opinion: The invisible effects of human activity on nature.](https://www.nsnews.com/highlights/opinion-the-invisible-effects-of-human-activity-on-nature-7119209) The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
