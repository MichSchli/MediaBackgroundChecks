History
Grand Forks Gazette is a newspaper published in Grand Forks, British Columbia, Canada. The website grandforksgazette.ca covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Grand Forks, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Grand Forks Gazette. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Grand Forks Gazette covers news for Grand Forks and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Woman dies after 14-hour wait at B.C. hospital; family wants answers](https://www.grandforksgazette.ca/news/woman-dies-after-14-hour-wait-at-bc-hospital-family-wants-answers-7288626). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Grand Forks Gazette does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
