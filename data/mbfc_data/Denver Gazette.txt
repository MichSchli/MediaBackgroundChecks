History
[Founded in 2020](https://www.coloradoindependent.com/2020/08/28/denver-gazette-newspaper-colorado-media/), the Denver Gazette is an e-newspaper from Denver, Colorado. The paper features city hall and legislative news, suburban and statewide reporting, business, national and international coverage, outdoor trends, entertainment, and local editorials. The current editor is [Vince Bzdek](https://denvergazette.com/contact/). [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Denver Gazette is published by the
[Colorado Springs Gazette](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/colorado-springs-gazette/), owned by Clarity Media. The [Clarity Media Group](https://www.sourcewatch.org/index.php/Clarity_Media_Group) also owns other right-leaning publications such as the [San Francisco Examiner](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-francisco-examiner/) and the [Washington Examiner](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-examiner/). The paper is funded through a subscription and advertising model.
