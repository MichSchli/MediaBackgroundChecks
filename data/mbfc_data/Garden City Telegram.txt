History
The Garden City Telegram is a newspaper published in Garden City, Kansas. gctelegram.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the Newton Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Garden City Telegram is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Garden City Telegram covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Newton, Kansas, area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[City approves traffic signal for Fleming and Spruce Streets intersection](https://www.gctelegram.com/2022/10/06/city-approves-traffic-signal-for-fleming-and-spruce-streets-intersection/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
