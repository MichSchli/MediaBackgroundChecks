History
Founded in 1921, the
[Society for Science & the Public ](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Society_for_Science_and_the_Public)(SSP), formerly known as Science Service, is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization dedicated to the promotion of science through its science education programs and publications, including the bi-weekly [Science News](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/science-news/) magazine and the free-accessible online Science News for Students. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
The Society for Science & the Public provides accurate science news and opportunities to subscribing members and alumni of our competitions worldwide. The blog provides news primarily about the society, such as this
[Eight Broadcom MASTERS International delegates building the future today.](https://www.societyforscience.org/blog/eight-broadcom-masters-international-delegates-building-the-future-today/) The website also features a link to their publication [Science News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/science-news/) that Media Bias Fact Check rates [Pro-Science](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pro-science/) and very-high in factual reporting. In general, SSP is Pro-science and very high in factual reporting as well.
