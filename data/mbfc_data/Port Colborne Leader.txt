History
The Port Colborne Leader is a newspaper published in Port Colborne, Ontario, Canada.
[stcatharinesstandard.ca/local-port-colborne/](https://www.stcatharinesstandard.ca/local-port-colborne/) provides online local, national, and world news for the greater Peterborough, Ontario region.

Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of Port Colborne Leader is Metroland Media Group Ltd., a subsidiary of Nordstar Capital LP. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In 2015 and
[2021](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2021/09/19/the-stars-editorial-board-endorses-liberals-for-2021-federal-election.html), the [Toronto Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/) (Nordstar), which owns the Port Colborne Leader publisher Metroland Media Group, [endorsed](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2015/10/09/for-justin-trudeau-and-the-liberal-party-editorial.html) the Liberal Party under Justin Trudeau.
In review, Port Colborne Leader uses low-biased wording when reporting local news such as this:
[Impact of on-beach parking ban in Port Colborne up in the air](https://www.stcatharinesstandard.ca/local-port-colborne/news/2023/03/14/impact-of-on-beach-parking-ban-in-port-colborne-up-in-the-air.html). National news is derived from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/), while world news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, a review of op-eds slightly favors the left. In general, they are fact-based and hold a left-leaning editorial bias.
