History
The Victoria News is a newspaper published in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. The website vicnews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Victoria area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Victoria News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Victoria News covers news for Victoria and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[300 without power, snow in the forecast for Greater Victoria](https://www.vicnews.com/news/300-without-power-snow-in-the-forecast-for-greater-victoria/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Victoria News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
