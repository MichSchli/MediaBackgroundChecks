History
Launched in August 2013,
[Bearing Arms](https://bearingarms.com/page/about-bearing-arms/) is a pro-gun blog site that covers Second Amendment issues, self-defense, the firearms industry, crime, and home security. [Bob Owens](https://bearingarms.com/ba-staff/2013/10/01/bearing-arms-names-longtime-second-amendment-writer-and-activist-bob-owens-as-new-editor/), a Second Amendment writer and activist, [was](https://bearingarms.com/page/about-bearing-arms/) the editor of Bearing Arms. According to the [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/), he [passed away in 2017](https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/post-nation/wp/2017/05/09/bearing-arms-editor-bob-owens-dead-from-apparent-self-inflicted-gunshot-wound/?utm_term=.4267b43302fb) due to an apparent suicide from a self-inflicted gunshot. Currently, [Jenn Jacques](https://bearingarms.com/page/about-bearing-arms/), who describes herself as an “outdoorswoman, target, skeet shooter and a Second Amendment supporter and defender and an advocate for concealed carry, “ is listed as editor. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The conservative news site
[Townhall](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/townhall/) owns Bearing Arms. According to [Bearing Arms](https://bearingarms.com/ba-staff/2013/10/01/bearing-arms-names-longtime-second-amendment-writer-and-activist-bob-owens-as-new-editor/) and Townhall, they are owned by [Salem Communications](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salem_Media_Group), which is owned by Edward Atsinger III and Stuart W. Epperson, who is also a member of the conservative Council for National Policy (“CNP”). Advertising and membership fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Bearing Arms blog publishes gun-related news, mainly sourcing from other pro-gun sites such as
[Guns.com](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/guns-com/), Decatur Daily, and the [NRA](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/national-rifle-association-nra/), which has a [mixed track record for facts](http://www.politifact.com/personalities/national-rifle-association/). They almost always report news from a right-leaning perspective. They utilize loaded words in their headlines and articles, such as: [“Hillary Clinton Is The Greatest Threat to the Second Amendment Ever.”](https://bearingarms.com/beth-b/2016/10/14/hillary-clinton-the-greatest-threat-to-the-second-amendment/) However, they also source from credible mainstream media such as the [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/), [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/), and social media through [heavy quoting](https://bearingarms.com/jenn-j/2017/07/21/trump-appoints-outspoken-gun-control-advocate/). In general, Bearing Arms promotes right-leaning pro-gun advocacy.
