History
604 Now is an
[alternative daily](https://604now.com/about/) serving Metro Vancouver and the southwest corner of British Columbia. They focus on events, food, pop culture, nightlife, and local news. The website lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership or an editor. [Read our profile on Canadian media and government](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
604 Now does not disclose ownership, and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, 604 Now is primarily an entertainment guide for the Vancouver Metro area. They also publish community news with minimal bias, such as this
[HERE’S THE FIRST LOOK INSIDE G&F FINANCIAL GROUP’S NEW FLAGSHIP LOCATION](https://604now.com/gf-financial-group-edmonds-flagship/).
Editorially, they report on political issues from a left-leaning perspective such as
[HEALTH CANADA FINALLY APPROVED A BIRTH CONTROL IMPLANT FOR YOUR ARM](https://604now.com/health-canada-birth-control-arm-implant/), and this [VANCOUVER IS HOSTING ANOTHER PROTEST FOR GEORGE FLOYD THIS WEEK](https://604now.com/vancouver-protest-george-floyd-june-5/). The news is generally reported factually and with a left-leaning editorial bias.
