History
[Infobae](https://argentina.mom-rsf.org/en/owners/companies/detail/company/company/show/grupo-infobae/) is a news website founded in 2002 and based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Originally, it was founded in 1998 as Buenos Aires Económico (BAE). In 2001, Sergio Szpolski and businessman/journalist Daniel Hadad purchased the newspaper, and the following year, Szpolski transferred his shares to Hadad, and its name became Infobae Diario. In 2002, the website Infobae.com was launched.
Infobae focuses on news, politics, sports, entertainment, business, and technology and has a digital TV channel (Infobae TV). Currently, Daniel Hadad is the executive director of the group. Tomás Eurnekian has been the major shareholder since 2018.

Analysis / Bias
In review, Infobae publishes articles with moderate emotionally loaded language such as these:
[“The details of the controversial decree that allows importing garbage from other countries”](https://www.infobae.com/sociedad/2019/09/04/los-detalles-del-polemico-decreto-que-permite-importar-basura-de-otros-paises/) and “ [The president said there was no increase in the tax applied to the rural sector. “We are leaving standing what (Mauricio) Macri put in.”](https://www.infobae.com/politica/2019/12/15/alberto-fernandez-sobre-las-retenciones-todos-tenemos-que-hacer-un-esfuerzo-el-campo-tambien/)
Infobae has a
[content alliance](https://origin.infobae.arcpublishing.com/noticias/2017/05/21/infobae-presenta-su-alianza-de-contenidos-con-vice-com-y-the-washington-post/) with the left-leaning [Vice.com](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/vice-news/) and the [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/) when it comes to sourcing. Further, Infobae often [hyperlinks to itself](https://www.infobae.com/america/eeuu/2019/12/11/greta-thunberg-fue-elegida-como-la-persona-del-ano-por-la-revista-time/) and credible sources such as [AFP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/afp-agence-france-presse/) and [The New York Times.](https://www.infobae.com/america/the-new-york-times/2019/12/17/los-regalos-de-segunda-mano-y-las-experiencias-siguen-en-los-primeros-lugares-de-las-listas-navidenas/) When covering world news about the USA, they cover the Biden administration with a favorable tone, such as in this article [The US Congress gave Biden powers to lend more weapons to Ukraine](https://www.infobae.com/america/eeuu/2022/04/29/el-congreso-de-eeuu-dio-poderes-a-biden-para-prestar-mas-armamento-a-ucrania/). In general, Infobae reports news factually as evidenced by a [clean fact check record](https://factualsearch.news/#?fns.type=fact-checking&gsc.tab=0&gsc.q=infobea&gsc.sort=).
