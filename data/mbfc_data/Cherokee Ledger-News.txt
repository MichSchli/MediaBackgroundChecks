History
The Cherokee Ledger-News is a newspaper based in Cherokee County, Georgia, USA. The paper mainly focuses on local news and politics, sports, and business related to the Cherokee area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Cherokee Ledger-News is owned by
[Times-Journal Inc](https://www.editorandpublisher.com/stories/times-journal-inc-acquires-six-metro-atlanta-newspapers,240906). through the Brumby family. They publish around 20 papers in the Georgia and southeast markets. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Cherokee Ledger-News covers local news for the Cherokee area through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Cherokee County Population to Surpass 400,000 by 2050](https://www.tribuneledgernews.com/local_news/arc-cherokee-county-population-to-surpass-400-000-by-2050/article_e72bfe4c-cb77-11ee-9d74-bf63e514ff18.html). The paper does not cover national and international news, focusing on local and regional news related to Georgia.
Editorially, story selection, and op-eds are reasonably balanced and reflect both sides. Generally, the Cherokee Ledger-News is fact-based and presents a minimal bias in reporting.
