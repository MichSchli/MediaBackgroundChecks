History
[Founded in 2008](https://www.thenewcivilrightsmovement.com/about/), by David Badash, The New Civil Rights Movement (NCRM) is a website news source that promotes civil rights for LGBTQ people. According to their about page the website was created to “galvanize a community of progressives to help advance civil rights for LGBT people.” David Badash is also the editor-in-chief and writes most original content.
Read our profile on
[USA government influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The New Civil Rights Movement is owned by AlterNet Media, which also publishes the progressive left news website
[alternet.org](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/alternet/). Revenue is derived through advertising and donations for an ad-free version of the website.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The New Civil Rights Movement summarizes news stories and publishes opinion-based original commentary from a progressive liberal perspective. Headlines and articles often utilize emotionally loaded wording that favors the left, such as this:
[Pelosi is Doing What No One Else Had the Balls to Do: Making Trump Squirm](https://www.thenewcivilrightsmovement.com/2019/12/pelosi-is-doing-what-no-one-else-had-the-balls-to-do-making-trump-squirm/). This story is sourced entirely to Twitter. NCRM also republishes articles from [Raw Story](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/raw-story/) and [Alternet](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/alternet/).
Editorially, all commentary comes from a progressive liberal perspective and often denigrates the right such as this
[BUSTED: Rudy Giuliani Outed for ‘Shadow Diplomacy’ With Venezuela — Similar to His Ukraine Scandal](https://www.thenewcivilrightsmovement.com/2019/12/busted-rudy-giuliani-outed-for-shadow-diplomacy-with-venezuela-similar-to-his-ukraine-scandal/) and this [DOW Plunges After ‘Moron’ Treasury Secretary Mnuchin’s Message From Mexico Spooks Markets](https://www.thenewcivilrightsmovement.com/2018/12/dow-plunges-after-moron-treasury-secretary-mnuchins-message-from-mexico-spooks-markets/). This latter story is sourced from [Raw Story](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/raw-story/), which has a Mixed record with fact-checkers. In general, all stories favor the left and often utilize left-biased sources.
