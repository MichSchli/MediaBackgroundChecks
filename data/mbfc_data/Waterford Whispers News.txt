Analysis / Bias
Waterford Whispers is a satire website because, under their disclaimer,
[they state](https://waterfordwhispersnews.com/disclaimer/), “Waterford Whispers News is a satirical newspaper and website published by Waterford Whispers News.” This is all the evidence we need.
