History
Founded in 1956, WISC – Madison News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Madison, Wisconsin, United States. Channel3000.com is the associated news website for the TV Station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WISC – Madison News is owned by
[Morgan Murphy Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morgan_Murphy_Media), which owns 14 TV stations and several radio stations throughout the Midwest. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WISC – Madison News reports news, sports, weather, and crime for the Madison, Wisconsin region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Police seize 2 guns, drugs and cash after stolen vehicle crash, shooting on far east side](https://www.channel3000.com/police-seize-2-guns-drugs-and-cash-after-stolen-vehicle-crash-shooting-on-far-east-side/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally provides national and international news.
Editorially, WISC – Madison News does not publish opinion pieces; however, they report on politics primarily through the Associated Press. The news is generally reported factually and with minimal bias due to a lack of editorializing.
