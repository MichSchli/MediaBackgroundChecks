History
The Thompson Citizen is an English-language newspaper in Thompson, Manitoba, Canada. stalbertgazette.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Thompson Citizen. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
The Thompson Citizen publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater St. Albert, Alberta region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[End of summer update: Street paving and water system renewal progress](https://www.thompsoncitizen.net/local-news/end-of-summer-update-street-paving-and-water-system-renewal-progress-7522564). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Thompson Citizen’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Editorial: Symbols an important part of reconciliation, but not enough](https://www.thompsoncitizen.net/opinion/editorial-symbols-an-important-part-of-reconciliation-but-not-enough-6892230). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
