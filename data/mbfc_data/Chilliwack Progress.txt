History
The Chilliwack Progress is a newspaper published in Chilliwack, British Columbia, Canada. The website theprogress.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Chilliwack area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Chilliwack Progress. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Chilliwack Progress covers news for Chilliwack and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Chilliwack and area real estate sales numbers continue to cool](https://www.theprogress.com/news/chilliwack-and-area-real-estate-sales-numbers-continue-to-cool/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Chilliwack Progress does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left, such as this
[Global Climate Strike at Five Corners Friday](https://www.theprogress.com/news/global-climate-strike-at-five-corners-friday/). Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
