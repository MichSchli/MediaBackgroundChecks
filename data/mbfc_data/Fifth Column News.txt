History
[Founded in 2015](http://whois.domaintools.com/thefifthcolumnnews.com), The Fifth Column is a news and opinion website that publishes from a mostly progressive perspective. According to their [about page](https://thefifthcolumnnews.com/about-our-sections/) “The Fifth Column provides you with insights that aren’t available on other news outlets. With a focus on long-form journalism and exclusive reports, The Column strives for excellence in adversarial journalism. Our exclusive reports find their way into one of our unique sections.”
The website lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership and article authors do not have detailed bios.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Fifth Column does not disclose ownership. Revenue is derived through advertising. There is a link to an online store that is not working at the time of this review.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Fifth Column reports original news and commentary. Headlines are minimal to moderately emotional in a tone such as this
[Greek Police vs Antifa: ‘we blocked the road, they threw stuff’](https://thefifthcolumnnews.com/2018/09/greek-police-vs-antifa-we-blocked-the-road-they-threw-stuff/). This story is sourced to the Questionable Russian Propaganda source [RT News.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/rt-news/) Politically, stories lean left with support for immigration and refugees, while denigrating the Trump administration such as this: [World to Refugees: Go to Hell](https://thefifthcolumnnews.com/2018/06/world-to-refugees-go-to-hell/). This story is properly sourced to numerous credible media outlets such as the [New Yorker](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-yorker/), [CBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbc-news-canadian-broadcasting/), and [USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/). The Fifth Column reports negatively on establishment Democrats such as [Hillary Clinton](https://thefifthcolumnnews.com/2018/03/there-she-goes-again-clintons-blame-game-mumbai-edition/), while favoring more progressive candidates such as [Bernie Sanders](https://thefifthcolumnnews.com/2016/07/sanders-endorsement-helps-reveal-american-politics-to-be-a-farce/), who they criticized for endorsing Clinton, whom they call a “right-wing candidate.” Further, they consistently report negatively and criticize [President Donald Trump](https://thefifthcolumnnews.com/2018/10/trumps-clarion-call-to-re-weaponize-drug-war-2/).
When reporting on science they
[support the consensus](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/) on human-influenced [climate change](https://thefifthcolumnnews.com/2017/07/opinion-the-threat-climate-change-poses-to-americas-poor/), however, when it comes to [GMOs](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-gmo-dangers/) they tend to reject the consensus in favor of [alarmism](https://thefifthcolumnnews.com/?s=GMO).
