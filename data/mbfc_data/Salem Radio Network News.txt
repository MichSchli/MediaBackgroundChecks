History
Salem Radio Network News (SRN News) is a part of the Salem Radio Network. They essentially publish news that
[originates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salem_Radio_Network) from the radio network as well as original journalism and republished material. The network focuses on syndicated Christian political talk, music, and conservative news/talk programming. The website does not feature an about page. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Salem Radio Network News is owned by the Salem Radio Group that also publishes right-leaning radio personalities HughHewitt.com, MikeOnline.com,
[DennisPrager.com](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/prageru/), SebGorka.com, MetaxasTalk.com, and LarryElder.com. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, SRN News features original reporting as well as republished news from 3rd party sources. Original content often utilizes loaded language such as this
[Biden hits campaign trail in Georgia after top Republicans acknowledge his win](https://www.srnnews.com/biden-hits-campaign-trail-in-georgia-after-top-republicans-acknowledge-his-win-3/) and [$2K reward offered in Wyoming grizzly bear killing case](https://www.srnnews.com/2k-reward-offered-in-wyoming-grizzly-bear-killing-case/). Republished news is often from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/).
Editorially, they lean right as they offer a
[religious news section](https://www.srnnews.com/religious-news/) that favors Christianity. When it comes to science they are pro-science in regards to Covid-19 as well as climate change. They also post clips from their radio hosts that lean right and may not always be factual. In general, the text-based news reported on the website is factual and leans right editorially.
