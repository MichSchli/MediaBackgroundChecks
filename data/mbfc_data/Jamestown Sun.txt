History
The Jamestown Sun is a newspaper published in Jamestown, North Dakota. The website jamestownsun.com features local and national news, politics, sports, entertainment, business, classifieds, and health.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Jamestown Sun is owned by
[Forum Communications Company](https://www.forumcomm.com/brands-overview/), which is based in North Dakota and has additional locations across South Dakota, Minnesota, and Wisconsin. Advertising and subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Jamestown Sun reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and entertainment for the greater Grand Forks, ND region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[Sun archives move to University of Jamestown. ](https://www.jamestownsun.com/news/sun-archives-move-to-university-of-jamestown)Further, the website publishes national and international news from credible sources such as the [LA Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/la-times-los-angeles-times/) and [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/). Finally, all information is sourced via first-person accounts or through credible media.
Editorially, The Jamestown Sun reasonably represents both sides. However, a review of columnists demonstrates more favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with right-leaning editorial bias.
