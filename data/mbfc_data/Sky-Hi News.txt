History
Sky-Hi News is a daily newspaper serving Grand County, Colorado. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Sky-Hi News is published by Swift Communications, which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the western USA. Ogden Newspapers owns Swift. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Sky-Hi News publishes news for Grand County, Colorado, and the surrounding area. Local news is covered through reporting with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Colorado lakes and reservoirs are free of invasive mussels](https://www.skyhinews.com/news/colorado-lakes-and-reservoirs-are-free-of-invasive-mussels/). The paper and website do not cover national and international news.
A review of editorials demonstrates a reasonable balance without extremes such as this
[A dangerous game of chicken on the Colorado River.](https://www.skyhinews.com/opinion/a-dangerous-game-of-chicken-on-the-colorado-river/) In general, they exhibit low bias and produce fact-based news.
