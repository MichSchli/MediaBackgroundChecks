History
The Derry News is a newspaper published in Derry, New Hampshire. derrynews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Derry News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Derry News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Derry, New Hampshire region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Associate planner told to stay away while job position is considered](https://www.derrynews.com/news/londonderry/associate-planner-told-to-stay-away-while-job-position-is-considered/article_e1f97482-a17e-11ed-adcf-1749d4ed43db.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
