History
[Founded in 2001](http://whois.domaintools.com/opposingviews.com), Opposing Views (also known as OV) is an American digital news website headquartered in Los Angeles. According to their [about page](https://www.opposingviews.com/about), “Opposing Views provides balanced coverage in an increasingly partisan media climate. We give an unfiltered look at politics, societal issues, international affairs, and culture to help you make informed decisions on what matters most.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Tikuno Media LLC owns Opposing Views. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Opposing Views presents original and aggregated news articles from across the political spectrum, with the primary theme being sensationalized stories designed to garner clicks. Here are some sample headlines and stories:
[Woman Says Restaurant Threw Her Out Over Trump Hat](https://www.opposingviews.com/category/woman-thrown-out-restaurant-over-message-hat-photo)– Original Source the Questionable [Daily Mail](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-mail/) [Man Finally Wakes Up From Night Of Heavy Drinking, Gets Biggest Shock Of His Life (Video)](https://www.opposingviews.com/society/man-finally-wakes-night-heavy-drinking-gets-biggest-shock-his-life-video)– Source Daily Mail [Woman Left In Tears After Trip To Salon Takes Unfortunate Turn](https://www.opposingviews.com/health/woman-left-in-tears-after-trip-to-salon-takes-unfortunate-turn)
Currently, most stories are published in their Society section, focusing on strange news and crime stories.
Editorially, Opposing Views often reports negatively on President Biden with loaded emotional headlines such as this:
[President Biden’s Outfit Turns Heads, Some Critics Call It ‘Unpresidential](https://www.opposingviews.com/politics/president-bidens-outfit-turns-heads-some-critics-call-it-unpresidential).’ The website also reports negatively on the left with stories such as this: [Socialist Group That Pushes For $20 Minimum Wage Offers $13 In Job Ad](https://www.opposingviews.com/category/socialist-group-pushes-20-minimum-wage-offers-much-less-job-ad). This story is sourced to the factually Mixed [Newsmax](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/newsmax/). They [support the consensus](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/) with [human-influenced climate change](https://www.opposingviews.com/politics/poll-most-americans-support-government-action-climate-change), [vaccinations](https://www.opposingviews.com/politics/poll-most-americans-support-government-action-climate-change), and [GMOs](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-gmo-dangers/) when it comes to science.
