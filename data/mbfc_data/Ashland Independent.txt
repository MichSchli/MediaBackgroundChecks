History
The Ashland Independent is a newspaper published in Ashland, Kentucky. dailyindependent.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Ashland Independent. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Ashland Independent covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Ahsland, Kentucky, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Agreement puts wheels in motion for Greenup County ambulance service.](https://www.dailyindependent.com/news/agreement-puts-wheels-in-motion-for-greenup-county-ambulance-service/article_aea15cf8-6ece-11ee-ab95-5f738cc34a0c.html) The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
