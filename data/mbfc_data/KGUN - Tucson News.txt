History
Founded in 1956, KGUN – Tucson News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Tucson, Arizona, United States. kgun9.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KGUN – Tucson News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KGUN – Tucson News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Tucson, Arizona, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[MISTRIAL: Jury reaches no decision in murder case of 6-year-old Isabel Celis](https://www.kgun9.com/news/local-news/mistrial-jury-reaches-no-decision-in-murder-case-of-6-year-old-isabel-celis). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[President Biden had cancerous lesion removed](https://www.kgun9.com/news/national/president-biden-had-cancerous-lesion-removed). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
