History
The Coldwater Daily Reporter is a newspaper that serves Coldwater, Michigan, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. thedailyreporter.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Coldwater Daily Reporter. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Coldwater Daily Reporter covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Cheboygan, Michigan area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Gretchen Whitmer wins a second term as Michigan governor, defeating Tudor Dixon](https://www.thedailyreporter.com/story/news/politics/elections/2022/11/09/michigan-governor-whitmer-dixon-results/69619056007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Coldwater Daily Reporter does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Cameron Brown: America, Bryant and Machiavelli](https://www.thedailyreporter.com/story/opinion/columns/2022/10/22/cameron-brown-america-bryant-and-machiavelli/69572323007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
