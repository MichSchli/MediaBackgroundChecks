History
Preeceville Progess is an English-language newspaper in Preeceville, Saskatchewan, Canada. sasktoday.ca/central/preeceville-progress is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Preeceville Progess. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
Preeceville Progess publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Precceville, Sask region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[RCMP Report: Art supplies stolen from Canora Composite School](https://www.sasktoday.ca/central/canora-courier/rcmp-report-art-supplies-stolen-from-canora-composite-school-7278542). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Preeceville Progess’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Commentary: Inflation soars as Bank of Canada hands out millions in bonuses](https://www.sasktoday.ca/highlights/commentary-inflation-soars-as-bank-of-canada-hands-out-millions-in-bonuses-7271606). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
