History
The Joplin Globe is a newspaper published in Joplin, Missouri. joplinglobe.com.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Joplin Globe. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Joplin Globe covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Joplin, Missouri, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[New technology at Freeman provides control of test pressure](https://www.joplinglobe.com/news/local_news/new-technology-at-freeman-provides-control-of-test-pressure/article_e5c30d6c-6f64-11ee-b0a7-4b6d99b3dbc8.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
