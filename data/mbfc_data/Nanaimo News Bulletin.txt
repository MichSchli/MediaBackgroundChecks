History
The Nanaimo News Bulletin is a newspaper published in Nanaimo, British Columbia, Canada. The website nanaimobulletin.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Nanaimo area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Nanaimo News Bulletin. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Nanaimo News Bulletin covers news for Nanaimo and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Catholic church settles lawsuit around historical sexual abuse of 10-year-old in Nanaimo](https://www.nanaimobulletin.com/news/catholic-church-settles-lawsuit-around-historical-sexual-abuse-of-10-year-old-in-nanaimo/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Nanaimo News Bulletin does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
