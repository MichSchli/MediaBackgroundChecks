History
Founded in 1956 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station, WAGM – Maine News serves Presque Isle, Maine, and the surrounding area. Wagmtv.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WAGM – Maine News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WAGM – Maine News provides news coverage for Presque Isle, Maine, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Senate confirms McElwee as Maine’s top federal prosecutor](https://www.wagmtv.com/2021/10/06/senate-confirms-mcelwee-maines-top-federal-prosecutor/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WAGM – Maine News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
