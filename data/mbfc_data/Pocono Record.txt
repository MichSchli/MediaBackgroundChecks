History
[Founded in 1894](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pocono_Record), The Pocono Record is a daily newspaper published in print and online in Stroudsburg, Pennsylvania, United States. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Pocono Record. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Pocono Record covers local news through the use of journalists and reporters. The national news is typically covered by the
[Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) Articles and headlines rarely use loaded words and all information is properly sourced. Editorially, the Pocono Record does not provide candidate endorsements, and its [opinion page](http://www.poconorecord.com/opinion/columns?start=2) is diversified.
