History
KTUC – 1400 AM – Tucson is a Tucson, Arizona, talk radio station. The radio station features conservative news talk shows, while the website publishes National and political news. The web domain for the station is freedom1400.com.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTUC – 1400 AM – Tucson is owned by
[Cumulus Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cumulus-media-bias/), a publicly-traded American broadcasting company that owns over 400 radio stations. Cumulus Media is owned by its shareholders, including hedge funds and institutional investors. A review of [political donations](https://www.opensecrets.org/campaign-expenditures/vendor?cycle=2020&vendor=Cumulus+Media) indicates more favoring Republican candidates and PACS. Advertising through radio and the web generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KTUC – 1400 AM – Tucson does not publish political-related news on the website.
The radio station and website broadcast a daily lineup of news radio hosts who generally hold conservative perspectives. They also feature nationally syndicated Cumulus hosts like
[Dan Bongino](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/dan-bongino-bias-rating/) and [Ben Shapiro](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/ben-shapiro-bias-rating/), who have poor fact-check records.
Editorially, the majority of radio hosts hold conservative viewpoints and have been known to advance conspiracy theories and misinformation. In general, the information presented on the website is usually factual, while radio shows exhibit a conservative bias and sometimes promote misleading or false claims.
