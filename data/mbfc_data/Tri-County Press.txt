History
The Tri-County Press is a newspaper published in Ogle County, Illinois. shawlocal.com/ogle-county-news/ is the website covering news, crime, sports, and business for the Ogle County area.

Funded by / Ownership
The Tri-County Press is owned by
[Shaw Media](https://shawmedia.com/our-markets/), which is an American, Dixon, Illinois-based publisher of about 80 newspapers and news websites in Illinois and Iowa. Revenue is derived from sponsored content, advertising, and subscription fees.
