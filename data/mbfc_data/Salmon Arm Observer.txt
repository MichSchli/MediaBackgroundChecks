History
Salmon Arm Observer is a newspaper published in Salmon Arm, British Columbia, Canada. The website saobserver.net covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Salmon Arm, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Salmon Arm Observer. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Salmon Arm Observer covers news for Salmon Arm and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Parents applaud policies restricting cellphone access at Salmon Arm schools](https://www.saobserver.net/local-news/parents-applaud-policies-restricting-cellphone-access-at-salmon-arm-schools-4505052). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Salmon Arm Observer does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
