History
The Greensburg Daily News is a newspaper published in the city of Greensburg, Indiana. greensburgdailynews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Greensburg Daily News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Greensburg Daily News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Greensburg, Indiana, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[2022 Our Hospice Summer Concert fundraising nets $147,000](https://www.greensburgdailynews.com/news/local_news/2022-our-hospice-summer-concert-fundraising-nets-147-000/article_527d2070-3e8b-11ed-89bb-6739c8149b9d.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
