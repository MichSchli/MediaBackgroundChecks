History
Founded in 1956, KFSN – ABC30 Fresno News News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/) television network licensed to Fresno, California, United States. abc30.com is the associated website that provides news, weather, and sports for the Fresno Metropolitan region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KFSN – ABC30 Fresno News is owned by the ABC Owned Television Stations subsidiary of
[The Walt Disney Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the KFSN – ABC30 Fresno News website covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the Fresno Metro area. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Washburn Fire: Yosemite’s Highway 41 entrance closed, 1,190 acres charred](https://abc30.com/yosemite-fire-washburn-shut-down-mariposa-grove/12029365/). Many local stories contain embedded videos from the TV station. National and world news is covered by [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/), [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [ESPN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/espn/), and other ABC stations.
Editorially, KFSN – ABC30 Fresno News does not provide opinion pieces; however, they report on politics. They provide original reporting with a slight left-leaning bias, such as this
[Abortion rights supporters fill the streets of cities nationwide following Friday bombshell](https://abc30.com/supreme-court-abortion-rights-roe-v-wade-overturned-protests/11995349/). They also rely on political news from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
