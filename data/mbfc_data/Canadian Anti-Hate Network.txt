History
Founded in 2018 and based in Toronto, the Canadian Anti-Hate Network is an independent nonprofit run by a team of experts on countering hate and hate crimes. Their
[stated aim](https://www.antihate.ca/about) is to “monitor, research and counter hate groups by providing education, information, and resources. Antihate.ca offer practical solutions for countering hate movements.” [Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Canadian Anti-Hate Network is a nonprofit organization funded through donations, crowdfunding, and a
[$268,400 grant](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/toronto/canadian-anti-hate-network-forms-toronto-profiles-far-right-groups-1.4653148) from the [Canadian Government’s Anti-Racism Action](https://www.canada.ca/en/canadian-heritage/news/2022/06/government-of-canada-and-the-canadian-anti-hate-network-launch-anti-hate-toolkit-for-canadian-schools.html) program. Further, they report a relationship with the [Southern Poverty Law Center](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/southern-poverty-law-center/). The organization is run by a [six-person board](https://www.antihate.ca/about) consisting of Chair Bernie Farber and Executive Director Evan Balgord.
Analysis / Bias
The Canadian Anti-Hate Network website (antihate.ca) publishes news and analyses on hate groups, crimes, and trends. Antihate.ca counters all forms of hate, such as racism, homophobia, and transphobia, primarily in Canada.
They frequently use emotionally loaded language when reporting on right-wing extremism, such as
[RIGHT-WING FEAR MONGERING AROUND MAR-A-LAGO RAID REACHES CANADA](https://www.antihate.ca/right_wing_fear_mongering_mar_a_lago_raid_reaches_canada). Articles and headlines rely on credible sources such as [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/) and [Scientific American](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/scientific-american/).
Editorially, the Canadian Anti-Hate Network holds progressive left-leaning perspectives such as pro-choice on abortion
[LEAKED ROE V. WADE DECISION ENERGIZES ANNUAL ANTI-ABORTION MARCH](https://www.antihate.ca/leaked_roe_v_wade_decision_energizes_annual_anti_abortion_march). They also promote [LGBTQ ](https://www.antihate.ca/christian_anti_lgbtq_movement_in_canada)equity and [opposition](https://www.antihate.ca/antisemitic_islamophobic_racist_conspiracy_theorist_dominating_international_anti_mask_movement) to antisemitism and Islamophobia. In general, they are an anti-hate activist group with a strong left-leaning bias that reports information factually.
