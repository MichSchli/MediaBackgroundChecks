History
Koco News 5 is a TV News program and website that reports news, weather, and sports from Oklahoma City and around the state. It is an ABC-affiliated television station licensed to Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, United States. Since we are unable to watch the newscast, this review is focused on their website only.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Koco News 5 is owned by Hearst Communications, which owns newspapers, magazines, television channels, and television stations, including the
[San Francisco Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-francisco-chronicle/), the [Houston Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/houston-chronicle/), and [Esquire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/esquire-magazine/). According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Hearst donated [70% to Republican candidates](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/recipients?id=D000034391) and causes in 2020. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KOCO News 5 reports local news through journalists with minimal bias such as this:
[Another 16-year-old arrested in connection with man’s shooting death in Moore.](https://www.koco.com/article/16-year-old-arrested-in-connection-with-mans-shooting-death-in-moore/37272252) The national news is derived from other Hearst papers as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/).
Editorially, KOCO News 5 publishes op-eds that slightly favor the left, such as this
[Editorial: It’s up to us to educate ourselves, families to embrace inclusion as we celebrate Juneteenth](https://www.koco.com/article/editorial-it-s-up-to-us-to-educate-ourselves-families-to-embrace-inclusion-as-we-celebrate-juneteenth/36774863). However, right-leaning perspectives are presented as well, just not at the same frequency as left-leaning opinions.
