History
The Hazleton Standard-Speaker is a newspaper published in Hazelton, Pennsylvania, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Hazleton and the surrounding area. citizensvoice.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Times-Shamrock Communication](https://timesshamrock.com/) owns and publishes the Hazleton Standard-Speaker. They publish newspapers, radio stations, and information-based websites in North Eastern Pennsylvania. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
