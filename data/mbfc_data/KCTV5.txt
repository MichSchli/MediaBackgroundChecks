History
[Founded in 1963](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KCTV), KCTV5 is a [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Kansas City, Missouri, United States and serving the Kansas City metropolitan area. The associated news website is kctv5.com publishing local news, sports, weather, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KCTV5 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KCTV5 reports local news through reporters with minimal bias such as this
[Man dies in shooting after police respond to Independence hotel](https://www.kctv5.com/news/local_news/man-dies-in-shooting-after-police-respond-to-independence-hotel/article_ffc83bd6-8030-11eb-bc35-779384d80568.html?block_id=991162). Many local stories are accompanied by a video from the TV Station. National and international news is provided by other Gray properties as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, KCTV5 does not produce op-eds. In general, they report the news factually and with low bias.
