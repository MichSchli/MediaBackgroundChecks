History
[Founded in 1998](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universe_Today), Universe Today is a popular Canada-based non-commercial space and astronomy news website. The website was founded and created by Frasier Cain. [Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Universe Today is owned by Frasier Cain and is funded through advertising and membership fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Universe Today covers space and astronomy news with minimal bias. Most articles are written through research and summaries from other sources. These sources are typically within the
[pro-science](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pro-science/) category, though some we have not rated as of yet.
Editorially, they do not publish opinion content.
