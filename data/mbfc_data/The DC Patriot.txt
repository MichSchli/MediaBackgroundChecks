History
[Founded in 2019](https://whois.domaintools.com/thedcpatriot.com) by Matt Couch, The DC Patriot is a conservative news and opinion blog that hosts Matt Couch’s Podcast. The website generally lacks transparency as they do not provide an about page describing their conservative bias nor openly disclose ownership. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The DC Patriot does not disclose ownership on the website; however, the website is owned by Matt Couch through America First Media. A
[Patreon page](https://www.patreon.com/AFMG) states, “My name is Matt Couch, and I’m the Founder of America First Media & The DC Patriot.” Revenue is derived through advertising, donations, and a shop that sells conservative-themed merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The DC Patriot provides a link to Matt Couch’s podcast and publishes summarized news stories with additional conservative commentary. All stories favor the right and utilize loaded emotional wording such as this
[SHOCKER: Amazon Offers to Help Biden with COVID Vaccinations](https://thedcpatriot.com/shocker-amazon-offers-to-help-biden-with-covid-vaccinations/), and this [ON DAY ONE BIDEN BEGINS HIS MISSION OF DESTROYING TRUMP’S LEGACY AND AMERICA’S FUTURE](https://thedcpatriot.com/%e2%80%8bon-day-one-biden-begins-his-mission-of-destroying-trumps-legacy-and-americas-future/). The first story is sourced from [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/), while the second is not sourced at all. The DC Patriot sometimes relies on unreliable sources such as [Trending Politics](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/trending-politics/) and [OANN.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2020/05/04/daily-source-bias-check-one-america-news-oan/) Editorially, all stories favor the right and, in particular former [President Donald Trump](https://thedcpatriot.com/sources-trump-offers-his-dc-hotel-to-troops-after-biden-admin-sends-them-to-freezing-cold-garage/).
During 2020, The DC Patriot frequently
[promoted](https://thedcpatriot.com/?s=mask) [anti-mask propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-mask-misinformation/) as well as [election fraud misinformation](https://thedcpatriot.com/?s=election+fraud).
