History
Founded in 1953, KSBY – San Luis Obispo News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to San Luis Obispo, California, United States. ksby.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KSBY – San Luis Obispo News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KSBY – San Luis Obispo News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater San Luis Obispo, California, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Volunteers help clear debris near Arroyo Grande Creek](https://www.ksby.com/news/local-news/volunteers-help-clear-debris-near-arroyo-grande-creek). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden lawyer: FBI searching Biden’s Rehoboth Beach home](https://www.ksby.com/news/national-news/biden-lawyer-fbi-searching-bidens-rehoboth-beach-home). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
