History
The Parkersburg Eclipse News-Review is a newspaper published in Parkersburg,
[Indiana](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-governors-bias-ratings/), United States. The paper covers news, sports, lifestyle, and opinion for Parkersburg and the surrounding area. parkersburgeclipse.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Mid America Publishing owns and publishes the Parkersburg Eclipse News-Review. Mid America Publishing publishes
[several community newspapers](https://www.midampublishing.com/content/mid-america-publications) throughout Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Parkersburg Eclipse News-Review reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Parkersburg, Indiana region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[Historical Home Receives Significant Donation](http://www.parkersburgeclipse.com/content/historical-home-receives-significant-donation). The newspaper and website do not publish national news, focusing on local news reporting.
Editorially, the Parkersburg Eclipse News-Review reasonably represents both sides. A review of op-eds demonstrates a reasonably low bias; however, slightly more op-eds favor the right. While more op-eds favor the right, liberal perspectives have fair representation; therefore, they earn a least biased rating on the right side of the scale. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
