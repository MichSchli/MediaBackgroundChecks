History
[Founded in 1912](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Marcos_Daily_Record), the San Marcos Daily Record is a daily broadsheet newspaper published in San Marcos, Texas.
Funded by / Ownership
The San Marcos Daily Record is owned by
[Moser Media](https://www.mosercommedia.com/), which manages 32 small market newspapers in the USA. The paper is funded through advertising and subscriptions.
