History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, HRDive is an online news platform that focuses on the Human Resources industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.hrdive.com/about/), “HR Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping human resources.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates HRDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and FacilitiesDive. HRDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
HRDive publishes news and information related to HR management. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[Background checks include ‘lots of inaccuracies,’ researchers find](https://www.hrdive.com/news/background-checks-inaccurate/708351/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like
[Black talent remains underrepresented in advertising industry](https://www.hrdive.com/news/black-representation-advertising/708345/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
