History
[Founded in 2006](http://whois.domaintools.com/dailysnark.com), the Daily Snark is a hybrid sports satire website that publishes mostly real sports news. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not disclose ownership, and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Daily Snark discloses they are a satire site on the bottom of each page with this disclaimer: Daily Snark is your sports, news, and entertainment satire website. We provide you with the latest breaking news, memes, and videos! As mentioned above, this is a hybrid site that publishes real news, such as
[Cleveland Browns Parting Ways With GM John Dorsey](https://dailysnark.com/breaking-cleveland-browns-parting-ways-with-gm-john-dorsey/), along with sports news from a humorous perspective.
