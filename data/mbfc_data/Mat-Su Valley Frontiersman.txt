History
The Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman is a newspaper based in Wasilla, Alaska, serving the Matanuska-Susitna Borough and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at frontiersman.com.
Funded by / Ownership
Wick Communications owns the Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman and several small community papers across the Pacific Northwest. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Missing Wasilla woman found dead](https://www.frontiersman.com/news/missing-wasilla-woman-found-dead/article_2a86867c-b3d2-11ec-9ba5-a39127faa24e.html). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Mat-Su Valley Frontiersman leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. The news is generally reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
