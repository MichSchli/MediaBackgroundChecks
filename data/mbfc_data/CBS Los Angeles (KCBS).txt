History
[Founded in 1931](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KCBS-TV), KCBS-TV is a [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) owned-and-operated television station licensed to Los Angeles, California, United States. The associated website is [losangeles.cbslocal.com](https://losangeles.cbslocal.com/), which features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Los Angeles area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
CBS Los Angeles (KCBS) is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the CBS Los Angeles website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater Los Angeles region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording such as this: [OC Lawmaker Introduces Bill To Prevent Inmate EDD Fraud](https://losangeles.cbslocal.com/2020/12/17/edd-insurance-fraud-inmates-california/). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published by other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, CBS Los Angeles does not publish op-eds; however, they report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[Biden transition barrels forward while Trump administration stalls](https://www.cbsnews.com/live-updates/biden-presidency-transition-trump-administration-stall/) and this [Cheering, Celebrations Erupt In Streets Of Los Angeles After Joe Biden Projected To Win Presidency](https://losangeles.cbslocal.com/2020/11/07/cheering-celebrations-erupt-in-streets-of-los-angeles-after-joe-biden-projected-to-win-presidency/). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
