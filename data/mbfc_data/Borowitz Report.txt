History
The Borowitz Report is a satirical column from the
[New Yorker,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-yorker/) written by Andy Borowitz. Andy Borowitz is an American writer, comedian, satirist, and actor. Borowitz is a [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/)-bestselling author who won the first National Press Club award for humor. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Borowitz Report is owned and published by the left-leaning New Yorker magazine. The New Yorker generates revenue through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
The Borowitz Report is a political satire source that pokes fun at conservatives such as this
[Dr. Oz Hopes to Replace Rand Paul as Biggest Quack in Senate](https://www.newyorker.com/humor/borowitz-report/dr-oz-hopes-to-replace-rand-paul-as-biggest-quack-in-senate). In general, all Borowitz stories favor the left and denigrate the right, but every article disclaims the satirical nature of the content.
