History
The Southwest Orange Observer is a newspaper published in Winter Garden, Florida, United States. orangeobserver.com/southwest-orange is the website for the paper covering local news, sports, crime, government, and opinion.

Funded by / Ownership
The Southwest Orange Observer is owned and published by the
[Observer Media Group](https://www.yourobserver.com/contact-us) based in Sarasota, Florida. The Observer Media Group, founded by [Matt Walsh](https://www.crunchbase.com/person/matt-walsh-72d8) in 1995, publishes 11 newspapers and several magazines in Florida. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Southwest Orange Observer publishes local and regional news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Foundation student creates sweet treat for DG Doughnuts.](https://www.orangeobserver.com/photo-gallery/foundation-student-creates-sweet-treat-for-dg-doughnuts) The newspaper does not publish national or international news.
Editorially, the Southwest Orange Observer publishes an appropriate mix of right and left-leaning opinions. However, more favor the right. In general, they report factually and with credible sources, while op-eds are mostly balanced with differing viewpoints slightly leaning right.
