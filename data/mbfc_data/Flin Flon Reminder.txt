History
Flin Flon Reminder is an English-language newspaper in Flin Flon, Manitoba, Canada. thereminder.ca is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Flin Flon Reminder. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Flin Flon Reminder publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Flin Flon, Mantioba region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Investigations underway into string of break-ins, thefts: RCMP](https://www.thereminder.ca/local-news/investigations-underway-into-string-of-break-ins-thefts-rcmp-6913593). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Flin Flon Reminder’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[MLA Report: Preparing to elect a government that helps the north](https://www.thereminder.ca/opinion/mla-report-preparing-to-elect-a-government-that-helps-the-north-6405960). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
