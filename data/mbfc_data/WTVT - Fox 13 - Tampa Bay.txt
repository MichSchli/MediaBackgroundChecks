History
Launched in 1955, WTVT – Fox 13 – Tampa Bay is a
[Fox](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Tampa Bay, Florida, United States. fox13news.com is the associated website covering news, sports, crime, and weather for the Tampa Bay, FL, area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTVT – Fox 13 – Tampa Bay is owned by Fox Television Stations, a subsidiary of Fox Corporation and, in turn, owned by Rupert Murdoch. Fox Corporation owns 30 Fox Television Stations. Revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WTVT – Fox 13 – Tampa Bay reports the Tampa area’s news, sports, crime, and weather. Local news is provided with minimal bias in wording, such as
[Tampa man showed no remorse after violently killing Clearwater man on bike with tire iron, police say](https://www.fox13news.com/news/tampa-man-showed-no-remorse-after-violently-killing-clearwater-man-on-bike-with-tire-iron-police-say). Most news reports also contain video footage from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other Fox properties provide national and international news.
Editorially, they do not offer opinion content and cover politics with minimal bias, often citing the Associated Press for national politics. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
