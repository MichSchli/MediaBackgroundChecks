History
Founded in 1917, the Jewish Telegraphic Agency (JTA) is an international news agency and wire service serving Jewish community newspapers and media around the world, with 88 subscriber outlets listed on its website. The JTA has been
[described](http://forward.com/news/israel/179453/fledgling-jewish-news-service-rocks-boat-with-stri/) as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) of the Jewish Community. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The JTA is a not-for-profit corporation governed by an independent Board of Directors through 70 Faces Media. The Jewish Telegraphic Agency is funded through donations and online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In reviewing articles, the JTA displays minimal bias, such as this
[Man charged with hate crime in alleged robbery and assault on Jews in Beverly Hills on Shabbat](https://www.jta.org/2023/12/11/united-states/man-charged-with-hate-crime-in-alleged-robbery-and-assault-on-jews-in-beverly-hills-on-shabbat). News stories are sourced from other credible outlets like [Haaretz](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/haaretz/) and [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/).
Editorially, they report with balance on the Israel-Palestine conflict like this
[As it fights in Gaza, Israel is girding itself for another potential war in Lebanon](https://www.jta.org/2023/12/07/israel/as-it-fights-in-gaza-israel-is-girding-itself-for-another-potential-war-in-lebanon) and [More than 500 staffers of Jewish groups, most of them progressive, appeal to Biden to press for ceasefire in Israel-Hamas war](https://www.jta.org/2023/12/07/politics/more-than-500-staffers-of-jewish-groups-most-of-them-progressive-appeal-to-biden-to-press-for-ceasefire-in-israel-hamas-war). In general, the Jewish Telegraphic Agency demonstrates minimal bias through balanced, fact-based reporting.
