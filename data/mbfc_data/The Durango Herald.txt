History
[Founded in 1881](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Durango_Herald), The Durango Herald is a newspaper in Durango, Colorado. The Durango Herald is a small newspaper with a circulation of 6,200 daily and 7,000 on Sundays. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Durango Herald is owned by
[Ballantine Communications](https://ballantinecommunicationsinc.com/) and is funded through advertising and paid subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Durango Herald covers local news via journalists in the area. National/international news comes from the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). There is minimal use of loaded language and information is sourced properly. Editorially, The Durango Herald tends to [endorse more Republican candidates](https://durangoherald.com/articles/245805). Opinion columns are fairly balanced but lean a little more to the right overall.
