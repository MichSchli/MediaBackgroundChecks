History
The Lancaster Eagle-Gazette is a newspaper that serves Lancaster, Ohio, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. lancastereaglegazette.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Lancaster Eagle-Gazette. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Lancaster Eagle-Gazette covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Lancaster, Ohio, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Logan man indicted on 14 counts including photographing and videoing a minor](https://www.lancastereaglegazette.com/story/news/local/2023/11/02/logan-man-charged-with-illegal-use-of-a-minor-in-nudity-oriented-material-or-performance/71385042007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Lancaster Eagle-Gazette does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Ebel: Celebrating National Public Health Week](https://www.lancastereaglegazette.com/story/opinion/2023/04/02/ebel-celebrating-national-public-health-week/70055257007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
