History
[Autostraddle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Autostraddle) is an independently owned online magazine and social network for lesbian, bisexual, and queer women (cis and trans) as well as non-binary people. The website is a “politically progressive queer feminist media source” that features content covering LGBT and feminist news, politics, opinion, culture, arts and entertainment as well as lifestyle content such as DIY crafting, sex, relationships, fashion, food, and technology. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by /Ownership
The Excitant Group, LLC indicated they own Autostraddle. Advertising and a shop that sells merchandise support the company.
Analysis / Bias
In order to formulate a rating for this site, we looked at the political news section. AutoStraddle uses
[loaded emotional words](https://www.autostraddle.com/president-trump-bans-trans-people-from-military-service-via-fcking-twitter-388646/) in some headlines; however, the information contained within the articles is always well-sourced to most mainstream media outlets such as the [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/), [The Atlantic](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-atlantic/), etc. The majority of the articles were anti-right wing and aligned with the issues progressives support.
