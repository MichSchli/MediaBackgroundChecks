History
Bashaw Star is a newspaper published in Bashaw, Alberta, Canada. The website bashawstar.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Bashaw, Alberta, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Bashaw Star. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Bashaw Star covers news for Bashaw and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Woman charged with attempted murder after shooting in Camrose County](https://www.bashawstar.com/local-news/woman-charged-with-attempted-murder-after-shooting-in-camrose-county-7312691). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Bashaw Star does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
