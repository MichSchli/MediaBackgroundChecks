History
Ann Arbor News is a newspaper that is a part of the Michigan news portal (Mlive.com) that delivers content from nine websites/newspapers. The following are the sources for the MLive website: The Ann Arbor News, The Bay City Times, The Flint Journal,
[The Grand Rapids Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/grand-rapids-press/), Jackson Citizen Patriot, Kalamazoo Gazette, Muskegon Chronicle, The Saginaw News, and Advance Newspapers. [Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
MLive Media Group owns the Ann Arbor News. MLive is a part of Advance Publications Inc., controlled by the Newhouse Family, which owns numerous media publications such as Conde Nast,
[Vanity Fair](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/vanity-fair/), and the [New Yorker](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-yorker/). Advertising, sponsored content, and subscriptions generate revenue for the company.
Analysis / Bias
The Ann Arbor News publishes local news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Ann Arbor Coffee Roasting Co. to take over former State Street Espresso Royale location](https://www.mlive.com/news/ann-arbor/2021/07/ann-arbor-coffee-roasting-co-to-take-over-former-state-street-espresso-royale-location.html). National and international news is reported through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other Advance publications.
Editorially, they appropriately label opinion content from a wide, but never extreme, and reasonably balanced perspective. Ann Arbor News is a reliable, low-biased, and factual source of information for Michigan News.
