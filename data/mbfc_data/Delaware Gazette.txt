History
The Delaware Gazette is a newspaper based in Delaware, Ohio, serving Delaware and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at delgazette.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Delaware Gazette and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Delaware Gazette publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Housing study to be conducted in county](https://www.delgazette.com/news/94680/housing-study-to-be-conducted-in-county). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Delaware Gazette leans right in its views through the consistent publication of op-eds and news from a Christian perspective
[Find glory by accepting Christ into your heart](https://www.delgazette.com/opinion/94389/find-glory-by-accepting-christ-into-your-heart). In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
