History
Pacific Standard, formerly Miller-McCune, is an
[American magazine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Standard), published bimonthly in print and continuously online by the nonprofit Social Justice Foundation, headquartered in Santa Barbara, California. Pacific Standard primarily covers the topics of Economics, Education, Environment, and Social Justice. Its [target readers](https://www.mediabistro.com/go-freelance/how-to-pitch/how-to-pitch-pacific-standard/) are “influentials” who read [The Economist](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-economist/), [The Atlantic](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-atlantic/), [Mother Jones](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mother-jones/), and [Wired](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/wired-magazine/). The current editor is [Nicholas Jackson](https://psmag.com/page/about). [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Pacific Standard is owned and published by the nonprofit
[Social Justice Foundation](https://psmag.com/page/about), which strives to “promote meaningful public dialogue by reporting, in clear and concise language, the latest and most relevant scientific research in the areas of economic, educational, environmental, and social justice.” Revenue is derived from grants, donations advertising, and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Pacific Standard reports news related economics, education, the environment, and social justice from a liberal perspective. There is minimal use of loaded language in headlines such as this,
[WHY ARE HOMICIDE RATES SPIKING IN CALIFORNIA’S COUNTY JAILS?](https://psmag.com/social-justice/why-are-homicide-rates-spiking-in-california-county-jails). This story was republished from the credible [ProPublica](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/propublica/). [Original content](https://psmag.com/social-justice/how-media-shaped-the-narrative-around-stonewall) is also properly sourced to such media outlets such as the [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/), [ACLU](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/american-civil-liberties-union-aclu/), [LA Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/la-times-los-angeles-times/), and [The Conversation.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-conversation/) In general, story selection frequently favors the left through the [promotion](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/) of [climate change policy](https://psmag.com/environment/chennai-india-is-running-out-of-water-other-cities-will-be-soon), [LGBTQ](https://psmag.com/news/americans-underestimate-many-americans-support-sex-marriage-65902) and [pro-labor rights](https://psmag.com/news/why-labor-is-at-the-heart-of-the-latest-big-prison-strike).
