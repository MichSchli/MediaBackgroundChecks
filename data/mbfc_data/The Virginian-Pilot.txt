History
Founded in 1865,
[The Virginian-Pilot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Virginian-Pilot) is a daily newspaper based in Norfolk, Virginia. Commonly known as The Pilot, it is Virginia’s largest daily. It serves the five cities of South Hampton Roads and several smaller towns across southeast Virginia and northeast North Carolina. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Virginian-Pilot is owned by
[Tribune Publishing](https://www.tribpub.com/#), which owns numerous newspapers across the United States. Tribune Publishing is owned by [Alden Global Capital LLC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alden_Global_Capital); a New York-Based Hedge Fund described as “ [Vulture Capitalists](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/04/07/business/media/denver-post-opinion-owner.html)” by former [Denver Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/denver-post/) writers for the cuts made to newsrooms. Subscription fees and advertising generate revenue for the paper.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Virginian-Pilot covers local news through journalists with minimal bias in wording such as this
[The Block’ in Virginia Beach has new police surveillance cameras](https://www.pilotonline.com/news/crime/vp-nw-police-cameras-0605-20210604-zfnt746j6zbi5jzrmevdzn45de-story.html). Local news is sourced through quotes, interviews, and accounts, while National and world news comes directly from the [Associated Press](https://www.bozemandailychronicle.com/news/city/bozeman-continues-to-foster-midtown-development/article_51464c29-97fa-57b9-9535-05375f0853c8.html).
Editorially, the Virginian-Pilot tends to
[endorse](https://ballotpedia.org/The_Virginian-Pilot) Democratic candidates, though not 100%. A review of op-eds reveals more favor the left, such as this [Opinion: Politics shouldn’t dictate when to stand with LGBTQ Virginians](https://www.pilotonline.com/opinion/columns/vp-ed-column-turner-0602-20210601-kfkm4jrpsvasddeunbhctncnpe-story.html). The Virginian-Pilot reports news factually and with a left-leaning bias through editorial positions.
