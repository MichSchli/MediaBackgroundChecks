History
Launched in February 2002, Fars News is an English-language news website from Iran. The Editor in chief is Morteza Ghamari Vafa and Seyyed Nizamdin Mousavi is the managing director.
In addition to Persian reporting, Fars News Agency also provides Turkish and Arabic news, which features World and National Politics, Opinions, Society and Culture, Sci-Tech, and more.
[Fars News Agency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fars_News_Agency) is described as a pro-government news agency.

Analysis / Bias
[Reporters Without Borders](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reporters-without-borders/) has [reported](https://rsf.org/en/iran) Iran as “One of the most oppressive countries” for press freedom.
In review, the content of headlines and articles use minimal loaded words about national news, such as “
[Energy Ministry Caretaker: Iran to Reconstruct Syria’s Power lines, Power Plants](http://en.farsnews.com/newstext.aspx?nn=13960621001400).” However, they poorly source their articles, heavily quoting without providing links to sources such as [this article](http://en.farsnews.com/newstext.aspx?nn=13960621001542).
According to The Guardian, In September 2012, the Fars News Agency sourced
[The Onion](https://www.theguardian.com/world/us-news-blog/2012/sep/28/iran-fars-reports-onion-article-ahmadinejad) website’s news on Ahmadinejad as actual news without crediting the [Onion](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-onion/). In April 2013, Fars News Agency ran a story about an Iranian Scientist who claimed to build a [“Time Machine](http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2013/13/130412-iranian-time-machine-time-travel-grandfather-paradox/).” Further, according to the [Huffington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/huffington-post/), in January 2014, Fars News reported Nazi space aliens have run the US.
