History
[Founded in 2005](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Voice_of_San_Diego), the Voice of San Diego is a nonprofit news organization focused on issues affecting the San Diego region. They are online only. According to their about page. ” we reveal why things are the way they are and expose facts that people in power might not want out there. We also take the time to explain complex local public policy issues so you can be engaged and make good decisions.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Voice of San Diego is a non-profit that generates revenue through advertising, donations, and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Voice of San Diego covers local news, entertainment, lifestyle, and politics for the greater San Diego area. Articles and headlines often contain minimal to moderately loaded wording, such as this
[Demand for Housing Solutions Has Reached New Heights](https://www.voiceofsandiego.org/topics/politics/demand-for-housing-solutions-has-reached-new-heights/). When it comes to politics, they are often negative toward the right, such as in this [Politics Report: The Trump Conundrum](https://www.voiceofsandiego.org/topics/politics/politics-report-the-trump-conundrum/).
In general, the news is reported as factual with a left-leaning bias.
