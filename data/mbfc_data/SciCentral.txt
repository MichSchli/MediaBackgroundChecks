History
[Founded in 1997](https://whois.domaintools.com/scicentral.com) according to Whois, SciCentral is a science news website that aggregates content from mostly legitimate scientific sources. According to their [about page](https://www.scicentral.com/about.html) “SciCentral editors have been aggregating breaking research news from the most reputable and reliable sources. The service has received over 30 Web awards and enthusiastic reviews from leading science publishers (e.g.; [Science Magazine](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/science-magazine/), [The Lancet](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-lancet/), [The New Scientist](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-scientist/)) for the quality of its service. Over 700 other websites point to SciCentral as a trusted source of information.” [Read our profile on France’s Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/france-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
SciCentral is owned by
[Guy Orgambide](https://www.guyorgambide.com/), who is a Ph.D. scientist. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, SciCentral does not produce original content but rather aggregates science-related news through credible sources such as
[BBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bbc/), [Phys.org](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/phys-org/), and [TechXplore](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/techxplore/). While we cannot guarantee every news story on this page is factual and pro-science a great majority are pro-science based on the sources they choose to aggregate.
