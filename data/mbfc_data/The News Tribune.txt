History
[Founded in 1883](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_News_Tribune), The News Tribune is a daily newspaper in Tacoma, Washington, in the United States. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Tacoma News-Tribune t is owned by
[Chatham Asset Management,](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2018-08-30/behind-trump-s-tabloid-king-a-connected-and-flush-hedge-fund) a New Jersey-based hedge fund that holds a significant equity stake in Postmedia, and majority ownership of American Media Inc., which owns the [National Enquirer](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/national-enquirer/). [David Pecker](https://www.thestar.com/business/2016/10/19/executive-from-national-enquirer-parent-joins-postmedia-board.html), who owns American Media, Inc., joined the Postmedia board and then [resigned ](https://www.canadalandshow.com/postmedia-david-pecker-donald-trump/)from the board of Postmedia in 2018 due to his involvement of hush payments on behalf of his “friends,” including President Trump and Harvey Weinstein. Advertising and subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Tacoma News Tribune covers local news with minimal bias, such as this
[More than 300 new COVID-19 cases reported Wednesday in Pierce County](https://www.thenewstribune.com/news/coronavirus/article248036460.html#storylink=topdigest_latest). National and international news is covered by the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the McClatchy network.
Editorially, the Tacoma News Tribune clearly labels its opinion pieces through its Opinions section. During the 2020 election, they did not offer endorsements; however, a scan of op-eds reveals more favor for the left, such as this
[In a year of plague, Pierce County Republicans attempt to bring down health department](https://www.thenewstribune.com/opinion/editorials/article247616560.html). Generally, the news is reported factually, with more op-eds favoring the left.
