History
The Delta Optimist is an English-language newspaper in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. delta-optimist.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Delta Optimist. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Delta Optimist publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Vancouver BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[B.C. city planners en route to densifying single-family home neighbourhoods](https://www.delta-optimist.com/highlights/bc-city-planners-en-route-to-densifying-single-family-home-neighbourhoods-6820286). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Delta Optimist’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Most British Columbians don’t oppose the ’15-minute city’ — even if they don’t understand it](https://www.delta-optimist.com/opinion/most-british-columbians-dont-oppose-the-15-minute-city-even-if-they-dont-understand-it-6783702). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
