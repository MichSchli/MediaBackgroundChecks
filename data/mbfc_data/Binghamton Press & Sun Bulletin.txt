History
The Binghamton Press & Sun-Bulletin is a newspaper that serves Binghamton, New York, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. pressconnects.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Binghamton Press & Sun-Bulletin. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Binghamton Press & Sun-Bulletin covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Binghamton, New York area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Suspects in armed robbery caught after chase from Johnson City to Endwell, deputies say](https://www.pressconnects.com/story/news/local/2023/03/23/2-charged-in-armed-robbery-after-chase-from-johnson-city-to-endwell/70040862007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Binghamton Press & Sun-Bulletin does not publish op-eds; therefore, editorial bias is determined by story selection, which minimally leans left. In general, they are factual and least biased.
