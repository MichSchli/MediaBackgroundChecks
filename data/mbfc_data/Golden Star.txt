History
Golden Star is a newspaper published in Golden, British Columbia, Canada. The website thegoldenstar.net covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Golden, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Golden Star. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Golden Star covers news for Golden and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[More than 1K properties on alert, evacuation orders issued for wildfire near Invermere](https://www.thegoldenstar.net/news/300-hectare-wildfire-breaks-out-near-invermere-posing-threat-to-public/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Golden Star does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
