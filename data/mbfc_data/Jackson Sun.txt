Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Jackson Sun. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Jackson Sun covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Jackson, Mississippi area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Tyre Nichols case: Tennessee board suspends Memphis first responder licenses for inaction](https://www.jacksonsun.com/story/news/2023/02/03/tennessee-board-suspends-memphis-emt-licenses-in-tyre-nichols-case/69870414007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Jackson Sun does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented. After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
