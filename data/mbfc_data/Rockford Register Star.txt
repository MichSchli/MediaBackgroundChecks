History
The Rockford Register Star serves Rockford, Illinois, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. rrstar.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns the Rockford Register Star. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Rockford Register Star covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Rockford, Illinois area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Coroner: Victim of CherryVale Mall shooting was 25-year-old Rockford man](https://www.rrstar.com/story/news/local/2022/04/04/police-cherryvale-mall-shooting-victim-25-year-old-rockford-man/9464740002/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, the Rockford Register Star does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented such as this that favors the left:
[Violence divides us; we need to unite to solve racial inequities](https://www.rrstar.com/story/opinion/editorials/2020/06/01/violence-divides-us-we-need-to-unite-to-solve-racial-inequities/114293652/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
