History
The Bluefield Daily Telegraph is a newspaper published in the city of Bluefield, West Virginia. bdtonline.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Bluefield Daily Telegraph. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Bluefield Daily Telegraph covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Bluefield, West Virginia region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[$218 million in private investment: Virginia coalfield regions welcomed 725 jobs in 2022](https://www.bdtonline.com/news/218-million-in-private-investment-virginia-coalfield-regions-welcomed-725-jobs-in-2022/article_086480fa-6794-11ed-99a3-7b5b479ff5a3.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
