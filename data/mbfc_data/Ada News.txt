History
The Ada News is a newspaper published in Ada, Oklahoma. theadanews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Ada News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Ada News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Ada, Oklahoma region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Firefighters get quick stop on 6th Street blaze](https://www.theadanews.com/news/local_news/firefighters-get-quick-stop-on-6th-street-blaze/article_9680c59a-3162-11ee-81a1-439225d5502f.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
