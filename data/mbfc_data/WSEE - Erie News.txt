History
Founded in 1954, WSEE- Erie News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Erie, Pennsylvania. erienewsnow.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Erie region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lilly Broadcasting, based in Pennsylvania, owns WSEE. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WSEE- Erie News publishes news for the Erie area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[State Police Trooper Charged with DUI while on Duty](https://www.erienewsnow.com/story/47877445/state-police-trooper-charged-with-dui-while-on-duty). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WSEE- Erie News does not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
