History
Founded in 1954 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station, WTOC – Savannah serves Savannah, Georgia, and the surrounding area. Wtoc.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTOC – Savannah is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WTOC – Savannah provides news coverage for Savannah, Georgia, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Savannah City Council approves plan for old fairgrounds property](https://www.wtoc.com/2021/10/29/savannah-city-council-approves-plan-old-fairgrounds-property/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WTOC – Savannah does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
