History
[Founded in 1981](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Health_(magazine)), Health is an American magazine focused on women’s health. The magazine’s topics range from diet to dealing with life issues such as relationships and stress. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
It was purchased by
[Time Inc](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/time/). in 1991. The company now operates as a part of Time’s [Southern Progress Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_Progress_Corporation). Revenue is derived from advertising and magazine sales.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Health Magazine is not a hard science magazine. In fact, it is a magazine you will find in the checkout line of your local supermarket. A typical cover of the magazine will feature multiple articles on fad diets, anti-aging, and natural cures. Here is a sample cover:

Health Magazine typically does not use loaded language, and they usually source their information to mostly credible sources. Some of the claims made by Health Magazine are not fully supported by
[scientific evidence](http://www.health.com/health/gallery/0,,20430736,00.html). However, we are not rating this source pseudoscience because most of the information is credible. We are just suggesting caution when attempting their diets or natural cures.
