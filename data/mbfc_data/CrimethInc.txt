History
Founded in 1996,
[CrimethInc](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CrimethInc.).—also known as CWC, which stands for either “CrimethInc. Ex-Workers Collective,” is a decentralized anarchist collective of autonomous cells. CrimethInc. emerged in the mid-1990s, initially as the hardcore zine Inside Front, and began operating as a collective in 1996. It has since published widely read articles and zines for the anarchist movement and distributed posters and books of its own publication. Funded by / Ownership
CrimethInc. Ex-workers Collective owns and publishes CrimethInc. Donations represent the primary source of funding.
Analysis / Bias
The CrimethInc website covers news on political uprisings worldwide, especially those favoring anti-capitalist measures. Articles and headlines accurately reflect the content, such as this
[Colombia Has Lost Its Fear](https://crimethinc.com/2021/05/05/colombia-has-lost-its-fear-a-nationwide-uprising-continues-in-the-face-of-state-violence) and [What Work Steals from Us.](https://crimethinc.com/2021/04/15/what-work-steals-from-us-steal-something-from-work-day-2021) For the most part, they favor worker rebellions and those who oppose capitalism around the world.
