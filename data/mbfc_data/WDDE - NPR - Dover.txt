History
WDDE – NPR – Dover is an NPR FM radio station (91.1 FM) based in Dover, Delaware, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WDDE – NPR – Dover is owned and broadcast by Delaware First Media Corporation. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
