History
The Pomeroy Daily Sentinel is a newspaper based in Pomeroy, Ohio, serving Meigs County and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at mydailysentinel.com.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Pomeroy Daily Sentinel and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Pomeroy Daily Sentinel publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Suspects in custody in homicide investigation](https://www.mydailysentinel.com/news/71133/suspects-in-custody-in-homicide-investigation). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Pomeroy Daily Sentinel leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
