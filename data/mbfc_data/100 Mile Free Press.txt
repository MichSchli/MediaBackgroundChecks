History
The 100 Mile Free Press is a newspaper published in 100 Mile House, British Columbia, Canada. The website 100milefreepress.net covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater 100 Mile House area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns 100 Mile Free Press. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, 100 Mile Free Press covers news for 100 Mile House and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording such as this
[Minimal overnight growth at Keremeos Creek fire in South Okanagan](https://www.100milefreepress.net/news/minimal-overnight-growth-at-keremeos-creek-fire-in-south-okanagan/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, 100 Mile Free Press does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left, such as this
[Climate change, new construction mean more ruinous fires](https://www.100milefreepress.net/news/climate-change-new-construction-mean-more-ruinous-fires/). Generally, news reporting is factual with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
