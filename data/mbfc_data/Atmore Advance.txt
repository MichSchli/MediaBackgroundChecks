History
Founded in 1927, The Atmore Advance is a broadsheet newspaper published in Atmore, Alabama. atmoreadvance.com covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Atmore Advance is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Atmore Advance primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Fire destroys tractor trailer Thursday](https://www.atmoreadvance.com/2022/07/22/fire-destroys-tractor-trailer-thursday/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Atmore Advance labels their opinion section appropriately with more op-eds favoring the right, such as this:
[More gun control is not the answer](https://www.atmoreadvance.com/2022/06/08/more-gun-control-is-not-the-answer/). In general, the Atmore Advance is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
