History
Founded in 1976, WTXL – Tallahassee News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Tallahassee, Florida, United States. wtxl.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTXL – Tallahassee News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WTXL – Tallahassee News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Tallahassee, Florida, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Leon County Property Appraiser holds extended hours to assist with exemptions](https://www.wtxl.com/news/local-news/leon-county-property-appraiser-holds-extended-hours-to-assist-with-exemptions). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[White House physician releases details on Biden’s physical](https://www.wtxl.com/news/national/white-house-physician-releases-details-on-bidens-physical). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
