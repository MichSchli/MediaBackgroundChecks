History
Founded in 1948, NBC Chicago – WMAQ is an NBC-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Chicago, Illinois, United States. NBCChicago.com is the associated website covering news, weather, sports, crime, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
NBC Chicago – WMAQ is owned by NBCUniversal a subsidiary of Comcast, which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
