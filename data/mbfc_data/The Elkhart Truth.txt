History
Founded in 1889, The Elkhart Truth is a daily newspaper published in Elkhart, Indiana. Elkharttruth.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Paxton Media Group,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paxton_Media_Group) which owns several right-leaning newspapers across the USA, such as the [High Point Enterprise](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/high-point-enterprise/). Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Elkhart Truth reports national news by republishing articles from credible newsagents such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). They report local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias using neutral headlines such as [“Democratic candidate forum set for Jan. 18”](http://www.elkharttruth.com/news/democratic-candidate-forum-set-for-jan/article_c3b2dff1-aaf9-510f-9f6e-c2b8bec027dd.html) and [“Salvation Army Week proclaimed beginning May 14.”](http://www.elkharttruth.com/hometown/elkhart/salvation-army-week-proclaimed-beginning-may/article_aafdfe64-1051-5ce7-94b1-924f8d2a6360.html)
Editorially, there is a reasonable balance between left and right-leaning opinions, with slightly more favoring the right such as this:
[Obsessed with Trump](https://www.elkharttruth.com/opinion/columnists/obsessed-with-trump/article_83cc31f9-e3e6-5d61-a38f-3a5262447234.html).
