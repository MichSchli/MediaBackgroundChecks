History
Two radio hosts, Clay Travis and Buck Sexton, founded the conservative news and opinion website
[ClayandBuck.com](https://www.clayandbuck.com/about-clay-and-buck/). It was introduced in 2021 as an addition to The Clay Travis & Buck Sexton Show, their daily radio program. Politics, sports, and culture from a conservative perspective are covered on the website.
The Clay and Buck radio show took over the time slot of the late
[Rush Limbaugh](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/rushlimbaugh-com/). [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website and the radio show are both owned by Premiere Networks, which is a subsidiary of iHeartMedia, a publicly traded company owned by institutional and individual investors. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
ClayandBuck.com presents news from a strongly right-leaning perspective. The website features news stories, opinion pieces, and podcasts on politics, culture, and sports. It often criticizes Democrats and liberal policies while promoting conservative positions, such as this
[Sen. Blackburn Blasts the Left’s False Narratives](https://www.clayandbuck.com/sen-blackburn/).
The website employs strong language in its headlines and articles that favor conservative viewpoints, which may draw in a particular audience looking for news that supports their beliefs. The headline “
[Biden Regime Finally Shoots Down Chinese Spy Balloon](https://www.clayandbuck.com/biden-regime-finally-shoots-down-chinese-spy-balloon/),” for instance, demonstrates strong right-leaning bias.
Editorially, ClayandBuck.com is mostly an opinion radio show and website that consistently favors a conservative perspective. Individually, Clay and Buck have made false statements regarding
[Covid-19](https://healthfeedback.org/claimreview/denmark-didnt-ban-covid-19-vaccines-for-people-under-50-clay-travis-toby-young/) and the [January 6, 2021, riot at the Capitol](https://www.politifact.com/article/2021/dec/15/2021-lie-year-lies-about-jan-6-capitol-attack-and-/). In general, ClayandBuck.com hold strong right-leaning biases and promotes propaganda, conspiracy theories, and pseudoscience.
