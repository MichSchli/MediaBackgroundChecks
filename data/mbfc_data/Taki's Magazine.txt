History
Founded on February 5, 2007, Taki’s Magazine is an extreme right-wing online magazine of politics and culture published by journalist
[Taki Theodoracopulos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taki_Theodoracopulos). According to a [Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com/media/1999/aug/02/mondaymediasection.pressandpublishing) article entitled “The way of the Jackal,” Taki Theodoracopulos has racist views. [Taki’s Magazine](http://takimag.com/about#axzz4t213pJAw) proclaims on its about page: “Our only ideology is to be against the junk culture foisted upon us by Hollywood and the mainstream media, and mirages of a new world order.” Taki’s Magazine’s former Executive Editor was [Richard Spencer,](https://www.linkedin.com/in/richard-spencer-5040135) an American white supremacist who coined the term [Alternative Right](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_B._Spencer). [Gavin McInnes](http://takimag.com/contributor/gavinmcinnes/151), the [founder](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/10/16/nyregion/proud-boys-gavin-mcinnes.html) of the hate group known as the Proud Boys, wrote his last column entitled “ [So Long, Taki!”](http://takimag.com/article/so_long_tak_gavin_mcinnes#axzz4t213pJAw) He has been described in media reports as a significant figure within the alt-right movement. Gavin McInnes has also been featured in Hatewatch articles by the Southern Poverty Law Center, found [here](https://www.splcenter.org/hatewatch/2017/06/21/hatewatch-headlines-6212017) and [here](https://www.splcenter.org/hatewatch/2017/08/10/do-you-want-bigots-gavin-because-how-you-get-bigots). Funded by / Ownership
[Taki Theodoracopulos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taki_Theodoracopulos) owns and publishes Taki’s Magazine. Advertising and donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Taki’s Magazine features syndicated columns by such conservative figures as
[Pat Buchanan](http://www.politifact.com/personalities/pat-buchanan/), [Ann Coulture](https://www.politifact.com/personalities/ann-coulter/), and [Michelle Malkin](http://www.politifact.com/personalities/michelle-malkin/), who have mixed records with fact-checkers. Taki’s Magazine published an article, [“The Talk: Nonblack Version,”](http://takimag.com/article/the_talk_nonblack_version_john_derbyshire#axzz1rHzlfgy8) written by John Derbyshire, making questionable racial suggestions.
Editorially, they are anti-immigration such as this
[Coming to America](https://www.takimag.com/article/coming-to-america/). In general, Taki’s promotes a far-right perspective and uses writers who consistently fail fact checks.
