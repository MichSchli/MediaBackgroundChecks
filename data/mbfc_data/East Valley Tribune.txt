History
The East Valley Tribune is a newspaper published in Tempe, Arizona, United States. The paper covers local and national news, crime, business, and sports.

Funded by / Ownership
[Times Media Group](https://timespublications.com/#brands) owns the newspaper, a company that owns numerous newspapers and websites in Arizona. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
