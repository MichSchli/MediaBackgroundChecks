History
Launched in 1981, Now Toronto, also known as Now Magazine, is an alternative entertainment weekly from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Now Magazine coverage features news, culture, arts, and entertainment.
[Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Now Magazine is owned by Michael Hollett and Alice Klein. According to their about page,
[Now Magazine](https://nowtoronto.com/about) is a certified B Corporation. B is for “benefit,” and B Corps are a new global designation for sustainable businesses. Now publishes on recycled paper with vegetable inks. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Now utilizes moderate loaded words in their headlines such as “
[Inquiry into alleged Bruce McArthur murders puts chief in hot seat”](https://nowtoronto.com/news/why-police-inquiry-into-alleged-mcarthur-murders-should-not-/) and typically they source to credible sources such as [The Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/), [CBC News (Canadian)](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbc-news-canadian-broadcasting/), and [NY Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/); however, they also use unreviewed sources such as strategyonline.ca, aqualitas.ca, and cannabisatwork.com.
Now Magazine presents left-biased coverage of USA news such as
[“Trump’s Colonel Blimp brigade pushing Trudeau government to rapidly increase military spending.](https://nowtoronto.com/news/donald-trump-trudeau-military-defence-spending/)” They also [criticize](https://nowtoronto.com/news/justin-trudeau-marijuana-townhall/) PM Justin Trudeau’s Liberal government, for example, “Libs’ marijuana legalization tour touches down in the Big Smoke, and things quickly get wacky. Message to Justin: dude, the hypocrisy is becoming a little too hard to take.”
