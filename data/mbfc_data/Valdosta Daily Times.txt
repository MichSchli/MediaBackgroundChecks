History
The Valdosta Daily Times is a daily newspaper published in the city of Valdosta, Georgia. valdostadailytimes.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Valdosta Daily Times. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Valdosta Daily Times covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Valdosta, Georgia, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Group protests death of Hancock County woman while in custody](https://www.valdostadailytimes.com/news/local_news/group-protests-death-of-hancock-county-woman-while-in-custody/article_29cabf11-4531-563a-8b5f-e0500fc51160.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
