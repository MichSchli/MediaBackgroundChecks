History
[Founded in 1992](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skeptic_(U.S._magazine)), Skeptic, colloquially known as Skeptic magazine, is a quarterly science education and science advocacy magazine published internationally by The Skeptics Society, a nonprofit organization devoted to promoting scientific skepticism and resisting the spread of pseudoscience, superstition, and irrational beliefs. The current editor is [Michael Shermer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Shermer). Funded by / Ownership
Skeptic Magazine is owned and published by The Skeptics Society, which is a nonprofit funded through donations and a shop that sells merchandise.
