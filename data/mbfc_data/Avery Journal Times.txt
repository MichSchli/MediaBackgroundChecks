History
The Avery Journal Times is a newspaper published in Avery County, North Carolina, United States, that serves the surrounding area. The website is located at averyjournal.com, covering news, sports, crime, and weather.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The newspaper is owned by
[Adams Publishing Group](https://www.poynter.org/business-work/2017/who-are-the-adams-family-and-why-are-they-buying-newspapers-by-the-dozen/), which owns over one hundred papers across the USA. Stephen Adams is the owner and [donates to Republicans](https://web.archive.org/web/20080724023422/http://www.madcowprod.com/billboardenvy.htm), raising 1 million dollars for George W. Bush in 2000. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Avery Journal Times covers local news through journalists with minimal bias in wording, such as
[Avery Community Foundation announces nearly $11,000 in grant funding](https://www.averyjournal.com/news/community/avery-community-foundation-announces-nearly-11-000-in-grant-funding/article_b96f91b6-81c7-11ee-862d-4b0b8441a989.html). Local information is sourced through quotes, interviews, and accounts, while National and world news comes directly from the [Associated Press](https://www.bozemandailychronicle.com/news/city/bozeman-continues-to-foster-midtown-development/article_51464c29-97fa-57b9-9535-05375f0853c8.html).
Editorially, The Avery Journal Times provides a balance in opinions, with slightly more favoring the left. They generally report the news factually and with a slight Left-leaning bias.
