History
[Founded in 1954](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WTVF), WTVF is a [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Nashville, Tennessee, United States. Newschannel5.com is the associated news website for the TV station that covers Nashville news, sports, weather, crime, and lifestyle. [Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTVF – NewsChannel5 is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Advertising and sponsored content generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, NewsChannel5 covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Nashville area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Nashville moving to Phase 3 on Oct. 1; bars can increase capacity starting today](https://www.newschannel5.com/rebound/nashville-to-begin-phase-3-on-oct-1-bars-can-increase-capacity-starting-friday). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. National news is derived from a variety of sources, with most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Trump appeals order blocking exclusion in district drawing](https://www.newschannel5.com/news/election-2020/trump-appeals-order-blocking-exclusion-in-district-drawing). In general, the news is reported factually and with low bias.
