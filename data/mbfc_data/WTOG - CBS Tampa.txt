History
Founded in 1966, WTOG – CBS Tampa Miami is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-owned-and-operated television station through CW 44 licensed to St. Petersburgh, Florida, United States. The associated website is tampa.cbslocal.com that features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Tampa area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTOG – CBS Tampa is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the WTOG – CBS Tampa website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater St. Petersburgh and Tampa Bay Metro region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording: [Tampa Police Investigate Shooting At Downtown Apartment Complex](https://tampa.cbslocal.com/2021/08/17/tampa-police-investigate-shooting-at-downtown-apartment-complex-1/). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published from other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WTOG – CBS Tampa does not publish op-eds; however, they do report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[DeSantis Pushes Election Changes, Doesn’t Brief Supervisor Of Elections In Hillsborough County](https://tampa.cbslocal.com/2021/02/20/desantis-pushes-election-changes-doesnt-brief-supervisor-of-elections-in-hillsborough-county-3/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
