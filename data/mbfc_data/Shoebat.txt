History
[Shoebat.com](https://whois.domaintools.com/shoebat.com) was founded by Walid Shoebat, a Muslim-turned-Christian journalist who is a self-proclaimed “Former Muslim Brotherhood Member Now Peace Activist.” His son, Theodore Shoebat, also provides articles occasionally. Content primarily includes criticism of Islam, including Christian end-times conspiracies. [Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Shoebat.com does not disclose ownership but appears to be owned by Walid Shoebat. Revenue is primarily derived through donations.
