History
Founded in 1949, WTVJ – NBC 6 – Miami is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Miami, Florida, United States. NBCmiami.com is the associated website covering news, weather, sports, crime, and lifestyle. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTVJ – NBC 6 – Miami is owned by NBCUniversal a subsidiary of Comcast, which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
