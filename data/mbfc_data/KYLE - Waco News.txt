History
Founded in 1988, KYLE – Waco News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station licensed to Waco, Texas. The associated website is fox44news.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KYLE – Waco News is owned by
[Nexstar Media Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nexstar_Media_Group), which owns around 200 TV stations nationwide. The founder, chairman, president, and CEO of Nexstar is Perry A. Sook. [According](https://www.opensecrets.org/pacs/pacgot.php?cmte=C00567388&cycle=2018) to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Nexstar Media group gave more money to Republicans in the 2018 election cycle (45% to Democrats, 55% to Republicans). However, in 2020 they gave more to Democrats (55% Democrats, 45% to Republicans). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KYLE- Waco News website publishes news for the greater Waco, Texas area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias such as this:
[Cove police arrest suspected burglars, recover property](https://www.fox44news.com/news/local-news/cove-police-arrest-suspected-burglars-recover-property/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through Nexstar Media Wire, the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[Gov. Greg Abbott holds campaign rally in McAllen, vows to keep oil and gas jobs running](https://www.fox44news.com/news/state/gov-greg-abbott-holds-campaign-rally-in-mcallen-vows-to-keep-oil-and-gas-jobs-running/). Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoiding using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
