History
Red State Watcher is a conservative news and opinion website based in the United States. A
[Whois search](http://whois.domaintools.com/redstatewatcher.com) reveals the domain was purchased in 2015. [Read our profile on the United States media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
According to its about page, redstatewatcher.com is owned and operated by Sanh Oriyavong. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Red State Watcher is a conservative news website that has a strong right-wing bias in reporting choices. They frequently use loaded emotional wording in there headlines and articles:
[‘Marxist Lesbian’ American Library Association head says conservative book bans are ‘attacks on children’.](https://redstatewatcher.com/aggregate.asp?id=216798) When it comes to sourcing they usually rely on tweets and sometimes questionable sources such as [Breitbart](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/breitbart/). Red State Watcher frequently promotes pro-Trump [propaganda](http://redstatewatcher.com/article.asp?id=133890).
Editorially, all stories favor a conservative perspective like this
[Virginia parents appeal to remove a so-called anti-racist policy from school district: ‘Racist at its core’](https://redstatewatcher.com/aggregate.asp?id=216799). In general, Red State Watcher holds far-right biases and is not always factual.
