History
[Jewish Unpacked](https://jewishunpacked.com/about/) is a website launched by OpenDor Media, a US registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization founded in 2009. Unpacked is a brand created by OpenDor Media for young people to address issues related to Israel and Judaism. OpenDor Media [states](https://opendormedia.org/why-media/), “We are proudly and transparently positive about Israel and Zionism, Jewish values and ideals.” Founder [Raphael Shore](https://opendormedia.org/about/#executive-team), CEO Andrew Savage, and EVP Noam Weissman head the organization. A complete list of the [team](https://opendormedia.org/about/#executive-team) members is available. [Read our profile on the United States Media and Government](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)

The ownership of Jewish Unpacked’s parent company is undisclosed, and the publication is financed through donations, online advertising, and merchandise sales.

Analysis / Bias
Jewish Unpacked shares digital content on Jewish issues from OpenDor and other contributors to promote the organization’s mission to a wider audience.
OpenDor produces documentaries, such as “
[Unsafe Spaces,”](https://jewishunpacked.com/unsafe-spaces/) which examines the marginalization of Jewish students in progressive spaces. The film’s introduction uses emotionally loaded, right-leaning language, claiming that Jewish students are marginalized from progressive spaces just for being Jewish. However, the experiences shared by the Jewish activists featured in the film are subjective and based on their perspectives and experiences.
This article from Jewish Unpacked discusses Joe Biden’s self-identification as
[“The “non-Jewish Zionist”](https://jewishunpacked.com/joe-biden-the-non-jewish-zionist/) despite being a devout Catholic. It highlights his family’s connections to Judaism through his children’s marriages to Jewish spouses. It also mentions Biden’s repeated statements expressing his love for Israel, such as “My name is Joe Biden, and everybody knows I love Israel.” sourced to [YnetNews](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/ynetnews/).
Regarding Israeli Politics, Jewish Unpacked publishes articles with emotionally loaded language, such as
[“When does legitimate criticism become ‘incitement’?”](https://jewishunpacked.com/when-does-legitimate-speech-cross-the-line-into-incitement/) The article focuses on recent comments by Israeli public figures that were seen as inciting violence against Prime Minister Netanyahu and his government, related to proposed judicial reforms that have triggered mass protests in Israel. This story is sourced from the [Jerusalem Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-jerusalem-post/), a right-center news outlet. In general, Jewish Unpacked is fact-based and holds right-leaning pro-Israeli biases.
