History
[Founded in 2010,](http://whois.domaintools.com/activistpost.com) Activist Post is a left-leaning conspiracy and pseudoscience website but unique due to its longevity. The website completely lacks transparency as they do not have an About page and does not disclose an editor or ownership.
Funded by / Ownership
Activist Post does not disclose ownership, and revenue is derived through donations and advertisements.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Activist Post is a strongly left-leaning conspiracy and pseudoscience website that focuses on police state abuses and other government measures to control people. Some of the information you will find on this website relates to
[anti-vaccination](https://web.archive.org/web/20190807183229/https://www.activistpost.com/2019/06/vaccine-damage-is-far-greater-than-long-held-views.html) [propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/), [9/11 conspiracies](https://web.archive.org/web/20190806210109/https://www.activistpost.com/2019/07/ny-fire-commissioners-call-for-new-9-11-investigation-citing-overwhelming-evidence-of-explosives.html), [chemtrails](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-chemtrails/), and [5G conspiracies](https://www.activistpost.com/2019/12/hundreds-of-birds-dead-in-north-wales-could-5g-have-played-a-role.html). In general, Activist Post holds left-leaning anti-authoritarian positions while consistently promoting unproven claims.
