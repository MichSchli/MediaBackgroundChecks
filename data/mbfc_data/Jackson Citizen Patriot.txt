History
The Jackson Citizen Patriot is a newspaper that is a part of the Michigan news portal (Mlive.com) that delivers content from nine websites/newspapers. The following are the sources for the MLive website:
[The Ann Arbor News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/ann-arbor-news-mlive-com-bias/), [The Bay City Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bay-city-times-bias/), [The Flint Journal](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/flint-journal-bias/), [The Grand Rapids Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/grand-rapids-press/), Jackson Citizen Patriot, Kalamazoo Gazette, Muskegon Chronicle, The Saginaw News, and Advance Newspapers. [Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
MLive Media Group owns the Jackson Citizen Patriot. MLive is a part of Advance Publications Inc., controlled by the Newhouse Family, which owns numerous media publications such as Conde Nast,
[Vanity Fair](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/vanity-fair/), and the [New Yorker](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-yorker/). Advertising, sponsored content, and subscriptions generate revenue for the company.
