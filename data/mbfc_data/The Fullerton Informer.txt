History
Founded in 2013 by Joe Imbriano, The Fullerton Informer is a right-wing conspiracy and pseudoscience website. The website does not have an about page, but at the top of every page, it states, “EVERYTHING YOU HAVE BEEN TOLD IS A LIE AND A FRAUD. JOE IMBRIANO IS THE FULLERTON INFORMER, AND BECAUSE YOU HAVE THE RIGHT TO KNOW, WE DARE TO PUSH THE ENVELOPE.”
Joe Imbriano was an
[unsuccessful candidate](https://www.fppc.ca.gov/enforcement/EnfDivCaseResults/stipulated-agreements/2017-sdo/february-sdo/joe-imbriano-4-fullerton-city-council.html) for Fullerton City Council in the November 8, 2016, General Election. Funded by / Ownership
The Fullerton Informer is owned and run by Joe Imbriano. The website does not appear to have a source of revenue; however, there is an associated
[Youtube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCVt3STOc1XNEWOIWYW0UKaA) with 121K subscribers, which may generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Fullerton Informer is an over the top tin foil hat conspiracy website where they routinely promote misinformation such as this:
[THE CORONAVIRUS HOAX IS A MASSIVE PSYCHOLOGICAL WARFARE OPERATION TO USHER IN FORCED VACCINATIONS, QUARANTINES, RELOCATIONS, INCARCERATIONS, TRAVEL RESTRICTIONS, FORCED MEDICAL PROCEDURES, FOOD SHORTAGES, AND AN END TO ALL OF YOUR RIGHTS](https://thefullertoninformer.com/the-coronavirus-hoax-is-a-massive-psychological-warfare-operation-to-usher-in-forced-vaccinations-quarantines-relocations-incarcerations-travel-restrictions-forced-medical-procedures-and-an-end-t/). This story is not sourced. Other topics discussed on this website are 5g dangers, [anti-Mask propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-mask-misinformation/), 9/11 hoax, and [anti-vaccination misinformation](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/). In general, almost everything on this website is false or deliberate misinformation.
