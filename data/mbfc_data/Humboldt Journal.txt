History
Humboldt Journal is an English-language newspaper in Humboldt, Saskatchewan, Canada. sasktoday.ca/north/humboldt-journal is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Humboldt Journal. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Humboldt Journal publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Humboldt, Saskatchewan region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Province calling for help to fight against aquatic invaders](https://www.sasktoday.ca/north/humboldt-journal/province-calling-for-help-to-fight-against-aquatic-invaders-6989356). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Humboldt Journal’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Opinion: Geopolitical catastrophes in the making](https://www.sasktoday.ca/opinion/geopolitical-catastrophes-in-the-making-6984879). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
