History
[Founded in 2002](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Complex_Networks), Complex was an entertainment and lifestyle print magazine until 2016. Since 2016, they have resided online at compex.com. Complex covers Music, Style, Pop Culture, Sports, Life, Sneakers, and Shows. The website lacks transparency as they do not provide an about page, name editors, or clearly discloses ownership. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Complex indicates that Complex Media owns them; however, they do not state that Complex Media is owned equally by Verizon Communications and Hearst Communications. Revenue is derived through affiliate marketing programs and a shop that sells merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Complex publishes original news stories focusing on entertainment and lifestyle. They also have a Youtube Channel that receives over 1 billion views per year. Articles not pertaining to politics utilize moderately loaded wording such as this
[Funk Flex Says Drake Would’ve Been Better Than Jay-Z If Reference Tracks Never Surfaced](https://www.complex.com/music/2021/01/funk-flex-drake-would-have-been-better-than-jay-z-if-reference-tracks-never-surfaced). Stories are generally appropriately sourced.
Editorially, Complex does not publish op-eds; however, under their Life section, they report on politics with a moderate left-leaning bias such as this
[Trump Reportedly Told Pence He Could Be ‘Patriot’ or ‘P*ssy’ Prior to Capitol Riot](https://www.complex.com/life/2021/01/trump-told-pence-he-could-be-patriot-or-pussy-prior-to-capitol-riot) and [These Companies, Banks, and Organizations Are Cutting Ties With Trump and Other Republicans](https://www.complex.com/life/2021/01/companies-banks-organizations-cutting-ties-with-trump-and-republicans/). Further, some of their stories are speculative and do not rely on verified information such as this [‘Stop the Steal’ Organizer Says Several GOP Congressmen Helped Plan the Rally](https://www.complex.com/life/2021/01/stop-the-steal-organizer-says-several-gop-congressman-helped-plan-rally). While this story may be true, the accuser’s credibility is suspect; however, the report did cover the accused’s perspective and denials. The news is generally reported mostly factually and with moderate Left-Center bias.
