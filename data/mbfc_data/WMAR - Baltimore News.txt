History
Founded in 1947, WMAR – Baltimore News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Baltimore, Maryland, United States. Wmar2news.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WMAR – Baltimore News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WMAR – Baltimore News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Baltimore area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[‘We’re all fine. We’re going to be okay:’ Baltimore shootout brings Highlandtown neighbors closer](https://www.wmar2news.com/news/local-news/were-all-fine-were-going-to-be-okay-shootout-brings-highlandtown-neighbors-closer). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[President Biden announces ban on Russian ships](https://www.wmar2news.com/news/national/russia-ukraine-conflict/president-biden-announces-ban-on-russian-ships). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
