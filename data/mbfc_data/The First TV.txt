History
Launched in October 2019, The First is an online TV station that broadcasts conservative opinion personalities and news. According to their
[about page](https://www.thefirsttv.com/welcome-to-the-first/), “The First is a new network for free speech and big ideas. It’s a home for people who don’t automatically believe the first thing they read in the [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/) or hear on [NPR](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/npr/), [Fox](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news/), or [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/). A platform for people who are a little bit skeptical, a little bit curious, and a whole lot interested in diving much deeper into the issues that matter.”
The First can be
[watched](https://www.thefirsttv.com/watch/) on their website or through an app on Youtube and Pluto TV platforms. The current lineup as of this date is Bill O’Reilly, Dana Loesch, Buck Sexton, Jesse Kelly, Mike Slater, and other contributors. The website also features news and opinion articles written by contributors. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The First lacks transparency in disclosing ownership as it is not clearly stated on the website. According to their Terms of Service page, they are owned by
[The First Digital Inc](https://www.thefirsttv.com/thefirsttv-com-website-terms-of-service/). We are unable to find information on The First Digital Inc. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The First provides a TV station that broadcasts conservative commentary from hosts such as Bill O’Reilly, Dana Loesch, Buck Sexton, Jesse Kelly, and Mike Slater. The website also provides text-based news and opinion, primarily written by the hosts mentioned above and other contributors. Articles and headlines often contain loaded emotional wording such as this
[Was it racist when Obama did it too? | Mike Slater](https://www.thefirsttv.com/was-it-racist-when-obama-did-it-too-mike-slater/). This article, like many, provides a short text summary and then a link to a video clip of one of the hosts, Mike Slater. In another article, they promote coronavirus misinformation such as this [Dr. Fauci was wrong | Dana Loesch](https://www.thefirsttv.com/dr-fauci-was-wrong-dana-loesch/), and during the Presidential Election of 2020, they promoted [ unproven election fraud information](https://www.thefirsttv.com/nevada-voter-fraud-evidence-revealed-buck-sexton/).
