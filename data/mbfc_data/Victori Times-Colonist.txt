History
Founded in 1884, The Times Colonist is an English-language daily newspaper in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. Timescolonist.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Times Colonist. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Times Colonist publishes sports, weather, and national, world, and local news for the greater Victoria, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Sandstone development plan a ‘game changer for south Nanaimo](https://www.timescolonist.com/real-estate/sandstone-development-plan-a-game-changer-for-south-nanaimo-1.24181781).’ The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, The Times Colonist’s Op-eds tend to favor the left with stories such as this
[Healing the planet through global collaboration](https://www.timescolonist.com/opinion/blogs/spiritually-speaking/healing-the-planet-through-global-collaboration-1.24180051). This paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
