History
[Founded in 2005](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MapLight), MapLight is a nonpartisan, nonprofit research organization that reveals and tracks the influence of money in politics in the United States. The website also publishes news related to the influence of money in politics. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Maplight is a nonprofit that is funded through donations. Some notable donors include left-leaning foundations such as the
[Sunlight Foundation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sunlight_Foundation), [Open Society Foundations](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/open-society-foundations/), [MacArthur Foundation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MacArthur_Foundation), [Ford Foundation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ford_Foundation), and the [Tides Foundation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tides_(organization)).
Analysis / Bias
In review, The organization publishes a free public database linking money and political data sources, including campaign contributions to politicians, how politicians vote on bills, and support and opposition to legislation. MapLight provides data on both campaign finance and voting behavior in one database. MapLight uses an in-house research team in addition to data sources that include the National Institute on Money in State Politics, the
[Center for Responsive Politics](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), and [Govtrack](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/govtrack/).
The website also publishes news with a left-leaning editorial bias such as this:
[Manufacturers of Forever Chemicals Give Generously to House Members](https://maplight.org/story/manufacturers-of-forever-chemicals-give-generously-to-house-members/) and this [We Must Address Online Voter Suppression to Protect Our Democracy](https://maplight.org/story/we-must-address-online-voter-suppression-to-protect-our-democracy/). They also frequently report negatively on the [Trump administration](https://maplight.org/?s=trump) and [conservatives.](https://maplight.org/story/conservative-legal-interests-funneled-2-7-million-to-nra-freedom-partners-around-gorsuch-fight/)
