History
Cell Host & Microbe Journal is a peer-reviewed open-access journal published by Cell Press that covers Microbes. According to its
[about page](https://www.cell.com/cell-host-microbe/aims), “Cell Host & Microbe will publish novel findings related to microbes (which includes bacteria, fungi, parasites, and viruses) from molecular and cellular biology to translational studies with particular emphasis on the interface between the microbe and its host (vertebrate, invertebrate, or plant; unicellular or multicellular).”
Cell Press, based in Cambridge, Massachusetts, publishes over
[50 scientific journals](https://www.cell.com/). Cell Press Journals are 50% open-access as of 2022. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Cell Host & Microbe Journal is published by Cell Press which is a subsidiary of
[Elsevier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elsevier), a Dutch information and analytics company and one of the world’s foremost providers of scientific, technical, and medical information. It was established in 1880 as a publishing company. Elsevier’s parent company is RELX plc, a British multinational information and analytics company headquartered in London, England. The journal generates revenue through subscription fees and fees for open-access publishing.
Analysis / Bias
Cell Host & Microbe Journal is a hybrid (some open-access) journal that publishes information and research related to Microbes. The journal primarily publishes research such as this
[Global translational induction during NLR-mediated immunity in plants is dynamically regulated by CDC123, an ATP-sensitive protein](https://www.cell.com/cell-host-microbe/fulltext/S1931-3128(23)00039-2).
Cell Press is an open-access/hybrid publisher of scientific journals. There are two types of Open Access Journals, legitimate and predatory, both of which charge the author for publication.
[Predatory journals](https://predatoryjournals.com/about/) often lack peer review and are generally considered not credible. The Cell Host & Microbe Journal is considered a [legitimate, open-access publisher](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5017830/) that is frequently indexed in [PubMed](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pubmed/). However, Cell Press has been criticized for [retracting some studies.](https://retractionwatch.com/category/by-journal/cell/)
Although Cell Press has retracted some studies in other publications, Cell Host & Microbe Journal publishes credible scientific studies that are factual and appropriately peer-reviewed.
