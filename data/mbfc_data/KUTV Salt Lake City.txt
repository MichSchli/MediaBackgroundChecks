History
[Kutv.com](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KUTV) is a CBS-affiliated television station licensed to Salt Lake City, Utah, United States. Broadcasting since 1954, KUTV particularly focuses on news about Utah. KUTV delivers local news, breaking news, weather, and sports. [Read our country media profile of the USA.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KUTV is owned by the
[Sinclair Broadcasting Group](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/sinclair-broadcast-group/). Sinclair owns numerous right-leaning TV stations throughout the USA. They are funded through an advertising and subscription model.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KUTV republishes articles from credible sources such as the
[AP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and utilizes sources such as [http://blog.udot.utah.gov](http://blog.udot.utah.gov), and [utahpolicy.com](http://utahpolicy.com). In reporting, KUTV sometimes utilizes emotional language in their headlines, such as [“Utah doctor’s response to Trump tweet on climate change goes viral”](http://kutv.com/news/local/utah-doctors-response-to-trump-tweet-goes-viral) and [“New poll shows Romney with huge lead in Utah Senate race”](http://kutv.com/news/local/new-poll-shows-romney-with-huge-lead-in-utah-senate-race).
According to a
[Salt Lake Tribune](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/salt-lake-tribune/) article titled “ [Statement mandated by KUTV’s right-wing owner only highlights its hypocrisy](https://www.sltrib.com/artsliving/tv/2018/03/30/scott-d-pierce-statement-mandated-by-kutvs-right-wing-owner-only-highlights-its-hyprocrisy/)”, KUTV is running a statement about fake news, which is mandated by the right-leaning Sinclair Broadcast Group, which owns the station.
