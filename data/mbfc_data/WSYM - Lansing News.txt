History
Founded in 1982, WSYM – Lansing News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated station licensed to Lansing, Michigan, United States. fox47news.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WSYM – Lansing News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WSYM – Lansing News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Lansing, Michigan area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Zahra, Bernstein reelected to Michigan Supreme Court](https://www.fox47news.com/news/election-2022/zahra-bernstein-reelected-to-michigan-supreme-court). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Governor Gretchen Whitmer addresses supporters following re-election victory](https://www.fox47news.com/news/election-2022/governor-gretchen-whitmer-addresses-supporters-following-re-election-victory). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
