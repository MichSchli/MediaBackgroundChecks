History

He studied history at McMaster University; he served as an Infantry soldier in the Canadian Forces, according to his about page. In his own words, he is “a Scholar, Author, Film Maker, and a God-fearing Catholic, who loves women for their illogical nature.” He also has a bio with [IMDB](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm6816523/bio?ref_=nm_ov_bio_sm).
