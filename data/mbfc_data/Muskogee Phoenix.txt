History
The Muskogee Phoenix is a newspaper published in Muskogee, Oklahoma. muskogeephoenix.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Muskogee Phoenix. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Muskogee Phoenix covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Muskogee, Oklahoma, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[OSSAA adopts new transfer rule](https://www.muskogeephoenix.com/sports/ossaa-adopts-new-transfer-rule/article_3bb0537c-982f-11ee-96e8-9fdd2e2c7d32.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
