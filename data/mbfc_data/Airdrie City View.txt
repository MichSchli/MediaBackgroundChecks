History
The Airdrie City View is an English-language daily newspaper in Airdrie, Alberta, Canada. airdrietoday.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Airdrie City View. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Airdrie City View publishes sports, weather, and national, world, and local news for the greater Victoria, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Long cold winter still ahead for RVC residents](https://www.airdrietoday.com/rocky-view-news/long-cold-winter-still-ahead-for-rvc-residents-6303584). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, The Airdrie City View’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[EDITORIAL: Libraries are community hubs](https://www.airdrietoday.com/opinion/editorial-libraries-are-community-hubs-6211358). This paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
