Analysis / Bias
In review, Mental Daily covers mental health, sociology, psychopharmacology, and cyberpsychology news. News stories are derived from pro-science and peer-reviewed sources such as this
[Study Shows How The Effects Of Bisphenol A Exposure In The Placenta May Impact Fetal Brain Development](https://www.mentaldaily.com/article/2022/01/study-shows-how-the-effects-of-bisphenol-a-exposure-in-the-placenta-may-impact-fetal-brain-development). During the Covid-19 pandemic, they reported accurately on coronavirus and vaccines [Study Indicates India And U.S. Were Breeding Grounds For The Dissemination Of COVID-19 Misinformation](https://www.mentaldaily.com/article/2021/12/study-indicates-india-and-u-s-were-breeding-grounds-for-the-dissemination-of-covid-19-misinformation).
Editorially, they offer an opinion page that leans left with stories such as this
[A Cerebral Anatomization Of Conspiratorial Beliefs](https://www.mentaldaily.com/article/2020/01/misinformation-vs-disinformation). The news is generally factual and pro-science with a left-leaning political bias.
