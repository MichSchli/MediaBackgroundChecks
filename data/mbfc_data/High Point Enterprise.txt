Analysis / Bias
In review, the High Point Enterprise reports local news with minimal bias, such as this
[Wake Baptist sets up temporary clinics](https://hpenews.com/news/14934/wake-baptist-sets-up-temporary-clinics/). National News is through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
