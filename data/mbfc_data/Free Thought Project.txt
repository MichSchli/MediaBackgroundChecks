History
According to their
[about page](https://thefreethoughtproject.com/about-us/), the Free Thought Project is “a hub for Free Thinking conversations about the promotion of liberty and the daunting task of government accountability.” The Free Thought Project was founded in 2013 by [Jason Bassler](https://www.facebook.com/jason.bassler.1?fref=hovercard) and [Matt Agorist.](https://twitter.com/MattAgorist) [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website is funded through online advertising and an ad-free paid subscription model.
Analysis / Bias
The Free Thought Project reports some credible news and conspiracies such as
[Chemtrails](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-chemtrails/), [Geoengineering](https://thefreethoughtproject.com/geoengineering-spray-chemicals-dim-sun-co2/) by the government, and [anti-vaccination](https://thefreethoughtproject.com/?s=vaccinations) [propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/). The Free Thought Project also uses sensational headlines that favor the left, such as this: [WATCH: Trump Melts Down as Man Holds Up Photo of Him and Billionaire Pedophile Epstein at Rally](https://thefreethoughtproject.com/watch-trump-melts-down-as-man-holds-up-photo-of-him-and-billionaire-pedophile-epstein-at-rally/).
