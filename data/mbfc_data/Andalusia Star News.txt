History
Founded in 1948, The Andalusia Star News is a broadsheet newspaper published in Andalusia, Alabama. andalusiastarnews.com covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Andalusia Star News is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Andalusia Star News primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Barber joins White and McClung accounting firm](https://www.andalusiastarnews.com/2022/07/14/barber-joins-white-and-mcclung-accounting-firm/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Andalusia Star News labels their opinion section appropriately with more op-eds favoring the right, such as this:
[Lessons of fatherhood from the greatest Father](https://www.andalusiastarnews.com/2022/06/19/lessons-of-fatherhood-from-the-greatest-father/). In general, the Andalusia Star News is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
