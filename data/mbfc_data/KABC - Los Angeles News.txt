History
Founded in 1947, KABC – Los Angeles News is the west coast flagship station of the ABC television network, licensed to Los Angeles, California, United States. ABC7.com is the associated website that provides news, weather, and sports for the Los Angeles Metropolitan region.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KABC – Los Angeles News is owned by the ABC Owned Television Stations subsidiary of
[The Walt Disney Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the KABC – Los Angeles News website covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the Los Angles Metro area. Reporters cover local news with minimal bias, such as this
[1 killed, 2 injured in South Los Angeles shooting, police say](https://abc7.com/south-los-angeles-la-shooting-deadly-shootings/12364955/). Many local stories contain embedded videos from the TV station. National and world news is covered by [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/), [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [ESPN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/espn/), and other ABC stations.
Editorially, KABC – Los Angeles News does not provide opinion pieces; however, they report on politics. They provide original reporting with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden’s student loan forgiveness plan temporarily halted by appeals court](https://abc7.com/student-loan-forgiveness-gop-states-lawsuit-rejected-debt-relief-program-judge-henry-edward-autrey/12355588/). They also rely on political news from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
