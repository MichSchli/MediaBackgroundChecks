History
Founded in 2012, The Atlanta Blackstar is a website that mainly focuses on the African American perspective regarding politics. The co-founders of Atlanta Black Star are Neil Nelson, Tracy Dornelly, and Andre Moore. According to their about page, “Atlanta Black Star is a narrative company. We publish narratives intentionally and specifically to enlighten and transform the world.”
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Diamond Diaspora Media, registered to Neil Nelson, owns the Atlanta Black Star. Advertising and donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Editorially, they are sometimes balanced in presenting different political perspectives, such as
[this opinion piece](http://atlantablackstar.com/2017/05/01/black-woman-wants-la-jefferson-monument-stay-standing-says-shes-right-side-history/) where a writer supports preserving confederate monuments, which is a right-wing position, and [this one](http://atlantablackstar.com/2017/08/21/world-responding-trumps-remarks-charlottesville/) where they are highly critical of Donald Trump. They also frequently report negatively on conservatives such as this [‘Shame on You’: Toyota Faces Calls for Boycott After It’s Reported That Company Donated to Republicans Who Objected to Certifying Presidential Election](https://atlantablackstar.com/2021/06/29/shame-on-you-toyota-faces-calls-for-boycott-after-its-reported-that-company-donated-to-republicans-who-objected-to-certifying-presidential-election/).
Articles and headlines contain moderately emotional wording such as this
[‘We Suffer Disproportionately High Rates of Anxiety’: Why Brown Girl Jane Built a Business Around Providing CBD Products Specific to Black Women’s Wellness](https://atlantablackstar.com/2021/07/14/we-suffer-disproportionately-high-rates-of-anxiety-why-brown-girl-jane-built-a-business-around-providing-cbd-products-specific-to-black-womens-wellness/) but is well-sourced. In general, Atlanta Black Star is mostly factual and reports with a left-leaning bias.
