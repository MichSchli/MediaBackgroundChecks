History
Founded in 1948, KPIX – CBS San Francisco is a CBS-owned-and-operated television station licensed to San Francisco, California, United States. The associated website is
[sanfrancisco.cbslocal.com](http://sanfrancisco.cbslocal.com) that features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Chicago area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KPIX – CBS San Francisco is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the KPIX – CBS San Francisco website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater San Francisco Bay Area region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording: [San Jose Approves Nation’s 1st Mandatory Gun Liabililty Insurance, Annual Fees.](https://sanfrancisco.cbslocal.com/2021/06/30/san-jose-gun-liability-insurance-annual-fees/) A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published from other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, KPIX – CBS San Francisco does not publish op-eds; however, they do report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[No Parade, But Plenty Of Joy As Fully-Reopened San Francisco Celebrates Pride Weekend](https://sanfrancisco.cbslocal.com/2021/06/25/sfpride-2021-fully-reopened-san-francisco-celebrates-without-parade/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
