History
The Saanich News is a newspaper published in Saanich, British Columbia, Canada. The website saanichnews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Saanich area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Saanich News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Saanich News covers news for Saanich and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Kaela Mehl pleads guilty to the second-degree murder of her 18-month-old daughter](https://www.saanichnews.com/news/mom-accused-of-killing-toddler-scheduled-for-sentencing-in-victoria-court/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Saanich News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
