History
[Founded in 1952](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KUSA_(TV)), KUSA-TV is an [NBC-affiliated](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/) television station licensed to Denver, Colorado, United States. 9News.com is the TV station website that reports on news and programming. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
9News KUSA-TV is owned and operated by TEGNA Inc., which operates 66 television stations in 54 markets. They state TEGNA’s purpose is to serve the greater good of our communities. Revenue is based on advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, 9News covers news, sports, traffic, weather, and crime for the Denver area. Articles and headlines rarely use load language such as this:
[‘This will be a different summer in Denver:’ Mayor planning for life after stay-at-home order](https://www.9news.com/article/news/health/coronavirus/mayor-hancock-update-covid-19-response/73-46e7db37-9e66-416b-b112-3fc0627eb67a). A video accompanies many news stories from the TV station. National and international news is written by staff reporters as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, 9News does not publish opinion pieces; however, they cover politics with minimal bias:
[Trump uses daily coronavirus briefing to air complimentary video](https://www.9news.com/article/news/health/coronavirus/trump-uses-pandemic-virus-briefing-to-air-laudatory-video/73-ea7b12c3-b90e-44aa-b140-9899cc1d5569). Finally, the website features fact checks from TEGNA’s fact check service [VERIFY Fact check.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/verify-fact-check-bias/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
