History
People For the American Way (
[PFAW](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/People_for_the_American_Way)) is a progressive advocacy group in the United States. Organized as a 501(c)(4) non-profit organization, PFAW was registered in 1981 by the television producer [Norman Lear](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Norman_Lear). PFAW was founded in opposition to the publicized agenda of the Moral Majority, a formerly prominent and influential American political organization associated with the Christian right. According to their [mission](https://www.pfaw.org/about-us/): “People For the American Way works to build a democratic society that implements the ideals of freedom, equality, opportunity, and justice for all. We encourage civic participation, defend fundamental rights, and fight to dismantle systemic barriers to equitable opportunity. We fight against right-wing extremism and the injustice it fosters.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
In review, People for the American Way (PFAW) is an organization that supports progressive causes such as civil rights. PFAW has a blog that reports news with a progressive Left bias such as this
[THE NATION: The Right’s War on History and Truth](https://www.pfaw.org/blog-posts/the-nation-the-rights-war-on-history-and-truth/). This story, like most, is appropriately sourced.
Editorially, they focus on voting rights and frequently report negatively on conservatives and former President Trump
[Trump Judges Strike Down Rules to Improve Fuel Efficiency and Cut Air Pollution from Truck Trailers: Our Courts, Our Fight](https://www.pfaw.org/blog-posts/trump-judges-strike-down-rules-to-improve-fuel-efficiency-and-cut-air-pollution-from-truck-trailers-our-courts-our-fight/). They advocate for [climate change](https://www.pfaw.org/press-releases/people-for-the-american-way-applauds-biden-focus-on-climate-and-environmental-justice/) action and support LGBTQ rights. They favor the Biden Administration but often advocate for a more progressive agenda than Biden is willing to pursue.
