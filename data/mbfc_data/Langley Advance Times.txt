History
The Langley Advance Times is a newspaper published in Langley, British Columbia, Canada. The website ;angleyadvancetmes.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Langley area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Langley Advance Times. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
