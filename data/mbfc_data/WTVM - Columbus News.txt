History
Founded in 1953 and an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station, WTVM – Columbus News serves Columbus, Georgia, and the surrounding area. wtvm.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTVM – Columbus News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WTVM – Columbus News provides news coverage for Columbus, Georgia, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Longtime Lee County Coroner Bill Harris dies](https://www.wtvm.com/2022/06/29/longtime-lee-county-coroner-bill-harris-dies/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WTVM – Columbus News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
