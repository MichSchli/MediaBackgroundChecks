History
The Lake County Press is a newspaper published in Lake County, Minnesota. lakecountypress.news covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the Lake County Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Lake County Press is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Lake County Press covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Lake County, Minnesota, area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Vacancy declared for District 1 county commissioner](https://www.lakecountypress.news/2023/02/17/vacancy-declared-for-district-1-county-commissioner/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
