History
Founded in 1953, WKOW – Madison News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Madison, Wisconsin, United States. wkow.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Madison area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WKOW – Madison News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WKOW – Madison News publishes news for Madison and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[577 guns surrendered during Dane County gun buyback event](https://www.wkow.com/news/577-guns-surrendered-during-dane-county-gun-buyback-event/article_3e82908c-1d7a-11ed-9b87-5b777255b17d.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
