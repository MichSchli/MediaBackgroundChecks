History
Founded by Dash MacIntyre, The Halfway Post is a satire website that publishes liberal satire and humor. According to their
[about page](https://halfwaypost.com/about/): “Based out of St. Louis, The Halfway Post publishes America’s most halfway true satirical news. When it comes to contemporary conservatism and Donald Trump’s egomania, I tell the quiet part out loud! [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Halfway Post is owned by Dash MacIntyre and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Halfway Post produces original satire from a liberal perspective. Most articles mock conservatives such as this
[It’s Apparent Christians Aren’t Praying Hard Enough For Racism To Go Away](https://halfwaypost.com/2020/06/03/its-apparent-christians-arent-praying-hard-enough-for-racism-to-go-away/) and Donald Trump, [Trump Had The Army Clear Protesters So He Could Walk To McDonalds](https://halfwaypost.com/2020/06/02/trump-had-the-army-clear-protesters-so-he-could-walk-to-mcdonalds/).
The Halfway Post clearly identifies that they are a satire site on their about page as well as in their tagline which is displayed on each page: A Gazette of Halfway Real Satirical News.
