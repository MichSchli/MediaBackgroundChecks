History
The Barrhead Leader is an English-language newspaper in Barrhead, Alberta, Canada. townandcountrytoday.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Barrhead Leader. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Barrhead Leader publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Barrhead, Alberta region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Sentencing delayed for man caught with sawed-off shotgun](https://www.townandcountrytoday.com/athabasca-news/sentencing-delayed-for-man-caught-with-sawed-off-shotgun-6406885). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Barrhead Leader’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Stop playing Russian roulette](https://www.townandcountrytoday.com/opinion/stop-playing-russian-roulette-2265224). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
