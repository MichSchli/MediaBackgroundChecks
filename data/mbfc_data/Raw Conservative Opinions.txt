History
[Founded in 2016](http://whois.domaintools.com/rawconservativeopinions.com), Raw Conservative Opinions is a hyper-partisan conservative website that lacks transparency. According to Whois Domain Search and a [contact page](https://rawconservativeopinions.com/contact/) they are located in Louisville, Kentucky. The website does not offer up any other names associated with the site, nor do they provide an about page or disclose ownership. However, most stories published come from Mark Hale, who does not offer a bio. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Raw Conservative Opinions do not disclose ownership and revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
