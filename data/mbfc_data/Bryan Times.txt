History
The Bryan Times is a newspaper based in Bryan, Ohio, serving Bryan and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at bryantimes.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Bryan Times and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Bryan Times publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Defiance man dies in 1 car crash](https://www.bryantimes.com/news/local/defiance-man-dies-in-1-car-crash/article_042a5f4e-f143-5e45-b32b-82a9968ba76d.html). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Bryan Times leans right in its views through the consistent publication of op-eds and news from a Christian perspective,
[Stand up for what is right, just, holy](https://www.bryantimes.com/news/religion/stand-up-for-what-is-right-just-holy/article_694e0813-c199-5bbe-b943-43fa70b9a8ef.html). Generally, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
