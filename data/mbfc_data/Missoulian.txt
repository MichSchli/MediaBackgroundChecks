History
Founded in 1870, the Missoulian is a print newspaper serving the city of Missoula and Western Montana.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Lee Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lee_Enterprises), which according to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/) has donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) since 1990. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Missoulian covers local news with journalists and reporters throughout the Missoula and greater western Montana region. For national news, they use the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). All news is reported with minimal loaded words and bias. The Missoulian always sources their information properly. [Editorially,](http://missoulian.com/opinion/editorial/#tracking-source=menu-nav) there is a balance between right and left-leaning opinions with just a slight edge to the left.
