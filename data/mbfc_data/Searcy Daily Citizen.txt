History
The Searcy Daily Citizen is a daily newspaper published in Searcy, Arkansas. thedailycitizen.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Paxton Media Group,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paxton_Media_Group) which owns several right-leaning newspapers across the USA, such as the [Elkhart Truth](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-elkhart-truth/). Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Searcy Daily Citizen reports national news by republishing articles from credible newsagents such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). They report local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias using neutral headlines such as [Beebe 42-year-old reportedly put knives to wife’s throat, told her calling police ‘would be the last thing she did](https://www.thedailycitizen.com/news/beebe-42-year-old-reportedly-put-knives-to-wifes-throat-told-her-calling-police-would/article_167111d6-c72b-5a7f-98d0-5b3b1d7a1c03.html)‘. All information is properly sourced.
Editorially, there is a reasonable balance between left and right-leaning opinions, with slightly more favoring the right such as this:
[Quiet Catholic Hill speaks for the persecuted](https://www.thedailycitizen.com/opinion/steve-brawner-quiet-catholic-hill-speaks-for-the-persecuted/article_6a3c7190-6a7d-53f4-8546-c5229d313434.html). In general, the paper is fact-based and holds a slight right-leaning editorial bias.
