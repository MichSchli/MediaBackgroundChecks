History
Founded in 1961, KIFI – Idaho Falls News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)/ [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Idaho Falls, Idaho. The associated website is localnews8.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns KIFI – Idaho Falls News and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KIFI – Idaho Falls News website publishes news for the greater Idaho Falls, Idaho area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:
[Construction continues on Idaho Central Credit Union Bengal Alumni Center](https://localnews8.com/news/pocatello/2022/07/08/construction-continues-on-idaho-central-credit-union-bengal-alumni-center/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [CBS News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/), [CNN Newswire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[Lawmakers call for gas-tax holiday in Special Legislative Session](https://localnews8.com/life/gas-prices/2022/06/08/lawmakers-call-for-gas-tax-holiday-in-special-legislative-session/). Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoiding using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
