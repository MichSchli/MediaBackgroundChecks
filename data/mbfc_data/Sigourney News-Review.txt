History
The Sigourney News-Review is a newspaper published in Sigourney,
[Indiana](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-governors-bias-ratings/), United States. The paper covers news, sports, lifestyle, and opinion for Sigourney and the surrounding area. sigourneynewsreview.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Mid America Publishing owns and publishes the Sigourney News-Review. Mid America Publishing publishes
[several community newspapers](https://www.midampublishing.com/content/mid-america-publications) throughout Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Sigourney News-Review reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Sigourney, Indiana region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[Retired Postmaster Chosen as Grand Marshall](http://www.sigourneynewsreview.com/content/retired-postmaster-chosen-grand-marshall). The newspaper and website do not publish national news, focusing on local news reporting.
Editorially, the Sigourney News-Review reasonably represents both sides. A review of op-eds demonstrates a reasonably low bias; however, slightly more op-eds favor the right. While more op-eds favor the right, liberal perspectives have fair representation; therefore, they earn a least biased rating on the right side of the scale. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
