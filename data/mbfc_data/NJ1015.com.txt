History
NJ1015.com is the website for Trenton, NJ based radio station WKXW. The website covers news, traffic, weather and provides links to listen live to the radio station.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
NJ1015.com is owned by
[Townsquare Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Townsquare_Media), which owns over 300 radio stations across the country in over 60 markets. Revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, NJ1015.com covers local news for Trenton, NJ, and the surrounding area through reporters. Articles and headlines often do not use loaded emotional language such as this
[Hospitals, already reeling, face $38M in cuts in new NJ budget](https://nj1015.com/hospitals-already-reeling-face-38m-in-cuts-in-new-nj-budget/). However, there are some articles that do such as this [Jersey’s top Republican goes on rant about Murphy’s ‘Baby Bonds’.](https://nj1015.com/jerseys-top-republican-goes-on-rant-about-murphys-baby-bonds-opinion/) Information is typically properly sourced. National news is reported by the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, NJ1015.com leans conservative with several radio hosts supporting a conservative perspective such as
[Bill Spadea](https://savejersey.com/2015/11/jim-gearhart-bill-spadea-nj-101-5-morning-program/). Further, op-eds frequently report favorably on President Donald Trump and negatively on Democrats such as this [CNN screws up Murphy endorsement location (Opinion)](https://nj1015.com/cnn-screws-up-murphy-endorsement-location-opinion/). In general, story selection and editorial positions moderately favor the right.
