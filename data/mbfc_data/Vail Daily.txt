History
Vail Daily is a daily newspaper serving Vail, Colorado. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Vail Daily is published by Swift Communications, which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the western USA. Ogden Newspapers owns Swift. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Vail Daily publishes news for Vail, Colorado, and the surrounding area. Local news is covered through reporting with minimal bias in wording, such as
[Body discovered in Vail’s Gore Creek, police investigating](https://www.vaildaily.com/news/vail-police-investigating-after-body-is-discovered-in-gore-creek/). The paper and website do not cover national and international news.
A review of editorials demonstrates a reasonable balance without extremes such as this
[Our View: Don’t wait to find out with new variant](https://www.vaildaily.com/opinion/our-view-dont-wait-to-find-out-with-new-variant/). In general, they exhibit low bias and produce fact-based news.
