History
Quesnel Cariboo Observer is a newspaper published in Quesnel, British Columbia, Canada. The website quesnellobserver.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Quesnel, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Quesnel Cariboo Observer. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Quesnel Cariboo Observer covers news for Prince Rupert and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Police investigating Quesnel motorcycle crash](https://www.quesnelobserver.com/news/police-investigating-quesnel-motorcycle-crash/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Quesnel Cariboo Observer does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
