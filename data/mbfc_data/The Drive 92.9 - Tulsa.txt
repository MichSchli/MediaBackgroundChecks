History
The Drive 92.9 – Tulsa is a radio station based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. 929thedrive.com is the website for the radio station and its news programming.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Griffin Communications, a media company based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, owns The Drive 92.9 – Tulsa. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Drive 92.9 – Tulsa primarily covers local news, crime, entertainment, and sports for the Tulsa, Oklahoma, area. Local news is covered by the Least Biased
[KOTV – News On 6](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/kotv-newson6-bias/) TV station with minimal bias through reporters like this [Emergency Crews Battle Fire At Tulsa Home](https://www.929thedrive.com/story/631f4f6509d721072c54f4c1/emergency-crews-battle-fire-at-tulsa-home-). The Drive 92.9 – Tulsa does not offer editorials and rarely covers political news. Most political news is derived from third-party sources such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). In general, news reporting is factual and displays minimal bias.
