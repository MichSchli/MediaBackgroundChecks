History
Founded in 1953 and an ABC-affiliated television station, WBAY-TV serves Green Bay, Wisconsin, and the surrounding area. Wbay.com is the associated website for the news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WBAY-TV is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WBAY Action 2 News provides news coverage for Green Bay, Wisconsin, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Manitowoc student with handgun arrested after fight near school.](https://www.wbay.com/2021/04/22/manitowoc-student-with-handgun-arrested-after-fight-near-school/) The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WBAY Action 2 News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
