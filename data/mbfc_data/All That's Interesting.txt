History
Founded in 2010, All That’s Interesting is a digital media outlet that covers a wide range of topics, including history, science, culture, and current events. According to their
[about page](https://allthatsinteresting.com/about-all-thats-interesting), “All That’s Interesting is a place for seekers. From the lesser-known byways of human history to the uncharted corners of today’s world, All That’s Interesting seeks out the stories that illuminate our past, present, and future.” The website is based in New York City. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website lacks transparency as they do not clearly disclose ownership. Research indicates they may be owned by the
[PBH Network](https://www.linkedin.com/company/pbh-network/about/). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
All That’s Interesting is divided into four main sections: History, Science, News, and Today in History. They curate unusual and amusing stories related to crime, historical photos and stories, and feature scientific studies. The website aggregates content from other sources, including scientific journals, local TV stations, and YouTube, with occasional original content. The stories cover a wide range of topics, from inspiring to quirky to informative.
The website provides accurate attribution for photos and aggregated reporting, and headlines generally reflect the content of articles such as this
[Scientists Just Discovered Prehistoric Footprints Made By A Gigantic Amphibian Older Than The Dinosaurs](https://allthatsinteresting.com/rhinesuchid-temnospondyl-footprints).
Editorially, All That’s Interesting does not take political positions; however, they report negatively on former President Trump, such as this
[Three Times Donald Trump Made Millions By Screwing Over Hundreds Of People](https://allthatsinteresting.com/donald-trump-fraud). They also report negatively on current President Joe Biden; however, in a much more lighthearted manner like this [‘I Am A Gaffe Machine’: 30 Joe Biden Quotes That Prove Him Right](https://allthatsinteresting.com/joe-biden-quotes). In general, most content is factual and holds a moderate left-leaning bias.
