History
The Smith’s Falls Record News is a newspaper published in Smith’s Falls, Ontario, Canada.
[insideottawavalley.com/ontario-communities/smiths-falls](https://www.insideottawavalley.com/ontario-communities/smiths-falls/) provides online local, national, and world news for the greater Port Perry, Ontario region.
Read our profile on the
[Canadian government’s influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of Smith’s Falls Record News is Metroland Media Group Ltd., a subsidiary of Nordstar Capital LP. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In 2015 and
[2021](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2021/09/19/the-stars-editorial-board-endorses-liberals-for-2021-federal-election.html), the [Toronto Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/) (Nordstar), which owns the Smith’s Falls Record News publisher Metroland Media Group, [endorsed](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2015/10/09/for-justin-trudeau-and-the-liberal-party-editorial.html) the Liberal Party under Justin Trudeau.
In review, Smith’s Falls Record News uses low-biased wording when reporting local news such as this:
[‘Defrauded the victim’: Police arrest Smiths Falls female on several fraud charges over $44,500](https://www.insideottawavalley.com/news/defrauded-the-victim-police-arrest-smiths-falls-female-on-several-fraud-charges-over-44-500/article_33c1400c-83ee-5244-9e5b-0b0908e60da2.html). National news is derived from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/), while world news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, a review of op-eds slightly favors the left. In general, they are fact-based and hold a left-leaning editorial bias.
