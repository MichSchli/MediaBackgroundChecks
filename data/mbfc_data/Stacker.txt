History
[Stacker](https://stackermedia.com/about/) is a media company that creates and shares data-based articles and works with partners on joint projects. Stacker aims to help publishers and brands cut costs and save time by providing ready-to-publish content. They also offer custom content creation services. Stacker.com is their website that publishes news and information. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
Stacker creates data-driven content purchased and republished by small and large newsrooms. Some of their customers include Nexstar, Lee Enterprises, Hearst, and
[MSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/msn-com/). Articles and headlines contain minimal bias focusing on data trends such as this [Highest-paying jobs with no formal education requirements](https://stacker.com/business-economy/highest-paying-jobs-no-formal-education-requirements). This article is appropriately sourced from the BLS.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on government from a data-driven perspective, such as this:
[Gun control legislation that passed and failed over the last 20 years](https://stacker.com/government/gun-control-legislation-passed-and-failed-over-last-20-years). In general, all content reviewed was fact-based and contained minimal bias.
