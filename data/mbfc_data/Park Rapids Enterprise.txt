History
The Park Rapids Enterprise is a newspaper published in Park Rapids, Minnesota. The website parkrapidsenterprise.com features local and national news, politics, sports, entertainment, business, classifieds, and health.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Park Rapids Enterprise is owned by
[Forum Communications Company](https://www.forumcomm.com/brands-overview/), which is based in North Dakota and has additional locations across South Dakota, Minnesota, and Wisconsin. Advertising and subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Park Rapids Enterprise reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and entertainment for the greater Park Rapids, MN region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[Court rules that sheriff’s blockade of Line 3 opponents’ camp was not legal](https://www.parkrapidsenterprise.com/news/local/court-rules-that-sheriffs-blockade-of-line-3-opponents-camp-was-not-legal). Further, the website publishes national and international news from credible sources such as the [LA Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/la-times-los-angeles-times/) and [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/). Finally, all information is sourced via first-person accounts or through credible media.
Editorially, The Park Rapids Enterprise reasonably represents both sides. However, a review of columnists demonstrates more favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with right-leaning editorial bias.
