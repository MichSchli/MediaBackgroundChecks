History
The Parry Sound North Star is a newspaper published in Parry Sound, Ontario, Canada. parrysound.com provides online local, national, and world news for the greater Parry Sound, Ontario region.

Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of the Parry Sound North Star is Metroland Media Group Ltd., a subsidiary of Nordstar Capital LP. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In 2015 and
[2021](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2021/09/19/the-stars-editorial-board-endorses-liberals-for-2021-federal-election.html), the [Toronto Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/) (Nordstar), which owns the Parry Sound North Star publisher, Metroland Media Group, [endorsed](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2015/10/09/for-justin-trudeau-and-the-liberal-party-editorial.html) the Liberal Party under Justin Trudeau.
In review, the Parry Sound North Star uses low-biased wording when reporting local news such as this:
[Latest update on COVID-19 cases in North Bay Parry Sound health unit area](https://www.parrysound.com/news-story/10264687-latest-update-on-covid-19-cases-in-north-bay-parry-sound-health-unit-area/). National news is derived through the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/), while world news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, a review of op-eds slightly favors the left. In general, they are fact-based and hold a left-leaning editorial bias.
