History
The Mexico News Daily curates news stories from Spanish in English. According to their
[about page](https://mexiconewsdaily.com/about/), they “offer clear and concise coverage of news and current affairs in Mexico in English. We do so by curating existing news sources, mostly Spanish-language media outlets that publish content online.” [Read our profile on Mexico’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mexico-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website is owned by
[Travis and Tamanna Bembenek](https://mexiconewsdaily.com/news/a-farewell-message-from-founder-tony-richards/). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Mexico News Daily curates news from a wide variety of sources with a left-leaning bias in story selection. Some articles use loaded language and
[do not](https://mexiconewsdaily.com/news/new-government-wont-police-migrants/) favor the previous right-leaning USA Government.
