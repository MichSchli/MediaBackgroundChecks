History
[Founded in 1864](https://www.mariettatimes.com/news/2017/10/a-letter-to-our-readers/), The Marietta Times is a daily newspaper serving Marietta and Washington County in Ohio. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Marietta Times is owned by
[Ogden Newspapers Inc.,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ogden_Newspapers_Inc.) which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the eastern half of the USA. Ogden has historically donated to Republican candidates. According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), since 1992, they have [donated 93% to Republicans](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000036358&cycle=2018). [Robert Nutting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Nutting) is the current CEO. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Marietta Times publishes news for the Marietta and Washington Country region of Ohio. Local news is covered through reporting with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Over 200 homes to gain access to better internet speeds](https://www.mariettatimes.com/news/local-news/2020/12/over-200-homes-to-gain-access-to-better-internet-speeds/). National and international is republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
A review of editorials demonstrates a reasonable balance without extremes such as this
[Corrupt officials should be exposed](https://www.mariettatimes.com/opinion/editorials/2020/12/corrupt-officials-should-be-exposed/). In general, they demonstrate low bias and produce fact-based news.
