History
The Paragould Daily Press is a daily newspaper published in Paragould, Arkansas. paragoulddailypress.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Paxton Media Group,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paxton_Media_Group) which owns several right-leaning newspapers across the USA, such as the [Elkhart Truth](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-elkhart-truth/). Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Paragould Daily Press reports national news by republishing articles from credible newsagents such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). They report local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias using neutral headlines such as [Greene County tax auction raises $449,000](https://www.paragoulddailypress.com/news/greene-county-tax-auction-raises-449-000/article_205f017a-0e76-5b29-9fcb-ee6b1387f925.html). All information is properly sourced.
Editorially, there is a reasonable balance between left and right-leaning opinions, with slightly more favoring the right such as this:
[IRS hiring spree is the biggest expansion of the police state in American History](https://www.paragoulddailypress.com/opinion/irs-hiring-spree-is-the-biggest-expansion-of-the-police-state-in-american-history/article_c3eb90f0-1266-533e-bd29-562ec0cc3cf5.html). In general, the paper is fact-based and holds a slight right-leaning editorial bias.
