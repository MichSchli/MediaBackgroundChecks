History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, C-StoreDive is an online news platform that focuses on the Convenience Store industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.cstoredive.com/about/), “C-Store Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the news and trends shaping the convenience store industry. The newsletter and website cover topics such as food service, fuels, operations, and more.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates C-StoreDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and CybersecurityDive. C-StoreDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
C-StoreDive publishes news and information related to the Convenience Store industry. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[7-Eleven could face up to $77M in penalties in FTC lawsuit](https://www.cstoredive.com/news/7-eleven-77m-penalties-ftc-lawsuit/701614/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like this
[In address to Congress, Biden touts drug pricing law and pushes insulin cost caps](https://www.biopharmadive.com/news/biden-sotu-pharma-drug-pricing-insulin/642273/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
