History
Founded in 1955, WPXI-TV is an NBC-affiliated television station licensed to Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, United States. The associated website WPXI.com covers news, sports, crime, and weather for Pittsburgh and the greater Allegheny country and southwest Pennsylvania area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Cox Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cox_Enterprises) owns WPXI. They are a company that owns numerous newspapers, television stations, and radio stations such as the [Atlanta Journal-Constitution](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/atlanta-journal-constitution/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WPXI.com covers news, sports, crime, and lifestyle for the greater southwestern Pennsylvania region. Local news is covered with minimally loaded language such as this
[PWSA pleads guilty to dumping sludge into Allegheny River, falsifying reports](https://www.wpxi.com/news/top-stories/pwsa-pleads-guilty-dumping-sludge-into-allegheny-river-falsifying-reports/DJY3DTFMXFDR3LK67CIE4ISQOM/). Many local news stories contain videos from the TV station. National news is primarily republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WPXI.com does not publish op-eds; however, when covering topics such as Coronavirus, they present scientifically sound and low biased information such as this
[FDA meeting to consider COVID-19 vaccines as local hospitals prepare for distribution](https://www.wpxi.com/news/top-stories/fda-meeting-consider-covid-19-vaccines-local-hospitals-prepare-distribution/4LOMXFKSWJCXJH7CK6CK2MYXMY/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
