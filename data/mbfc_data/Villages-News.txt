History
[Founded in 2013](https://whois.domaintools.com/villages-news.com), Villages-News provides online news coverage of government, crime, classifieds, and local events for The Villages, Florida, and surrounding communities. The Villages is one of the [largest retirement communities](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Villages,_Florida) in the United States. According to their about page, it is the only source that provides “Real News, Right Now” for the community. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
According to their
[FAQ page](https://www.villages-news.com/faq/), Villages-News.com is 100% independently locally owned and operated. However, they lack transparency as they do not disclose the owner. Advertising and subscription fees for a newsletter generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Villages-News.com provides news coverage of government, crime, classifieds, and local events for The Villages, Florida, and its surrounding communities in Lake Sumter, and Marion counties. Headlines reflect the story and are low biased such as this
[Villager free on bond in pending DUI case jailed after caught driving again](https://www.villages-news.com/2021/09/14/villager-free-on-bond-in-pending-dui-case-jailed-after-caught-driving-again/). Stories are properly sourced via hyperlinks to credible media outlets. The website rarely covers national news, focusing on local and Florida news.
Editorially, Villages-News publishes opinion pieces from columnists and guests such as Republican Congressman Daniel Webster
[Biden Administration incentivizes Americans to stay out of the workforce](https://www.villages-news.com/2021/09/09/biden-administration-incentivizes-americans-to-stay-out-of-the-workforce/). In comparison, they publish left-leaning viewpoints such as this [Crucial opportunity for climate action](https://www.villages-news.com/2021/08/30/crucial-opportunity-for-climate-action/); however, much more favor a conservative perspective. The website also frequently publishes letters to the editor, revealing a vocal conservative readership with strong support for former President Donald Trump [More evidence of Trump Derangement Syndrome](https://www.villages-news.com/2021/09/12/more-evidence-of-trump-derangement-syndrome/). Finally, a review of Covid-19 coverage reveals fact-based reporting. In general, they report news factually and with right-leaning editorial bias.
