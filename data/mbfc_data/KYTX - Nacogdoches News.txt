History
Founded in 1991, KYTX – Nacogdoches News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated TV station based in Nacogdoches, Texas. cbs19.com is the website for KYTX that covers news, sports, and weather.
On June 29, 2015, the Gannett Company split in two, specializing in print media and the other specializing in broadcast and digital media. The broadcasting company retained KYTX, which took the name
[TEGNA](https://www.tegna.com/). [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KYTX – Nacogdoches News is owned and operated by TEGNA Inc., which operates 66 television stations in 54 markets. They state TEGNA’s purpose is to serve the greater good of our communities. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KYTX – Nacogdoches News reports local news primarily through TV news videos and accompanying text. Local information is presented with minimally loaded wording:
[Tyler man sentenced to 33 years in prison for death of his children’s mother](https://www.cbs19.tv/article/news/local/tyler-man-sentenced-to-33-years-in-prison-for-death-his-childrens-mother/501-9f86fe5c-dd62-4c41-98f9-b0f56ce9d50d). National and International news is provided by the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other TEGNA TV stations.
Editorially, KYTX – Nacogdoches News does not produce op-eds and reports on politics with minimal bias. Finally, the website features fact checks from TEGNA’s fact check service
[VERIFY Fact check.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/verify-fact-check-bias/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
