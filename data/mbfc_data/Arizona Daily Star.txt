History
Founded in 1877, The Arizona Daily Star is the major morning daily newspaper that serves Tucson and the surrounding districts of southern Arizona in the United States.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett and Lee Enterprises jointly own the Arizona Daily Star. In August 2019,
[Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. [Lee Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lee_Enterprises), which according to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), has donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) since 1990. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Arizona Daily Star covers news, sports, and entertainment for the southern Arizona region. Reporters write local news with minimal bias such as this:
[Arizona now has 5 confirmed ](https://tucson.com/news/local/arizona-now-has-confirmed-cases-of-coronavirus/article_5061c3da-6090-11ea-976b-579e9fe19d7d.html#tracking-source=home-top-story-1)coronavirus cases. This story is properly sourced to azhealth.gov. The Associated Press provides national and international news.
Editorially, the Arizona Daily Star has
[endorsed Democratic Presidential Candidates](https://noahveltman.com/endorsements/) since 1980; however, in 2016, they chose not to endorse a Presidential Candidate. The Daily Star has also endorsed Republicans such as John McCain for Senate. A review of editorials and op-eds reveals a left-leaning bias with more articles favoring the left such as this: [Local Opinion: Why Bernie can win: A brief political memoir](https://tucson.com/opinion/local/local-opinion-why-bernie-can-win-a-brief-political-memoir/article_49b26124-d3db-58a2-8a5f-94929e7cf5e8.html). Generally, the news is reported factually with a slightly left-leaning editorial bias.
