History
Founded in 2001 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, KCWY – Casper News serves Casper, Wyoming, and the surrounding area. Wyomingnewsnow.tv is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KCWY – Casper News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KCWY – Casper News provides news coverage for Casper, Wyoming, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[The Wyoming 67th Legislative Session starts Tuesday](https://www.wyomingnewsnow.tv/2023/01/12/wyoming-67th-legislative-session-starts-tuesday/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KCWY – Casper News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
