History
The Cleburne Times Review is a newspaper published in the city of Cleburne, Texas. cleburnetimesreview.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Cleburne Times Review. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Cleburne Times Review covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Cleburne, Texas region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Lady Zebras take 1st place at Rio Vista Holiday Classic](https://www.cleburnetimesreview.com/sports/lady-zebras-take-1st-place-at-rio-vista-holiday-classic/article_81eb0208-86f9-11ed-b33c-b7b342594338.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
