History
Launched in
[2019](https://spn.org/blog/heartland-institute-launches-stopping-socialism-project/) by the Heartland Institute and The Henry Dearborn Institute for Liberty, Stopping Socialism is a website focused on the moral dangers associated with socialism and communism. The primary focus is the fight against the [Great Reset](https://stoppingsocialism.com/2020/11/glenn-beck-justin-haskins-2020-election-socialism/), which is a theory promoted by Glen Beck. He regularly promotes this through a podcast featuring Justin Haskins. Heartland Institute Executive Editor Justin Haskins leads the website. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[The Heartland Institute](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/heartland-institute/), funded by the fossil fuel industry, manages and funds the [Stopping Socialism Project](https://www.heartland.org/news-opinion/news/taking-the-fight-to-socialists-and-the-climate-left) website. [Justin Haskins](https://www.heartland.org/about-us/who-we-are/justin-haskins) is the editor of the site. Revenue is generated through advertising and a store that sells merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Stopping Socialism typically focuses on conspiracies promoted by right-leaning commentators such as Glenn Beck. For example, they promote the Great Reset Theory that is a supposed plan by global elitists to redistribute wealth on an international level
[“Glenn Beck Reveals How the Great Reset Would Destroy Individual Liberty and Transform the World](https://stoppingsocialism.com/2020/12/glenn-beck-the-great-reset-survey-rasmussen-heartland-institute/).” Many sources consider this a conspiracy theory, including the [Anti-Defamation League](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/anti-defamation-league/). They also publish articles with strong emotional wording such as [“Biden’s ‘Climate Czar’ Makes Shocking Comments About the Anti-Capitalism Great Reset: ‘It Will Happen’”](https://stoppingsocialism.com/2020/11/john-kerry-joe-biden-great-reset-it-will-happen/) in which they source National Review.
Editorially, Stopping Socialism publishes Creators Syndicate columnists articles such as this by syndicated right-leaning columnist Patrick Buchanan
[“A dark winter for the Grand Old Party”](https://thebrunswicknews.com/opinion/editorial_columns/a-dark-winter-for-the-grand-old-party/article_e4d96ccc-4230-5650-8bcd-3fc0c9c3a34e.html) and [Veronique de Rugy](https://www.cato.org/people/veronique-derugy) who is an adjunct scholar at the [Cato Institute](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cato-institute/) “ [Romney’s Family Security Act Redistributes Wealth, Creates Distortions, Grows Government](https://stoppingsocialism.com/2021/02/romneys-family-security-act-redistributes-wealth/).”
From a scientific standpoint, they
[promote](https://stoppingsocialism.com/2020/08/bjorn-lomborg-false-alarm-climate-change/) [ climate change denialism](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/), and during the 2020 Presidential election, they promoted theories about [voter fraud](https://stoppingsocialism.com/2020/10/voter-fraud-election-2020-justin-haskins/).
