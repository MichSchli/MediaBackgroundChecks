History
The Moultrie Observer is a daily newspaper published in the city of Moultrie, Georgia, and covers Colquitt County. moultrieobserver.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Moultrie Observer. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Moultrie Observer covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Moultrie, Georgia, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Expo Field Day postponed due to weather](https://www.moultrieobserver.com/news/local_news/expo-field-day-postponed-due-to-weather/article_eccaccfa-0442-11ed-a388-270246477aad.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left, such as this
[COLUMN: Colquitt County Branch of NAACP celebrates 49 years](https://www.moultrieobserver.com/opinion/columns/column-colquitt-county-branch-of-naacp-celebrates-49-years/article_c495b384-c01e-11ec-9897-8717430a6227.html). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
