History
According to their
[about page](https://www.insidescience.org/about), “Inside Science is an editorially independent nonprofit science news service run out of the [American Institute of Physics](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-american-institute-of-physics/) (Rated Pro-Science by MBFC) and supported by a coalition of underwriters. We produce breaking news stories, features, essays, op-eds, documentaries, animations, and news videos. These appear on insidescience.org as well as on partner sites across the web and in print.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Inside Science is funded by a group of underwriters that include the
[American Institute of Physics](http://www.aip.org/), [Acoustical Society of America](http://acousticalsociety.org/), and the [American Physical Society](http://www.aps.org/), among other scientific organizations.
