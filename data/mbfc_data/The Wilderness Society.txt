History
Founded in 1964,
[The Wilderness Society](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Wilderness_Society_(United_States)) is an American non-profit land conservation organization dedicated to protecting natural areas and federal public lands in the United States. They advocate for the designation of federal wilderness areas and other protective designations, such as national monuments. They support balanced uses of public lands and advocate for federal politicians to enact various land conservation and balanced land-use proposals. Funded by / Ownership
The Wilderness Society is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that is funded through donations.
Analysis / Bias
The Wilderness Society reports news on its blog with a left-center bias in reporting. While they always source from credible information and are factual in reporting they do take political positions such as support for climate change action and responsible energy usage on public lands, that aligns with the political left. Due to their political affiliation, we rate this source as left-center biased.
