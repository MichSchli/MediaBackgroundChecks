History
The Glenwood Springs Post Independent is a daily newspaper serving Glenwood Springs, Colorado. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information.

Funded by / Ownership
The Glenwood Springs Post Independent is published by Swift Communications, which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the western USA. Ogden Newspapers owns Swift. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
