History
Founded in 1953, KTVQ – Billings News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Baltimore, Maryland, United States. Ktvq.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTVQ – Billings News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KTVQ – Billings News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Billings area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Montana abortion rights governed by state law, not SCOTUS](https://www.ktvq.com/news/montana-abortion-rights-governed-by-state-law-not-scotus). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Could Congress or President Biden act if the Supreme Court overturns Roe v. Wade?](https://www.ktvq.com/news/national-politics/could-congress-or-president-biden-act-if-the-supreme-court-overturns-roe-v-wade) In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
