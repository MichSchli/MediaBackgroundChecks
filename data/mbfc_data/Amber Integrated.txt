History
Amber Integrated (ASR) is based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, and specializes in market research. According to its
[about page](https://www.amberintegrated.com/about-ai) Amber Integrated is an award-winning public affairs firm with expertise in government relations, public relations, marketing, political consulting, and survey research. We are a one-stop shop that delivers powerful results for a diverse array of clients, including Fortune 500 companies, non-profits, and elected officials. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Amber Integrated is a private company.
[The sale](https://www.amberintegrated.com/integrated-public-affairs) of surveys, market research, and political consultancy generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Amber Integrated conducts market research and polling via various mediums. The website does not generally report on politics.
[FiveThirtyEight](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fivethirtyeight/), an expert on measuring and rating pollster performance, has [evaluated 8 polls by ASR](https://projects.fivethirtyeight.com/pollster-ratings/amber-integrated/), indicating they are accurate 88% of the time or High Factual. They also conclude that their polling favors the left with a score of +2.1, which equates to a Left-Center bias in polling. In general, ASR is an accurate pollster that moderately favors the left (Democratic Candidates).
