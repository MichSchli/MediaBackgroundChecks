History
Vanderhoof Omineca Express is a newspaper published in Vanderhoof, British Columbia, Canada. The website ominecaexpress.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Vanderhoof, BC area.

Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Vanderhoof Omineca Express. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
