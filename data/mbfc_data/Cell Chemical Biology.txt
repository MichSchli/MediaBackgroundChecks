History
Cell Chemical Biology Journal is a peer-reviewed open-access journal published by Cell Press that covers chemical biology. According to its
[about page](https://www.cell.com/cell-chemical-biology/aims), “Cell Chemical Biology is a Cell Press journal that aims to publish the most important advances in chemical biology research of exceptional interest for our diverse community, from basic scientists to clinicians.”
Cell Press, based in Cambridge, Massachusetts, publishes over
[50 scientific journals](https://www.cell.com/). Cell Press Journals are 50% open-access as of 2022. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Cell Chemical Biology Journal is published by Cell Press which is a subsidiary of
[Elsevier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elsevier), a Dutch information and analytics company and one of the world’s foremost providers of scientific, technical, and medical information. It was established in 1880 as a publishing company. Elsevier’s parent company is RELX plc, a British multinational information and analytics company headquartered in London, England. The journal generates revenue through subscription fees and fees for open-access publishing.
Analysis / Bias
Cell Chemical Biology Journal is a hybrid (some open-access) journal that publishes information and research related to chemical biology. The journal primarily publishes research such as this
[Targeting VPS41 induces methuosis and inhibits autophagy in cancer cells](https://www.cell.com/cell-chemical-biology/fulltext/S2451-9456(23)00023-5).
Cell Press is an open-access/hybrid publisher of scientific journals. There are two types of Open Access Journals, legitimate and predatory, both of which charge the author for publication.
[Predatory journals](https://predatoryjournals.com/about/) often lack peer review and are generally considered not credible. The Cell Chemical Biology Journal is considered a [legitimate, open-access publisher](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5017830/) that is frequently indexed in [PubMed](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pubmed/). However, Cell Press has been criticized for [retracting some studies.](https://retractionwatch.com/category/by-journal/cell/)
Although Cell Press has retracted some studies in other publications, Cell Chemical Biology Journal publishes credible scientific studies that are factual and appropriately peer-reviewed.
