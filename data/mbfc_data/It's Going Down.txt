History
It’s Going Down or IGD News is an anti-fascist and anarchist news site. According to their Facebook page
[itsgoingdown.org](https://www.facebook.com/pg/IGDnews/about/?ref=page_internal) produces “Reports and analysis of revolt and social movements from a revolutionary anarchist perspective across so-called North America.” The Guardian [describes](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/feb/02/facism-alt-right-activists-trump-milo-yiannopoulos) IGD News as “a website that promotes anti-fascist organizing.”
However, IGD News states on their
[about page](https://itsgoingdown.org/about/) “IGD is an anarchist news website and platform. We do not organize demonstrations or carry out actions; therefore, we cannot represent something we did not organize.” It’s Going Down state their [mission](https://itsgoingdown.org/about/) as “Our mission is to provide a resilient platform to publicize and promote revolutionary theory and action.” They also state on their about page that they publish original content alongside anonymous submissions as well as repost articles from websites that share their perspectives. It’s Going down covers the topics under four content categories LIBRARY, IGDCAST, COLUMNS, and SHOP. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
It’s Going down does not specifically state who the owner is, but they are funded through donations and an online store that sells merchandise. In their
[shop](https://itsgoingdown.org/shop/), they sell items like IGD shirts and items with messages like “No nations, No Borders, Fuck Law & Order, Hospitalize Your Local Fascist, Love Antifa Hate Cops.”
Analysis / Bias
In review, The content of their op-ed articles use loaded words and headlines such as “
[Trump’s Katrina”: Disaster Statism Vs Autonomous Relief](https://itsgoingdown.org/trumps-katrina-disaster-statism-vs-autonomous-relief/)” and they quote left-leaning credible sources such as [World Socialist Web Site](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/world-socialist-web-site/), [Mother Jones](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mother-jones/), [Democracy Now](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/democracy-now/) and [New Scientist](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-scientist/). However, It’s Going Down also publishes articles such as “ [Dispatches from the Borderlands: Liberation not Deportation”](https://itsgoingdown.org/dispatches-borderlands-escaping-together/) with factually mixed sources such as the [Daily Mail](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-mail/), [Truth Out](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/truth-out/), [Vice News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/vice-news/), and unverified sources such as godsandradicals.org.
