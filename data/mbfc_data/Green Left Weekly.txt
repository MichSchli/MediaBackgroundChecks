History
Green Left Weekly is an
[Australian socialist ](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green_Left_Weekly)newspaper written by progressive activists to “present the views excluded by the big business media.” Subjects frequently covered by the Green Left Weekly include workers’ rights, refugees, women’s rights, global warming, environmental destruction, Australian Aboriginal land rights, and interventionist foreign policy.
Funded by / Ownership
Green Left Media does not disclose ownership on its website. The website is funded through a combination of subscriptions and donations.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Green Left Weekly covers local, national, and international news and provides left-leaning analysis and debate to strengthen the
[anti-capitalist](https://www.greenleft.org.au/content/make-capitalism-history) and socialist movements. There is moderate use of loaded words in headlines and articles that exclusively favor the left. Green Left Weekly typically sources their information correctly from credible sources; however other times, they do not [source at all](https://www.greenleft.org.au/content/campaign-launched-against-gmo-food).
Editorially, Green Left Weekly favors socialist positions such as worker’s rights, LGBTQ rights, and environmental protection. Most content is fact-based but entirely one-sided and poorly sourced.
