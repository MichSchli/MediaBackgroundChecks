History
The Cloverdale Reporter is a newspaper published in Cloverdale, British Columbia, Canada. The website cloverdalereporter.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Cloverdale area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Cloverdale Reporter. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Cloverdale Reporter covers news for Cloverdale and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Air quality at ‘very high risk’ in Eastern Fraser Valley due to multiple fires burning](https://www.cloverdalereporter.com/news/air-quality-at-very-high-risk-in-eastern-fraser-valley-due-to-multiple-fires-burning/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Cloverdale Reporter does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left, such as this
[PHOTOS: South Surrey students participate in climate-change protest](https://www.cloverdalereporter.com/photo-galleries/photos-south-surrey-students-participate-in-climate-change-protest/). Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
