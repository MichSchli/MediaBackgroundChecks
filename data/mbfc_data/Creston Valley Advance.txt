History
Creston Valley Advance is a newspaper published in Creston. British Columbia, Canada. The website crestonvalleyadvance.ca covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Creston Valley, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Creston Valley Advance. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Creston Valley Advance covers news for Creston and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Rain forces Whitewater Ski Resort to push back opening day](https://www.crestonvalleyadvance.ca/local-news/rain-forces-whitewater-ski-resort-to-push-back-opening-day-7119503). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Creston Valley Advance does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
