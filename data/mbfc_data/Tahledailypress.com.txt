History
The Tahlequah Daily Press is a newspaper published in Tahlequah, Oklahoma. tahlequaldailypress.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Tahlequah Daily Press. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Tahlequah Daily Press covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Tahlequah, Oklahoma region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Charges filed against convicted felon after firearms found in his possession](https://www.tahlequahdailypress.com/news/charges-filed-against-convicted-felon-after-firearms-found-in-his-possession/article_3e876bfa-267c-11ee-ae9e-f72fae514c2f.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
