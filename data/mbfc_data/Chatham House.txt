History
Founded by
[Lionel Curtis](https://www.chathamhouse.org/about-us/our-history) in 1920, [Chatham House](https://www.chathamhouse.org/about-us) (Royal Institute of International Affairs) is a British think tank specializing in foreign policy, international economics, and regional studies. Chatham House is headquartered in London-UK. The Chatham House pioneered [The Chatham House Rule](https://uk.practicallaw.thomsonreuters.com/7-201-2615?transitionType=Default&contextData=(sc.Default)&firstPage=true) for discussing controversial topics, particularly in politics and public affairs, which is called Chatham House Rule. Sir Nigel Sheinwald is Chair. [Read our profile on the United Kingdom government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
Chatham House publishes articles with neutral headlines, such as:
[“How Russia’s invasion changed German foreign policy.”](https://www.chathamhouse.org/2022/11/how-russias-invasion-changed-german-foreign-policy) Regarding sourcing, they utilize credible sources such as the website of the German Federal Government Bundesregierungn, and journals like “International Affairs 98: 6, 2022.” In the article [“How will Sweden’s right turn affect its foreign policy priorities?”](https://www.chathamhouse.org/2022/11/how-will-swedens-right-turn-affect-its-foreign-policy-priorities) they utilize [The Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/), [BBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bbc/), [AP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [Le Monde](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/le-monde-bias/), [Financial Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/financial-times/) sources, and [The Swedish Psychological Defence Agency.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/emmawoollacott/2022/01/05/sweden-launches-psychological-defense-agency-to-counter-disinformation/?sh=6d28d0d34874)
In analysis, the articles are balanced and well-sourced. When reporting on climate change, they publish articles such as “
[Climate change risk assessment 2021. The risks are compounding, and without immediate action, the impacts will be devastating.”](https://www.chathamhouse.org/2021/09/climate-change-risk-assessment-2021) Chatham House takes pro-science positions regarding climate change stating the consensus. In general, Chatham House produces low-biased research that is well-sourced and fact-based.
