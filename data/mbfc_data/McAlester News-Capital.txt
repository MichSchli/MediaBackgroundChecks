History
The McAlester News-Capital is a newspaper published in McAlester, Oklahoma. mcalesternews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The McAlester News-Capital. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The McAlester News-Capital covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the McAlester, Oklahoma region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Police: No weapons in MHS fight](https://www.mcalesternews.com/community/police-no-weapons-in-mhs-fight/article_0f6ffa9c-e04c-11ed-948a-2755819811c0.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
