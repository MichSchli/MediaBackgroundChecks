History
Launched in 1998, by
[Abdul Rahman](https://www.thejakartapost.com/news/2012/02/07/detikcom-undergoes-structural-switch.html) and [Budiono Darsono,](https://www.cnnindonesia.com/teknologi/20160929134809-213-162095/budiono-darsono-pensiun-sebagai-ceo-detikcom) detik.com or detikcom is a news website based in Jakarta, Indonesia. Detik.com is focused on breaking news, politics, business, economy, and sports. The editor-in-chief is [ARIFIN ASYDHAD](https://www.detik.com/dnewgeneration/).
Funded by / Ownership
Detik.com is owned by Trans Corp, which is part of
[CT Corporation](http://ctcorpora.com/business-group.php) (formerly known as the Para Group) that is owned by [Chairul Tanjung ](https://www.wsj.com/market-data/quotes/ID/XIDX/MEGA/company-people/executive-profile/228816)who is a businessman and former Coordinating Minister for Economics in the Indonesian Cabinet. Trans Media also owns TV stations such as Trans TV, Trans 7, CNN Indonesia, and TransVision. Revenue is based on subscriptions and advertisements.
Analysis / Bias
[Read our profile on Indonesia’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/indonesia-media-profile/)
In review, detik.com publishes articles with emotionally loaded headlines such as
[“Inaugurating the School, Anita Tanjung Emphasizes the Importance of Quality Education”](https://news.detik.com/berita/d-4814018/resmikan-sekolah-anita-tanjung-tekankan-pentingnya-pendidikan-berkualitas?_ga=2.222884763.1033621608.1595419399-669904822.1595419399) and [“Trans TV Provides Aid of 300 Lebanese Basic Food Packages to Mampang Prapatan Residents.”](https://news.detik.com/berita/d-5022055/trans-tv-beri-bantuan-300-paket-sembako-lebaran-ke-warga-mampang-prapatan?_ga=2.127441805.1033621608.1595419399-669904822.1595419399) (Anita Tanjung is the wife of the owner) Detik.com reports favorably on the Government such as [“Give Capital Assistance to Traders, Jokowi: Don’t Use Your Mobile Phone!”](https://finance.detik.com/berita-ekonomi-bisnis/d-5102359/beri-bantuan-modal-ke-pedagang-jokowi-jangan-dipakai-beli-hp?_ga=2.122395531.1033621608.1595419399-669904822.1595419399) and [“RI Peringkat 3 di Dunia, Jokowi Targetkan 2030 Bebas Tuberculosis.](https://news.detik.com/berita/d-5101782/ri-peringkat-3-di-dunia-jokowi-targetkan-2030-bebas-tuberkulosis/1)” If you [search the President’s name](https://www.detik.com/tag/joko-widodo) on the website they only report what he did or says and does not offer criticism.
Furthermore, they report favorably on coalition partners such as
[Prabowo Subianto](https://nasional.kompas.com/read/2020/07/21/21424671/media-austria-sebut-prabowo-incar-15-pesawat-eurofighter-typhoon) who is the current Minister of Defence and is also the head of the conservative Great Indonesia Movement Party (Gerindra) such as here [“4 Reasons Jokowi Choose Prabowo to Become Commander of the Food Barn Project.”](https://finance.detik.com/berita-ekonomi-bisnis/d-5093492/4-alasan-jokowi-pilih-prabowo-jadi-komandan-proyek-lumbung-pangan?_ga=2.223985563.1033621608.1595419399-669904822.1595419399)
When it comes to sourcing, they primarily use quotes from other sources, however, they do not link to the source. Lastly, detik.com covers world news pertaining to the USA by quoting
[CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Xinhua](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/xinhua-news-agency/) News Agency with a neutral tone such as this [“Trump: Corona’s Pandemic in the US Will Get Worse Before Improving.”](https://news.detik.com/internasional/d-5103602/trump-pandemi-corona-di-as-akan-memburuk-sebelum-membaik/1)
In general, detik.com reports news factually and with minimal bias, however, they do not provide hyperlinked sourcing for readers to verify quotes and information and they do not have an about page with owner information.
