History
Founded in 2010 and a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station, KEVN – Rapid City News serves Rapid City, South Dakota, and the surrounding area. blackhillsfox.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KEVN – Rapid City News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KEVN – Rapid City News provides news coverage for Rapid City, South Dakota, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Rapid City man arrested following death of horses](https://www.blackhillsfox.com/2023/01/06/rapid-city-man-arrested-following-death-horses/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KEVN – Rapid City News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
