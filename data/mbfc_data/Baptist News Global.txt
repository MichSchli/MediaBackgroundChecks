History
[Baptist News Global](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baptist_News_Global) is an independent Baptist news agency. It was founded in 2014 as a merger of Associated Baptist Press (ABP), which was founded in 1990, and the Religious Herald, which was founded in 1828. According to their [about page](https://baptistnews.com/more/about/#.XtwglmhKiUk), “Baptist News Global is a reader-supported, independent news organization providing original and curated news, opinion and analysis about matters of faith. BNG covers the people, events, and ideas shaping American culture and Baptist life. We do so from a Baptist perspective in heritage and ecumenical in spirit.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Baptist News Global is a nonprofit organization that is funded through reader-supported
[donations](https://baptistnews.com/donate/#.Xtwg2GhKiUk).
Analysis / Bias
In review, Baptist News Global publishes original news reporting and curated news from other sources related to religion and the Baptist Church. Original reporting is often well-sourced and uses moderate loaded language such as this:
[COVID-19 pandemic creating long-lasting food shortages for poor](https://baptistnews.com/article/covid-19-pandemic-creating-long-lasting-food-shortages-for-poor/#.XtwQWmhKiUk). Curated news comes from a wide variety of credible sources such as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [The Atlantic](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-atlantic/), [Christianity Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/christianity-today/), and The [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/).
Baptist News Global also has a section for opinion pieces that slightly favor the left, such as this:
[George Floyd and the silence of white evangelical America](https://baptistnews.com/article/george-floyd-and-white-evangelical-america/). They also frequently report negatively on the Trump administration, such as this: [The First Amendment and religious freedom compel me to refute Trump’s position on churches reopening](https://baptistnews.com/article/the-first-amendment-and-religious-freedom-compel-me-to-refute-trumps-position-on-churches-reopening/#.Xtwe_2hKiUk). They generally report news factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
