History
KBRW – NPR – Barrow is an AM radio station (680) based in Barrow, Alaska, that broadcasts National Public Radio (NPR).
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KBRW – NPR – Barrow is owned and broadcast by Silakkuagvik Communications, Inc. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
