History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, FashionDive is an online news platform that focuses on the fashion industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.fashiondive.com/about/), “Fashion Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping fashion & apparel.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates FashionDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and FacilitiesDive. FashionDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
FashionDive publishes news and information related to facilities management. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[Prada and L’Oreal sign license agreement for Miu Miu beauty line](https://www.fashiondive.com/news/loreal-miumiu-beauty-license-prada/707185/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like
[Lawmakers should ensure Shein is complying with US laws, the government report says](https://www.fashiondive.com/news/shein-government-report-temu-law-compliance/648052/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
