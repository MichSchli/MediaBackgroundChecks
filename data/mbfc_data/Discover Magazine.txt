History
Discover is an American general audience science magazine launched in October 1980 by
[Time Inc](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/time/). They focus more on light science that is easily understood and readable by a lay audience. The current editor is [Becky Lang](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Becky_Lang&action=edit&redlink=1). [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Discover Magazine is owned by
[Kalmbach Publishing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kalmbach_Publishing), which publishes special interest magazines such as [Astronomy](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/astronomy-magazine/). Revenue is derived through subscription fees, advertising, and a shop that sells merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Discover publishes news and information on
[THE SCIENCES,](https://www.discovermagazine.com/the-sciences) [MIND,](https://www.discovermagazine.com/mind) [TECHNOLOGY,](https://www.discovermagazine.com/technology) [HEALTH,](https://www.discovermagazine.com/health) [ENVIRONMENT](https://www.discovermagazine.com/environment), and [PLANET EARTH](https://www.discovermagazine.com/planet-earth). Articles and headlines are low biased such as this [Keeping Coronavirus Vaccines at Subzero Temperatures During Distribution Will Be Hard, But Likely Key to Ending Pandemic.](https://www.discovermagazine.com/health/keeping-coronavirus-vaccines-at-subzero-temperatures-during-distribution) This story is correctly sourced to credible media outlets such as [The Conversation](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-conversation/) and science-based outlets such as the [CDC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/centers-disease-control-prevention-cdc/).
Regarding
[climate change](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/), they adhere to the consensus of science, such as [The Arctic Hasn’t Been This Warm for 3 Million Years — and That Foreshadows Big Changes for the Rest of the Planet](https://www.discovermagazine.com/environment/the-arctic-hasnt-been-this-warm-for-3-million-years-and-that-foreshadows-big). A thorough review indicates they are pro-science through the promotion of consensus science.
