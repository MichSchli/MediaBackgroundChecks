History
The Calhoun Times is a newspaper based in Calhoun, Georgia, USA. The paper mainly focuses on local news and politics, sports, and business related to the Calhoun area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Calhoun Times is owned by
[Times-Journal Inc](https://www.editorandpublisher.com/stories/times-journal-inc-acquires-six-metro-atlanta-newspapers,240906). through the Brumby family. They publish around 20 papers in the Georgia and southeast markets. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Calhoun Times covers local news for the Calhoun area through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Couple Charged With Embezzling $200K From Calhoun Church](https://www.northwestgeorgianews.com/calhoun_times/news/local/couple-charged-with-embezzling-200k-from-calhoun-church/article_3ec17882-c5c2-11ee-b28e-7767dbea3153.html). The paper does not cover national and international news focusing on local and regional news related to Georgia.
Editorially, story selection, and op-eds are reasonably balanced and reflect both sides. Generally, the Calhoun Times is fact-based and presents a minimal bias in reporting.
