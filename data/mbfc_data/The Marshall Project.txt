History
Founded in 2014, The Marshall Project is a nonprofit, nonpartisan online journalism organization focusing on issues related to criminal justice in the United States, led by former hedge fund manager Neil Barsky and former
[New York Times](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/) executive editor Bill Keller. The current editor is [Susan Chira](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Susan_Chira). [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
