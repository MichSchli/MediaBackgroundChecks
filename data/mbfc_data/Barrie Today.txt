History
Barrie Today is a 24/7 news source from Barrie, Ontario. According to their
[about page](https://www.barrietoday.com/about-us), “BarrieToday is an exclusively online news and information site that provides breaking news to you, our reader, as fast as you want it, wherever you are..” [Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Village Media owns Barrie Today, and revenue is generated through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
Barrie Today reports the news for Barrie, Ontario, and the surrounding region with low bias, such as this
[Teen charged following double stabbing in Wasaga Beach](https://www.barrietoday.com/police-beat/teen-charged-following-double-stabbing-in-wasaga-beach-3906889). National and world news is derived from the [Canadian Press,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/) similar to the [AP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/) for Canada.
Barrie Today does not publish op-ed’s; however, when reporting on politics, more stories favor the left, such as this
[‘Toxicity’ and ‘obstructionism’: Trudeau tells Canadians Parliament is dysfunctional](https://www.barrietoday.com/national-news/toxicity-and-obstructionism-trudeau-tells-canadians-parliament-is-dysfunctional-3896538). In general, the news is reported factual and with a very slight left-leaning story selection bias.
