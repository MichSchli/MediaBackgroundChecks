History
Kimberley Bulletin is a newspaper published in Kimberley, British Columbia, Canada. The website kimberleybulletin.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Kimberley, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Kimberley Bulletin. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Kimberley Bulletin covers news for Kimberley and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[16-year old faces charges related to theft of vehicle from Wasa area](https://www.kimberleybulletin.com/news/16-year-old-faces-charges-related-to-theft-of-vehicle-from-wasa-area-7290860). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Kimberley Bulletin does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
