History
KTBS is a television station affiliated with the
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/) network and licensed to Shreveport, Louisiana. The station was founded in 1955. The website KTBS.com serves as the online platform for the station and provides news, weather, sports, and other local content. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTBS is owned by the Wray family and funded through advertising revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KTBS.com is a local news source that provides coverage of news, weather, sports, and other content related to the Shreveport area of Louisiana. The reporting is generally factual and presented straightforwardly, with minimal use of loaded language, such as this
[Driver accused of killing three people on I30 arrested](https://www.ktbs.com/news/driver-accused-of-killing-three-people-on-i30-arrested/article_c9fe7362-d0fb-11ed-beca-639221e90512.html). The website provides breaking news updates and features videos and other multimedia content.
Editorially, KTBS.com does not produce op-eds. The website focuses primarily on local news and does not frequently cover national politics. However, political news story selection slightly favors the right. In general, they are fact-based and on the right side of least biased.
