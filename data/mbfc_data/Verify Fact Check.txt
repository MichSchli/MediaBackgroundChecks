History
[Founded in 2021](https://digiday.com/media/tegna-verify-brand-fact-checking-local-vertical/), VERIFY Fact Check is a fact-checking website from the local newsrooms of Tegna TV stations. They currently work with 49 TEGNA newsrooms across the USA.
According to their
[about page](https://www.verifythis.com/about-us), “VERIFY is dedicated to helping the public distinguish between true and false information. With help from questions submitted by the audience, the VERIFY team tracks the spread of stories or claims that need clarification or correction. We provide trustworthy, transparent information to prove or disprove them. VERIFY’s sources are always provided, allowing the audience to see exactly how the team determines the veracity of any claims.”
On June 29, 2015, the Gannett Company split in two, specializing in print media and the other specializing in broadcast and digital media. The broadcasting company took the name
[TEGNA](https://www.tegna.com/). [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
VERIFY Fact Check is owned and operated by TEGNA Inc., which operates 66 television stations in 54 markets. They state TEGNA’s purpose is to serve the greater good of our communities. Advertising on TEGNA’s station websites generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
VERIFY Fact Check provides fact checks to TEGNA’s TV news websites. Verifythis.com is a standalone website featuring all the fact checks from 49 different newsrooms. TEGNA’s TV station websites each have a VERIFY category that publishes their fact-checks. Headlines are low biased and clearly state the nature of the fact check such as this
[No, the video of Russian President Vladimir Putin announcing peace isn’t real](https://www.verifythis.com/article/news/verify/world-verify/ukraine-verify/video-of-putin-announcing-peace-not-real/536-bc4b140f-214c-4a54-ad7e-e39341555fd0). All fact checks rely on evidence through credible sourcing. What makes this website unique is many of their fact-checks are in video format, such as this [Fact-checking if the video of Ukrainian President Zelenskyy urging surrender is real](https://www.verifythis.com/watch).
A review of fact-check selection bias indicates they are willing to fact-check both sides. For example, they fact-check President Biden,
[Fact-checking President Biden’s first State of the Union address](https://www.verifythis.com/article/news/verify/joe-biden/fact-check-president-joe-biden-state-of-union-2022/536-22a16a29-d50a-486a-b99f-5e17c8c7d212), and former President Trump [Yes; former President Trump made a deal with the Taliban to pull US troops out of Afghanistan by May 2021](https://www.verifythis.com/article/news/verify/afghanistan/afghanistan-troops-withdraw-deal-taliban-trump-biden/536-a68f951a-6bfe-4408-9d3b-96e85e425e45). VERIFY is a credible fact-checker that demonstrates minimal bias in what or who they fact check.
