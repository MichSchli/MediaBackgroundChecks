History
[Founded in 2003](https://whois.domaintools.com/naturalcures.com), NaturalCures.com is a website that publishes news, alternative medicine, and natural cures for a variety of ailments. The website lacks transparency as they do not provide an about page, names of editors, or disclosure of ownership. Funded by / Ownership
The website does not openly disclose ownership; however, on the contact page, they indicate they are owned by
[Snowflake Media Ltd](https://snowflakemedia.co.uk/), which is a web design and marketing company. Revenue is derived through a [lifetime membership](https://naturalcures.com/why-join/) fee for $99 and advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, NaturalCures.com provides a searchable database of natural cures for many diseases and conditions such a
[High Blood Pressure](https://naturalcures.com/cure/high-blood-pressure/#1498219893995-6af5827d-93c87bdb-688a89ba-a818). This will direct you to a page that highlights the causes and symptoms of the ailment, however, when you click on Natural Remedies for the condition it asks for a membership. The website also provides the latest news which focuses on alternative health such as this [COVID-19 What Is The Alternative Experts View?](https://naturalcures.com/covid-19-what-is-the-alternative-experts-view/) This story gives the views of several quack pseudoscientists such as [Mercola](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mercola/), [Dr. Oz](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/doctoroz-com/), and Mike Adams from [Natural News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/natural-news/). Finally, NaturalCures.com features original articles that do not offer hyperlinked sourcing such as this [Resisting Media Hype](https://naturalcures.com/article/resisting-media-hype/).
NaturalCures.com frequently promotes pseudoscience such as the
[promotion](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/) of [Anti-Vaccination books](https://naturalcures.com/a-shot-in-the-dark-danger-and-vaccines/), [natural prevention](https://naturalcures.com/kombucha-1/), and [cures for cancer](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-alternative-cancer-treatments/) as well as misinformation regarding [Coronavirus](https://naturalcures.com/covid-19-what-is-the-alternative-experts-view/).
