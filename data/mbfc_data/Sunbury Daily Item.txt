History
The Sunbury Daily Item is a newspaper published in Sunbury, Pennsylvania. dailyitem.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Sunbury Daily Item. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Sunbury Daily Item covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Sunbury, Pennsylvania region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Lewisburg Area increases wages for 29 school employees](https://www.dailyitem.com/news/lewisburg-area-increases-wages-for-29-school-employees/article_1a99c6ee-37e2-11ee-8b16-4fd7b63bd46f.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
