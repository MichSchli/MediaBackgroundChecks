History
Founded in 2011, The Atlanta Banana is a satire website. According to their
[about page](https://atlbanana.com/about/) “If we, by parodying hypocrisy or satirizing media mega-hype, can introduce the idea in even a single person’s brain that they should question everything they read, we have succeeded.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Jim Hodgson is the owner of The Atlanta Banana. The website does not have an obvious source of income.
Analysis / Bias
Reviews for satire websites focus on whether they properly disclose their intentions. The Atlanta Banana makes it very clear they are a satire website.
