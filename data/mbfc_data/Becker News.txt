History
[Founded in 2020](https://whois.domaintools.com/beckernews.com) by [Kyle Becker](https://beckernews.com/about/), Becker News is a far-right news and opinion website that frequently promotes conspiracy theories. Kyle Becker is a [freelance writer](https://muckrack.com/kyle-becker/bio) who has worked as an associate producer for [Fox News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news/) and was director of viral media and senior managing editor for the [Independent Journal Review](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/independent-journal-review/). He was also formerly the Chief Editor of [Bizpac Review](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bizpac-review/) and social media manager for Conservative News Today.
In 2017, Kyle Becker was
[suspended](https://www.snopes.com/news/2017/03/21/ijr-obama-conspiracy-theory/) from IJR for writing an article that promoted a false conspiracy theory regarding former President Obama. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Becker News lacks transparency as they do not clearly list ownership; however, Kyle Becker owns the website. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Becker News promotes news and opinions that align with the right. Articles and headlines are often emotional and favor a conservative perspective such as this
[Pelosi Just Fined 4 House Members for Violating Mask Mandate, But Watch Her Walk Through a Crowd of Democrats](https://beckernews.com/pelosi-just-fined-4-house-members-for-violating-mask-mandate-but-watch-her-walk-through-a-crowd-of-democrats-39211/). This story uses a variety of media outlets, including the questionable [Breitbart](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/breitbart/).
Kyle Becker has spread debunked conspiracy theories through Twitter and written articles
[claiming](https://www.mediamatters.org/january-6-insurrection/right-wing-media-spread-false-wash-times-report-antifa-was-identified-pro) that a facial recognition technology company had identified “Antifa” members among the insurrection and that others actually control [President Biden](https://www.newsfromtheperimeter.com/home/2021/3/3/the-shadow-presidents-3-hidden-figures-people-believe-are-really-running-the-biden-presidency). Further, after the 2020 Presidential election, Becker News has frequently promoted former President Trump’s election fraud conspiracy theories such as this [Donald Trump Relays Urgent Message About ‘Massive Fraud’ in Arizona Election in New Interview](https://beckernews.com/donald-trump-relays-urgent-message-about-massive-fraud-in-arizona-election-in-new-interview-39220/). Finally, they have made [false claims](https://www.politifact.com/factchecks/2021/apr/26/viral-image/no-biden-wasnt-only-world-leader-wear-mask-during-/) regarding President Biden and Covid-19 such as this [Biden Becomes Laughing Stock as Only World Leader on Conference Call with a Mask On](https://beckernews.com/3-new-biden-becomes-laughing-stock-as-only-world-leader-on-conference-call-wearing-a-mask-38736/). In general, Becker News rarely publishes factual news and holds a far-right editorial bias.
