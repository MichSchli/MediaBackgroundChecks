History
The Ottumwa Courier is a newspaper published in the city of Ottumwa, Iowa. ottumwacourier.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Ottumwa Courier. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Ottumwa Courier covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Ottumwa, Iowa, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Some cities could be left behind on lead pipe replacements](https://www.ottumwacourier.com/news/national_news/some-cities-could-be-left-behind-on-lead-pipe-replacements/article_89505560-7768-57af-821c-848183492c41.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
