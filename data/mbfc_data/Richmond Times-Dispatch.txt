History
Founded in 1850, The Richmond Times-Dispatch is the primary daily newspaper in Richmond, the capital of Virginia, and the primary newspaper of record for the state of Virginia.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
In March 2020, Berkshire Hathaway sold the Richmond Times-Dispatch to Lee Enterprises, which according to
[Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/) has donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) since 1990. The paper and website are funded through advertising and subscriptions.

Editorially, The Richmond Times
[has supported Republican Presidential candidates since 1980](https://noahveltman.com/endorsements/), with the exception of 2016 when they supported the Libertarian candidate Gary Johnson. In other words, they have not supported a Democrat in at least 40 years. In general, the news is reported factually with a right-leaning editorial bias.
