History
[The Daily Tar Heel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Daily_Tar_Heel) (DTH) is the independent student newspaper of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. It was founded on February 23, 1893, and became a daily newspaper in 1929. The paper focuses on university news and sports, but it also includes heavy coverage of Orange County and North Carolina. The paper focuses on University news and sports, but it also includes regular coverage of Orange County and North Carolina state news. Articles and headlines occasionally utilize emotional language, such as this
