History
The Guardians of Democracy, according to their own definition is a
[transpartisan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transpartisan) organization that consist of citizen journalists and grassroots activists that present news. A [domain search](http://whois.domaintools.com/theguardiansofdemocracy.com) reveals a private registration in January 2017. The website does not disclose ownership or an editor, but Adrian Garcia is listed as the top author. He is the CEO and [founder](https://www.linkedin.com/in/adrian-garcia-a02034b3?src=aff-lilpar&veh=aff_src.aff-lilpar_c.partners_pkw.123201_net.mediapartner_plc.adgoal%20GmbH_pcrid.449670_learning&trk=aff_src.aff-lilpar_c.partners_pkw.123201_net.mediapartner_plc.adgoal%20GmbH_pcrid.449670_learning&clickid=31TRN51GDVSpWMpQNWSq7xJQUklQ3WxNpwPDyo0&irgwc=1) of the website [The Gaily Grind](https://thegailygrind.com/), which publishes LGBTQ news. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Guardians of Democracy does not disclose ownership. Revenue is derived from advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Guardians of Democracy has a strong left bias in reporting choices and wording. While Guardians of Democracy does provide credible sources to back their articles, the bias in word choices is relatively obvious. Note the use of the word choice “brags”, which is meant to portray President Trump negatively
[“NEWS Trump Brags To Boy Scouts About Spending Time Partying On Yachts, Hints At Sex Yacht Party.](https://theguardiansofdemocracy.com/trump-brags-boy-scouts-spending-time-partying-yachts-hints-sex-yacht-party/) Another example of emotionally loaded language can be found here: [In Wild Rant, Trump Blames Obama For Ruining The White House’s Air Conditioning System](https://theguardiansofdemocracy.com/in-wild-rant-trump-blames-obama-for-ruining-the-white-houses-air-conditioning-system/). This story is sourced to Twitter and video. In general, story selection always favors the left and denigrates the right, in particular Donald Trump.
