History
Founded in 1949, WJXT – News4Jax is an independent television station licensed to Jacksonville, Florida, United States. News4Jax.com is the associated website that reports news, crime, sports, weather, and lifestyle for the greater Jacksonville area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WJXT – News4Jax is owned by
[Graham Holdings](https://www.ghco.com/), which owns seven TV stations and the website [Slate](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/slate/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WJXT – News4Jax provides news, sports, weather, crime reporting, and lifestyle for the greater Jacksonville metro area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this
[Man doing burnouts charged with aggravated assault on police, hit & run: JSO](https://www.news4jax.com/news/local/2022/07/06/video-man-doing-burnouts-charged-with-aggravated-assault-on-police-hit-run/). Many stories provide videos from the WJXT TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally offers national and world news.
Editorially, they do not produce Op-Eds; however, they do report on politics through the Associated Press and original reporting. Political news is generally balanced and presented with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden tells Griner’s wife he’s working to get her home](https://www.news4jax.com/news/politics/2022/07/06/biden-tells-with-griners-wife-hes-working-to-get-her-home/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
