History
[Project Censored](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_Censored) is a media research, education, and advocacy initiative started at Sonoma State University in 1976. It is currently housed at Diablo Valley College in Pleasant Hill, California. Project Censored was founded by Carl Jensen to expose censorship in propaganda and mass media. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Media Freedom Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization sponsors Project Censored. Donations support the website.
Analysis / Bias
Project Censored annually publishes a book, has weekly radio programs, and publishes news focusing on news censorship, propaganda, free speech, and politics.
In review, Project Censored uses loaded emotional wording in headlines and articles such as this
[Journalists Investigating Financial Crimes Threatened by Global Elites](https://www.projectcensored.org/journalists-investigating-financial-crimes-threatened-by-global-elites/). This story, like all reviewed, relies on credible sources for information.
Editorially, they promote a left-leaning perspective by promoting worker’s rights
[Google’s Union-Busting Methods Revealed](https://www.projectcensored.org/googles-union-busting-methods-revealed/), and concern for privacy rights [Zoom Classrooms Raise Concerns about Law Enforcement Surveillance and 4th Amendment Rights](https://www.projectcensored.org/zoom-classrooms-raise-concerns-about-law-enforcement-surveillance-and-4th-amendment-rights/). Further, they frequently reported negatively on former President Trump [Trump’s Connection to Global Spending by Anti-LGBTQ Religious Organizations.](https://www.projectcensored.org/trumps-connection-to-global-spending-by-anti-lgbtq-religious-organizations/) However, they also report negatively on Joe Biden’s centrist platform [Biden Backs Legislation Preventing $150 Billion of Student Loan Debt from being Discharged or Renegotiated](https://www.projectcensored.org/biden-backs-legislation-preventing-150-billion-student-loan-debt-discharged-renegotiated/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a left-leaning editorial bias.
