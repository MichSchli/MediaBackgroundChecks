History
[Founded in 1969](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WENY-TV), WENY-TV is an ABC/CBS/CW+ affiliated television station licensed to Elmira, New York, serving the Western [Twin Tiers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Twin_Tiers) of Southern Upstate New York and Northern Pennsylvania. WENY.com, also known as WENY News, is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Twin Tiers Region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lilly Broadcasting, based in Pennsylvania, owns WENY. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WENY.com publishes news for the Twin Tiers area through reporters and with minimal bias such as this
[New York restaurants can soon applying for grants amid pandemic.](https://www.weny.com/story/43145549/new-york-restaurants-can-soon-applying-for-grants-amid-pandemic) Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WENY.com does not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
