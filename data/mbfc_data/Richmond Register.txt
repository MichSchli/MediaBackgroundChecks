History
The Richmond Register is a newspaper published in Richmond, Kentucky. richmondregister.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Richmond Register. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Richmond Register covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Richmond, Kentucky region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Officials say safety a factor in Hwy 1016 roundabout decision](https://www.richmondregister.com/news/officials-say-safety-a-factor-in-hwy-1016-roundabout-decision/article_a0986ba0-0cb4-11ee-866a-0f8708488b9d.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
