History
[Founded in 2015](http://whois.domaintools.com/differencebetween.net), DifferenceBetween.net is a website that researches and compares two things, such as the difference between Socialism and Communism. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not disclose ownership, and they appear to be primarily funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, DifferenceBetween.net does exactly as it says it does. It compares and contrasts the differences between the two topics. There is little use of
[loaded language](http://www.differencebetween.net/miscellaneous/religion-miscellaneous/difference-between-anabaptists-and-evangelicals/), and all information is thoroughly sourced from credible media. When covering politics, DifferenceBetween.net does not convey bias and [covers both sides](http://www.differencebetween.net/miscellaneous/difference-between-conservatives-and-liberals/) in a balanced way.
