History
The Panola Watchman is a daily broadsheet newspaper independently published in Panola, Texas. The newspaper serves Panola and the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The newspaper is owned by
[M. Roberts Media](https://mrobertsmedia.com/), which publishes 6 newspapers in the eastern Texas region. The paper is funded through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Panola Watchman reports local news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Panola County quilters make donation to HeartsWay Hospice](https://www.panolawatchman.com/lifestyles/panola-county-quilters-make-donation-to-heartsway-hospice/article_50fc56a6-3906-11ed-8043-5377da5b2936.html). National and world news comes almost exclusively from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Panola Watchman tends to lean left. In general, the Panola Watchman is factual, uses credible sources, and holds a slightly Left-leaning editorial bias.
