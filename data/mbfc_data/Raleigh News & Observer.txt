History
[Founded in 1865](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_News_%26_Observer), The News & Observer is a regional daily newspaper that serves the greater Triangle area based In Raleigh. The paper is the second largest in the state after [The Charlotte Observer](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/charlotte-observer/). The paper has also been awarded three Pulitzer Prizes. [Read our country media profile of the USA.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Raleigh News and Observer is owned by
[The McClatchy Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/McClatchy), which owns numerous papers across the United States, including the [Fresno Bee](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fresno-bee/) and the [Kansas City Star.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/kansas-city-star/) Revenue is derived from advertising and subscription fees.
