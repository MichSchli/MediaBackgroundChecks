History
Castor Advance is a newspaper published in Castor, Alberta, Canada. The website castoradvance.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Castor, Alberta, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Castor Advance. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Castor Advance covers news for Castor and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Community initiative supporting project workers coming to Paintearth County started](https://www.castoradvance.com/news/community-initiative-supporting-project-workers-coming-to-paintearth-county-started-7314159). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Castor Advance does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
