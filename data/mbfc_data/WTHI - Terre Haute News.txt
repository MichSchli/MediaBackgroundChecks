History
Founded in 1954, WTHI – Terre Haute News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Terre Haute, Indiana, United States. wthitv.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Terre Haute area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WTHI – Terre Haute News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WTHI – Terre Haute News publishes news for Terre Haute and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Two weak tornadoes cause minor damage in central Indiana](https://www.wthitv.com/news/two-weak-tornadoes-cause-minor-damage-in-central-indiana/article_663a7dae-e8c5-11ec-838c-b769f0fa882f.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
