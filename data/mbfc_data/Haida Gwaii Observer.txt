History
The Haida Gwaii Observer is a newspaper published in Haida Gwaii, British Columbia, Canada. The website coastmountainnews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Haida Gwaii area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Haida Gwaii Observer. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Haida Gwaii Observer covers news for Haida Gwaii and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[High-speed internet access coming to remote Gitanyow, Stewart](https://www.haidagwaiiobserver.com/news/high-speed-internet-access-coming-to-remote-gitanyow-stewart/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Haida Gwaii Observer does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
