History
Fernie Free Press is a newspaper published in Fernie, British Columbia, Canada. The website thefreepress.ca covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Fernie, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Fernie Free Press. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Fernie Free Press covers news for Fernie and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Man dead after Highway 3 collision near Sparwood](https://www.thefreepress.ca/news/man-dead-after-highway-3-collision-near-sparwood-7285636). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Fernie Free Press does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
