History
[Founded in 1916](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Palm_Beach_Post), The Palm Beach Post is an American daily broadsheet newspaper serving Palm Beach County in South Florida and parts of the Treasure Coast. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Palm Beach Post. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Palm Beach Post reports local news using journalists with minimally loaded words and proper sourcing, such as this:
[Police ID man shot, killed in West Palm dispute over unleashed dog.](https://www.palmbeachpost.com/news/20190205/update-police-id-man-shot-killed-in-west-palm-dispute-over-unleashed-dog) National and world news is covered by the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, The Palm Beach Post mostly endorses
[Democratic candidates,](https://www.palmbeachpost.com/news/20181022/post-editorial-boards-nov-2018-election-endorsements) while their opinion pieces also favor the left, such as this: [Editorial: Sea-level rise is not just a worry for those who live on South Florida’s waterfront](https://www.palmbeachpost.com/opinion/20190130/editorial-sea-level-rise-is-not-just-worry-for-those-who-live-on-south-floridas-waterfront) and this [Editorial: Don’t dishonor Parkland shooting victims by reversing gun safety laws](https://www.palmbeachpost.com/opinion/20190123/editorial-dont-dishonor-parkland-shooting-victims-by-reversing-gun-safety-laws). In general, The Palm Beach Post reports straight news with minimal bias and proper sourcing; however, editorially, there is a distinct left-leaning bias.
