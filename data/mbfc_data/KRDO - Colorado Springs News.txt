History
Founded in 1953, KRDO – Colorado Springs News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Colorado Springs, Colrado. The associated website is krdo.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns KRDO – Colorado Springs News and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KRDO – Colorado Springs News website publishes news for the greater Colorado Springs, Colorado area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this: Shooting near
[Colorado Springs high school connected to Citadel Mall shooting](https://krdo.com/news/top-stories/2022/05/27/shooting-near-colorado-springs-high-school-connected-to-citadel-mall-shooting/) Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [CBS News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/), [CNN Newswire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[Student loan forgiveness divides Americans more by party and age than by education.](https://krdo.com/politics/cnn-us-politics/2022/05/28/student-loan-forgiveness-divides-americans-more-by-party-and-age-than-by-education/) Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoiding using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
