History
The Vancouver Island Free Daily is a newspaper published on Vancouver Island, British Columbia, Canada. The website vancouverislandfreedaily.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Vancouver Island area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Vancouver Island Free Daily. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Vancouver Island Free Daily covers news for Vancouver Island and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Sidney Island residents to vote on plan to eradicate invasive deer](https://www.vancouverislandfreedaily.com/news/sidney-island-residents-to-vote-on-plan-to-eradicate-invasive-deer/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Vancouver Island Free Daily does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
