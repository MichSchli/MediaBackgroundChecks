History
Clearwater Times is a newspaper published in Clearwater, British Columbia, Canada. The website clearwatertimes.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Clearwater, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Clearwater Times. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Clearwater Times covers news for Clearwater and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Lightning sparks several new fires across northern parts of Cariboo Fire Centre](https://www.clearwatertimes.com/news/lightening-sparks-several-new-fires-across-northern-parts-of-cariboo-fire-centre/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Clearwater Times does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
