History
[Founded in 1890](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Opelika-Auburn_News), The Opelika Auburn News is a daily print newspaper published in Opelika-Auburn, Alabama, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Dothan and the surrounding area. dothaneagle.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lee Enterprises owns and publishes the Opelika Auburn News. According to
[Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Lee Enterprises donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) from 1990 until 2016. However, during the 2020 election cycle, they donated 70% to Democratic candidates and causes. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
