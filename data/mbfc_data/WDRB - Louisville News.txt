History
Founded in 1971 and a
[Fox](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station, WDRB – Louisville News serves Louisville, Kentucky, and the surrounding area. wdrb.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
Funded by / Ownership
WDRB – Louisville News is owned by
[Block Communications](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Block_Communications), which also owns Pittsburgh Press and the [Pittsburgh Post-Gazette](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pittsburgh-post-gazette/). The TV Station and website are funded through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WDRB – Louisville News provides news coverage for Louisville, Kentucky, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Metro Council approves controversial low-income housing plan for Prospect](https://www.wdrb.com/in-depth/metro-council-approves-controversial-low-income-housing-plan-for-prospect/article_c4871e9a-7cb7-11ed-afbd-d38b0bfc0478.html). The national and international news is provided through other Block Communications affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WDRB – Louisville News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
