History
Founded in 1953, WSIL – Harrisburg News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Harrisburg, Illinois, United States. wsiltv.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Harrisburg area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WSIL – Harrisburg News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WSIL – Harrisburg News publishes news for Harrisburg and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[New health clinic opening August 29 in Goreville](https://www.wsiltv.com/news/new-health-clinic-opening-august-29-in-goreville/article_2072174e-2706-11ed-bbdf-5fef36714eb6.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
