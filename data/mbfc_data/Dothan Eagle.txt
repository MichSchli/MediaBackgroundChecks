History
[Founded in 1908](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dothan_Eagle), The Dothan Eagle is a daily print newspaper published in Dothan, Alabama, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Dothan and the surrounding area. dothaneagle.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lee Enterprises owns and publishes the Dothan Eagle. According to
[Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Lee Enterprises donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) from 1990 until 2016. However, during the 2020 election cycle, they donated 70% to Democratic candidates and causes. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Dothan Eagle reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and entertainment for the greater Dothan, Alabama region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[Flu cases rising, but still rates are lower than 2019-2020 figures](https://dothaneagle.com/news/local/flu-cases-rising-but-still-rates-are-lower-than-2019-2020-figures/article_658f4ae0-56e6-11ec-b6a7-9310c989f708.html). Further, the website publishes national and international news from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). Finally, all information is sourced via first-person accounts or through credible media.
Editorially, the Dothan Eagle reasonably represents both sides. A review of editorial articles demonstrates a reasonable low bias; however, slightly more op-eds favor the right such as this
[Cancel Culture](https://dothaneagle.com/opinion/editorial/cancel-culture/article_7931dfd0-3cd4-11ec-91d0-b7961dc19ca3.html). While more op-eds favor the right, liberal perspectives have fair representation; therefore, they earn a least biased rating on the right side of the scale. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
