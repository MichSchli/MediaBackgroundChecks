History
[Launched in 2020](https://whois.domaintools.com/brandnewtube.com), Brand New Tube is a video upload website based in the United Kingdom. The aim appears to be a replacement for Youtube channels that have been de-platformed. According to their [about page](https://brandnewtube.com/terms/about-us), “we will not de-platform you, remove your content simply because it may question a particular narrative or upset someone else’s point of view.” [Read our profile on UK media and government](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Brand New Tube does not clearly state who owns them. However, on the
[contact page](https://brandnewtube.com/contact-us), they list an address to [MY MEDIA WORLD LTD.](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/12602950/officers) There is little information about this company. Revenue is derived from the sale of branded merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
Brand New Tube is a video uploading service that does not appear to vet videos. Almost every video on the service is related to conspiracy theories or pseudoscience. For example, they frequently publish unproven information regarding Covid-19, such as this
[Covid-19 Vaccines Are Weapons of Mass Destruction – and Could Wipe out the Human Race](https://brandnewtube.com/watch/covid-19-vaccines-are-weapons-of-mass-destruction-and-could-wipe-out-the-human-race_GcjtJu9dY1RcSNh.html). The contents of this video are [Unproven](https://www.politifact.com/factchecks/2021/mar/31/facebook-posts/no-covid-19-vaccines-are-not-weapons-mass-destruct/). The video is from Vernon Coleman, who used to be a general practicing Doctor. Vernon Coleman has been described as an English “ [conspiracy theorist](https://inews.co.uk/opinion/columnists/how-we-can-fight-back-against-anti-vaxxer-scaremongering-on-social-media-923435),” “ [anti-vaccination activist](https://www.scientificamerican.com/article/covid-has-created-a-perfect-storm-for-fringe-science/),” who claims [AIDS does not exist](https://www.vernoncoleman.com/aids.htm), blogger, and novelist who writes on topics related to human health, politics, and animal issues.
Besides promoting pseudoscientific videos related to Covid-19, they also promote conspiracy theories related to numerous topics. These topics range from
[Chemtrails](https://brandnewtube.com/watch/who-owns-us_Fp2zXlbXCUimQOO.html), [False Flags](https://brandnewtube.com/watch/jacob-peterson-blm-george-floyd-false-flag-riots_BBxqry7qaRsoGd8.html), [9-11 inside job conspiracies](https://brandnewtube.com/watch/ae911truth-not-naming-names-or-going-after-who-did-9-11-with-thermite-and-pentagon-contradictions_ZK22rMbls1Gvz9c.html), [5g](https://brandnewtube.com/watch/5g-and-coronavirus-exposed-by-ex-vodafone-boss_Gq77j4qyfBjMSCr.html), and copious amounts of [anti-vaccination propaganda](https://brandnewtube.com/watch/the-truth-about-vaccines_AgMcwsFkhSclTDE.html). Further, videos by [Alex Jones from Infowars](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/infowars-alex-jones/) often appear on this service. In general, it is difficult to find any factual content on this service. Most videos project a right-leaning bias and are not factual.
