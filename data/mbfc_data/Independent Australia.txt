History
Founded in 2010, Independent Australia is an Australian news and opinion website based in Australia / Isle of Capri, Qld. The founder and managing editor is
[David Donovan.](https://independentaustralia.net/profile-on/david-donovan,7) According to their [“about us page](https://independentaustralia.net/about),” Independent Australia is a “progressive journal focusing on politics, democracy, the environment, Australian history, and Australian identity. It contains news and opinion from Australia and around the world.” [Read our profile on Australia’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/australia-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Independent Australia is founded by David Donovan, and according to
[thecitizen.org](https://www.thecitizen.org.au/articles/not-quite-mainstream-politi-bloggers-looking-make-splash-election-year) “Founder David Donovan has spent $60,000 of his own money, and much more of his time. Has small advertisers and is experimenting with crowd-funding.”
Analysis / Bias
Under the category politics, Independent Australia publishes news stories utilizing emotionally loaded headlines such as:
[“It’s okay to be gay — Pope Francis says so”](https://independentaustralia.net/life/life-display/its-okay-to-be-gay-pope-francis-says-so,11537) and sources credible media such as [The Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/), [advocate.com](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/advocate/), and factually mixed sites such as [life site news.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/life-site-news/) They also use sources that we have not rated, such as “ncregister.com,” “Medyascope.tv” and ”news.rambler.ru.”
Independent Australia reports national news with emotionally loaded words and left bias using credible media sources through quotes and links such as
[“News.com.au”](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/news-com-au/) and [The Sydney Morning Herald](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/news-com-au/). Independent Australia has a [negative ](https://independentaustralia.net/politics/politics-display/editorial-malcolm-turnbulls-safe-phrase,11390)editorial piece about former PM Turnbull (Malcolm Turnbull is the former Prime Minister of Australia and Leader of the Liberal Party. [The Liberal Party of Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Liberal_Party_of_Australia) is a major center-right [political party in Australia.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_political_parties_in_Australia)”)
For International News, Independent Australia utilizes left-leaning credible sources such as the
[New Republic](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-republic/) and the [LA Times (Los Angeles Times)](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/la-times-los-angeles-times/). For example: [“Let’s take Donald Trump’s tweeting seriously.”](https://independentaustralia.net/politics/politics-display/lets-take-donald-trumps-tweeting-seriously,10490)
