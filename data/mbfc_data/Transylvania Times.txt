History
The Transylvania Times is a newspaper published in Brevard, North Carolina. transylvaniatimes.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Transylvania Times. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Transylvania Times covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Brevard, NC, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Trails Carolina campers placed with Department of Social Services](https://www.transylvaniatimes.com/news/trails-carolina-campers-placed-with-department-of-social-services/article_3578d136-ccd8-11ee-a372-d7bf41afcb0b.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
