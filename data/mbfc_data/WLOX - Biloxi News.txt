History
Founded in 1962 and an ABC/CBS-affiliated television station, WLOX – Biloxi News serves Biloxi, Mississippi, and the surrounding area. wlox.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WLOX – Biloxi News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WLOX – Biloxi News provides news coverage for Biloxi, Mississippi, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Gulfport’s Gaston Point community takes stand against violence](https://www.wlox.com/2022/01/12/gulfports-gaston-point-community-takes-stand-against-violence/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WLOX – Biloxi News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
