History
Launched in 1971, KRIV – Fox 26 – Houston is a
[Fox](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Houston, Texas, United States. fox26houston.com is the associated website covering news, sports, crime, and weather for the Houston area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KRIV – Fox 26 – Houston is owned by Fox Television Stations, a subsidiary of Fox Corporation and, in turn, owned by Rupert Murdoch. Fox Corporation owns 30 Fox Television Stations. Revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KRIV – Fox 26 – Houston reports the Houston area’s news, sports, crime, and weather. Local news is provided with minimal bias in wording, such a
[Experts ‘confident’ in the Texas power grid as many brace for Arctic blast](https://www.fox26houston.com/news/experts-confident-in-the-texas-power-grid-as-many-brace-for-arctic-blast). Most news reports also contain video footage from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other Fox properties provide national and international news.
Editorially, they do not offer opinion content and cover politics with minimal bias, often citing the Associated Press for national politics. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
