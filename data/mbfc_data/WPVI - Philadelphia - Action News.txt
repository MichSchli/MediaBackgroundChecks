History
Founded in 1946, WPVI – Philadelphia – Action News is an ABC television network licensed to Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States. 6ABC.com is the associated website that provides news, weather, and sports for the Philadelphia Metropolitan region.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WPVI – Philadelphia – Action News is owned by the ABC Owned Television Stations subsidiary of
[The Walt Disney Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the WPVI – Philadelphia – Action News website covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the Philadelphia Metro area. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Officials identify Philadelphia firefighter killed after building collapse in Fairhill](https://6abc.com/philadelphia-firefighter-dies-building-collapse-philly-fire-west-indiana-avenue-fairhill/11973173/). Many local stories contain embedded videos from the TV station. National and world news is covered by [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/), [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [ESPN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/espn/), and other ABC stations.
Editorially, WPVI – Philadelphia – Action News does not provide opinion pieces; however, they report on politics. They provide original reporting with a slight left-leaning bias, such as this
[Biden signs executive order to combat anti-LGBTQ legislation](https://6abc.com/joe-biden-lgbtq-executive-order-lgbtq-rights/11963275/). They also rely on political news from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
