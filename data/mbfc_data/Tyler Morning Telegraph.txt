History
[Founded in 1877](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tyler_Morning_Telegraph), The Tyler Morning Telegraph is a daily broadsheet newspaper independently published in Tyler, Texas. The newspaper serves Tyler and the surrounding area. [Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The newspaper is owned by
[M. Roberts Media](https://mrobertsmedia.com/), which publishes 6 newspapers in the eastern Texas region. The paper is funded through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Tyler Morning Telegraph reports local news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Fire destroys home south of Bullard](https://tylerpaper.com/news/local/fire-destroys-home-south-of-bullard/article_1a17fe66-2ebf-11ed-8b0c-77102f7fff43.html). National and world news comes almost exclusively from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Tyler Morning Telegraph does not produce political opinion pieces; however, they republish political news through
[The Center Square](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/watchdog-org/), a right-leaning nonprofit. In general, the Tyler Morning Telegraph is factual, uses credible sources, and holds a slightly Right-leaning editorial bias.
