History
Founded in 2012, and according to their
[about page:](https://educateinspirechange.org/us/) EducateInspireChange (EiC) was created to help keep people informed, to encourage people to expand their consciousness, and to inspire people to reach for their dreams. This is an alternative news website. [Read our country profile on the UK’s media influence.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The founder, editor, and owner of EducateInspireChange is Kasim Khan from Scotland. Khan is also the owner of
[EIC media limited](https://beta.companieshouse.gov.uk/company/SC498272). The website is funded through advertising and donations.
Analysis / Bias
In reviewing EIC there is a mix of real science news from credible sources and then many news stories that are junk science. EIC
[rejects the consensus of science](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-gmo-dangers/) regarding the [safety of GMOs](http://educateinspirechange.org/alternative-news/60-percent-food-eat-everyday-hiding-potentially-deadly-secret/). They also promote miracle natural health cures such as [this](http://educateinspirechange.org/health/turmeric-lemonade-treats-depression-naturally-better-prozac/). Lastly, the primary source they seem to rely on for most of the quackery is [Natural News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/natural-news/), which is a well known purveyor of pseudoscience and conspiracy theories.
