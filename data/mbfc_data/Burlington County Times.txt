History
The Burlington County Times is a newspaper that serves Willingboro, New Jersey, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. burlingtoncountytimes.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Burlington County Times. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Burlington County Times covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Willingboro (Burlington County), New Jersey area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Grocery-delivery firm to shed hundreds of workers at Delanco facility](https://www.burlingtoncountytimes.com/story/news/local/2023/02/10/misfits-market-says-distribution-center-shutdown-will-bring-layoffs-in-delanco/69889153007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Burlington County Times does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Here’s how you can help tell the truth about New Jersey’s slave history | Opinion](https://www.burlingtoncountytimes.com/story/opinion/2023/02/10/nj-slave-history-must-be-preserved/69891576007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
