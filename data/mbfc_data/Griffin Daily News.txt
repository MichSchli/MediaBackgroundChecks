History
The Griffin Daily News is a daily newspaper published in Griffin Georgia. griffindailynews.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Paxton Media Group,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paxton_Media_Group) which owns several right-leaning newspapers across the USA, such as the [Elkhart Truth](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-elkhart-truth/). Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Griffin Daily News reports national news by republishing articles from credible newsagents such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). They report local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias using neutral headlines such as [Spalding holds first public hearing over millage rate proposal](https://www.griffindailynews.com/news/spalding-holds-first-public-hearing-over-millage-rate-proposal/article_60e99406-80c0-573a-ad85-a3f36c90b192.html). All information is properly sourced.
Editorially, there is a reasonable balance between left and right-leaning opinions, with slightly more favoring the right such as this:
[CDPH sees spike in fentanyl overdoses in Douglas County](https://www.douglascountysentinel.com/douglasville_sentinel/cdph-sees-spike-in-fentanyl-overdoses-in-douglas-county/article_7857fe1b-f1d9-5c3b-a78f-90cf3b757b97.html). In general, the paper is fact-based and holds a slight right-leaning editorial bias.
