History
The Pottsville Republican-Herald is a newspaper published in Pottsville, Pennsylvania, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Pottsville and the surrounding area. Republicanherald.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.

Funded by / Ownership
[Times-Shamrock Communication](https://timesshamrock.com/) owns and publishes the Pottsville Republican-Herald. They publish newspapers, radio stations, and information-based websites in North Eastern Pennsylvania. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
