History
The Hillsboro Times-Gazette is a newspaper based in Hillsboro, Ohio, serving Highland County and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at timesgazette.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Hillsboro Times-Gazette and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Hillsboro Times-Gazette publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Unemployment rises in Highland Co.](https://www.timesgazette.com/news/63918/unemployment-rises-in-highland-co-2) The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Hillsboro Times-Gazette leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
