Analysis / Bias
Newsview is an anonymously published conservative news and opinion website that routinely publishes false and misleading claims. For example, they reported that
[Biden’s Climate Requirements: Cut 90% of Red Meat From Diet; Americans Can Only Eat One Burger Per Month](https://www.newsview.gr/2021/04/bidens-climate-requirements-cut-90-of-red-meat-from-diet-americans-can-only-eat-one-burger-per-month.html), this was [rated Pants on Fire](https://www.politifact.com/factchecks/2021/apr/26/fox-news-channel/joe-biden-banning-burgers-fox-news-gop-politicians/) by [Politifact](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/politifact-news/). This story was originally published by the Questionable [Daily Mail](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-mail/) and then amplified by [Fox News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news/).
They also frequently promote fear and false information regarding the Coronavirus such as this
[Juan O Savin: Will Vaccinated People Kill The Unvaccinated?? – Part 2 – Must Video](https://www.newsview.gr/2021/04/juan-o-savin-will-vaccinated-people-kill-the-unvaccinated-must-video.html). Further, they consistently use misleading headlines such as this that seem to indicate former President Trump is still in charge [President Trump Alarms The Military To Secure The Arizona Election Audit.](https://www.newsview.gr/2021/04/president-trump-alarms-the-military-to-secure-the-arizona-election-audit.html) The sources used for their false claims come from conspiracy websites such as [x22](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/x22-report/), [Mike Lindell](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/lindell-tv/), and [Truedefender.com](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-true-defender/). In general, almost all content on this website is extreme right biased and either false, misleading, conspiracy related, or fake.
