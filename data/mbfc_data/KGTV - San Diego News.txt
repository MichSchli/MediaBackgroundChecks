History
Founded in 1953, KGTV – San Diego News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to San Diego, California, United States. 10news.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KGTV – San Diego News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KGTV – San Diego News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater San Diego, California, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[1,500 Sharp Grossmont Hospital workers vote to unionize](https://www.10news.com/news/1-500-sharp-grossmont-hospital-workers-vote-to-unionize). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden lawyer: No classified documents found at president’s beach home](https://www.10news.com/news/national/biden-lawyer-fbi-searching-bidens-rehoboth-beach-home). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
