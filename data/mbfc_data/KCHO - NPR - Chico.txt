History
KCHO – NPR – Chico (North State Public Radio) is an NPR FM radio station (91.7 FM) based in Chico, California serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KCHO – NPR – Chico is owned and broadcast by California State University, Chico. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
