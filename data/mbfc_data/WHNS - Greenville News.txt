History
Founded in 1953 and a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station, WHNS – Greenville News serves Greenville, South Carolina, and the surrounding area. Foxcarolina.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WHNS – Greenville News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WHNS – Greenville News provides news coverage for Greenville, South Carlina, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Body found overnight at Worth Street, Palmetto Avenue](https://www.foxcarolina.com/2022/07/24/body-found-overnight-worth-street-palmetto-avenue/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WHNS – Greenville News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
