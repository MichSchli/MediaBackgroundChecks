History
Vernon Morning Star is a newspaper published in Vernon, British Columbia, Canada. The website vernonmorningstar.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Vernon, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Vernon Morning Star. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Vernon Morning Star covers news for Vernon and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Taps turn on North Okanagan water source](https://www.vernonmorningstar.com/local-news/taps-turn-on-north-okanagan-water-source-6517927). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Vernon Morning Star does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
