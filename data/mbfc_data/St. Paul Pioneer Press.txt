History
The St. Paul Pioneer Press is a newspaper based in Saint Paul, Minnesota, primarily serving the Twin Cities metropolitan area. The Pioneer Press was founded in 1847.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The
[MediaNews Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MediaNews_Group) has owned the St. Paul Pioneer Press since 2016. MediaNews Group is owned by [Alden Global Capital LLC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alden_Global_Capital), a New York-Based Hedge Fund, described as “ [Vulture Capitalists](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/04/07/business/media/denver-post-opinion-owner.html)” by former [Denver Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/denver-post/) writers for the cuts made to newsrooms. Subscription fees and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the St. Paul Pioneer Press is factual in reporting international and national news using sources such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/). Local news is written by Minnesota journalists that is also factual and utilize minimally loaded language [St. Paul man pleads guilty to killing 4-week old son in 2020; prosecution asks for 40 years.](https://www.twincities.com/2021/11/10/st-paul-man-pleads-guilty-to-killing-4-week-old-son-in-2020-prosecution-asks-for-40-years/)
Editorially, The St. Paul Pioneer Press has
[endorsed](https://noahveltman.com/endorsements/) both Republicans and Democrats; however, in 2012, they stopped endorsement. A review of Editorials indicates slightly more favor the right such as this [Other voices: Spend-borrow-repeat will be the ‘debt’ of us](https://www.twincities.com/2021/07/25/other-voices-spend-borrow-repeat-will-be-the-debt-of-us/). This op-ed is republished through the right-leaning [Chicago Tribune](https://www.twincities.com/2021/07/25/other-voices-spend-borrow-repeat-will-be-the-debt-of-us/). They also republish left-leaning editorials; however, more favor the right. In general, they are a factual newspaper that holds a slight right-leaning bias.
