History
[DefenseScoop](https://fedscoop.com/about/) is a digital news media platform that delivers community-driven news dedicated to the U.S. military’s acquisition, development, and use of technology as a force for modern defense. It is headquartered in Washington, DC, and is part of the Scoop News Group, a leading public sector tech media company in the United States. Founder & CEO [Goldy Kamali](https://www.linkedin.com/in/goldykamali/) leads the company. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
DefenseScoop is part of the
[Scoop News Group](https://scoopnewsgroup.com/ourbrands/); ownership is not publicly disclosed. Goldy Kamali is the Founder & CEO, and a [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/) [article](https://www.washingtonpost.com/express/wp/2011/06/13/techsavvy-talk-government-it-company/) indicates she also owns Scoop News Group, which monetizes through [advertisements.](https://scoopnewsgroup.com/oursolutions/)
Analysis / Bias
DefenseScoop covers a wide range of topics related to the U.S. military’s acquisition, development, and use of technology. The articles on the platform do not use loaded language, such as this
[NSA opening AI Security Center](https://defensescoop.com/2023/09/28/nsa-opening-ai-security-center/). This story is properly sourced.
The content on the website is well-researched and factual, with a focus on providing accurate and up-to-date information to its readers. There is no evident bias in the articles, and the platform seems to maintain a neutral stance on the topics it covers.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds and, therefore, are least biased and fact-based.
