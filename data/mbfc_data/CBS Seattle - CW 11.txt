History
[Founded in 1953](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KSTW), CBS Seattle – CW 11 is a CBS-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Tacoma, Washington, United States. The associated website is seattle.cbslocal.com that features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater New York City area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
CBS Seattle – CW 11 is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the CBS Seattle – CW 11 website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater Seattle-Tacoma region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording: [Fireworks Trigger Brush and House Fires Across Contra Costa County.](https://seattle.cbslocal.com/video/5749874-fireworks-trigger-brush-and-house-fires-across-contra-costa-county/) A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published from other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, CBS Seattle – CW 11 does not publish op-eds; however, they do report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[‘Biden Voters Keep Out’: Cigar Shop Owner Puts Shocking, Divisive Political Sign In Store Window.](https://seattle.cbslocal.com/2021/01/11/cigar-shop-sign-biden-voters-keep-out/) In general, the news is reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
