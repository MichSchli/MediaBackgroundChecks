History
The Dodge City Daily Globe is a newspaper published in Dodge City, Kansas. dodgeglobe.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the Dodge City Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Dodge City Daily Globe is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Dodge City Daily Globe covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Dodge City, Kansas, area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[How the Dodge City school district increased postsecondary success](https://www.dodgeglobe.com/2022/09/24/how-the-dodge-city-school-district-increased-postsecondary-success/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
