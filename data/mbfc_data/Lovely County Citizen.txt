History
The Lovely County Citizen is a daily newspaper published in Lovely County, Arkansas. lovelycitizen.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for Lovely County.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Lovely County Citizen is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Lovely County Citizen covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Lovely County, Arkansas area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Eureka Springs residents could see increase in water rate](https://www.lovelycitizen.com/2022/06/16/es-residents-could-see-increase-in-water-rate/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
