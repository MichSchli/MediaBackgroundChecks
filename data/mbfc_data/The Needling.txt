History
The Needling is a Seattle-based American
[satirical news website](https://theneedling.com/2018/04/22/seattles-only-real-fake-news-in-the-real-news/) claiming to be “Seattle’s Only Real Fake News.” Founded in Seattle in 2018, the website mainly tackles political and community issues in the Seattle metropolitan area and posts national articles. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Needling does not openly disclose ownership, and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Needling, although satirical, appears to hold a left-leaning bias, publishing satire articles with titles that often go against the right, particularly in its National section on its website
[Conservative Uncle Excited to Pretend to Care About Budget Deficits Again](https://theneedling.com/2020/12/16/conservative-uncle-excited-to-pretend-to-care-about-budget-deficits-again/). However, The Needling does disclose that they are satirical, in an [article](https://theneedling.com/2018/04/22/seattles-only-real-fake-news-in-the-real-news/) with the [Seattle Post-Intelligencer](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/seattle-post-intelligencer/), with the title as “‘We’re not trying to fool anybody’: Satire site ‘The Needling’ takes on Seattle’s stereotypes”. In general, they are classified as a satire website based on proper disclosure of their intentions.
