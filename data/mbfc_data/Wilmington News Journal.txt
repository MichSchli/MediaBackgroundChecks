History
The Wilmington News Journal is a newspaper based in Wilmington, Ohio, serving the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at wnewsj.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Wilmington News Journal and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Wilmington News Journal publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Wilmington City Schools, Denver Place coping with loss of fifth-grade teacher](https://www.wnewsj.com/news/199591/wilmington-city-schools-denver-place-coping-with-loss-of-fifth-grade-teacher). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Wilmington News Journal leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
