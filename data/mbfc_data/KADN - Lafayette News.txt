History
Founded in 1980, KADN – Lafayette News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station licensed to Lafayette, Louisiana, United States. Kadn.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Lafayette area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns KADN – Lafayette News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KADN – Lafayette News publishes news for Lafayette and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Family of California woman killed in Lafayette believes her death is a result of domestic violence](https://www.kadn.com/news/family-of-california-woman-killed-in-lafayette-believes-her-death-is-a-result-of-domestic/article_cadfc1be-0aea-11ed-8ae2-939762ba27ac.html)
Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
