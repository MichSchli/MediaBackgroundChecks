History
Launched in 2014,
[MyNewsLA.com](https://mynewsla.com/about/) is a news website that covers local news for Los Angeles and Orange County. MyNewsLA.com is based in Los Angeles and focuses on providing breaking news, sports, business, crime, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[MyNewsLA.com](https://mynewsla.com/faq/), a [sister site](https://timesofsandiego.com/opinion/2014/10/20/sister-site-mynewsla-com-covers-greater-los-angeles/) of the [Times of San Diego](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/times-san-diego/), is owned by Chris Jennewein through [CalNews, Inc.](https://www.californiaexplore.com/company/03619584/calnews-inc) Chris Jennewein writes for Times of San Diego LLC. The website lacks transparency as they do not identify Chris Jennewein as the owner of CalNews, Inc. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, MyNewsLA.com
[offers content](https://timesofsandiego.com/opinion/2014/10/20/sister-site-mynewsla-com-covers-greater-los-angeles/) through “official sources, wire services, the public relations community, and its own staff of five editors.” Its stories tend to focus on the local greater Los Angeles area issues. Headlines utilize minimally loaded language, and information is sourced through quotes rather than hyperlinks. Such as here [“Mayor Eric Garcetti Unveils Proposed $11.8 Billion Budget,” ](https://mynewsla.com/crime/2022/04/20/mayor-eric-garcetti-unveils-proposed-11-8-billion-budget-3/) [ “VP Harris to Speak at LA Fundraiser for Democratic National Committee,”](https://mynewsla.com/government/2022/04/18/vp-harris-to-speak-at-la-fundraiser-for-democratic-national-committee/) and [“Conservative Group Seeks Records Related to UCLA Law Professor.”](https://mynewsla.com/education/2022/02/17/conservative-group-seeks-records-related-to-ucla-law-professor-2/)
Editorially, MyNewsLA.com does not provide opinion content. Story selection is balanced and straightforward in reporting with minimal editorializing. In general, they are a low-biased website that publishes straight news stories.
