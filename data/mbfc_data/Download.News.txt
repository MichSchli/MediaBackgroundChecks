History
Download.News is a news aggregation website that relies exclusively on right-wing, questionable, and conspiracy sources. The website entirely lacks transparency as it does not disclose that the site is owned by conspiracy theorist
[Alex Jones of Infowars](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/infowars-alex-jones/). [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Although not stated, the website is owned by Alex Jones or one of his companies. Donations and membership fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Download.News aggregates conservative news from poor sources. The website lists the sources they utilize as
[INFOWARS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/infowars-alex-jones/), [War Room](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/war-room-steve-bannon-bias/), [Natural News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/natural-news/), [Expose-News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-daily-expose/), [One America News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/one-america-news-network/), [Red Voice Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/red-voice-media/), [The Post Millennial](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-post-millennial/), [Human Events](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/human-events/), Valiant News, [National File](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/national-file/), [Frank Speech](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/frank-speech-mike-lindell/), [World Net Daily](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/world-net-daily-wnd/), [Life Site News,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/life-site-news/) [RSBN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/right-side-broadcasting-network-rsbn/), [The Epoch Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-epoch-times/), [Louder with Crowder](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/louder-with-crowder/), [Steve Quayle Brighteon](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/brighteon/), [Daily Clout](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/dailyclout-bias/), [The New American](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-new-american/), [Information Liberation](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/information-liberation/), and [Next News Network](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/next-news-network/).
Editorially all aggregated content favors a conservative perspective and routinely promotes conspiracy theories and pseudoscience, and makes false claims. This source lacks credibility.
