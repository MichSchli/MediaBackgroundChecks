History
We Got This Covered is an online news website that focuses on movies, TV shows, gaming, and celebrities. The website covers a wide range of topics, including news, reviews, and spoilers. It aims to provide “all the latest news, trailers, & reviews for movies, TV, celebrities, Marvel, Netflix, anime, and more.”
[Read our profile on Australian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/australia-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
We Got This Covered is owned by Sydney, Australia-based
[Gamurs](https://wegotthiscovered.com/aboutus/), an esports media and entertainment publisher. Revenue is generated through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
We Got This Covered offers a variety of articles that cover both news and entertainment. Articles and headlines contain loaded emotional wording such as this:
[An utterly abysmal supernatural serial killer thriller fully deserving of its 0% Rotten Tomatoes score pulls off one last job on streaming](https://wegotthiscovered.com/movies/an-utterly-abysmal-supernatural-serial-killer-thriller-fully-deserving-of-its-0-rotten-tomatoes-score-pulls-off-one-last-job-on-streaming/). Although they are generally factual, the site does delve into speculative content, especially regarding upcoming movies and TV shows.
Regarding the editorial perspectives of We Got This Covered, the website focuses more on entertainment rather than political or social issues, maintaining a neutral stance in its reporting.
