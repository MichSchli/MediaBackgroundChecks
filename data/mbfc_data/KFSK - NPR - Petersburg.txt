History
KFSK – NPR – Petersburg is an NPR FM radio station (100.9) based in Petersburg, Alaska, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KFSK – NPR – Petersburg is owned and broadcast by Narrows Broadcasting Corp. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
