History
[Founded in 2020](https://whois.domaintools.com/greatawakening.win), [GreatAwakening.win](https://greatawakening.win/), or Great Awakening is a platform hosting a range of content from general discussions to specific political and social topics. As a community-driven site, it emphasizes user-generated content and discussions. The site aims to “red-pill normies,” indicating a goal to enlighten people about their perceived truth. It identifies as the public face of the [QAnon](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/qanon-pub/) movement, a group of anonymous conspiracy theorists who believe in a deep-state conspiracy against [President Trump](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/donald-trump-republican-presidential-candidate-bias-profile/). The site’s location and origins are unspecified. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website completely lacks transparency, as ownership and funding details are not disclosed.
