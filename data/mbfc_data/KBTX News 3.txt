History
Founded in 1957, KBTX is a television station in Bryan, TX that serves the Waco – Temple – Bryan television market. The station runs programming from the CBS network. Kbtx.com is the associated website for the news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.

Funded by / Ownership
KBTX News 3 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KBTX News 3 provides news coverage for Bryan, TX, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Dozens of shots fired, two bystanders injured in Cameron shooting](https://www.kbtx.com/2021/04/26/dozens-of-shots-fired-two-bystanders-injured-in-cameron-shooting/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KBTX-TV does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
