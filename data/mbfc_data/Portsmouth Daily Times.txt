History
The Portsmouth Daily Times is a newspaper based in Scioto County, Ohio, serving Scioto County and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at portsmouth-dailytimes.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Portsmouth Daily Times and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Portsmouth Daily Times publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[SCCTC completes community service projects](https://www.portsmouth-dailytimes.com/news/73935/scctc-completes-community-service-projects). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Portsmouth Daily Times leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
