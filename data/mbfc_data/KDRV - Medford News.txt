History
Founded in 1984, KDRV – Medford News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Medford, Oregon, United States. KDRV.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Medford area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns KDRV – Medford News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KDRV – Medford News publishes news for the Medford city and surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Overnight stabbing in Medford, suspect on the loose](https://www.kdrv.com/news/top-stories/overnight-stabbing-in-medford-suspect-on-the-loose/article_0ba1eb80-db54-11ec-9681-1b0ca536c7bc.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
