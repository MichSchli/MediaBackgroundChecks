History
The domain
[Abort73.com](http://whois.domaintools.com/abort73.com) was first purchased in 2003, however, the website shows an original copyright date of 2005. According to their about page “Abort73.com is part of Loxafamosity Ministries, Inc. (LMI), a non-profit 501(c)3 Christian education corporation. Abort73.com is an online resource designed to creatively and comprehensively educate people about the injustice of abortion, and provide them with simple tools to help pass that education along to others.” The founder and director of the organization are [Michael Spielman.](http://www.facebook.com/people/Michael-Spielman/507189774) [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Abort73 is owned by the Loxafamosity Ministries, Inc., a non-profit 501(c)3 Christian education corporation. According to
[Guidestar](https://www.guidestar.org/profile/52-2400448), Loxafamosity is an advocacy, pro-life, and anti-abortion group. The website is funded through advertising, donations, and an online store that [sells merchandise](https://abort73.com/gear/shirts/).
Analysis / Bias
In review, Abort73 publishes news and anti-abortion propaganda such as this:
[All abortion methods violate the most basic medical tenet: “Do No Harm.”](https://abort73.com/abortion/abortion_techniques/) This story is mainly sourced to Abort73 or Amazon affiliate book links (sales). The website also lists facts about abortion that are culled from mostly credible sources. Abort73 is not a fake news website nor a conspiracy site; they are simply a right-wing Christian site that advocates for anti-abortion policy.
