History
Founded in 1984 by
[Art Bell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Art_Bell), Coast to Coast AM is an American late-night radio talk show with a large audience that deals with various topics. Most frequently, the topics relate to either the paranormal or conspiracy theories. George Noory currently hosts the show. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The radio show is syndicated by
[Premiere Networks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Premiere_Networks), an American radio network. It is the largest syndication company in the United States. It is a wholly-owned subsidiary of [iHeartMedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IHeartMedia) and is headed by Julie Talbott. Coast to Coast AM is funded through advertising and a subscription model for different tiers of listening and web access.
Analysis / Bias
Coast to Coast AM deals with UFOs, strange occurrences, life after death, and other unexplained (and often inexplicable) phenomena. The Coast to Coast AM format consists of a combination of live callers and long-format interviews. The subject matter usually covers bizarre topics followed by personal stories about callers, junk science, pseudo-experts, and non-peer-reviewed scientists.
