History
Towleroad is a blog founded by Andy Towle and is based in New York City. Towle, who is gay, started his blog Towleroad in 2003. The blog
[focuses](https://www.towleroad.com/about-us/) on the gay lifestyle, pop culture, photography, politics, media, entertainment, technology, and travel. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not explain that Andy Towle is the owner, and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Towleroad website reports news and information from a left-leaning perspective, such as this
[Top Federal Election Official Schools Trump: ‘Counting Ballots — All of ‘Em — Is the Appropriate, Proper, and Very Legal Way to Determine Who Won](https://www.towleroad.com/2020/10/federal-election-official-trump/).’ This story is sourced from [Common Dreams](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/common-dreams/). Original reporting also features loaded language such as this [Trump Lies to Voters: Most States Let You Change Your Vote from Biden to Me – ‘Go Do It!’](https://www.towleroad.com/2020/10/trump-change-vote/) This story is properly sourced.
Generally, most news is factual and well-sourced with a strong left-leaning bias.
