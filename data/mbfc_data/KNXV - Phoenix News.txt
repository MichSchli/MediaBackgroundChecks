History
Founded in 1979, KNXV – Phoenix News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Phoenix, Arizona, United States. abc15.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KNXV – Phoenix News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KNXV – Phoenix News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Phoenix, Arizona, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Suspect shot, killed by police was a ‘violent criminal’ involved in Scottsdale sexual assault](https://www.abc15.com/news/region-northeast-valley/scottsdale/suspect-shot-killed-by-police-was-a-violent-criminal-involved-in-scottsdale-sexual-assault). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[AG Garland appoints special counsel after classified documents were found at Biden’s Delaware home](https://www.abc15.com/news/national/attorney-classified-documents-were-found-at-bidens-delaware-home). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
