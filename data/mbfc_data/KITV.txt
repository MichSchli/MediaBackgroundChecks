History
Founded in 1954, KITV is an ABC-affiliated television station licensed to Honolulu, Hawaii, United States. KITV.com, also known as Island News, is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Honolulu area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns KITV. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KITV.com publishes news for the Honolulu area through reporters and minimal bias, such as this
[Honolulu Police arrest 31-year-old man wanted for manslaughter.](https://www.kitv.com/story/42895322/honolulu-police-arrest-31yearold-man-wanted-for-manslaughter) Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, KITV.com does not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
