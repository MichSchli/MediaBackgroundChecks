History
Launched in 1938, WNYW – Fox 5 – New York is a
[Fox](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to New York City, New York, United States. fox5ny.com is the associated website covering news, sports, crime, and weather for the New York City area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WNYW – Fox 5 – New York is owned by Fox Television Stations, a subsidiary of Fox Corporation and, in turn, owned by Rupert Murdoch. Fox Corporation owns 30 Fox Television Stations. Revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WNYW – Fox 5 – New York reports the New York City area’s news, sports, crime, and weather. Local news is provided with minimal bias in wording, such as
[Man accused of making threats against NYC Jewish community charged](https://www.fox5ny.com/news/man-accused-of-making-threats-against-nyc-jewish-community-charged). Most news reports also contain video footage from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other Fox properties provide national and international news.
Editorially, they do not offer opinion content and cover politics with minimal bias, often citing the Associated Press for national politics. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
