History
Founded in 1956 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station, KOSA – CBS7 serves Odessa, Texas, and the surrounding area. CBS7.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KOSA – CBS7 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KOSA – CBS7 provides news coverage for Odessa, Texas, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Midland Mayor says Jenny Cudd has ‘suffered enough’, recommends no punishment for role in Jan. 6 riots](https://www.cbs7.com/2022/03/18/midland-mayor-says-jenny-cudd-has-suffered-enough-recommends-no-punishment-role-jan-6-riots/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KOSA – CBS7 does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
