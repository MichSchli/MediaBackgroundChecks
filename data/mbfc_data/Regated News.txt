History
[Founded in 2016](https://whois.domaintools.com/regated.com), Regated is a news and information site based in Buffalo, New York. According to their [about page](https://regated.com/about-us/) “Regated is one of the best web portals in the world for publishing news or stories for readers and as a result, will continue to be one of the most important news brands.” The website lacks transparency as they do not disclose owners or editors. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Regated does not disclose ownership and revenue is derived through Amazon Affiliate Advertising.
