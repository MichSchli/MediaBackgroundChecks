History
[Founded in 1976](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Modern_Healthcare), Modern Healthcare is a twice-monthly business publication targeting executives in the healthcare industry. According to their [about page](https://www.modernhealthcare.com/about-us), “Modern Healthcare is the industry’s leading source of healthcare business and policy news, research and information. We report on important healthcare events and trends as they happen, through our print magazine, websites, e-newsletters, mobile products, and events.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Modern Healthcare is owned by
[Crain’s](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/crains-detroit/) Communications, a 3rd generation family-owned business based in Detriot, Michigan. Advertising, subscriptions, and events generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Modern Healthcare magazine and website publishes news and commentary on the USA healthcare industry. They primarily focus on healthcare policy and the business aspects of the industry. Articles and headlines use minimally emotional wording, such as this
[Massachussetts OKs Optum’s $236 million not-for-profit Atrius Health deal](https://www.modernhealthcare.com/mergers-acquisitions/optums-236-million-atrius-health-deal-gets-massachusetts-approval). Articles are sourced adequately from credible media sources and organizations. National and international news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, they appropriately label opinion content and focus on issues related to healthcare, such as this
[It’s time to change the language physicians use in medical records](https://www.modernhealthcare.com/opinion-editorial/its-time-change-language-physicians-use-medical-records). They also frequently cover issues with Medicaid/Medicare, such as this [Commentary: Medicaid needs partners to smooth the off-ramp for millions of Americans](https://www.modernhealthcare.com/opinion-editorial/commentary-medicaid-needs-partners-smooth-ramp-millions-americans). Finally, their coverage of Covid-19 is evidence-based and [pro-science](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pro-science/). In general, Modern Healthcare provides fact-based news with minimal bias.
