History
[The San Diego Free Press](https://sandiegofreepress.org/about-the-san-diego-free-press/) is a news and commentary website that offers grassroots news and progressive viewpoints. It is a volunteer organization consisting of local writers, bloggers, community activists, and citizen journalists. They are associated with the OB Rag, which is an underground newspaper published between 1970 and 1975.
Funded by / Ownership
The San Diego Free Press is a volunteer organization that is funded through donations.
Analysis / Bias
San Diego Free Press covers state politics and issues like the economy, education, the environment, social justice, gay rights and reproductive rights. San Diego Free Press occasionally uses
[loaded words](https://sandiegofreepress.org/2017/08/congressman-duncan-hunters-take-on-trump-hes-our-asshole/) with a left bias in headlines/articles, but they consistently source their information factually.
