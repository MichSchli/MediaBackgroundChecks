History
[Founded in 1871](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Longview_News-Journal), the Longview News-Journal is a major paper for the city of Longview, Texas, and 11 surrounding counties in East Texas. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Longview News-Journal is owned by
[Texas Community Media LLC.](https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=208079875) They are funded through an advertising and subscription model.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Longview News-Journal covers local news using journalists and local writers. National/International news is republished from the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). Editorials use minimally loaded words such as this: [Editorial: Big increase in flu deaths makes vaccination vital](https://www.news-journal.com/opinion/editorial-big-increase-in-flu-deaths-makes-vaccination-vital/article_7cf6bbd4-cce5-11e8-9bc0-9b87973e363d.html) and are properly sourced. Further, some editorials are republished from the left-center [Houston Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/houston-chronicle/). Regarding political endorsements, they have endorsed both Democrats and Republicans, but certainly more Republicans in their recent history.
