History
Founded in 1906, The Brewton Standard is a newspaper published in Brewton, Alabama. brewtonstandard.com covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Brewton Standard is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Brewton Standard primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[City approves building demolition](https://www.brewtonstandard.com/2022/07/26/city-approves-building-demolition/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Brewton Standard labels their opinion section appropriately with more op-eds favoring the right. In general, the Brewton Standard is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
