History
[Healthcare Finance](http://www.healthcarefinancenews.com/about-healthcare-finance-news) is a news source for developments in the healthcare industry. Healthcare Finance is one of the core brands of HIMSS Media. The [Healthcare Information and Management Systems Society (HIMSS)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Healthcare_Information_and_Management_Systems_Society) is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to improving health care in quality, safety, cost-effectiveness, and access through the best use of information technology and management systems. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
HIMSS Media owns Healthcare Finance. Advertising generally generates revenue.
