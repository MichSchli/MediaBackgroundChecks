History
The Fayette Tribune is a newspaper published in Oak Hill, West Virginia. fayettetribune.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Fayette Tribune. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Fayette Tribune covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Oak Hill, WV, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Fayette County Commission sets LRA into motion](https://www.fayettetribune.com/news/fayette-county-commission-sets-lra-into-motion/article_5f210828-48f8-11ee-9e9d-d36c1f9f531f.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
