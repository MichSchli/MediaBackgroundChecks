History
The Kilgore News Herald is a daily newspaper independently published in Kilgore, Texas. The newspaper serves Kilgore and the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The newspaper is owned by
[M. Roberts Media](https://mrobertsmedia.com/), which publishes 6 newspapers in the eastern Texas region. The paper is funded through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Kilgore News Herald reports local news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Rusk County Sheriff warns of phone scam](https://www.kilgorenewsherald.com/news/rusk-county-sheriff-warns-of-phone-scam/article_c6dcb60c-3b60-11ed-b49e-4bd0d3e1a39c.html). National and world news comes almost exclusively from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Kilgore News Herald tends to lean left. In general, the Kilgore News Herald is factual, uses credible sources, and holds a slightly Left-leaning editorial bias.
