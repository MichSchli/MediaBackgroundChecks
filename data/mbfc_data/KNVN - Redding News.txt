History
Founded in 1985, KNVN- Redding News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Chico, California, United States. Actionnewsnow.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Chico-Redding area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns KNVN- Redding News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KNVN- Redding News publishes news for Redding and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[2 killed in plane crash at Oroville airport identified](https://www.actionnewsnow.com/news/local/2-killed-in-plane-crash-at-oroville-airport-identified/article_67e64c68-e367-11ec-ba50-6bbcfd88337e.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
