History
The Lanett Valley Times-News is a newspaper published in Lanett, Alabama. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.

Funded by / Ownership
The Lanett Valley Times-News is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Lanett Valley Times-News primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[One dead in West Point shooting at East 12th, MLK Street](https://www.valleytimes-news.com/2022/11/one-dead-in-west-point-shooting-at-east-12th-mlk-street/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Lanett Valley Times-News labels its opinion section appropriately, with more op-eds favoring the right. In general, Troy Messanger is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
