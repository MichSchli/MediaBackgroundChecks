History
The Jacksonville Progress is a newspaper published in Jacksonville, Texas. jacksonvilleprogress.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Jacksonville Progress. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Jacksonville Progress covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Jacksonville, Texas region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Austin Bank employees receive promotions](https://www.jacksonvilleprogress.com/news/local_news/austin-bank-employees-receive-promotions/article_d9080d00-ca48-11ed-b1ea-971773110a75.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
