History
News24.com is an English-language South African online news publication created in October 1998 by the multinational media company Naspers. News24 covers local and international news, politics, business, entertainment, technology, and sport.
For details on South African Government and Media, click here:
[South Africa Government and Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/south-africa-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
Media24 owns and publishes News24.com. They publish several magazines, newspapers, and online publications in South Africa. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
News24.com covers local and international news, business, entertainment, sports, technology, and politics. Articles and headlines pertaining to local news use minimal biased wording, such as this
[Man arrested in connection with mass killing in Khayelitsha](https://www.news24.com/news24/southafrica/news/man-arrested-in-connection-with-mass-killing-in-khayelitsha-20220327). World News is usually republished from large credible media sources such as [AFP](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/afp-agence-france-presse/), [Bloomberg](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bloomberg/), [Al Jazeera](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/al-jazeera/), and [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/).
Editorially, News24.com tends to cover both left and right-leaning perspectives, with slightly more favoring the right. According to the
[Reuters Institute](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/), 83% of South Africans trust News24 vs. 8% who do not, ranking them the #1 most trusted [South African media source](https://reutersinstitute.politics.ox.ac.uk/digital-news-report/2021/south-africa). News24.com publishes fact-based news on the right side of least biased.
