History
The Daily Herald is a daily newspaper
[founded in 1871](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daily_Herald_(Arlington_Heights,_Illinois)), based in Arlington Heights, Illinois. The current editor is John Lampinenis. The Daily Herald covers both international and national news as well as news local to its [circulation area](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daily_Herald_(Arlington_Heights)) (Chicago and Suburbs).
The Daily Herald is based in the United States. Read our profile on
[USA government influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by Paddock Publications. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Daily Herald uses credible sources in their reporting; for example, they often republish articles from the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) under their News category: [“2 former presidents break with tradition to denounce Trump](http://www.dailyherald.com/article/20171021/news/310219975/).” The Daily Herald states their editorial position as: “The institutional philosophy of our editorial board is fiscally conservative, socially moderate or occasionally even progressive.”
