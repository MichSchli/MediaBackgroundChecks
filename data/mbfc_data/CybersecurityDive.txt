History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, CybersecurityDive is an online news platform that focuses on the construction industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.cybersecuritydive.com/about/), “Cybersecurity Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping cybersecurity.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates CybersecurityDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and CybersecurityDive. CybersecurityDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
CybersecurityDive publishes news and information related to the cybersecurity industry. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[Ivanti Connect Secure attacks part of deliberate espionage operation](https://www.cybersecuritydive.com/news/ivanti-connect-secure-espionage/704475/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like this
[White House warns US of possible Russian cyberattack linked to Ukraine invasion](https://www.cybersecuritydive.com/news/white-house-warns-russian-cyberattack-ukraine/620755/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
