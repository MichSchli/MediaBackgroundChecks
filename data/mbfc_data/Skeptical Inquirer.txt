History
Founded in 1976, Skeptical Inquirer is a bimonthly American magazine published by the
[Committee for Skeptical Inquiry (CSI)](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/committee-for-skeptical-inquiry/) with the subtitle: The Magazine for Science and Reason. According to their [about pa](https://skepticalinquirer.org/about/) [ge](https://skepticalinquirer.org/about/) they “promote scientific inquiry, critical investigation, and the use of reason in examining controversial and extraordinary claims.” [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Skeptical Inquirer is owned by the
[Committee for Skeptical Inquiry (CSI)](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/committee-for-skeptical-inquiry/) and the [Center for Inquiry](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-inquiry/). Revenue is derived through subscription fees, donations, and a store that sells back issues of the magazine.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the magazine and website covers topics such as debunking the paranormal as well as other pseudosciences. For example, they debunk the
[Autism-vaccine](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-autism-vaccine-link/) link here: [The Scientific Frauds Underlying The False MMR Vaccine–Autism Link](https://skepticalinquirer.org/2020/11/the-scientific-frauds-underlying-the-false-mmr-vaccine-autism-link/). This article is very well-sourced from numerous credible medical journals including [BMJ](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-bmj-british-medical-journal/). Other topics [debunked](https://skepticalinquirer.org/2020/03/how-good-are-past-predictions-of-global-warming/) are [Climate Change Denialism](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/) [and](https://skepticalinquirer.org/2014/07/no-health-risks-from-gmos/) the [Dangers of GMOs](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-gmo-dangers/). In general, this is a pro-science source that primarily debunks pseudoscience and even conspiracy theories.
