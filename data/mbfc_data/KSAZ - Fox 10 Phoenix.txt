History
[Launched in 1957](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KTVU), KSAZ – Fox 10 Phoenix is a [Fox](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Phoenix, Arizona, United States, and serving the Phoenix Area. fox10phoenix.com is the associated website covering news, sports, crime, and weather for the Phoenix area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KSAZ – Fox 10 Phoenix is owned by Fox Television Stations, a subsidiary of Fox Corporation and, in turn, owned by Rupert Murdoch. Fox Corporation owns 30 Fox Television Stations. Revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KSAZ – Fox 10 Phoenix reports news, sports, crime, and weather for the Phoenix area. Local news is provided with minimal bias in wording, such as
[Police at scene of officer-involved shooting in north Phoenix](https://www.fox10phoenix.com/news/police-at-scene-of-shooting-investigation-in-north-phoenix). Most news reports also contain video footage from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other Fox properties provide national and international news.
Editorially, they do not offer opinion content, and they cover politics with minimal bias, often citing the Associated Press for national politics. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
