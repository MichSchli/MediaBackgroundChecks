History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson,
[AgricultureDive](https://www.fooddive.com/about/) is an online news platform that focuses on the agriculture industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its [about page](https://www.agriculturedive.com/about/), “Agriculture Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping the agriculture space. The newsletter and website cover topics such as technology, environmental regulations, labor, trade, manufacturing, and more.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates AgricultureDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BankingDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and CybersecurityDive. AgricultureDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
AgricultureDive publishes news and information related to the agriculture industry. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[USDA strengthens animal welfare requirements for organic label](https://www.agriculturedive.com/news/usda-organic-label-animal-welfare-requirements/697927/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like this
[Biden admin takes steps to protect workers from extreme heat](https://www.agriculturedive.com/news/biden-extreme-heat-osha-farm-workers/689482/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
