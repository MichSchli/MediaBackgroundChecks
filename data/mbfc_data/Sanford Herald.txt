History
The Sanford Herald is a newspaper published in Sanford, North Carolina. sanfordherald.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Paxton Media Group,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paxton_Media_Group) which owns several right-leaning newspapers across the USA, such as the [Elkhart Truth](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-elkhart-truth/). Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Sanford Herald reports national news by republishing articles from credible newsagents such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). They report local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias using neutral headlines such as [City seeks input on McNeil Park](https://www.sanfordherald.com/archives/city-seeks-input-on-mcneil-park/article_83118204-1d0d-5c65-b33f-935393c78572.html). All information is properly sourced.
Editorially, there is a reasonable balance between left and right-leaning opinions, with slightly more favoring the right. In general, the paper is fact-based and holds a slight right-leaning editorial bias.
