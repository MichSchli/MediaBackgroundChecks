History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, CioDive is an online news platform that focuses on the IT industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.ciodive.com/about/), “CIO Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping IT” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates CioDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and CybersecurityDive. CioDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
CioDive publishes news and information related to the IT Indistry. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[Accenture’s AI business booms as enterprises shift to deployment mode](https://www.ciodive.com/news/Accenture-generative-AI-adoption-data-strategy-McDonalds/703016/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like this
[White House warns US of possible Russian cyberattack linked to Ukraine invasion](https://www.ciodive.com/news/white-house-warns-russian-cyberattack-ukraine/620824/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
