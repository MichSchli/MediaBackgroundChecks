History
Founded in 1955, KCCI is a CBS-affiliated television station licensed to Des Moines, Iowa, United States. The associated website covering news, sports, weather, and crime is kcci.com.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KCCI is owned by Hearst Communications, which owns newspapers, magazines, television channels, and television stations, including the
[San Francisco Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-francisco-chronicle/), the [Houston Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/houston-chronicle/), and [Esquire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/esquire-magazine/). According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Hearst donated [75% to Republican candidates](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/recipients?id=D000034391) and causes in 2020. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KCCI.com reports on news, crime, sports, weather, and entertainment for the greater Des Moines, Iowa area. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias in wording such as this
[Developing: Doug Jensen no longer listed as Polk County Jail inmate.](https://www.kcci.com/article/doug-jensen-no-longer-in-polk-county-jail-custody-washington-dc-us-capitol-riots/35333473) A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/), [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and other Hearst affiliates typically cover the national news.
Editorially, KCCI.com has a section dedicated to editorials that are not overtly biased. They also report on national politics with minimal bias as the primary source is usually the Associated Press. In general, the news is reported factually with minimal editorializing.
