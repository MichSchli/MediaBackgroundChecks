History
[Founded in 1922](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Fresno_Bee), The Fresno Bee is a daily newspaper serving Fresno, California, and surrounding counties in that U.S. state’s San Joaquin Valley. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Fresno Bee is owned by
[Chatham Asset Management,](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2018-08-30/behind-trump-s-tabloid-king-a-connected-and-flush-hedge-fund) a New Jersey-based hedge fund with a significant equity stake in Postmedia and majority ownership of American Media Inc., which owns the [National Enquirer](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/national-enquirer/). [David Pecker](https://www.thestar.com/business/2016/10/19/executive-from-national-enquirer-parent-joins-postmedia-board.html), who owns American Media, Inc., joined the Postmedia board and then [resigned ](https://www.canadalandshow.com/postmedia-david-pecker-donald-trump/)from the board of Postmedia in 2018 due to his involvement in hush payments on behalf of his “friends,” including former President Trump and Harvey Weinstein. Advertising and subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Fresno Bee covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Coronavirus: Fresno mayor extends shelter-in-place order for two more weeks](https://www.fresnobee.com/news/coronavirus/article241524601.html#storylink=bignews_main), and this [Fresno Fairgrounds will be site of makeshift hospital with 250 beds to treat coronavirus](https://www.fresnobee.com/news/coronavirus/article241532196.html). National and international reporting comes from a combination of other Chatham-owned papers and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, The Fresno Bee has
[historically](https://noahveltman.com/endorsements/) endorsed Democratic Presidential Candidates such as Hillary Clinton in 2016. A review of editorials indicates that more favor the left, such as this [Go to restaurants during coronavirus outbreak? Ignore Devin Nunes’ dangerous advice](https://www.fresnobee.com/opinion/editorials/article241216106.html#storylink=mainstage). In general, the news is reported factually with a left-leaning editorial bias.
