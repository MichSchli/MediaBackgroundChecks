History
Miami County Republic is a newspaper published in Paola, Kansas. The associated website is Republic-online.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns the Miami County Republic and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Miami County Republic website publishes news for the greater Paola, Kansas area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:
[Paola USD 368 introduces new teachers](https://www.republic-online.com/education/paola-usd-368-introduces-new-teachers/article_ece04b84-2251-11ed-b008-6fcb35a3c83a.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other large, credible media outlets cover national and international news.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds while political news coverage is balanced. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
