History
Coda Story is an online news platform that focuses on investigative journalism and in-depth reporting on global issues, including politics, human rights, technology, and social justice. The website was launched in 2015. According to its
[about page](https://www.codastory.com/about/), “Coda Story reports on major currents shaping our world from disinformation to authoritarian technologies to the war on science.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Coda Story is owned and published by
[Coda Media](https://www.codastory.com/stayonthestory/about-us/), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit media company. Donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The website covers a wide range of topics from a global perspective, often focusing on issues related to authoritarianism, disinformation, human rights, and technology. The articles provide in-depth analysis and investigative reporting.
Articles and headlines often contain loaded emotional wording, such as this
[Italy’s pro-choice gynecologists reel from post-Roe shockwaves](https://www.codastory.com/disinformation/traditional-values/abortion-italy-roe-wade-meloni-conservative/). This story is appropriately sourced from credible outlets like [Bloomberg](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bloomberg/), [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/), and [Human Rights Watch](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/human-rights-watch/).
Editorially, Coda Story holds progressive views as they tend to highlight human rights abuses, climate change, and the impact of disinformation on society. For example, they frequently report negatively on conservatives who they perceive as threatening human rights, such as this
[Ahead of elections, Poland’s conservative media fires up anti-LGBTQ propaganda](https://www.codastory.com/disinformation/information-war/poland-elections-lgbtq/). The website also supports pro-choice advocates: [With help from American activists, Ukrainian women are being misled about abortion](https://www.codastory.com/disinformation/information-war/abortion-ukraine/), and reports negatively on [Florida Governor](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/florida-governor-ron-desantis-political-bias-profile/) Ron Desantis [Florida’s ban on transgender care pushes doctors to leave the state](https://www.codastory.com/waronscience/florida-doctors-transgender-care/). In general, Coda Story is fact-based and holds strongly left-leaning editorial biases.
