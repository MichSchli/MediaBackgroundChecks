History
West K News is a newspaper published in Kelowna, British Columbia, Canada. The website westknews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Kelowna, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns West K News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
West K News covers news for Kelowna and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Kelowna council spotlights names for Community Task Force on Performing Arts](https://www.westknews.com/local-news/kelowna-council-spotlights-names-for-community-task-force-on-performing-arts-6528568). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, West K News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
