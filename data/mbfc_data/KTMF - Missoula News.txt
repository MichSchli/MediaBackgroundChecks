History
KTMF – Missoula News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/) and [FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated TV station licensed to Missoula, Montana. The website nonstoplocal.com/missoula/ provides local news, sports, weather, crime, and business news. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTMF – Missoula News is owned by the
[Cowles Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cowles_Company), which owns several small TV stations and manufacturing companies in the Pacific Northwest. Revenue is derived through subscriptions, advertising, and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
KTMF – Missoula News covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Sheriff IDs Spokane Valley man who died after incident on Brooks St. in Missoula](https://www.nonstoplocal.com/missoula-kalispell/news/missoula/sheriff-ids-spokane-valley-man-who-died-after-incident-on-brooks-st-in-missoula/article_19189efc-ddf3-5025-9bbb-7ab9be85d82d.html). Some stories are accompanied by videos from the TV news station. National and international news is derived from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, KTMF – Missoula News does not provide opinion content. They maintain a neutral tone when reporting on politics, such as this
[Biden Tests Negative For Covid](https://www.nonstoplocal.com/tri-cities-yakima/news/biden-tests-negative-for-covid/article_55cd5f1a-47b9-5932-80ab-4a78c1da787a.html). In general, they are low-biased and fact-based in reporting.
