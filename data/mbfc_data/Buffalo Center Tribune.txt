History
The Buffalo Center Tribune is a newspaper published in Buffalo Center, Indiana, United States. The paper covers news, sports, lifestyle, and opinion for Buffalo Center and the surrounding area. thebuffalocentertribune.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Mid America Publishing owns and publishes the Buffalo Center Tribune. Mid America Publishing publishes
[several community newspapers](https://www.midampublishing.com/content/mid-america-publications) throughout Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Buffalo Center Tribune reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Buffalo Center, Indiana region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[MercyOne plans to close Buffalo Center clinic](http://www.thebuffalocentertribune.com/content/mercyone-plans-close-buffalo-center-clinic). The newspaper and website do not publish national news, focusing on local news reporting.
Editorially, the Buffalo Center Tribune reasonably represents both sides. A review of op-eds demonstrates a reasonably low bias; however, slightly more op-eds favor the right. While more op-eds favor the right, liberal perspectives have fair representation; therefore, they earn a least biased rating on the right side of the scale. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
