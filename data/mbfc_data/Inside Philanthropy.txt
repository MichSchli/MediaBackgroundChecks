History
Inside Philanthropy is a news website about large philanthropic foundations and wealthy donors, created by
[David Callahan in 2013](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inside_Philanthropy). It aims to increase oversight and transparency in large-scale philanthropy. The site has been noted for naming wealthy people who Inside Philanthropy considers as scoring relatively poorly on “relative generosity”, or percentage of wealth that a person has given away, even if they still give out a comparatively large amount of money in absolute terms. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Inside Philanthropy is owned by David Callahan. Revenue is derived through subscription fees and advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Inside Philanthropy presents news and information with a left-center bias as most issues covered
[favor the left](https://www.insidephilanthropy.com/home/2017/9/28/as-dicaprios-foundation-grows-new-grants-to-solar-lions-and-more). Inside Philanthropy properly sources their information and tends to use minimal loaded words. They also have a [negative view](https://www.insidephilanthropy.com/home/2016/11/9/philanthropy-in-the-age-of-trump-five-predictions) of former President Donald Trump and the effect he will have on philanthropy.
