History
[Founded in 1900](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinook_Observer), The Chinook Observer is a weekly newspaper serving the Long Beach Peninsula of Washington state. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Chinook Observer is owned by
[EO Media Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/East_Oregonian) and is funded through a subscription and advertising model.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Chinook Observer only reports local news with
[minimal bias](http://www.chinookobserver.com/co/local-news/20180921/body-found-in-water-near-astoria-bridge). All information is properly sourced as well. [Editorially](http://www.chinookobserver.com/co/editorials), more opinions lean left.
