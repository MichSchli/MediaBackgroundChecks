History
Founded in 1954, KIMT – Mason City News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Mason City, Iowa, United States. KIMT.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Mason City area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns KIMT – Mason City News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KIMT – Mason City News publishes news for the Mason City and surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Three Des Moines students injured in Post-Prom shootout early Sunday morning](https://www.kimt.com/news/iowa/three-des-moines-students-injured-in-post-prom-shootout-early-sunday-morning/article_0455ac8d-7a9c-5f6b-a728-f9b54261e7b4.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
