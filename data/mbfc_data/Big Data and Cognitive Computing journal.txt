History
Big Data and Cognitive Computing Journal is published by
[MDPI (Multidisciplinary Digital Publishing Institute)](https://www.mdpi.com/about/history), formerly Molecular Diversity Preservation International, an open-access journal publisher based in Basel, Switzerland. Big Data and Cognitive Computing is an international, [peer-reviewed](https://www.mdpi.com/editorial_process), open access journal on big data and cognitive computing published quarterly online by MDPI. MDPI publishes over [400 journals.](https://www.mdpi.com/about/journals) [Read our profile on Swiss Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/switzerland-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of Big Data and Cognitive Computing Journal is
[MDPI AG](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mdpi-multidisciplinary-digital-publishing-institute-bias/), which Shu-Kun Lin owns. Here is the list of [the Management Team.](https://www.mdpi.com/about/team) According to [“MDPI Open Access Information and Policy.”](https://www.mdpi.com/openaccess) MDPI articles and all its journals are open access where authors pay a one-time Article Processing Charge (APC) to cover the costs of peer review administration and management; here is [“General Information on MDPI’s Article Processing Charges (APC).”](https://www.mdpi.com/apc)
Analysis / Bias
Big Data and Cognitive Computing Journal covers the study of big data and cognitive computing. Studies are published via open access, such as this:
[Visual Explanations of Differentiable Greedy Model Predictions on the Influence Maximization Problem](https://www.mdpi.com/2504-2289/7/3/149). Like all others reviewed, this study is Pro-Science, well-researched and low-biased.
While Big Data and Cognitive Computing Journal has not failed a fact-check, its publisher has faced criticism as an open-access predatory journal. Read our full review on
[MDPI](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mdpi-multidisciplinary-digital-publishing-institute-bias/).
