History
Powell River Peak is an English-language newspaper in Powell River, British Columbia, Canada. prpeak.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Powell River Peak. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
Powell River Peak publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Powell River, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Cariboo-Prince George MP welcomes NDP bill to protect first responders](https://www.prpeak.com/highlights/cariboo-prince-george-mp-welcomes-ndp-bill-to-protect-first-responders-7249656). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Powell River Peak’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Monique Keiran: Canada Day was ‘Humiliation Day’ for Chinese immigrants](https://www.piquenewsmagazine.com/opinion/monique-keiran-canada-day-was-humiliation-day-for-chinese-immigrants-7226248). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
