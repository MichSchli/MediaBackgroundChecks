History
The Fairborn Daily Herald is a newspaper based in Fairborn, Ohio, serving Fairborn and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at fairborndailyherald.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Fairborn Daily Herald and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Fairborn Daily Herald publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[County to bring reliable internet to thousands](https://www.fairborndailyherald.com/news/48839/county-to-bring-reliable-internet-to-thousands). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Fairborn Daily Herald leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
