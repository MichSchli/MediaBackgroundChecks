History
Founded in 1879, The Daily Chronicle is a newspaper that covers DeKalb County in northern Illinois. daily-chronicle.com is the website covering news, crime, sports, and business for the DeKalb area.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Daily Chronicle is owned by
[Shaw Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shaw_Media_(United_States)), which is an American, Dixon, Illinois-based publisher of about 80 newspapers and news websites in Illinois and Iowa. Revenue is derived from sponsored content, advertising, and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, local news is published through journalists and with minimal bias, such as this:
[Sycamore City Council to discuss pot, building codes at meeting Monday](https://www.daily-chronicle.com/2019/09/13/sycamore-city-council-to-discuss-pot-building-codes-at-meeting-monday/ae1syn9/). National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other wire services.
Editorially, they have a page dedicated to
[op-eds](https://www.daily-chronicle.com/opinion/editorials/) that are basically balanced in content. Generally, news reporting is factual and low-biased, while op-eds are reasonably balanced.
