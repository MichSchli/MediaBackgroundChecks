History
Founded in 1996,
[Public News Service (PNS)](https://www.publicnewsservice.org/about.php) is a nonprofit organization that provides free content to engage, educate and advocate for the public interest. According to their [about page](https://www.publicnewsservice.org/about.php), PNS focuses on “critical issues that receive too little attention and help lift up marginalized voices that too often go unheard.” PNS is based in Boulder, Colorado, U.S. The founder and CEO is journalist [Lark Corbeil](https://www.publicnewsservice.org/producers.php?id=6).
Funded by / Ownership
[Public News Service (PNS)](https://www.publicnewsservice.org/about/bestpractices.php) is a family and employee-owned certified B Corp (Benefit Corporation) with large portions of revenue coming from some media contributions, grants, gifts, and memberships from individuals, NGOs, foundations, and social enterprises.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Public News Service (PNS) uses emotionally loaded headlines such as this
[“Report: Trump Campaign Linked to COVID Misinformation](https://www.publicnewsservice.org/2020-09-09/health-issues/report-trump-campaign-linked-to-covid-misinformation/a71398-1) and ‘ [More People Are Poor Than the Poverty Rate Says'”](https://www.publicnewsservice.org/2022-09-14/poverty/more-people-are-poor-than-the-poverty-rate-says/a80607-1) and [“NY Prepares Disadvantaged Communities for Climate Change Effects.”](https://www.publicnewsservice.org/2022-09-12/climate-change-air-quality/ny-prepares-disadvantaged-communities-for-climate-change-effects/a80582-1)
Public News Service (PNS) utilizes credible sources such as
[oacaa.org](https://oacaa.org/), [The Washington Spectator,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-washington-spectator/) annenbergpublicpolicycenter.org, uhc.com, [Whitehouse.gov](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/whitehouse-gov/), and pro-science agencies such as the [National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/national-oceanic-and-atmospheric-administration-noaa/). In general, reporting is consistently left-leaning and factual. Moreover, news coverage, on the whole, is favored by a liberal audience, which indicates content and story selection tend to lean left to target their primary demographic.
