History
Founded in 1952, WREX – Rockford News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Rockford, Illinois, United States. wrex.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Rockford area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WREX – Rockford News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WREX – Rockford News publishes news for Rockford and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as the
[Four hospitalized after Scott’s Law crash near Chicago early Sunday morning](https://www.wrex.com/news/illinois-news/four-hospitalized-after-scotts-law-crash-near-chicago-early-sunday-morning/article_a270663e-3218-11ed-ac33-db41f150b31a.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
