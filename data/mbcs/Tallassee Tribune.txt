History
The Tallassee Tribune is a newspaper published in Alexander City, Alabama. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.

Funded by / Ownership
The Tallassee Tribune is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
