History
The Burns Lake Lakes District News is a newspaper published in Burns Lake, British Columbia, Canada. The website burnlakelakesdistrictnews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Burns Lake area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Burns Lake Lakes District News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Burns Lake Lakes District News covers news for Burns Lake and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Report finds RCMP actions ‘unreasonable’ in 2019 fatal B.C. police shooting](https://www.burnslakelakesdistrictnews.com/news/report-finds-rcmp-actions-unreasonable-in-2019-fatal-b-c-police-shooting/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Burns Lake Lakes District News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
