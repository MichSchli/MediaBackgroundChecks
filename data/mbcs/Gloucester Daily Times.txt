History
The Gloucester Daily Times is a newspaper published in Gloucester, Massachusetts. gloucestertimes.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Gloucester Daily Times. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Gloucester Daily Times covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Gloucester, Massachusettes region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Essex seeks bids for Conomo Point repairs](https://www.gloucestertimes.com/news/local_news/essex-seeks-bids-for-conomo-point-repairs/article_a6bbd3c2-b9f4-11ed-8f4e-7b1e5501f4be.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
