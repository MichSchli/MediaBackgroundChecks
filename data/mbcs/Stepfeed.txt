History
Launched in 2015,
[StepFeed](https://stepfeed.com/) is a Dubai-based magazine gossip tabloid focused on delivering news coverage, entertainment, and celebrity gossip to Arab millennials. The Step Group is a partner with the [DMS group](http://aetoswire.com/news/dms-signs-partnership-with-step-group/en), which is the official digital arm of [Choueiri Group](http://www.choueirigroup.com/). [Chaker Khazaal](https://www.huffingtonpost.com/author/chaker-khazaal) is the editor-in-chief for StepFeed and [YallaFeed.com](http://YallaFeed.com). According to [Reporters without borders,](https://rsf.org/en/united-arab-emirates) UAE ranked 131 in the 2021 World Press Freedom Index, and The United Arab Emirates regularly participates in the online surveillance of journalists.

Analysis / Bias
In review, StepFeed publishes stories with sensational headlines such as “
[Egyptian singer arrested for music video with a banana](https://stepfeed.com/egyptian-singer-arrested-for-music-video-with-a-banana-9443),” “ [The average Arab breast size is either ‘A or B cup’… here’s a breakdown.”](https://stepfeed.com/the-average-arab-breast-size-is-either-a-or-b-cup-here-s-a-breakdown-6060) However, they utilize credible sources in their [political news](https://stepfeed.com/israelis-don-t-want-peace-with-palestinians-former-us-secretary-of-state-says-8383) such as [Newsweek](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/newsweek/), [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/), [Times of Israel,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/times-of-israel/) and occasionally factually mixed sources such as [RT news](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/rt-news/), [The Sun](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-sun/), and [Daily Mail](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-mail/). When covering political news stories, StepFeed slightly favors the left through story selection and wording.
StepFeed has moved to a Newsletter format that we are choosing not to subscribe to. This is a low-confidence review from years past.
