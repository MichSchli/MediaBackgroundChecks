History
The Gardner News is a newspaper published in Gardner, Kansas. gardnernews.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the Gardner Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Gardner News is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Gardner News covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Gardner, Kansas area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[NEW COUNCIL MEMBER MARK WIEHN APPOINTED TO GARDNER CITY COUNCIL](https://gardnernews.com/2022/08/19/new-council-member-mark-wiehn-appointed-to-gardner-city-council/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
