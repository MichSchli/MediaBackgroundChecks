History
[Founded in 2018](http://whois.domaintools.com/dailyworldupdate.us) by Christopher Blair, Daily World Update is satire website from the creator of [America’s Last Line Of Defense](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-resistance-the-last-line-of-defense/). The purpose of the website it to troll “gullible conservatives” into sharing these satirical stories as truth. [Christopher Blair](https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/resources/idt-sh/the_godfather_of_fake_news) has been called the “Godfather of Fake news.” [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Daily World Update is owned by Christopher Blair and is funded through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
According to their
[about page](https://dailyworldupdate.us/about/): “DailyWorldUpdate.us is a subsidiary of the “America’s Last Line of Defense” network of parody, satire, and tomfoolery, or as [Snopes](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/snopes/) calls it: Junk News. Because they’re too stupid to understand what “satire” means.” In general, Daily World Update exclusively pokes fun at conservatives with a tagline that reads “Satire for Flat Earthers, Trumpsters and Y’all Qaeda.” Here is a sample satire article that is meant to get gullible conservatives to share on social media: [Malia Obama Arrested Again, Complete Media Blackout](https://dailyworldupdate.us/malia-obama-arrest/).
