History
The Norman Transcript is a newspaper published in Norman, Oklahoma. normantranscript.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Norman Transcript. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Norman Transcript covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Norman, Oklahoma, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Oklahoma turnpike board greenlights first ACCESS turnpike expansion project](https://www.normantranscript.com/news/oklahoma-turnpike-board-greenlights-first-access-turnpike-expansion-project/article_b6d94170-9abf-11ee-9bb9-03547b4aa6df.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
