History
Founded in 1956 and an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station, KSPR – KY3 serves Springfield, Missouri, and the surrounding area. Ky3.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KSPR – KY3 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KSPR – KY3 provides news coverage for Springfield, Missouri, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Driver escapes concrete truck fire in Willard, Mo.](https://www.ky3.com/2021/06/23/driver-escapes-concrete-truck-fire-willard-mo/) The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KSPR – KY3 does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
