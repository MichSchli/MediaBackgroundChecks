History
[Launched in May 2021](https://whois.domaintools.com/whatregistrater.com), WhatRegistrater.com is an imposter website designed to look like [Fox News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/). Links on the website direct to another website that sells CBD oil. The website completely lacks transparency.
Funded by / Ownership
WhatRegistrater.com does not disclose ownership. Revenue is derived through serving as an imposter scam site that links to a CBD store.
Analysis / Bias
WhatRegistrater.com currently consists of one page that mirrors the
[Fox News website](https://whatregistrater.com/xq289/?fbclid=IwAR0XX70WfatZLRGM2PdQxZcR6tX5O__675qJC_L1ewE6eRk_Nn3gSIf2iJM). Here they publish a fake article claimed to be written by Britt Hume that promotes CDB oil. The article claims that pastor Charles Stanley is endorsing and starting a CBD business, [Big Pharma Outraged Over Charles Stanley’s Latest Business Venture – He Fires Back With This!](https://whatregistrater.com/xq289/?fbclid=IwAR0XX70WfatZLRGM2PdQxZcR6tX5O__675qJC_L1ewE6eRk_Nn3gSIf2iJM) Pastor Charles Stanley has denied this and [issued a warning](https://www.intouch.org/read/blog/cbd-oil-scam) about the scam.
In General, this is a fake imposter website designed to sell CDB products.
