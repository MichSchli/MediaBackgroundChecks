History
[Founded in 2014](https://whois.domaintools.com/thehardtimes.net), The Hard Times is a satire website that primarily focuses on satire directed at the music industry. According to their satirical [about page](https://thehardtimes.net/about/) “The Hard Times is a very real punk news site that you should not question. Just absorb the information as truth and move on. The historic satire site was founded in December 1976.” [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Hard Times lists Matt Saincome as the founder and CEO. Revenue is derived through advertising and an
[online shop](https://shop.thehardtimes.net/) that sells branded merchandise as well as vinyl and other items.
Analysis / Bias
In review, most satirical articles are related to music such as this
[Man Has Spine Removed To Bend Over Backward Defending Marilyn Manson.](https://thehardtimes.net/culture/man-has-spine-removed-to-bend-over-backward-defending-marilyn-manson/) They also publish political satire such as this [If God Wanted Me To Vote for a Progressive Then He Should Have Made Me More Open-Minded](https://thehardtimes.net/opinion/if-god-wanted-me-to-vote-for-a-progressive-then-he-should-have-made-me-more-open-minded/). In general, this is a satire website that openly disclaims so.
