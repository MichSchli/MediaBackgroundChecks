History
Le Monde Diplomatique is a French monthly newspaper first published in 1954. The newspaper was founded by
[Hubert Beuve-Méry](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/diplo/apropos/), the former director of the French newspaper Le Monde, and has since become known for its in-depth coverage of international affairs and its [critical perspective](https://www.lemonde.fr/actualite-medias/article/2017/02/14/le-lustre-retrouve-du-monde-diplo_5079218_3236.html) on economic and globalization issues. [Read our profile on France’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/france-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Le Monde Diplomatique is owned by the French publishing company Groupe Le Monde. According to its
[website](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/diplo/apropos/#revue), the newspaper is financially independent and relies on revenue from subscriptions and donations to fund its operations.
Analysis / Bias
The articles published by Le Monde Diplomatique are well-sourced and fact-based, often drawing on primary sources such as government reports and academic research. The newspaper is
[transparent](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/diplo/apropos/) about its perspective and supports its arguments with evidence. Articles and headlines use moderately loaded language that favor the left such as this [Labor purge in the UK.](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/2021/01/HATHERLEY/62667)
Editorially, Le Monde Diplomatique has a strongly left-leaning bias and is known for its critical reporting on
[neoliberal economic policies](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/carnet/2011-11-02-Chili) and support of [Labour](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/2021/09/HATHERLEY/63467). The newspaper often focuses on social justice and human rights issues and [generally](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/cartes/maghreb-changement-climatique) supports [progressive policies](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/2022/12/LE_PAIGE/65361). Further, they report negatively on right-leaning President Emmanuel Macron: [Emmanuel Macron’s flop](https://www.monde-diplomatique.fr/mav/174/PIRONET/62531).
In general, Le Monde Diplomatique is a reliable source of international news and analysis, providing well-sourced articles written by experts in their fields. While the newspaper has a left-leaning bias, it is transparent about its perspective and supports its arguments with evidence.
