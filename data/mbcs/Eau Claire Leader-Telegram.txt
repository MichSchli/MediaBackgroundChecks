History
[Founded in 1881](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eau_Claire_Leader-Telegram), the Eau Claire Leader-Telegram is a newspaper published in Eau Claire, Wisconsin. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Leader-Telegram is published by
[Adams Publishing Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Adams_Publishing_Group), which is owned by billionaire Stephen Adams, a billboard magnate who contributed $1,008,982 to the pro-Trump campaign as per a [Bluestem Prairie](http://www.bluestemprairie.com/bluestemprairie/2016/10/more-mn-media-fun-trump-billboard-tycoon-owns-dozens-of-north-star-state-news-papers.html) article. Revenue is derived through advertisements and paywall fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Leader-Telegram covers local news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Neighborhood store closing this weekend in Eau Claire](https://www.leadertelegram.com/news/front-page/neighborhood-store-closing-this-weekend-in-eau-claire/article_486d2396-bca3-57d7-b458-9a31858c1e88.html). National and international news is republished from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, we could not find political endorsements in this paper. Editorials are generally balanced with perhaps a slight lean to the right in story selection such as this:
[The case for censure over impeachment](https://www.leadertelegram.com/opinion/editorials/the-case-for-censure-over-impeachment/article_981c6856-f38b-5f3a-886d-3c7ff485af75.html). In general, news reporting is factual and mostly low-biased.
