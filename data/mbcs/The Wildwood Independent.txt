History
The Wildwood Independent is a daily newspaper serving Grass Valley, California. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Wildwood Independent is published by Swift Communications, which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the western USA. Ogden Newspapers owns Swift. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Wildwood Independent publishes news for Grass Valley, California, and the surrounding area. Local news is covered through reporting with minimal bias in wording, such as this

A review of editorials demonstrates a reasonable balance without extremes such as this
[Our View: Using the past to shape our future](https://www.theunion.com/opinion/our-view/our-view-using-the-past-to-shape-our-future/). In general, they exhibit low bias and produce fact-based news.
