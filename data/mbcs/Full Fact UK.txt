History
Founded in 2009, Full Fact is an independent fact-checking organization based in the UK which aims to “promote accuracy in public debate”.
[Read our report on government influence on UK’s media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
In 2014, Full Fact was granted non-profit charity status. The website is funded through donations. The
[top funders](https://fullfact.org/about/funding/), as listed on the website, are Facebook, Luminate, Google, and the [Nuffield Foundation](https://www.nuffieldfoundation.org/).
Analysis / Bias
In review, Full Fact is a signatory of the International Fact-Checking Network (IFCN). They do not use loaded words in their fact checks and always source properly. A factual search reveals they have not failed a fact check and are, in fact, a certified fact-checker.
Failed Fact Checks
- None. They are an IFCN fact-checker.
