History
[Founded in 1833](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Democrat_and_Chronicle), The Democrat and Chronicle is a daily broadsheet newspaper serving the greater Rochester, New York area. The Democrat and Chronicle is Rochester’s only daily circulated newspaper covering local news, sports, weather, and crime.

Analysis / Bias
In review, the Democrat and Chronicle report local news about the greater Rochester area with minimal bias, such as this
[Republicans withdraw controversial bill that would have stripped county executive powers.](https://www.democratandchronicle.com/story/news/2019/11/15/monroe-county-gop-withdraws-controversial-bill-would-have-stripped-executive-powers/4204642002/) The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and USA Today Network derive national and international news.
Editorially, the Democrat and Chronicle have
[endorsed](https://noahveltman.com/endorsements/) Democratic Presidential candidates since 1988. Op-Eds also currently favor the left.
