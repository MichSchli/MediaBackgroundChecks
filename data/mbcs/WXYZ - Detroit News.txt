History
Founded in 1948, WXYZ – Detroit News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Detroit, Michigan, United States. wxyz.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WXYZ – Detroit News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WXYZ – Detroit News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Detroit, Michigan area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[3-year-old girl in stable condition after being shot on Detroit’s west side](https://www.wxyz.com/news/3-year-old-girl-in-temporary-serious-condition-after-being-shot-on-detroits-west-side). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Stevens claims victory, Levin concedes in 11th congressional district](https://www.wxyz.com/news/democracy-2022/stevens-claims-victory-levin-concedes-in-11th-congressional-district). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
