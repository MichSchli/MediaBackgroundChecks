History
According to their
[about page:](https://conservativedailypost.com/about-us/) “Founded in the summer of 2016 during the tumultuous Trump-Clinton election cycle, Conservative Daily Post is an independent news organization that thrives on independent journalism and truth.” The current editor is unknown as the [editor’s page](https://conservativedailypost.com/editors/) is blank.
Funded by / Ownership
The Conservative Daily Post does not disclose ownership and is funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
The Conservative Daily Post is a news and opinion website that is hard to determine if it is satire or real. Since they do not offer a disclaimer, we assume this is an actual website. All stories favor the right and are anti-left that utilize strong emotional wording such as
[Racist Thugs Take Democrat Cue, 103-Year-Old Children’s Anthem Monument Targeted By Criminals.](https://conservativedailypost.com/racist-thugs-take-democrat-cue-103-year-old-childrens-anthem-monument-targeted-criminals/) Most articles are sourced to either credible media outlets that are spun to fit a conservative narrative or directly from highly questionable sources.
