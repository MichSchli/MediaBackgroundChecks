History
The Oskaloosa Herald is a newspaper published in the city of Oskaloosa, Iowa. oskaloosa.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Oskaloosa Herald. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Oskaloosa Herald covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Oskaloosa, Iowa, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Knoxville woman among 6 sentenced for gang crimes](https://www.oskaloosa.com/news/local_news/knoxville-woman-among-6-sentenced-for-gang-crimes/article_e22e1ac4-2024-11ed-bfe3-3bca84c35b77.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
