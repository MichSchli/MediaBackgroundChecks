History
Established in 2003, the host of Culpeper Times,
[InsideNoVA.com](https://www.insidenova.com/site/about.html) serves as a news website for Northern Virginia, covering various topics across counties such as Arlington, Fairfax, Prince William, Stafford, Culpeper, Fauquier, and Loudoun. It reports on news, sports, traffic, and weather. InsideNoVA’s parent company, Rappahannock Media LLC, is headquartered in Washington, Va. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Culpeper Times is owned by
[Rappahannock Media LLC](https://www.insidenova.com/site/contact.html), which is owned by [Dennis Brack,](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dennis-brack-5297441/) who serves as the president and owner of Rappahannock Media LLC. The key publications under Rappahannock Media LLC include [Rappahannock News](https://www.rappnews.com/site/about.html), InsideNoVa/Prince William, and the Piedmont Virginian magazine. Revenue is generated through advertising, [subscriptions,](https://www.insidenova.com/site/forms/subscription_services/) and donations.
Analysis / Bias
The Culpeper Times focuses on local news and community issues. Local news is covered with minimal bias such as this
[Culpeper elementary school’s interior design club unveils projects](https://www.insidenova.com/culpeper/culpeper-elementary-schools-interior-design-club-unveils-projects/article_106834d4-b2ef-11ee-bcc7-afde62aba5ee.html). The national news is covered by various outlets such as [Scripps News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/newsy/).
A review of InsideNoVa’s op-ed page demonstrates a range of opinions that cover both sides.
Generally, Culpeper Times provides relevant information to its readership in Northern Virginia through its comprehensive coverage of local news and community issues. The content maintains factual accuracy and a neutral tone, with no significant evidence of political or ideological bias in its reporting.
