History
The Snowmass Sun is a daily newspaper serving Snowmass, Colorado. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Snowmass Sun is published by Swift Communications, which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the western USA. Ogden Newspapers owns Swift. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
