History
The Meridian Star is a newspaper published in Meridian, Mississippi. meridianstar.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Meridian Star. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Meridian Star covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Meridian, Mississippi, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Meridian man pleads guilty to armed robberies](https://www.meridianstar.com/news/meridian-man-pleads-guilty-to-armed-robberies/article_11afc578-831a-11ee-9065-83b19200e7a2.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
