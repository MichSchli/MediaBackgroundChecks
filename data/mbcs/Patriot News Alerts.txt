History
[Founded in 2017](http://whois.domaintools.com/patriotnewsalerts.com), Patriot News Alerts is a conservative news and opinion blog. According to their [about page](https://www.patriotnewsalerts.com/about-us/): “America is under attack from the inside. The Deep State, the liberal media, social-justice Hollywood, leftist colleges – we’re under attack from every possible direction. That’s why we’re fighting back. In the Information Age, the best weapon is better information. That’s why Patriot News Alerts was launched. To better inform the Americans who care about their country. You don’t have to rely on the leftists for your news. You can get the truth here.”
Patriot News Alerts does not list an editor, founder, or owner on the website.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Patriot News Alerts is not transparent in ownership, and the website is funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Patriot News Alerts is a news and opinion blog that reports news with a right-wing bias in both story selection and wording. Here are some sample headlines that utilize strong loaded words that favor the right:
[Study suggests Obamacare has killed people](https://www.patriotnewsalerts.com/study-suggests-obamacare-has-killed-people/), [Press Secretary Sarah Sanders obliterates CNN for attacks on military personnel](https://www.patriotnewsalerts.com/sanders-obliterates-cnn/), and [Sarah Huckabee Sanders torches Senate Democrats over failure to protect American citizens](https://www.patriotnewsalerts.com/sarah-sanders-torches-democrats/). In general, Patriot News Alerts summarizes and editorializes news stories to favor the right. When it comes to sourcing they utilize a variety of mainstream media sources, as well as factually mixed right-leaning sources such as the [Daily Caller](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-caller/) and [Daily Wire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-daily-wire/).
