History
Willcox Range News is a newspaper based in Willcox, Arizona, that publishes news for Cochise County. The website is located at
[myheraldreview.com.](https://www.myheraldreview.com/news/willcox/)
Funded by / Ownership
Wick Communications owns the Willcox Range News and several small community papers across the Pacific Northwest. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Willcox Range News publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Thatcher man arrested in Willcox robbery.](https://www.myheraldreview.com/news/cochise_county/thatcher-man-arrested-in-willcox-robbery/article_ec50b008-b084-11ec-8422-43df43b43bf2.html) The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Willcox Range News leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. The news is generally reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
