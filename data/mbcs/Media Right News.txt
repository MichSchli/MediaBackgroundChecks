History
[Founded in 2019](https://whois.domaintools.com/mediarightnews.com), Media Right News is a website that publishes news and commentary from a conservative perspective. According to their [about page](https://mediarightnews.com/about-us/), they are a “News and opinion site in support of free speech, constitutional and traditional values, humor, and against political correctness. America should always come first.”
The website lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Media Right News lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership. According to other sources,
[Ian McDonald](https://api.newsguardtech.com/label/mediarightnews.com?cid=5beca98f-3100-40d0-8a9e-7adc09600d4f) is the owner and editor of the website. Advertising and donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Media Right News publishes news and commentary with a conservative bias. Most articles are derived from other sources such as the
[New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/), [The Hill](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-hill/), and [Politico](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/politico/). Headlines reflect the article’s content and usually contain moderately loaded emotional language that favors the right such as this [NEW FLORIDA GOVERNOR POLL SHOWS RON DESANTIS DOMINATING POTENTIAL DEMOCRAT OPPONENTS](https://mediarightnews.com/new-florida-governor-poll-shows-ron-desantis-dominating-potential-democrat-opponents/).
Editorially, story selection always favors the right and especially former President Trump, such as this
[FORMER PRESIDENT TRUMP THROWS DOWN GAUNTLET, SUES HILLARY, DNC, OTHERS FOR ‘UNTHINKABLE PLOT’ TO PUSH RUSSIAN COLLUSION NARRATIVE](https://mediarightnews.com/former-president-trump-throws-down-gauntlet-sues-hillary-dnc-others-for-unthinkable-plot-to-push-russian-collusion-narrative/). When reporting on Democrats, they frequently use denigrating language such as this [RADICAL LEFTIST BELLWETHER AOC SOUNDS THE ALARM, SAYS YOUNG PEOPLE NO LONGER EXCITED ABOUT JOE BIDEN](https://mediarightnews.com/radical-leftist-bellwether-aoc-sounds-the-alarm-says-young-people-no-longer-excited-about-joe-biden/). In general, Media Right News is mostly fact-based and holds a far-right editorial bias.
