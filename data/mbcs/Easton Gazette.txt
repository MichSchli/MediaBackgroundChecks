History
Founded in 2022 by
[L Todd Wood](https://ltoddwood.com/), The Easton Gazette is a conservative news and opinion website with a history of publishing misinformation. This website does not have a functioning About page at the time of review. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Creative Destruction Media, owned by L Todd Wood, publishes The Easton Gazette.
[CDM publishes several websites](https://creativedestructionmedia.com/cdm-sites/) using the names of old defunct newspapers. Advertising and ad-free subscriptions generate revenue.
