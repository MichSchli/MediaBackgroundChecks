History
The Franklin Daily Journal is a newspaper based in Franklin, Indiana, serving Franklin and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at dailyjournal.net.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns The Franklin Daily Journal and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Franklin Daily Journal publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Franklin to pass on full trash costs to residents](https://dailyjournal.net/2022/01/21/franklin-to-pass-on-full-trash-costs-to-residents/). The national news is covered by the least biased [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, The Franklin Daily Journal leans right in its views and endorsements
[Stop snooping: Nothing patriotic about government spying on us](https://dailyjournal.net/2021/10/30/stop-snooping-nothing-patriotic-about-government-spying-on-us/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
