History
[Founded in 1947](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WABC-TV), WABC-TV is the flagship station of the ABC television network, licensed to New York, New York, United States. ABC7NY.com is the associated website that provides news, weather, and sports for the New York City Metropolitan region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
ABC7 New York is owned by the ABC Owned Television Stations subsidiary of
[The Walt Disney Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the ABC7 New York website covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the New York City Metro area. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Suspect killed in a police-involved shooting in Queens](https://abc7ny.com/live-update-suspect-killed-in-police-involved-shooting-in-queens/6868871/). Many local stories contain embedded videos from the TV station. National and world news is covered by [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/), [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), ESPN, and other ABC stations.
Editorially, ABC7 New York does not provide opinion pieces; however, they report on politics. They provide original reporting with minimal bias, such as this
[Post-debate poll: Who won the debate, Mike Pence or Kamala Harris?](https://abc7ny.com/politics/who-won-the-debate-mike-pence-or-kamala-harris/6852875/) They also rely on political news from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
