History
[Founded in 1829](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Post-Standard), The Post-Standard is a Syracuse, New York-based broadsheet newspaper. The paper is published by [Advance Publications](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Advance_Publications), and they do not have a masthead or any information on who edits the paper or is in charge. The newspaper covers local, regional, and national news. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Post-Standard is owned by
[Advanced Media New York](https://www.advancemediany.com/about-us/) and is funded through a subscription and advertising model.

In 2012, they did not endorse a candidate, and in 2016, they endorsed Democrat Hillary Clinton for President.
