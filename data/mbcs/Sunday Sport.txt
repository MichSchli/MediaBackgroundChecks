History
Founded in 2012, Sunday Sport is a satire website based in the United Kingdom. It is a tabloid newspaper known not to be true.
[Read our profile on UK Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
David Sullivan owns the Sunday Sport through Sunday Sports LTD. Sunday Sport generates revenue through advertising and membership fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Sunday Sport indicates they are satire through their tagline “The World’s Most Outrageous Newspaper.” Because they disclose they are satire they are labeled as such.
