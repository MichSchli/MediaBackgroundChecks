History
[Founded in 1973,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Internationalist) New Internationalist (NI) is an independent, non-profit, publishing co-operative, based in Oxford, United Kingdom. Predominantly known for its monthly independent magazine, it describes itself as existing to ‘cover stories the mainstream media sidestep and provide alternative perspectives on today’s global critical issues. According to their [about page](https://newint.org/about) “New Internationalist is a leading independent media organization dedicated to socially conscious journalism and publishing.” [Read our profile on UK’s media and governement](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The magazine and website are published by New Internationalist Publications Limited. According to their
[about page](https://newint.org/about) they are owned by a multi-stakeholder co-operative co-owned by our workers and over [3,600 investors](https://newint.org/about/500club). Revenue is derived through donations and an Ethical Shop that sells earth-friendly merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
In review, New Internationalist publishes news and information related to global justice such as social and environmental issues. Articles and headlines utilize moderate loaded emotional wording such as this:
[HOW COUNTER-TERROR POLICE QUASH DISSENT](https://newint.org/features/2020/01/15/how-counter-terror-police-quash-dissent). This story is well sourced to the [Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/), Netpol, and the [BBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bbc/). As mentioned, New Internationalist frequently reports on environmental issues such as [climate change](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/): [WHEN A ‘CLIMATE ELECTION’ GOES WRONG](https://newint.org/features/2020/01/14/when-climate-election-goes-wrong). They also report favorably on socialism such as this: [WHAT IF…A SOCIALIST BECAME PRESIDENT OF THE USA?](https://newint.org/features/2020/01/10/what-if-socialist-became-president-usa). Further, they report very negatively on current conservative Prime Minister Boris Johnson: [F**K OFF BORIS!](https://newint.org/blog/2012/03/27/boris-johnson-olympic-clean-up) In general, New Internationalist reports news accurately and with a strong left-leaning (Socialist) bias.
