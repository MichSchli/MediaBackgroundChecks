History
Founded in 1878, the Victorville Daily Press serves Victorville, California, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. Vvdailypress.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns the Victorville Daily Press. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Victorville Daily Press covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Victorville, California area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Victorville adopts district-based election system months after lawsuit threat](https://www.vvdailypress.com/story/news/2021/12/22/victorville-adopts-district-based-election-system-after-lawsuit-threat/9001308002/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, the Victorville Daily Press does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented such as this that favors the left:
[Community solar can expand access to renewables for all Californians](https://www.vvdailypress.com/story/opinion/2021/11/16/community-solar-can-expand-access-renewables-all-californians/8642072002/). And this favoring the right [Around Town: Let’s ‘overfund’ the police so they can continue doing their job](https://www.vvdailypress.com/story/opinion/2021/11/24/around-town-lets-overfund-police/8729018002/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
