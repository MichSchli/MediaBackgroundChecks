History
Biomass Journal is published by
[MDPI (Multidisciplinary Digital Publishing Institute)](https://www.mdpi.com/about/history), formerly Molecular Diversity Preservation International, an open-access journal publisher based in Basel, Switzerland. Biomass is an international, [peer-reviewed](https://www.mdpi.com/editorial_process), open-access journal on biomass conversion and biorefinery published quarterly online by MDPI. MDPI publishes over [400 journals.](https://www.mdpi.com/about/journals) [Read our profile on Swiss Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/switzerland-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of Biomass Journal is
[MDPI AG](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mdpi-multidisciplinary-digital-publishing-institute-bias/), which Shu-Kun Lin owns. Here is the list of [the Management Team.](https://www.mdpi.com/about/team) According to [“MDPI Open Access Information and Policy.”](https://www.mdpi.com/openaccess) MDPI articles and all its journals are open access where authors pay a one-time Article Processing Charge (APC) to cover the costs of peer review administration and management; here is [“General Information on MDPI’s Article Processing Charges (APC).”](https://www.mdpi.com/apc)
Analysis / Bias
Biomass Journal covers the study of biomass conversion and biorefinery. Studies are published via open access, such as this:
[Admissibility Grid to Support the Decision for the Preferential Routing of Portuguese Endogenous Waste Biomass for the Production of Biogas, Advanced Biofuels, Electricity and Heat](https://www.mdpi.com/2673-8783/3/4/21). Like all others reviewed, this study is Pro-Science, well-researched and low-biased.
While Biomass Journal has not failed a fact-check, its publisher has faced criticism as an open-access predatory journal. Read our full review on
[MDPI](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mdpi-multidisciplinary-digital-publishing-institute-bias/).
