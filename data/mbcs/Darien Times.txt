History
The Darien Times is a weekly newspaper from Darien, Connecticut, distributed on Thursdays. The paper covers Darien news, politics, sports, schools, and feature stories and publishes a monthly section on arts and entertainment.

Funded by / Ownership
Darien Times is owned by Hearst Communications, which owns newspapers, magazines, television channels, and television stations, including the
[San Francisco Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-francisco-chronicle/), the [Houston Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/houston-chronicle/), and [Esquire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/esquire-magazine/). According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Hearst donated [70% to Republican candidates](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/recipients?id=D000034391) and causes in 2020. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Darien Times focuses on local news of Darien, CT, and the surrounding area through the use of journalists. They don’t report on national or international news. All information is sourced correctly, either through linking or quotations.
[Editorially](https://www.darientimes.com/category/opinion/editorial/), there is a reasonable balance between left-center and right-center viewpoints.
