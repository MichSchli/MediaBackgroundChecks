History
[Founded in 1875](http://www.burnettcountysentinel.com/site/about.html), the Burnett County Sentinel is a local weekly paper serving Burnett County, Wisconsin. The paper features local news, Crime & Safety and an Opinion section. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Eugene Johnson](http://www.burnettcountysentinel.com/site/about.html) and funded through advertising and donations.
Analysis / Bias
In review, local news reporting is done through local writers. There is little use of loaded language and information is generally sourced through quotations
