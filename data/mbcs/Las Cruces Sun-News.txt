History
[Founded in 1881](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Las_Cruces_Sun-News), the Las Cruces Sun-News is a daily broadsheet newspaper published in Las Cruces, New Mexico. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Las Cruces Sun-News is owned by Gannett (Gatehouse Media), which publishes
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/). In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. The newspaper is funded through advertising and paid subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Las Cruces Sun-News covers local news through staff journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[2 inmates in New Mexico charged with attempted murder after attack on prison guards](https://www.lcsun-news.com/story/news/local/community/2019/07/18/new-mexico-inmates-charged-attempted-murder-attack-prison-guards/1772968001/). National and international news is curated from USA Today and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, they have typically
[endorsed Democratic Presidential candidates](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newspaper_endorsements_in_the_2012_United_States_presidential_election), and their op-ed page tends to favor more [liberal policy](https://www.lcsun-news.com/story/opinion/2019/06/07/what-did-we-get-exactly-what-we-didnt-want/1386339001/). In general, news reporting is accurate and sourced, while editorially, this paper slightly favors the left.
