Funded by / Ownership
The website does not indicate ownership, however, a whois search reveals the domain was purchased in 2013. Mad World News appears to be primarily funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
Mad World News is an extreme right-wing biased news and opinion website that always favors the right. There is the frequent use of loaded emotional headlines such as this:
[NYC Muslim Wannabe Jihadist Tries To Set Fire To Gas Pump, Doesn’t End Well](https://madworldnews.com/nyc-muslim-jihadist-gas-pump/). When not reporting [pro-Trump propaganda](https://madworldnews.com/moore-film-trump-patriots/) they often report on crimes committed specifically by Muslims and [Africa Americans](https://madworldnews.com/convicted-robber-judge-verdict/).
Mad World News routinely misrepresents facts and has a poor track record with
[fact checkers](https://www.snopes.com/tag/mad-world-news/). It is also listed on the [Politifact fake news list](http://www.politifact.com/punditfact/article/2017/apr/20/politifacts-guide-fake-news-websites-and-what-they/?platform=hootsuite).
