History
The Marietta Daily Journal is a newspaper published in Marietta, Georgia. mdjonline.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports for the Marietta area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Times-Journal Inc.](https://www.editorandpublisher.com/stories/times-journal-inc-acquires-six-metro-atlanta-newspapers,240906), which publishes numerous community newspapers throughout Georgia and the southeast. Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
