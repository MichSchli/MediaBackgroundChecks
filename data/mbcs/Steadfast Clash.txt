History
[Founded in 2019](https://whois.domaintools.com/steadfastclash.com), Steadfast Clash is a website that publishes conservative news and commentary. According to their [about page](https://steadfastandloyal.com/about/), “Steadfast Clash came about as an outlet for the frustration those of us involved had with the political and media fields. While we are Conservative, we aim to always ensure that the information we base our articles about is as factual as possible.” The website’s about page is identical to the questionable [Steadfast and Loyal](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/steadfast-and-loyal/). [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Steadfast Clash lacks transparency as they do not indicate ownership. Advertising generates revenue. They disclaim how they generate revenue “SteadfastAndLoyal.com is a for-profit news curator. We generate revenue by showing ads on our website and have no corporate backing of any kind.”
Analysis / Bias
Steadfast Clash is an opinion and news website with a strong right-wing bias in reporting. Articles and headlines often contain loaded emotional language such as
[TIME TO JUMP SHIP – BIDEN MOVING TO CLOSE LARGEST OIL FIELD ACCOUNTING FOR 40% OF ALL DOMESTIC PRODUCTION](https://steadfastclash.com/the-latest/time-to-jump-ship-biden-moving-to-close-largest-oil-field-accounting-for-40-of-all-domestic-production/). This story contains stats but does not offer hyperlinking to any sources.
Editorially, all stories favor the right and denigrate the left, such as this
[CROOKED LIBERAL OLIGARCH TRYING TO SMOTHER THE VOICE OF CONSERVATIVE HISPANICS!](https://steadfastclash.com/the-latest/crooked-liberal-oligarch-trying-to-smother-the-voice-of-conservative-hispanics/) During the Covid pandemic, Steadfast Clash promoted vaccine misinformation such as this [SUBPOENAED FDA FILES PROVE DANGEROUS SIDE EFFECTS FROM PFIZER COVID VACCINE](https://steadfastclash.com/the-latest/subpoenaed-fda-files-prove-dangerous-side-effects-from-pfizer-covid-vaccine/).
Finally, after the 2020 Presidential Election, they continued to promote election fraud conspiracies such as this
[FINALLY! ELECTION FRAUD FOOTAGE WE’VE BEEN WAITING FOR! – DELETING FILES FROM SERVER BEFORE TURNING OVER DATA](https://steadfastclash.com/the-latest/finally-election-fraud-footage-weve-been-waiting-for-deleting-files-from-server-before-turning-over-data/). This story is [false](https://www.reuters.com/article/factcheck-maricopa-database-idUSL2N2N8266).
