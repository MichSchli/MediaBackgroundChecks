History
[Founded in 1948](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chicago_Sun-Times), The Chicago Sun-Times is a daily newspaper published in Chicago, Illinois. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
In 2022, it became part of the Chicago Public Media family of companies and now operates as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Revenue is derived through subscription fees and advertising.
