History
Founded in 1960, KEZI – Eugene News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Eugene, Oregon, United States. KEZI.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Eugene area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns KEZI – Eugene News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KEZI – Eugene News publishes news for the Eugene city and surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[UO students facing fines, frustrated with party patrols](https://www.kezi.com/news/uo-students-facing-fines-frustrated-with-party-patrols/article_8e6676ba-d598-11ec-a856-e7662de08c36.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
