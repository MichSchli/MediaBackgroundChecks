History
[Founded in 2006](https://whois.domaintools.com/dailyclimate.org), The Daily Climate is a publication of Environmental Health Sciences working to increase public understanding of climate change, including its scope and scale, potential solutions, and the political processes that impede or advance them. According to their [about page](https://www.ehn.org/about-environmental-health-news-2495507118.html), they are an “independent, reader- and foundation-funded organization that reports, publishes and curates journalism on environmental topics.” The current executive editor is Douglas Fischer. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Daily Climate is published by Environmental Health Sciences a nonprofit, organization funded by foundations and readers and does not accept money from interest groups.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Daily Climate provides original news on
[climate change](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/) that utilizes loaded words in emphasizing the dangers of climate change such as this [We ignored pandemic warnings and now look. Let’s not do the same for climate change.](https://www.dailyclimate.org/covid-19-and-climate-change-2649461255.html) This story is properly sourced. Other stories are curated through other sources such as yale.edu, [MSNBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/msnbc/), and the Sierra Club. Further, they have reported accurately during the covid-19 pandemic of 2020. Story selection generally favors the left due to their promotion of environmental concerns.
