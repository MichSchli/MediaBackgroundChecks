History
Sechelt Coast Reporter is an English-language newspaper in Sechelt, British Columbia, Canada. coastreporter.net is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Sechelt Coast Reporter. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Sechelt Coast Reporter publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Sechelt, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Further delay on hearing into ex-Sechelt CFO’s actions](https://www.coastreporter.net/local-news/further-delay-on-hearing-into-ex-sechelt-cfos-actions-6698993). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Sechelt Coast Reporter’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Watt’s Happening: You don’t have to be big to be green](https://www.coastreporter.net/opinion/watts-happening-you-dont-have-to-be-big-to-be-green-6661671). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
