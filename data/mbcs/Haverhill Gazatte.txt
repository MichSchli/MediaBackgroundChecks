History
The Haverhill Gazette is a newspaper published in Haverhill, Massachusetts. hagazette.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Haverhill Gazette. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Haverhill Gazette covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Haverhill, MA, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[State shelter system now caring for 6,300 families](https://www.hgazette.com/news/local_news/state-shelter-system-now-caring-for-6-300-families/article_2cd9cfd8-ee15-567a-98a4-95c82e21e664.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
