History
The Milledgeville Union-Recorder is a daily newspaper published in the city of Milledgeville, Georgia, and covers Baldwin County. unionrecorder.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Milledgeville Union-Recorder. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Milledgeville Union-Recorder covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Milledgeville, Georgia, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this

The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left, such as this
