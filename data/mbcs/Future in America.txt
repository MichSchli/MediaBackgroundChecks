History
Future in America is a conservative Super PAC that states on their
[about page](https://ourfutureinamerica.org/about/) “We believe that our future in America is bright, and are looking to connect with like-minded citizens who will join us in making that belief reality. Our Future in America is a political action committee supporting principled causes and candidates in federal campaigns across the United States.” [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Future in America is a PAC that is funded through donations.
[According](https://www.opensecrets.org/pacs/lookup2.php?strID=C00629592&cycle=2018) to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), they support Republican candidates and causes.
Analysis / Bias
In review, besides supporting Republican candidates, including President Trump, they also publish summarized news on the website such as this
[‘IF YOU DO VOTE FOR BIDEN, YOU DON’T KNOW HISTORY’](https://ourfutureinamerica.org/2020/09/if-you-do-vote-for-biden-you-dont-know-history/). This story leads to the full article at the right-leaning [Daily Wire.](https://ourfutureinamerica.org/2020/09/if-you-do-vote-for-biden-you-dont-know-history/) For the most part, they do not produce original content, but rather link to other sources such as [Fox News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news/), [Daily Caller](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-caller/), and the Questionable [Breitbart](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/breitbart/).
The
[editorial positions](https://ourfutureinamerica.org/vision/) of Future in America is as follows in their mission statement: “Assembling millions of like-minded American voters interested in actively working together to elect, and hold accountable, candidates committed to establishing nationwide safety — nominating authentic Constitutionally focused Supreme Court justices — rebuilding our military — protecting the elderly and the unborn — putting an end to slavery and human trafficking — and bringing jobs back to American workers.” In general. they align with the conservative Christian Right.
