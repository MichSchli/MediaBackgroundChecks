History
Founded in 1883 as the Danbury Evening News,
[The News-Times](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_News-Times) is a daily newspaper based in Danbury, Connecticut, USA. The founder is James Montgomery Bailey. The paper covers greater Danbury and the surrounding communities. [Paul Barbetta](http://www.hearst.com/newsroom/hearst-newspapers-names-paul-barbetta-group-publisher-and-president-of-hearst-connecticut-media-group) has been named group publisher and president of the Hearst Connecticut Media Group. [Barbara Roessner ](http://www.hearst.com/newsroom/hearst-newspapers-names-barbara-t-roessner-executive-editor-of-hearst-connecticut-newspapers-david-mccumber-named-washington-bureau-chief)is the Managing Editor of Hearst Connecticut Newspapers. Funded by / Ownership
[Hearst](http://www.hearst.com/about) Corporation is the owner of The News-Times since 2007. Hearst is an American mass media and business information conglomerate based in New York City. Hearst Newspapers publishes 15 dailies and 36 weeklies, including the [Houston Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/houston-chronicle/), [San Francisco Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-francisco-chronicle/), [San Antonio Express-News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-antonio-express-news/) and the [Albany Times Union](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/albany-times-union/). It also operates digital marketing services and directories businesses under the LocalEdge brand. The newspaper is funded through a subscription and advertising model.
Analysis / Bias
The News-Times covers a wide variety of topics, including news with a strong focus on local news, sports, entertainment, business, politics, and more. Editorially, The News-Times
[endorsed](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newspaper_endorsements_in_the_United_States_presidential_election,_2016) Hillary Clinton in the 2016 Presidential elections and [Barack Obama in 2012](https://www.newstimes.com/news/article/2012-election-endorsements-4004374.php). In general, more [editorials](https://www.newstimes.com/opinion/article/Editorial-End-cruel-policy-of-taking-kids-from-13007794.php) and opinion pieces slightly favor the left. The News-Times has a code of ethics that can be viewed [here](https://www.asne.org/resources-ethics-danbury).
The News-Times republishes stories from least biased sources, such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and utilizes credible right-center biased sources, such as the [Wall Street Journal.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/wall-street-journal/) Story selection often leans slightly left, and headlines utilize minimally emotionally loaded language such as “ [Connecticut environment groups file federal lawsuit against state”](https://www.newstimes.com/news/article/Connecticut-environment-groups-file-federal-12916340.php) and [“Study: Connecticut loses in tariff war.”](https://www.newstimes.com/business/article/Study-Connecticut-loses-in-tariff-war-13053645.php)
