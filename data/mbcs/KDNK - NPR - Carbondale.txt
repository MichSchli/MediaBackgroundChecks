History
KDNK – NPR – Carbondale is an NPR FM radio station (88.1 FM) based in Carbondale, Colorado, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KDNK – NPR – Carbondale is owned and broadcast by Carbondale Community Access Radio, Inc.. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
