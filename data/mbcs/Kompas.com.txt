History
Kompas was originally
[founded](https://www.linkedin.com/company/kgmediaid) in 1965 by Jakob Oetama. The Kompas online version launched in 1995 under the name Kompas Online and later became [Kompas.com](https://inside.kompas.com/about-us), which is a news website based in Jakarta, Indonesia, that is focused on politics, business, economy, and sports. The editor-in-chief is Wisnu Nugroho.
Funded by / Ownership
[Kompas.com](https://inside.kompas.com/about-us) is owned by [The Kompas Gramedia Group](https://www.kompasgramedia.com/about-kg/management) which was founded by Jakob Oetama and PK Ojong (who passed away 1980) that publishes 18 regional papers. The Kompas Gramedia Group’s CEO is Jakob Oetama’s son [Lilik Oetama](https://money.kompas.com/read/2019/11/21/153432226/kompas100-ajak-pemerintah-dan-ceo-diskusi-soal-digitalisasi). Revenue is based on subscriptions and advertisements.
Analysis / Bias
[Read our profile on Indonesia’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/indonesia-media-profile/)
In review, Kompas.com publishes articles that are neutral in a tone such as
[“Jokowi’s Son Follows in Father’s Footsteps in Mayoral Bid for Solo”](https://go.kompas.com/read/2020/07/18/054841074/jokowis-son-follows-in-fathers-footsteps-in-mayoral-bid-for-solo?page=2) and [“PDI-P Secretary-General: President Jokowi Has Strategic Considerations to Form a Covid-19 Committee”.](https://nasional.kompas.com/read/2020/07/21/17222801/sekjen-pdi-p-presiden-jokowi-punya-pertimbangan-strategis-bentuk-komite) They also report neutrally about coalition leaders such as [Prabowo Subianto](https://nasional.kompas.com/read/2020/07/21/21424671/media-austria-sebut-prabowo-incar-15-pesawat-eurofighter-typhoon) who is the current Minister of Defence and is also the head of the third-largest party Great Indonesia Movement Party (Gerindra) which is conservative.
When it comes to sourcing, they primarily use quotes from other sources, however, they do not link to the source. When reporting on political issues, particularly in regards to West Papua, Kompas.com reports with a
[balance](https://nasional.kompas.com/read/2020/06/18/11225861/vonis-7-tapol-papua-atas-kasus-makar-yang-dinilai-sebagai-shock-therapy?page=all), presenting both sides.
Lastly, Kompas.com covers world news pertaining to the USA in a neutral tone such as this
[“Trump claims US has the lowest death rate due to Covid-19.”](https://www.kompas.com/global/read/2020/07/21/093634770/trump-klaim-as-memiliki-tingkat-kematian-terendah-karena-covid-19) In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias, however they do not provide hyperlinked sourcing for readers to verify quotes and information.
