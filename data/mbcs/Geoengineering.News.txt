History
Geoengineering.News is a right-wing conspiracy and pseudoscience website that routinely publishes false information. The founder is Mike Adams, who owns several Questionable websites such as
[Natural News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/natural-news/) and [News Target](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/news-target/). Geoengineering.News is a part of the Natural News Network. This website lacks transparency and does not disclose ownership.
According to Politifact
[Health misinformation website rebrands as pro-Trump outlet to get around ban from Facebook](https://www.politifact.com/article/2020/jun/05/health-misinformation-website-rebrands-pro-trump-o/) [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Geoengineering.News is owned by Mike Adams, who owns numerous other fake and/or controversial right-wing conspiracy websites. The website, like all of Mike Adams’s properties, is funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Mike Adams is known as the
[Health Ranger](https://rationalwiki.org/wiki/NaturalNews) according to [Rationalwiki](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/rationalwiki/). However, they point out dozens of cases where his claims are false. Besides promoting pseudoscience, Geoengineering.News is an extreme right-wing biased source that frequently promotes false or misleading information regarding vaccines, alternative health, and government conspiracies. For more information read our review on [Natural News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/natural-news/).
In general, politically, all Mike Adam’s stories favor the right and promote pseudoscience such as
[chemtrails](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-chemtrails/), the Sandy Hook shooting being a false flag: [Sandy Hook was theater! Actor who played law enforcement sniper was recorded walking around carrying rifle by the magazine](https://www.newstarget.com/2016-07-07-sandy-hook-was-theater-actor-who-played-law-enforcement-sniper-was-recorded-walking-around-carrying-rifle-by-the-magazine.html). Further, they routinely publish [anti-vaccination propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/) and conspiracy theories. Lastly, this source denies the consensus on [climate change](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/) without evidence, as seen here: [Climate change cultists are now taking over your local weather forecast.](https://www.naturalnews.com/2017-05-09-climate-change-cultists-are-now-taking-over-your-local-weather-forecast.html)
