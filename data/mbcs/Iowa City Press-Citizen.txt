History
The Iowa City Press-Citizen serves Iowa City, Iowa, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. press-citizen.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns the Iowa City Press-Citizen. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Iowa City Press-Citizen covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Iowa City, Iowa area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Arizona medical marijuana card doesn’t allow woman to possess drug in Iowa, state Supreme Court rules](https://www.press-citizen.com/story/news/crime-and-courts/2022/05/27/iowa-medical-marijuana-card-not-allowed-arizona-possession-weed/9961616002/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, the Iowa City Press-Citizen does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Iowa Legislature’s latest voter suppression efforts authored by same party spreading ‘Big Lie’](https://www.press-citizen.com/story/opinion/editorials/our-view/2021/02/28/gop-using-big-lie-push-voter-suppression-efforts-iowa/6832860002/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
