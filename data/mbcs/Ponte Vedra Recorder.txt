History
Founded in 1932, the Ponte Vedra Recorder is an independent newspaper published in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida by Osteen Publishing Company.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
In review, Ponte Vedra Recorder reports local news via journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[New business makes derivatives trading easy](https://pontevedrarecorder.com/stories/new-business-makes-derivatives-trading-easy,31580). National and International news comes directly from the [Associated Press.](https://www.theitem.com/stories/sumter-school-board-holding-called-meeting-tuesday-night,322683)
Editorially, op-eds cover both perspectives without extremes. In general, straight news reporting has a minimal bias, and editorially they are least biased.
