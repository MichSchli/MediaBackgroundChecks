History
WFIT – NPR – Melbourne is an NPR FM radio station (89.5 FM) based in Melbourne, Florida, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WFIT – NPR – Melbourne is owned and broadcast by the Florida Institute of Technology. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
