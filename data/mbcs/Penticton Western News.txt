History
Penticton Western News is a newspaper published in Penticton, British Columbia, Canada. The website pentictonwesternnews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Penticton, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Penticton Western News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Penticton Western News covers news for Penticton and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Dog goes missing from dog show in Summerland](https://www.pentictonwesternnews.com/news/dog-goes-missing-from-dog-show-in-summerland/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Penticton Western News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
