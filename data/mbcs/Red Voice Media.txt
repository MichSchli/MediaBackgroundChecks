History
[Founded in late 2020](https://whois.domaintools.com/redvoicemedia.com) by Ray Dietrich and Zach Heilman, Red Voice Media is a conservative news and opinion website. The website also features the video shows of Ray Dietrich and Stew Peters. According to their [about page](https://www.redvoicemedia.com/about/) “We support FREE SPEECH and will not quit telling the truth just because big tech tyrants don’t like what we say. From this, RED VOICE MEDIA was born. We hope you will plug in and take the ride with us as we fight false narratives against conservatives and law enforcement.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Red Voice Media does not clearly indicate ownership; however, the co-founders most likely own the website. Red Voice Media generates revenue through a store that sells conservative-themed merchandise, donations, and advertising.
Analysis / Bias
Red Voice Media publishes video shows, news stories, and op-eds from a conservative perspective. Original news stories are written by staff and often accompanied by a video. Articles by Ray Dietrich focus on pro-police stories such as this
[VIDEO: Hero Cop Runs Towards Active Shooter To Save Lives At Texas Airport](https://www.redvoicemedia.com/2021/06/video-hero-cop-runs-towards-active-shooter-to-save-lives-at-texas-airport/). The website also republishes articles from [Lifezette](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/lifezette/), which we rate as a questionable source. Further, stories use moderately loaded language such as this [Matt Gaetz – FBI Covering Up COVID Origins In Concert With Chinese Communist Party?](https://www.redvoicemedia.com/2021/06/matt-gaetz-fbi-covering-up-covid-origins-in-concert-with-chinese-communist-party/) This information comes from Matt Gaetz, who has a poor track record with fact-checkers.
Editorially, all stories favor the right, and some promote conspiracy theories regarding 2020 election fraud
[(VIDEO) Trump Calls Out Election Hoax, They Used COVID & Mail In Ballots To Steal The Election](https://www.redvoicemedia.com/2021/06/video-trump-calls-out-election-hoax-they-used-covid-mail-in-ballots-to-steal-the-election/). They have also promoted conspiracies regarding Covid-19, such as this [Fauci Is In The Pocket Of Communist China – Donald Trump](https://www.redvoicemedia.com/2021/06/fauci-is-in-the-pocket-of-communist-china-donald-trump/). This op-ed concluded with a poll asking SHOULD DR. FAUCI BE CRIMINALLY INVESTIGATED? In general, Red Voice Media promotes a far-right biased perspective that is not always factual.
