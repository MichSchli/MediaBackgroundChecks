History
[Founded in 2015](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Right_Stuff_(blog)#Fash_The_Nation) as Fash the Nation, Dissident Mag is the website for the white nationalist, neo-nazi, fascist podcast hosted by Jazzhands McFeels and Marcus Halberstram. Fash the Nation was [banned](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/jan/17/right-stuff-alt-right-site-mike-enoch-revealed) from SoundCloud in October 2016. This podcast is affiliated with the [Right Stuff](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-right-stuff/) website, which is also a white nationalist hate group. The term “Fash” represents fascism and is a play on words mocking the credible program “Face the Nation.” [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
