History
[Founded in 1954](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WFMZ-TV), WFMZ-TV is an independent television station licensed to Allentown, Pennsylvania, United States. The station covers Allentown, Northwest New Jersey, and the Lehigh Valley region, including parts of Philadelphia. WFMZ.com is the official news and information website for the TV Station. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Maranatha Broadcasting Company locally owns WFMZ. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WFMZ.com covers news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the Allentown and Lehigh Valley region. Local news is covered with minimal bias, such as this
[Salisbury Township school board votes to start year all online](https://www.wfmz.com/news/area/lehighvalley/salisbury-township-school-board-votes-to-start-year-all-online/article_1f85f9c0-e2cc-11ea-bbae-ab6253bd1c10.html). National and world news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
WFMZ does provide editorial op-eds. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
