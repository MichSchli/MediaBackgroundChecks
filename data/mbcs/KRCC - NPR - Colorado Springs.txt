History
KRCC – NPR – Colorado Springs is an NPR FM radio station (91.5 FM) based in Colorado Springs, Colorado, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KRCC – NPR – Colorado Springs is owned and broadcast by Public Broadcasting of Colorado. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
