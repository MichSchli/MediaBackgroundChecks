History
Founded in 2001, KMOL – Victoria News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Victoria, Texas, United States. Crossroadstoday.com is the associated news website for the TV Station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KMOL – Victoria News is owned by
[Morgan Murphy Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morgan_Murphy_Media), which owns 14 TV stations and several radio stations throughout the Midwest. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KMOL – Victoria News reports news, sports, weather, and crime for the Victoria, Texas region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Fireworks sales not allowed under burn ban](https://www.crossroadstoday.com/fireworks-sales-not-allowed-under-burn-ban/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally provides national and international news.
Editorially, KMOL – Victoria News does not publish opinion pieces; however, they report on politics primarily through the Associated Press. The news is generally reported factually and with minimal bias due to a lack of editorializing.
