History
Cranbrook Townsman is a newspaper published in Cranbrook, British Columbia, Canada. The website castlegarnews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Castlegar, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Cranbrook Townsman. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Cranbrook Townsman covers news for Cranbrook and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[B.C. family says wolf-dog hybrid killed bulldog puppy at campground](https://www.cranbrooktownsman.com/news/bc-family-says-wolf-dog-hybrid-killed-bulldog-puppy-at-campground-7115809). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Cranbrook Townsman does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
