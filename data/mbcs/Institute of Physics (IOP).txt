History
[Founded in 1874](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Institute_of_Physics), The Institute of Physics (IOP) is a scientific charity that works to advance physics education, research, and application. It has a worldwide membership of over 50,000. The IOP supports physics in education, research, and industry. The IOP’s publishing company, [IOP Publishing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/IOP_Publishing), publishes more than 70 academic journals and magazines. The current President is Jonathan Flint.
