History
Founded in 1967 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, KQCD – Dickinson News serves Dickinson, North Dakota, and the surrounding area. kfyrtv.com.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KQCD – Dickinson News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KQCD – Dickinson News provides news coverage for Dickinson, North Dakota, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Judge: Injunction remains against North Dakota abortion ban](https://www.kfyrtv.com/2022/10/31/judge-injunction-remains-against-north-dakota-abortion-ban/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KQCD – Dickinson News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
