History
The Andover Townsman is a newspaper published in the city of Andover, Massachusetts. andovertownsman.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Andover Townsman. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Andover Townsman covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Andover, Massachusettes region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Andover moving forward with more study recommendations](https://www.andovertownsman.com/news/andover-moving-forward-with-more-study-recommendations/article_cb46cd2e-6200-11ed-9664-c32267d22029.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
