History
The Athol Daily News, based in Greenfield, Massachusetts, is a news website that focuses on arts, entertainment, and culture in the Athol region. The website covers a variety of topics, including local news, music, theater, art exhibits, and social issues.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Athol Daily News is owned by
[Newspapers of New England, Inc. (NNE)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newspapers_of_New_England,_Inc.), which is a privately owned publisher of nine daily and weekly newspapers in New Hampshire and Massachusetts. The paper is funded through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
The Athol Daily News publishes local news through staff journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Residents meet to offer suggestions for Rice’s Roots Farm](https://articles.atholdailynews.com/Residents-offer-suggestions-for-Rices-Roots-52930041). The paper and website do not cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of articles classified as “commentary” indicates more favor a left-leaning perspective, such as
[Fresh Air Fund bringing people together for 140 years](https://www.atholdailynews.com/AN-A6-MONDAY-LETTER-Fresh-Air-Fund-29290679). In general, the news is reported factually with a left-leaning editorial bias.
