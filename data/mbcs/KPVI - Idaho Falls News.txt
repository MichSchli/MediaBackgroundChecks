History
Founded in 1974, KPVI – Idaho Falls News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Idaho Falls, Idaho, United States. The associated website kpvi.com covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the Idaho Falls region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Cox Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cox_Enterprises) owns KPVI – Idaho Falls News through their subsidiary Cox Media Group. They are a company that owns numerous newspapers, television stations, and radio stations, such as the [Atlanta Journal-Constitution](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/atlanta-journal-constitution/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KPVI – Idaho Falls News covers news, sports, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Idaho Falls, Idaho area. Local news is covered with minimally loaded language, such as this
[BLM Crews Fighting the East Gap Fire near Pocatello](https://www.kpvi.com/news/local_news/blm-crews-fighting-the-east-gap-fire-near-pocatello/article_7a470058-0aa1-11ed-aac4-233961d93b9b.html). Many local news stories contain videos from the TV station. The national news is primarily republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, KPVI – Idaho Falls News does not publish op-eds; however, when covering politics, they present info with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden Tests Positive for COVID](https://www.kpvi.com/interests/biden-tests-positive-for-covid/article_c0d40ac9-23e9-5058-82fb-ea3ef834e445.html). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
