History
Founded in 1891, The Grand Rapids Press is a daily newspaper published in Grand Rapids, Michigan. The paper publishes news, weather, sports, crime, and business.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Grand Rapids Press is owned by Advance Publications, which owns eight newspapers in the state of Michigan. The newspaper is funded through a subscription and advertising model.
Analysis / Bias
The Grand Rapids Press reports news through the use of journalists. There is minimal use of loaded language such as this:
[Elon Musk sued by SEC for misleading Tesla investors with August tweet](https://www.mlive.com/auto/index.ssf/2018/09/elon_musk_sued_by_sec_for_misl.html#incart_river_index), and all information is sourced properly. National and international is derived from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Grand Rapids Press publishes slightly more right leaning opinions. Although they endorsed Hillary Clinton in the 2016 Presidential election, they have historically favored
[Republican candidates](https://www.mlive.com/opinion/grand-rapids/index.ssf/2012/10/grand_rapids_press_endorsement.html).
