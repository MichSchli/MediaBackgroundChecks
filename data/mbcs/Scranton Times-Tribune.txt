History
The Scranton Times-Tribune is a newspaper published in Scranton, Pennsylvania, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Pottsville and the surrounding area. thetimes-tribune.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.

Funded by / Ownership
[Times-Shamrock Communication](https://timesshamrock.com/) owns and publishes the Scranton Times-Tribune. They publish newspapers, radio stations, and information-based websites in North Eastern Pennsylvania. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
