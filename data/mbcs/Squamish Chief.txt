History
The Squamish Chief is an English-language newspaper in Squamish, Bristish Columbia, Canada. squamishchief.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Squamish Chief. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
The Squamish Chief publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Squamish, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Protest convoy challenges RCMP wildfire blockade in B.C.](https://www.squamishchief.com/highlights/protest-convoy-challenges-rcmp-wildfire-blockade-in-bc-7451632) The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Squamish Chief’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Editorial: Disaster planning helps ease burden when unthinkable strikes](https://www.squamishchief.com/highlights/editorial-disaster-planning-helps-ease-burden-when-unthinkable-strikes-7440440). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
