History
Riverbender is a portal serving local and national news, classifieds, event calendars, and Alton, Illinois, guides to restaurants, shopping, and attractions with coupons.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website indicates John Hentrich is the owner and CEO. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Riverbender provides local news with minimal bias for the Alton, Illinois region. Articles and headlines do not use loaded language such as this
[Madison County COVID-19 Clinics Now Accepting Walk-Ins](https://www.riverbender.com/articles/details/madison-county-covid19-clinics-now-accepting-walkins---49710.cfm). National stories originate from the [Associated Press](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
The Riverbender does not publish op-eds and reports the news factually.
