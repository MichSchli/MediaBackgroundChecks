History
[Launched in March 2013](https://www.facebook.com/pg/GlobalRiskInsights/about/?ref=page_internal), [Global Risk Insights (GRI)](https://www.linkedin.com/company/global-risk-insights), Headquartered in the UK, is a publication that provides political risk analysis for businesses and investors. Global Risk Insights (GRI) was [founded](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/global-risk-insights#/entity) by Evan Abrams, Sammy Halabi, Basim Al-Ahmadi, and Emil Graesholm. [Read our profile on UK Media and Government](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Global Risk Insights LLP owns the website; however, they do not make this clear on the website. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
GRI provides national featured stories, analysis of issues in the USA, Europe, Latin America, South and Central Asia, and more. They utilize minimally loaded words within their articles, and they do present
[low left-center biased coverage](http://globalriskinsights.com/2017/09/economic-implications-repealing-daca/) of USA news and utilize sources such as [The Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/), [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/), and [Stratfor](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/stratfor/). Politically, Global Risk Insights tends to favor left-leaning issues such as [pro-environment](http://globalriskinsights.com/2015/11/the-economic-impact-of-climate-change-in-southeast-asia/) and concern for [income inequality](http://globalriskinsights.com/2014/05/after-imf-inequality-report-whats-next-for-us-tax-policy/).
