History
Launched in 1992,
[New England Cable News](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_England_Cable_News) (NECN) is a cable news TV station owned and operated by [NBCUniversal](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/) (Comcast Corporation). NECN serves the entire New England region covering news, weather, sports, business, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
NECN is owned by NBCUniversal, a subsidiary of Comcast, which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
