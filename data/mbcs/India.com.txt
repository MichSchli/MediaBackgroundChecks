History
[India.com](https://www.india.com/about-us/) is an Indian news and media website based in Mumbai, India. India.com was launched in 2011 by Zee Media, an Indian mass media company owned by the Essel Group.
The website focuses on news, politics, sports, culture and entertainment, lifestyle, and Bollywood Gossip. In their own
[words,](https://www.timesinternet.in/products) they describe themselves as “Indiatimes is the world’s largest lifestyle network.” [Subhash Chandra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Subhash_Chandra) is the Chairperson and founder of Zee Media. [Read our report on how Government influences media in India.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/india-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Zee Media](https://www.india.com/about-us/) is the owner of India.com, an Indian mass media company owned by the Essel Group. Founded in 1926, Essel Group is India’s largest media and entertainment group, and [Subhash Chandra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Subhash_Chandra) is the group’s chairperson. Revenue is based on [advertising.](https://www.india.com/advertise-with-us/)
Analysis / Bias
In review, India.com publishes articles with emotionally loaded language and a pro-Narendra Modi tone (Current Prime Minister and right-wing nationalist party
[BJP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bharatiya_Janata_Party) leader) such as [“US Billionaire Ray Dalio Calls Narendra Modi ‘One Of The Best Leaders’ in The World.”](https://www.india.com/buzz/us-billionaire-ray-dalio-calls-narendra-modi-one-of-the-best-leaders-in-the-world-3835043/) However, they also cover the opposition’s critics of the Modi Government, such as [“Modi-Shah Not Bothered That CAA Will Shred India’s Soul, Sonia Gandhi at Bharat Bachao Rally | Highlights”](https://www.india.com/news/india/bharat-bachao-rally-live-news-and-updates-congress-corners-centre-over-caa-economic-slowdown-and-job-crisis-3877442/).
India.com also uses sensational headlines covering Bollywood celebrity gossip, such as
[“Shehnaz Gill Calls Paras Chhabra Her ‘First Love’, Siddharth Shukla Licks Off Chocolate From Arti Singh.”](https://www.india.com/showbiz/tv-news-bigg-boss-13-shehnaz-gill-calls-paras-chhabra-her-first-love-siddharth-shukla-licks-off-chocolate-from-arti-singh-3885438/) Regarding sourcing, they hyperlink to themselves and utilize [PTI](http://www.ptinews.com/aboutpti/aboutus.aspx) (The Press Trust of India Ltd.,) news agency.
When covering world news about the USA, they tend to cover the former Trump administration with a neutral tone, such as
[“Donald Trump Becomes 3rd President in US History to be Impeached.”](https://www.india.com/news/world/donald-trump-becomes-3rd-president-in-us-history-to-be-impeached-3882674/) On the other hand, they report negatively on President Joe Biden [Media Decries Joe Biden’s Statement on Afghanistan As One of the Most Shameful in History](https://www.india.com/news/world/media-decries-joe-bidens-statement-on-afghanistan-as-one-of-the-most-shameful-in-history-4892569/). Generally, news reporting is mostly factual while holding a right-leaning editorial bias.
