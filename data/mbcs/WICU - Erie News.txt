History
Founded in 1949, WICU – Erie News is an NBC-affiliated television station licensed to Erie, Pennsylvania. erienewsnow.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Erie region.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lilly Broadcasting, based in Pennsylvania, owns WICU. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WICU – Erie News publishes news for the Erie area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Man Arrested for DUI after Leading Police on High-Speed Chase, Knocking out Power, Injuring Passenger](https://www.erienewsnow.com/story/47793582/man-arrested-for-dui-after-leading-police-on-highspeed-chase-knocking-out-power-injuring-passenger). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WICU – Erie News does not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
