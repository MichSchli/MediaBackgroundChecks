History
Founded in 2008 by Vladimir Lyubovny, VladTV is a website that covers hip hops and “urban news.” According to their
[about page](https://www.vladtv.com/page/about), “VladTV is the world’s leading source of celebrity interviews and urban news.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Vladimir Lyubovny lists himself as the founder and CEO of VladTV, but the website lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
VladTV publishes interviews with hip hops artists, DJs and influential people within the urban music industry. The website also publishes national political news interspersed with music content. Interviews and music-related news are originally produced and often are not opinionated
[EXCLUSIVE: Fredro Starr on Young Dolph’s Murder: We Lost Another Hip Hop King](https://www.vladtv.com/article/278961/fredro-starr-on-young-dolphs-murder-we-lost-another-hip-hop-king).
Politically related news comes from other sources either through being republished or summarized. These stories are often low on opinion and reflect the bias of the sources. A sourcing review shows that more favor the left, such as
[NBC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/), [Axios](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/axios/), and [Newsweek](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/newsweek/). Editorially, political story selection favors the left with stories such as [Rep. Majorie Taylor Greene: Kwanzaa is a Fake Religion Created by Psychopath](https://www.vladtv.com/article/278987/rep-majorie-taylor-greene-kwanzaa-is-a-fake-religion-created-by?fbclid=IwAR3PCQEVXieGoHDtIHYab3mHmP1xaZTPyRBGqE1rwwccuOajtxW6ObyLlYA), and [Donald Trump Uses Antisemitic Tropes During Interview with Israeli Reporter](https://www.vladtv.com/article/278756/donald-trump-uses-antisemitic-tropes-during-interview-with-israeli-reporter). The news is generally reported mostly factually and with moderate left-leaning bias in story selection.
