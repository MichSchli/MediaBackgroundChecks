History
Founded in 1980, WUPA – CBS Atlanta is a CW-owned-and-operated television station through CW 69 licensed to Atlanta, Georgia, United States. The associated website is atlanta.cbslocal.com that features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Atlanta Metro area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WUPA – CBS Atlanta is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the WUPA – CBS Atlanta website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater Atlanta Metro region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording: [DNA Extracted In The Decades-Old Atlanta Child Murders Case.](https://atlanta.cbslocal.com/2021/07/19/atlanta-child-murders-wayne-williams-dna-cold-case-1/) A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published from other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WUPA – CBS Atlanta does not publish op-eds; however, they do report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[President Biden To Make First Primetime Address Later This Week Marking Roughly 1-Year Anniversary Of COVID-19 Pandemic In US](https://atlanta.cbslocal.com/2021/03/08/president-biden-first-primetime-address-1-year-anniversary-covid-19-pandemic/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
