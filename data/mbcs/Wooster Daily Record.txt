History
The Wooster Daily Record is a newspaper that serves Wooster, Ohio, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. the-daily-record.com.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Wooster Daily Record. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Wooster Daily Record covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Wooster, Ohio, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Most major Ohio rivers meeting water quality standards despite algal blooms, EPA reports](https://www.the-daily-record.com/story/news/local/stark-county/2023/08/09/ohio-epa-86-of-major-rivers-meet-water-quality-standards/70512062007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Wooster Daily Record does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Trump indictment recap: Jack Smith details how Trump allegedly tried to steal 2020 election](https://www.the-daily-record.com/story/news/politics/2023/08/01/donald-trump-indicted-election-fraud-live-updates/70476879007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
