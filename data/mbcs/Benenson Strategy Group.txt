History
The Benenson Strategy Group (BSG) is a research organization based in New York City. According to their
[about page](https://www.bsgco.com/), “From the war room to the board room, BSG helps clients meet their challenges head-on: from competitive threats to crisis management; from rebranding to building reputation and strengthening trust. From winning campaigns to growing market share.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Benenson Strategy Group is a privately owned company. Revenue is generated through the sales of services.
Analysis / Bias
The Benenson Strategy Group conducts political research and polling on a wide range of topics, including elections. The website publishes news related to politics that moderately favors the left like this
[Biden Has a Critical Advantage for 2024. He Should Make It Known](https://www.bsgco.com/post/biden-has-a-critical-advantage-for-2024-he-should-make-it-known). BSG also states that they provide research for [mostly Democrats](https://www.bsgco.com/clients), such as Obama 2008 and several congresspersons. [FiveThirtyEight](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fivethirtyeight/), an expert on measuring and rating pollster performance, has [evaluated 21 polls](https://projects.fivethirtyeight.com/pollster-ratings/) by BSG, earning 1.4 stars for accuracy, indicating they are Mixed Factual by MBFC’s criteria. They also conclude that their polling slightly favors the Left with a score of +1.2, which equates to a Left-Center polling bias. In general, BSG is considered moderately accurate and demonstrates a left-leaning bias in polling.
