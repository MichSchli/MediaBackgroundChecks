History
Ihealthtube does not have an about page; however, on their homepage, they explain, “iHealthTube.com is a free educational resource that delivers hard-to-find information on natural and alternative treatments.” A
[Whois search](http://whois.domaintools.com/ihealthtube.com) reveals the domain was purchased in 2007. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Ownership is not disclosed; however, the domain is registered to Scott Peters of Fargo, North Dakota. The website is funded through donations and online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, iHealthtube is a Youtube channel and website that primarily publishes
[alternative medicine](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-alternative-medicine/) pseudoscience and a moderate amount of conspiracies. iHealthtube promotes miracle [natural cures for cancer](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-alternative-cancer-treatments/), [anti-vaccination](https://www.ihealthtube.com/video/vaccines-could-be-causing-these-serious-conditions) [propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/), and a fair amount of [big pharma](https://www.ihealthtube.com/video/big-pharma-blame-lack-cancer-cures-watch) and [GMO conspiracies](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-gmo-dangers/).
They appear to have stopped publishing in 2021, but the website remains online.
