History
The Cooperstown Crier is a newspaper published in Cooperstown, New York. coopercrier.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Cooperstown Crier. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Cooperstown Crier covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Cooperstown, New York region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Otsego board approves weighted voting](https://www.coopercrier.com/news/local_news/otsego-board-approves-weighted-voting/article_b2187f57-e166-5a7e-bf11-9bce8070ec60.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
