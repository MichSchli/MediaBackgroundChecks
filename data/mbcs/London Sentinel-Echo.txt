History
The London Sentinel-Echo is a newspaper published in London, Kentucky. sentinel-echo.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The London Sentinel-Echo. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The London Sentinel-Echo covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the London, Kentucky, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Governor Beshear visits London with funding announcements](https://www.sentinel-echo.com/community/governor-beshear-visits-london-with-funding-announcements/article_090b165e-ba54-11ee-afa5-373d2d620aef.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
