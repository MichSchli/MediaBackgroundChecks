History
WPSD – Paducah News is an
[NBC-affiliated](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/) TV station based in Paducah, Kentucky. wpsdlocal6.com delivers local news, hometown politics, business news, local events, and sports.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The TV Station is owned by
[Paxton Media Group,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paxton_Media_Group) which owns several right-leaning newspapers across the USA, such as the [Elkhart Truth](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-elkhart-truth/). Revenue is derived from advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WPSD – Paducah News reports national news by republishing articles from credible newsagents such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/). They report local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias using neutral headlines, such as [Carlisle County man indicted on murder charge](https://www.wpsdlocal6.com/news/carlisle-county-man-indicted-on-murder-charge/article_4671f8ca-b728-11ee-bd2b-1fabd95d4b36.html). All information is properly sourced.
Editorially, there is a reasonable balance between left and right-leaning opinions, with slightly more favoring the right. In general, the station is fact-based and holds a
[slight right-leaning editorial bias](https://www.wpsdlocal6.com/american-truth/american-truth-capitalism-and-socialism/article_b4fb3b5a-6306-11ea-b3e0-db58e770aa01.html).
