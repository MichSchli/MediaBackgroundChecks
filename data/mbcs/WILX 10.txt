History
Founded in 1959 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, WILX 10 serves Lansing, Michigan, and the surrounding area. Wilx.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WILX 10 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WILX 10 provides news coverage for Lansing, Michigan, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Suspect arraigned on multiple charges following home invasion in Bingham Twp.](https://www.wilx.com/2021/06/25/suspect-arraigned-multiple-charges-following-home-invasion-bingham-twp/) The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WILX 10 does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
