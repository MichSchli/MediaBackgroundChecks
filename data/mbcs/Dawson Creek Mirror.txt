History
Dawson Creek Mirror is an English-language newspaper in Dawson Creek, British Columbia, Canada. dawsoncreekmirror.ca is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Dawson Creek Mirror. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Dawson Creek Mirror publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Dawson Creek, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[RCMP still look for answers to October 2022 homicide](https://www.dawsoncreekmirror.ca/local-news/rcmp-still-look-for-answers-to-october-2022-homicide-6767328). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Dawson Creek Mirror’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Opinion: If you struggle with mental health or addiction, I see you and I hear you](https://www.dawsoncreekmirror.ca/opinion/opinion-if-you-struggle-with-mental-health-or-addiction-i-see-you-and-i-hear-you-6744046). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
