History
The Williamsport Sun-Gazette is a
[morning newspaper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Williamsport_Sun-Gazette) published seven days a week in Williamsport, Pennsylvania in Lycoming County. Its earliest antecedents date to 1801. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Newspaper is owned by
[Ogden Newspapers Inc.,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ogden_Newspapers_Inc.) which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the eastern half of the USA. Ogden has historically donated to Republican candidates. According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), since 1992, they have [donated 93% to Republicans](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000036358&cycle=2018). [Robert Nutting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Nutting) is the current CEO. The paper is funded through both advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Williamsport Sun-Gazette publishes national and international news with low bias by using sources such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). Locally and editorially, the Sun-Gazette presents with a right-leaning bias that favors conservative causes.
