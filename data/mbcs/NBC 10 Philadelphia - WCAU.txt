History
Founded in 1946, NBC 10 Philadelphia – WCAU is an NBC-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States. NBCphiladelphia.com is the associated website covering news, weather, sports, crime, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
NBC 10 Philadelphia – WCAU is owned by NBCUniversal a subsidiary of Comcast, which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, NBC 10 Philadelphia – WCAU covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[2 Suspects Arrested in South Street Mass Shooting](https://www.nbcphiladelphia.com/news/local/girard-college-advisor-among-3-killed-in-south-street-mass-shooting/3262391/). Most stories are accompanied by videos from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other NBC-owned stations provide national and world news. All information is sourced from first-hand accounts and other credible media sources.
Editorially, NBC 10 Philadelphia – WCAU typically does not produce opinion content; however, they report on national politics with a slight left-leaning bias in wording and story selection
[Biden’s Decision on Student Loan Forgiveness Is Likely to Come in July Or August, Report Says](https://www.nbcphiladelphia.com/news/business/money-report/bidens-decision-on-student-loan-forgiveness-is-likely-to-come-in-july-or-august-report-finds/3262698/).
