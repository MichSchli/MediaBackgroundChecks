History
The Chillicothe Gazette is a newspaper that serves Chillicothe, Ohio, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. chillicothegazette.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Chillicothe Gazette. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Chillicothe Gazette covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Chillicothe, Ohio, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[‘We need to break the cycle and give kids hope.’ How Marion County closed its youth lockup](https://www.chillicothegazette.com/story/news/crime/2023/11/11/what-are-possible-solutions-to-juvenile-prison-crisis-in-ohio/70297566007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Chillicothe Gazette does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Ohio is creating ‘monsters’ at youth prisons. DeWine must act now to save kids | Our View](https://www.chillicothegazette.com/story/opinion/editorials/2023/11/11/ohio-dewine-fix-dys-juvenile-detention/71440064007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
