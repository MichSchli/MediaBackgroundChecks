History
Founded in November 2020 and based in the United Kingdom, The Daily Expose publishes Covid-19 conspiracy and anti-vaccination news. The website completely lacks transparency as they do not provide an about page, disclose authors, editors, or owners.
On April 2, 2021, The Daily Expose
[reports](https://dailyexpose.co.uk/2021/04/02/follow-us-dailyexposenews/) that Twitter suspended them for 12 hours due to violations. [Read our profile on UK Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Daily Expose does not disclose ownership on the website. Advertising and donations are the primary means of revenue generation.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Daily Expose reports on Covid-19 and Vaccines from a conspiratorial perspective. Articles and headlines offer sensationalized headlines such as this
[SHOCKING! – U.K. Government release the 9th report on Adverse Reactions to the Covid Vaccines](https://dailyexpose.co.uk/2021/04/02/shocking-u-k-government-release-9th-report-on-adverse-reactions-to-the-covid-vaccines/). They also report patently false information such as this [INVESTIGATION: 110K Deaths? NHS Data shows only 3000 people have died of COVID-19](https://dailyexpose.co.uk/2021/02/17/investigation-nhs-data-shows-only-3000-people-have-died-of-covid-19/). According to IFCN fact-checker [FullFact](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/full-fact-uk/), this is [not true](https://fullfact.org/online/daily-expose-3000-covid-deaths/).
The majority of this website promotes
[anti-vaccination propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/) such as this [Vaccination – The Greatest Lie ever told?](https://dailyexpose.co.uk/2021/04/03/vaccination-the-greatest-lie-ever-told-part-2/) They also promote [anti-mask propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-mask-misinformation/), such as this [Face masks cause PERMANENT brain damage, according to Neurologist](https://dailyexpose.co.uk/2021/02/02/face-masks-cause-brain-damage/). [There isn’t evidence](https://correctiv.org/faktencheck/2020/10/19/nein-das-tragen-von-alltagsmasken-fuehrt-nicht-zu-sauerstoffmangel-im-gehirn/) to suggest that wearing a mask is harmful. In general, The Daily Expose promotes quackery-level pseudoscience and tin-foil hat conspiracy theories.
