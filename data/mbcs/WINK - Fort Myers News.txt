History
Founded in 1954, WINK – Fort Myers News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Fort Myers, Florida, United States. Winknews.com is the associated website that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Fort Myers, FL area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WINK – Fort Myers News is owned by the McBride family through the Fort Myers Broadcasting Company. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WINK – Fort Myers News reports on news, crime, sports, weather, and entertainment for the greater Fort Myers, FL area. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Bicyclist injured in hit-and-run crash in Lehigh Acres, search on for vehicle](https://www.winknews.com/2022/07/09/bicyclist-injured-in-hit-and-run-crash-search-on-for-vehicle/). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically covers the national news.
Editorially, WINK – Fort Myers News does not publish editorial pieces, and when reporting on national politics, there is minimal bias as the primary source is usually the Associated Press. In general, they present fact-based, low-biased news.
