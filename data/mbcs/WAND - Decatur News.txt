History
Founded in 1953 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, WAND – Decatur News serves Decatur, Illinois, and the surrounding area. wandtv.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
Funded by / Ownership
WAND – Decatur News is owned by
[Block Communications](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Block_Communications), which also owns Pittsburgh Press and the [Pittsburgh Post-Gazette](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pittsburgh-post-gazette/). The TV Station and website are funded through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WAND – Decatur News provides news coverage for Decatur, Illinois, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Head-on collision on Lake Shore Drive leaves two injured](https://www.wandtv.com/news/head-on-collision-on-lake-shore-drive-leaves-two-injured/article_f5b2810e-7040-11ed-b565-7f7bb2a5ebd8.html). The national and international news is provided through other Block Communications affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WAND – Decatur News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
