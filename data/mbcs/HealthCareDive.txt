History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, HealthCare Dive is an online news platform that focuses on the Healthcare industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.healthcaredive.com/about/), “Healthcare Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping healthcare. “ [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates HealthCare Dive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and FacilitiesDive. HealthCare Dive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
HealthCare Dive publishes news and information related to the healthcare industry. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[Molina loses Medicaid contract in Virginia](https://www.healthcaredive.com/news/molina-virginia-medicaid-contract-loss/709017/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like
[Will the Biden administration’s short-term plan rule increase the uninsured rate?](https://www.healthcaredive.com/news/biden-short-term-rule-uninsurance-rate/687016/) In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
