History
Founded in 1949, KGO – ABC7 News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/) television network licensed to San Francisco, California, United States. abc7news.com is the associated website that provides news, weather, and sports for the San Francisco Metropolitan region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KGO – ABC7 News is owned by the ABC Owned Television Stations subsidiary of
[The Walt Disney Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the KGO – ABC7 News website covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the San Francisco Metro area. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[SF couple gets $1,500 fine for parking in their driveway despite doing so for nearly 40 years](https://abc7news.com/san-francisco-parking-violations-driveway-carport/12009975/). Many local stories contain embedded videos from the TV station. National and world news is covered by [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/), [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [ESPN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/espn/), and other ABC stations.
Editorially, KGO – ABC7 News does not provide opinion pieces; however, they report on politics. They provide original reporting with a slight left-leaning bias, such as this
[Not prosecuting Trump for Jan. 6 would fuel a ‘much graver threat,’ Liz Cheney says](https://abc7news.com/liz-cheney-donald-trump-ronald-reagan-library/12014272/). They also rely on political news from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
