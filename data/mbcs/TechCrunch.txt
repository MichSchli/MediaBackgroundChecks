History
TechCrunch is an
[American online publisher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/TechCrunch) of technology industry news. It primarily covers the business side of tech, reporting breaking tech news, opinions on new products, a thorough analysis of emerging trends in tech, and regular profiling of popular new tech businesses, from early-stage startups to publicly traded companies. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Apollo Global Management, Inc, owns TechCrunch. In September 2021, Apollo bought the Verizon Media group consisting of TechCrunch, Yahoo, and AOL for 5 billion dollars. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
