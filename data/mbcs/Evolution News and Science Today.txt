History
[Founded in 2004](https://whois.domaintools.com/evolutionnews.org), Evolution News and Science Today is a part of [The Discovery Institute](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/discovery-institute/), which is a non-profit Christian public policy think tank based in Seattle, Washington, best known for its advocacy of the pseudoscientific principle of intelligent design. According to their [about page](https://evolutionnews.org/about/), “original reporting and analysis about evolution, neuroscience, bioethics, intelligent design and other science-related issues, including breaking news about scientific research.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Evolution News and Science Today is owned by the Discovery Institute, which is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that is primarily funded through donations.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the website publishes information on intelligent design, conservative economics, and many other Christian perspectives. News articles focus on those that are opposed to Darwin’s theory of evolution and often link to other sources they own, such as
[Life’s Origin — A “Mystery” Made Accessible](https://evolutionnews.org/2020/12/lifes-origin-a-mystery-made-accessible/). The Discovery Institute also publishes news from a right-leaning economic perspective, such as this: [Discovery Fellow Christopher Rufo with Tucker Carlson on the West Coast Shoplifting Boom](https://www.discovery.org/a/rufo-on-tucker-the-west-coast-shoplifting-boom/).
In general, this source publishes pseudoscience related to biblical creationism. Finally, according to the left-leaning
[SPLC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/southern-poverty-law-center/), the Discovery Institute has [received funding](https://www.splcenter.org/fighting-hate/intelligence-report/2009/nevadan-james-mccrink-funds-hate-groups-through-his-foundation) from a known hate group. In general, they are a website that promotes pseudoscience regarding evolution.
