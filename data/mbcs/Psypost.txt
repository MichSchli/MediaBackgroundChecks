History
Founded in 2010, PsyPost is a psychology and neuroscience news website dedicated to reporting the latest research on human behavior, cognition, and society.
[They report](https://www.psypost.org/about) their “mission is to spread information about social science and neuroscience research.”
This website lacks transparency in ownership as well as authorship of articles.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Psypost is owned by Eric W. Dolan, who is also the founder. Revenue is derived through advertising.

This story comes from Cognitive science and is credible [Study uncovers some electrophysiological processes associated with the Dunning–Kruger effect](https://www.psypost.org/2021/01/study-uncovers-some-electrophysiological-processes-associated-with-the-dunning-kruger-effect-59390).
Editorially, Psypost sticks with news and info related to psychology.
