History
Godlike Productions (GLP) is a website/community of conspiracy theorists and various other kinds of woo-believers. Its main feature is a forum, though it also provides image and video hosting and a podcast service. In many ways, it’s similar to
[Above Top Secret](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/above-top-secret/).
Funded by / Ownership
Godlike Productions does not disclose ownership, and revenue is derived through sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Godlike Productions is primarily a forum that covers conspiracy theories and pseudoscience. They are almost a clone of
[Above Top Secret](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/above-top-secret/). Regardless, they promote conspiracy theories and pseudoscience that are usually debunked. We don’t usually do this, but [Rationalwiki](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/rationalwiki/) has already done the work, [See here.](https://rationalwiki.org/wiki/Godlike_Productions) In general, this source is not credible on any level. See Fact Checks below.
