History
Founded in 1883 (named Coconino Sun initially, taking its current name in 1946), the
[Arizona Daily Sun](https://www.linkedin.com/company/arizona-daily-sun) is a regional daily newspaper based in Flagstaff, Arizona. The Arizona Daily Sun delivers news from politics and business to sports, lifestyle, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Lee Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lee_Enterprises), which, according to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), has donated 88% of money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) since 1990. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, for national news, the Arizona Daily Sun reports news with minimal bias using credible media sources such as the
[Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) Local News is reported with minimal loaded words and bias such as [“Arizona teachers rally at State Capitol”](http://azdailysun.com/news/local/arizona-teachers-rally-at-state-capitol/article_daed51b3-9004-559e-b889-47fdf883107e.html) and sourced through quotes. They also feature guest columns from the [Philadelphia Inquirer](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/philadelphia-inquirer/), the Associated Press, and local journalists. Some editorial columns have a left-leaning bias such as: “ [McMaster’s exit, Bolton’s arrival foretell dangerous policy shift.”](http://azdailysun.com/opinion/columnists/guest-column-mcmaster-s-exit-bolton-s-arrival-foretell-dangerous/article_825b0116-10a6-593f-a152-bb8d9d29fb07.html) In general, news stories are factual with a slight left-leaning bias editorially.
