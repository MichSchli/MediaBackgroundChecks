History
The Effingham Daily News is a newspaper published in the city of Effingham, Illinois. effinghamdailynews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Effingham Daily News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Effingham Daily News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Effingham, Illinois, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Apartment fire Friday evening](https://www.effinghamdailynews.com/news/local_news/apartment-fire-friday-evening/article_2582e474-2b21-11ed-8a8c-63bb8db7a25a.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
