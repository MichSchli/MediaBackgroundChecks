History
The Jeffersonville News and Tribune is a newspaper published in the city of Jeffersonville, Indiana. newsandtribune.com.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Jeffersonville News and Tribune. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Jeffersonville News and Tribune covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Jeffersonville, Indiana, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[New district maps likely to fortify Indiana GOP supermajority](https://www.newsandtribune.com/news/new-district-maps-likely-to-fortify-indiana-gop-supermajority/article_09d88ccc-468f-11ed-b1ba-ff891ecf7d7e.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
