History
The Clanton Advertiser is a newspaper published in Clanton, Alabama. clantonadvertiser.com covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Clanton Advertiser is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Clanton Advertiser primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Gridiron action begins this week in Chilton County](https://www.clantonadvertiser.com/2022/08/15/gridiron-action-begins-this-week-in-chilton-county/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Clanton Advertiser labels their opinion section appropriately with more op-eds favoring the right. In general, the Clanton Advertiser is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
