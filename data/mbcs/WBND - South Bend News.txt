History
WBND – South Bend News is an ABS-affiliated television station licensed to South Bend, Indiana, United States. abc57.com is the associated website covering news, weather, sports, crime, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WBND – South Bend News is owned by
[Weigel Broadcasting Company](https://www.weigelbroadcasting.com/local/), which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WBND – South Bend News covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[U.S. 6 closed in Walkerton for crash](https://www.abc57.com/news/us-6-closed-in-walkerton-for-crash). Most stories are accompanied by videos from the TV station. [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/) and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) provide national and world news. All information is sourced from first-hand accounts and other credible media sources.
Editorially, WBND – South Bend News typically does not produce opinion content; however, they report on national politics with a slight left-leaning bias in wording and story selection
[President Joe Biden will visit Israel in high-stakes trip](https://abc57.com/news/president-joe-biden-will-visit-israel-in-high-stakes-trip). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering political issues.
