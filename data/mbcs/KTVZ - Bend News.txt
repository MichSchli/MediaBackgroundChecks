History
Founded in 1977, KTVZ – Bend News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Bend, Oregon. The associated website is ktvz.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns KTVZ – Bend News and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KTVZ – Bend News website publishes news for the greater Bend, Oregon area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:
[Jefferson County firefighters stop 3 small blazes along Hwy. 26; flames destroy fence, 5 tons of hay.](https://ktvz.com/news/fire-alert/2022/08/30/jefferson-county-firefighters-stop-3-small-blazes-along-hwy-26-flames-destroy-fence-5-tons-of-hay/) Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [CBS News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/), [CNN Newswire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[Unaffiliated gubernatorial candidate Johnson gets on ballot.](https://ktvz.com/news/ap-oregon-northwest/2022/08/25/unaffiliated-gubernatorial-candidate-johnson-gets-on-ballot-2/) Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoiding using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
