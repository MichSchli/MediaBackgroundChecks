History
KRCB – NPR – Rohnert Park is an NPR FM radio station (104.9 FM) based in Rohnert Park, California, serving the surrounding area.

Funded by / Ownership
KRCB – NPR – Rohnert Park is owned and broadcast by Northern California Public Media. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
