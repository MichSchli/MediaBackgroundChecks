History
Founded in 1955 and a CBS-affiliated television station, WTVY News4 serves Dothan, Alabama, and the surrounding area. Wtvy.com is the associated website for the news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTVY News4 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WTVY.com provides news coverage for Dothan, Alabama, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:

International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WTVY News4 does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
