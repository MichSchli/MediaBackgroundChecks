History
The Washington C H Record Herald is a newspaper based in Washington Court House, Ohio, serving the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at recordherald.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Record Herald and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Washington C H Record Herald publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Adena Greenfield Medical Center expansion continues](https://www.recordherald.com/features/health/73783/adena-greenfield-medical-center-expansion-continues). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Record Herald leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
