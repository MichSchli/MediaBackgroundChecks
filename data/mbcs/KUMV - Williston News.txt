History
Founded in 1957 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, KUMV – Williston News serves Williston, North Dakota, and the surrounding area. kfyrtv.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KUMV – Williston News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KUMV – Williston News provides news coverage for Williston, North Dakota, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Buildings, teachers, and more discussed during roundtable with Williston School Superintendent](https://www.kfyrtv.com/2022/10/03/buildings-teachers-more-discussed-during-roundtable-with-williston-school-superintendent/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KUMV – Williston News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
