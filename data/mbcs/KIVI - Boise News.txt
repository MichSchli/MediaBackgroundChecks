History
Founded in 1974, KIVI – Boise News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Nampa, Idaho United States. Kivitv.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KIVI – Boise News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KIVI – Boise News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Boise, Idaho area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[St. Luke’s suing over protest that shut down hospital campuses in March](https://www.kivitv.com/news/st-lukes-suing-over-protest-that-shut-down-hospital-campuses-in-march). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[President Biden calls on Congress to pass his plan to battle inflation](https://www.kivitv.com/news/national/president-biden-to-unveil-plan-to-fight-inflation). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
