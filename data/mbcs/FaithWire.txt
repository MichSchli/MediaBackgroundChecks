History
FaithWire is a news and opinion website dedicated to Christian Conservative news. FaithWire was launched by the
[Christian Broadcasting Network (CBN)](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/christian-broadcasting-network-launches-faithwirecom-300331835.html) in 2016, which we rate as a [Conspiracy-Pseudoscience](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/christian-broadcasting-network-cbn/) source. It should be noted they are run independently of CBN and is focused more on news content. [Read our country media profile of the USA.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Faithwire is owned by the Christian Broadcasting Network (CBN), which Televangelist Pat Robertson founded in 1961. Pat Robertson has made controversial and
[false claims](http://www.politifact.com/personalities/pat-robertson/statements/?list=speaker) throughout his long television career.
Analysis / Bias
In review, all articles favor right-leaning positions such as support for
[pro-life](http://www.faithwire.com/2018/06/29/another-gosnell-abortionist-robert-rho-imprisoned-for-killing-woman-in-grisly-late-term-abortion/), [anti-gay marriage](http://www.faithwire.com/2017/08/22/gods-word-is-clear-hillsong-church-pastor-speaks-out-on-gay-marriage/), [creationism](http://www.faithwire.com/2018/06/13/were-teaching-children-lies-hollywood-actress-takes-on-evolution-education-and-more/), and most other political positions affiliated with Christian conservatism. FaithWire uses minimal loaded words in its articles and, for the most part, uses credible sourcing. Faithwire is strongly pro-Trump as evidenced by this article: [President Trump Gives Encouraging Pro-Life Speech, Cites This Powerful Bible Verse.](http://www.faithwire.com/2018/05/23/president-trump-gives-encouraging-pro-life-speech-cites-this-powerful-bible-verse/)
