History

The website lacks transparency as they do not offer an about page, but they do list Alex Jones as the [Director and Publisher](https://www.prisonplanet.com/contact-information.html). The website frequently promotes far-right conspiracy theories and pseudoscience. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
