History
The Tri-City News is an English-language newspaper in Port Coquitlam, British Columbia, Canada. tricitynews.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Tri-City News. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
The Tri-City News publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Port Coquitlam, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Single B.C. legal regulator legislation delayed to spring 2024](https://www.tricitynews.com/highlights/single-bc-legal-regulator-legislation-delayed-to-spring-2024-7597014). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Tri-City News’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Trevor Hancock: The economy must serve the people and respect the Earth’s limits](https://www.tricitynews.com/opinion/trevor-hancock-the-economy-must-serve-the-people-and-respect-the-earths-limits-7591573). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
