History
The Tri-County Independent is a newspaper that serves Honesdale, Pennsylvania, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. tricountyindependent.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Tri-County Independent. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Tri-County Independent covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Honesdale, Pennsylvania, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Shapiro touts budget proposal, cites benefits to Honesdale’s downtown upgrade plans](https://www.tricountyindependent.com/story/news/local/2024/02/22/governor-josh-shapiro-tour-honesdale-budget-proposal/72633597007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Tri-County Independent does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[‘Healing Together’: Reality of sexual assault is more familiar than you think](https://www.tricountyindependent.com/story/opinion/columns/guest/2023/04/13/opinion-take-back-the-night-highlights-reality-of-sexual-violence-vip-pike-wayne-poconos-pa/70107764007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
