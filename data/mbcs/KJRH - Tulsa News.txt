History
Founded in 1954, KJRH – Tulsa News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Tulsa, Oklahoma, United States. kjrh.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KJRH – Tulsa News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KJRH – Tulsa News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Tulsa, Oklahoma, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Police investigating fatal apartment shooting near 51st and Memorial](https://www.kjrh.com/news/local-news/police-investigating-apartment-shooting-near-51st-and-memorial). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden reveals President Carter asked him to deliver eulogy](https://www.kjrh.com/news/national/biden-reveals-president-carter-asked-him-to-deliver-eulogy). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
