History
[Founded in 2016](https://whois.domaintools.com/freewestmedia.com), Free West Media is a conservative media outlet that aims to present broad and unbiased daily coverage of European and worldwide news from a western perspective. According to their [about page](https://freewestmedia.com/about/), “The people of Europe are revolting. They reject the old parties that have ruled for decades and demand access to propaganda-free news reports. In Germany, this mistrust in old-time’s media manifests itself most clearly in the German slogan “Lügenpresse The domain appears to originate from Sweden..” [Read our profile on Sweden’s Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/sweden-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Free West Media lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership. Donations appear to be the primary source of revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Free West Media reports summarized and republished news stories with a strong conservative bias. There is frequent use of loaded language such as this
[No surprise: Dereck Chauvin was found guilty on all three counts of murder](https://freewestmedia.com/2021/04/21/no-surprise-dereck-chauvin-found-guilty-on-all-three-counts-of-murder/).
Free West Media frequently reports conspiracy theories related to Election fraud during the 2020 election
[Majority of US voters believe the 2020 presidential election was rigged](https://freewestmedia.com/2021/04/21/majority-of-us-voters-believe-the-2020-presidential-election-was-rigged/), and constant Covid-19 denialism, such as [The developing Orwellian Covid terminology](https://freewestmedia.com/2021/03/24/the-developing-orwellian-covid-terminology/) and [Confusion about Covid-19 deaths is intentional, says German doctor](https://freewestmedia.com/2021/03/12/confusion-about-covid-19-deaths-is-intentional-says-german-doctor/). Free West Media also relies on poor sources to support their claims, such as [Steven Crowder](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/louder-with-crowder/). Generally, the news is reported with a strong right-leaning bias and is not always factual.
