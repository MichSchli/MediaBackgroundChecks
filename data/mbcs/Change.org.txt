History
Change.org is a petition website operated by Change.org, Inc., an American certified B corporation that claims to have over 408 million users and hosts sponsored campaigns for organizations. The purpose of Change is to facilitate petitions by the general public. According to their
[about page](https://www.change.org/about) “On Change.org, people everywhere are starting campaigns, mobilizing supporters, and working with decision-makers to drive solutions.” The current CEO is [Ben Rattray](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ben_Rattray). [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Change.org is a Public Benefit Corporation and is funded through donations to the
[Change.org Foundation](https://changefoundation.org/).
Analysis / Bias
In review, Popular topics of Change.org petitions are economic and criminal justice, human rights, education, environmental protection, animal rights, health, and sustainable food. In general, Change.org caters to a left-leaning audience.
