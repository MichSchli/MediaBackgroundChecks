History
NewsCenterMaine.com is the news website for television stations WCSH and WLBZ in Portland, Maine. The
[website](https://www.newscentermaine.com/about-us) covers news, sports, weather, traffic, and crime for the greater Portland area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
NewsCenterMaine.com is owned and operated by TEGNA Inc., which operates 66 television stations in 54 markets. They state TEGNA’s purpose is to serve the greater good of our communities. Revenue is based on advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, NewsCenterMaine.com reports local news primarily through TV news videos and accompanying text. Local news is presented with minimally loaded wording such as this:
[Abbott Laboratories receives federal approval for COVID-19 test that offers results in 15 minutes](https://www.newscentermaine.com/article/news/health/coronavirus/abbott-laboratories-receives-federal-approval-for-covid-19-test-that-offers-results-in-15-minutes/97-48be7053-c3e6-4195-a5d1-162a96062d31). National and International news is provided by the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other TEGNA TV stations.
NewsCenterMaine.com also has a section dedicated to politics covering national politics through the Associated Press and local Maine politics through the TV stations. Editorially more stories favor the left with some loaded wording, such as this
[Maine delegation reacts to Trump’s refusal to commit to a peaceful transition of power if he loses](https://www.newscentermaine.com/article/news/politics/maine-delegation-reacts-to-trumps-refusal-to-commit-to-a-peaceful-transition-of-power-if-he-loses/97-173ec018-f0d2-48bf-a6db-42d61ad94eef). Finally, the website features fact checks from TEGNA’s fact check service [VERIFY Fact check.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/verify-fact-check-bias/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
