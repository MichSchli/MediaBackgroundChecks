History
[Founded in 2020](https://www.dailyposter.com/p/introducing-the-daily-poster) by David Sirota as The Daily Poster, The Lever is a progressive news and opinion website and daily newsletter. [David Sirota](https://davidsirota.com/biography/) is a journalist who is the current editor at [Jacobin](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/jacobin/), an author, a columnist for the [Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/), and a former speechwriter for the Bernie Sanders 2020 presidential campaign. The website primarily covers politics from a progressive left perspective. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Lever is owned and published by David Sirota. Revenue is derived through voluntary subscription fees for full content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Lever publishes political news and opinions from a progressive left perspective. Many articles are written by David Sirota as well as other contributing writers. Articles and headlines often contain moderately loaded language, such as this
[Lawmakers Unify To Give Corporate Donors A License To Kill You.](https://www.dailyposter.com/p/lawmakers-unify-to-give-corporate) This story is adequately sourced from credible media outlets such as [Politico](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/politico/) and [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/). All articles are original journalism.
Editorially, The Lever aligns with the left. They frequently publish articles critical of conservatives, such as this
[Trump Holds Coup Meeting With DeVos-Funded Lawmakers](https://www.dailyposter.com/p/trump-holds-coup-meeting-with-devos). Although they are supportive of the Democratic Party, they are often critical of the corporate establishment Democrats, such as this [The Beltway Left Is Normalizing Corruption And Corporatism](https://www.dailyposter.com/p/the-beltway-left-is-normalizing-corruption). When it comes to positions, they promote [climate change action](https://www.dailyposter.com/p/news-bidens-first-climate-appointment), [civil rights](https://www.dailyposter.com/p/when-barrett-helped-companies-quash), [single-payer healthcare](https://www.dailyposter.com/p/we-are-guaranteed-only-one-thing), and [workerâ€™s rights](https://www.dailyposter.com/p/scoop-amazon-covid-outbreak-followed). In general, the information presented on The The Lever is factual and editorially aligns with the progressive left (Democratic Socialism).
