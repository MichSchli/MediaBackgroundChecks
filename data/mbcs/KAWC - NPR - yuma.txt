History
KAWC – NPR – Yuma is an NPR FM radio station (88.9 FM) based in Yuma, Arizona, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KAWC – NPR – Yuma is owned and broadcast by Arizona Western College. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.

[NPR](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/npr/) and often utilizes minimally loaded language, such as this [Arizona Gov.
