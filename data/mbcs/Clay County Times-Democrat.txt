History
The Clay County Times-Democrat is a daily newspaper published in Piggot, Arkansas. cctimesdemocrat.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for Clay County.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Clay County Times-Democrat is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Clay County Times-Democrat covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Clay County, Arkansas area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Property issues among topics discussed at Rector City Council meeting](https://www.cctimesdemocrat.com/2022/06/08/property-issues-among-topics-discussed-at-rector-city-council-meeting/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
