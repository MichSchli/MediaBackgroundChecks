History
Founded in 1953, KWWL – Waterloo News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Waterloo, Iowa, United States. kwwl.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Waterloo area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns KWWL – Waterloo News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KWWL – Waterloo News publishes news for Waterloo and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as the
[Arlington man arrested on multiple charges after vehicle pursuit](https://www.kwwl.com/news/crime-courts/arlington-man-arrested-on-multiple-charges-after-vehicle-pursuit/article_63afb964-3834-11ed-99b5-3705722b1765.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
