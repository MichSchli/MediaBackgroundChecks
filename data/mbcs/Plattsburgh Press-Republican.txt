History
The Plattsburgh Press-Republican is a newspaper published in Plattsburgh, New York. pressrepublican.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Plattsburgh Press-Republican. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Plattsburgh Press-Republican covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Plattsburgh, New York, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Woman shot by police still in stable condition in hospital](https://www.pressrepublican.com/news/update-woman-shot-by-police-still-in-stable-condition-in-hospital/article_95e2bfbe-a59b-11ee-9017-bfc72336b400.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
