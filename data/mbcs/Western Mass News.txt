History
Founded in 2015, WesternMassNews.com is the website for television stations
[WGGB](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WGGB-TV) and [WSHM](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WSHM-LD), which provides news coverage for the Western Massachusetts region of the United States. The website is transparent in that they offer [staff information](https://www.westernmassnews.com/site/station_info/staff/) and disclose ownership. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WesternmMassNews.com is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, local news is handled by reporters covering crime, sports, weather, traffic, and lifestyle. There is minimal use of loaded language as evidenced by this,
[Police investigating Springfield shooting](https://www.westernmassnews.com/news/police-investigating-springfield-shooting/article_b9c9c1c6-535f-11ea-8279-5b66b9f95882.html). National and international news is often handled by other Gray properties as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, WesternMassNews.com does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
