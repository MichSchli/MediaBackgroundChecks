History
[Founded in 2018](http://whois.domaintools.com/mixi.media), Mixi.Media is a news aggregation website that does not have an about page or ownership information. A domain search reveals the domain is registered privately.
Funded by / Ownership
Mixi.Media does not disclose ownership on the website. Revenue appears to be drawn from online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Mixi.Media aggregates right-leaning news from other sources. A review of sources indicates most come from the right such as
[Newsmax](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/newsmax/), [The Political Insider](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/political-insider/), and [Grabien](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/grabien-news/) as well as many from our questionable lists such as [The Blaze](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-blaze/), [RT](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/rt-news/), [Sputnik](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/sputnik-news/), and [Newsner](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/newsner/). The science category contains numerous [pseudoscience stories](https://mixi.media/?first_init=false&topic=1) from Sputnik. In general, this is a right-leaning news aggregation website that utilizes many poor sources that have poor track records with fact-checkers.
