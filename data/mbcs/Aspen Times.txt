History
Aspen Times is a daily newspaper serving Aspen, Colorado. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Aspen Times is published by Swift Communications, which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the western USA. Ogden Newspapers owns Swift. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Aspen Times publishes news for Aspen, Colorado, and the surrounding area. Local news is covered through reporting with minimal bias in wording, such as
[As trail use surges, so does volunteer interest in maintaining a healthy landscape](https://www.aspentimes.com/news/as-trail-use-surges-so-does-volunteer-interest-in-maintaining-a-healthy-landscape/). The paper and website do not cover national and international news.
A review of editorials demonstrates a reasonable balance without extremes such as this
[Aspen Times editorial: City officials need to pick up pace on restaurant space near new City Hall](https://www.aspentimes.com/opinion/aspen-times-editorial-city-officials-need-to-pick-up-pace-on-restaurant-space-near-new-city-hall/). In general, they exhibit low bias and produce fact-based news.
