History
Founded in 1954, WKBT – La Crosse News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to La Crosse, Wisconsin, United States. news8000.com is the associated news website for the TV Station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WKBT – La Crosse News is owned by
[Morgan Murphy Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morgan_Murphy_Media), which owns 14 TV stations and several radio stations throughout the Midwest. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WKBT – La Crosse News reports news, sports, weather, and crime for the La Crosse Wisconsin region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[La Crosse Public Library starts class to revive sewing skills](https://www.news8000.com/la-crosse-public-library-starts-class-to-revive-sewing-skills/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally provides national and international news.
Editorially, WKBT – La Crosse News does not publish opinion pieces; however, they report on politics primarily through the Associated Press. The news is generally reported factually and with minimal bias due to a lack of editorializing.
