History
Founded in 1949, KOTV – Newson6 is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Tulsa, Oklahoma. Newson6.com is the website for the TV station and its news programming. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Griffin Communications, a media company based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma owns KOTV – Newson6. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KOTV – Newson6 primarily covers local news, crime, entertainment, and sports for the Tulsa, Oklahoma area. Local news is covered with minimal bias through reporters like this
[1 Killed In Multi-Vehicle Crash; All Lanes On Highway 169 Reopened](https://www.newson6.com/story/62c6bc0ff20d7e0727f0b1e4/1-killed-in-multivehicle-crash-all-lanes-on-highway-169-reopened). The Associated Press provides national and international news.
KOTV – Newson6 does not offer editorials but does cover politics with minimal bias, such as this
[Sen. Lankford Introducing Legislation Against Declaring Abortions As Federal Health Emergency](https://www.newson6.com/story/62c7129cffb15f0727d90731/sen-lankford-introducing-legislation-against-declaring-abortions-as-federal-health-emergency). Most political news is derived from third-party sources such as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). In general, news reporting is factual and displays minimal bias.
