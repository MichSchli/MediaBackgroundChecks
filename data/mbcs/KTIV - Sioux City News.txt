History
Founded in 1954 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, KTIV – Sioux City News serves Sioux City, Iowa, and the surrounding area. Ktiv.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTIV – Sioux City News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KTIV – Sioux City News provides news coverage for Sioux City, Iowa, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Multiple counties, including Woodbury, lifting burn bans](https://www.ktiv.com/2022/05/03/multiple-counties-including-woodbury-lifting-burn-bans/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KTIV – Sioux City News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
