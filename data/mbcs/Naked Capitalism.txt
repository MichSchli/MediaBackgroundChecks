History
[Founded in 2005](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Naked_Capitalism) by Yves Smith (Susan Webber), Naked Capitalism is an American financial news and analysis blog that “chronicles the large scale, concerted campaign to reduce the bargaining power and pay of ordinary workers relative to investors and elite technocrats.” According to their [about page](https://www.nakedcapitalism.com/about), “we are now in the midst of a large scale, concerted campaign to reduce the bargaining power and pay of ordinary workers relative to investors and elite technocrats.” [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Naked Capitalism is owned by Aurora Advisors Incorporated. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Naked Capitalism reports news and information on economics and finance. Articles and headlines contain moderately loaded language such as this
[More Evidence That Private Equity Kills: Estimated >20,000 Increase in Nursing Home Deaths, 160,000 Life Years Lost Due to Cuts in Care](https://www.nakedcapitalism.com/2021/02/more-evidence-that-private-equity-kills-estimated-20000-increase-in-nursing-home-deaths-160000-life-years-lost-due-to-cuts-in-care.html). This story contains facts and stats but does not offer hyperlinked sourcing for the original source, however, most articles are properly sourced.
Editorially, Naked Capitalism is not a fan of Former President Donald Trump, but they hold more negative feelings toward corporate-leaning Democrats such a President Joe Biden
[As Trump Touts His ‘Great’ COVID Drugs, the Pharma Cash Flows to Biden, Not Him](https://www.nakedcapitalism.com/2020/10/as-trump-touts-his-great-covid-drugs-the-pharma-cash-flows-to-biden-not-him.html). In general, they hold negative views towards Wall Street and support workers’ rights.
