History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, AutomotiveDive is an online news platform that focuses on the automotive industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.automotivedive.com/about/), “Automotive Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping the automotive industry. The newsletter and website cover topics such as tech, supply chain, regulations & safety, labor, and more.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates AutomotiveDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BankingDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and CybersecurityDive. AutomotiveDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
AutomotiveDive publishes news and information related to the automotive industry. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[Hyundai’s EV manufacturing campus draws 3 more suppliers to Georgia](https://www.automotivedive.com/news/hyundai-metaplant-draws-3-more-suppliers-hwashin-hyundai-industrial-daechang-seat-corp-usa-georgia/699021/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like this
[DOE announces $192M for battery recycling, says lithium storage market could grow tenfold by 2030](https://www.automotivedive.com/news/lithium-battery-market-could-grow-ten-fold-recycling-DOE/652970/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
