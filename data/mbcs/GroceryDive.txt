History
Founded by Sean Griffey, Eli Dickinson, and Ryan Willumson, GroceryDive is an online news platform that focuses on the Grocery industry and is operated by Industry Dive, based in Washington, DC. According to its
[about page](https://www.grocerydive.com/about/), “Grocery Dive provides in-depth journalism and insight into the most impactful news and trends shaping the grocery industry. “ [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Industry Dive,](https://www.industrydive.com/who-we-are/about) now a subsidiary of [Informa Tech](https://www.informatech.com/brands/)—a London-based British group specializing in publishing, business intelligence, and exhibitions—was acquired by Informa PLC and operates GroceryDive along with [other verticals](https://www.industrydive.com/publications) like FoodDive, BioPharmaDive, CFODive, and FacilitiesDive. GroceryDive generates revenue from ads and sponsored content. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Analysis/Bias
GroceryDive publishes news and information related to Grocery store management. Articles and headlines are minimally biased in word choices, such as this
[Grocery Outlet snaps up discount grocery chain for $62M](https://www.grocerydive.com/news/grocery-outlet-snaps-up-discount-grocery-chain-for-62m/707773/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible media and information outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they do report on political issues related to the industry, like
[Biden administration presses grocers to lower prices](https://www.grocerydive.com/news/grocery-prices-inflation-biden/706541/). In reviewing dozens, most articles are pro-business in nature. In general, all content is factual and low-biased.
