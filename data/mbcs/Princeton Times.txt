History
The Princeton Times is a newspaper published in Bluefield, West Virginia. ptonline.net is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Princeton Times. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
