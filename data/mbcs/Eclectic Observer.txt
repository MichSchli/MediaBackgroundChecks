History
The Eclectic Observer is a newspaper published in Wetumpka, Alabama. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Eclectic Observer is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias

[Mercer selected as new Elmore County Commission chairman](https://www.thewetumpkaherald.com/news/mercer-selected-as-new-elmore-county-commission-chairman/article_9ed7f3c4-562c-11ed-b41d-c3a8bc818055.html).
