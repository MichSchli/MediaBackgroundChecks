History
The Ithaca Journal is a newspaper that serves Ithaca, New York, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. ithacajournal.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Ithaca Journal. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Ithaca Journal covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Ithaca, New York area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Schumer announces plan to address ‘unprecedented wave’ of swatting incidents](https://www.ithacajournal.com/story/news/2023/04/05/chuck-schumer-announces-plan-to-address-uptick-in-swatting-incidents/70085336007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Ithaca Journal does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Different type of response to mass shooting crisis | Opinion](https://www.ithacajournal.com/story/opinion/2023/04/07/education-sharing-day-different-response-mass-shootings/70085534007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
