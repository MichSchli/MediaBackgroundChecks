History
[Founded in 1841](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Commercial_Appeal), the Memphis Commerical Appear is a daily newspaper of Memphis, Tennessee, and its surrounding metropolitan area. The current editor is Mark Russell. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Memphis Commercial Appeal is owned by Gannett (Gatehouse Media), which publishes
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/). In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, local news is covered via reporters with minimal bias such as this
[In a South Memphis neighborhood known for its history, competing plans for the future emerge](https://www.commercialappeal.com/story/money/business/development/2020/09/24/after-decades-divestment-development-coming-south-memphis/3506999001/). National and international news is covered through the USA Today Network and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Commercial Appeal typically
[endorsed Republican candidates](https://noahveltman.com/endorsements/); however, since 2004, they have endorsed Democratic candidates such as Hillary Clinton in 2016. Op-eds are reasonably balanced with this from a left-leaning perspective [Ruth Bader Ginsburg: Why we will sorely miss the ‘Notorious’ RBG | Opinion](https://www.commercialappeal.com/story/opinion/2020/09/23/ruth-bader-ginsburgs-presence-and-impact-sorely-missed/3508872001/), and this from the right [Conservative principles must apply to the criminal justice system | Opinion.](https://www.commercialappeal.com/story/opinion/2020/09/23/conservative-values-must-present-criminal-justice-system/3510948001/) The news is generally reported factually and with a reasonable editorial balance that currently slightly leans left.
