History
The Bonner Country Daily Bee is a newspaper published in Bonners Ferry, Idaho, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Bonners Ferry and the surrounding area. bonnercountydailybee.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[The Hagadone Corporation](https://www.hagadone.com/communication/) owns and publishes the Bonner Country Daily Bee. The Hagadone Corporation, based in Coeur d’Alene, Idaho, publishes 16 newspapers in Idaho, Montana, and Washington State. According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), they have donated to [Republicans](https://www.opensecrets.org/political-parties/RPC/2012/expenditures?name=republican-party&t0-search=hagadone) in the past (2012). However, during the 2020 election cycle, they did not donate to either party. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
