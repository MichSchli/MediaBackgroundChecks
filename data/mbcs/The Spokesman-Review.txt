The Spokesman-Review is owned by the
[Cowles Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cowles_Company), which owns several small TV stations and manufacturing companies in the Pacific Northwest. Revenue is derived through subscriptions, advertising, and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Spokesman-Review covers local news with minimal bias, such as this
[Pullman police have issued 10 nuisance party infractions in less than a week](https://www.spokesman.com/stories/2020/sep/02/pullman-police-have-issued-10-nuisance-party-infra/). National and international news is derived from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, The Spokesman-Review endorsed Hillary Clinton for President in 2016. A review of op-eds also reveals a mixed bag of opinions. This is a mostly least-biased source based on balanced op-eds and no failed fact checks.
