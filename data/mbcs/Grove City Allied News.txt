History
The Grove City Allied News is a newspaper published in the city of Grove City, Pennsylvania. alliednews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Grove City Allied News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Grove City Allied News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Grove City, Pennsylvania, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Mercer County sheriff defends deputies shown in viral TikTok post](https://www.alliednews.com/news/mercer-county-sheriff-defends-deputies-shown-in-viral-tiktok-post/article_806ddc32-aad4-504f-b2a8-c60d0350672a.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
