History
TruthBeTold.news (formerly called HU Insight) is a non-profit, non-partisan website and digital network run and edited by Howard University’s Department of Media, Journalism, and Film in the School of Communications. It uses journalistic skills and crowdsourced information to examine claims about the black community in public debate.
The website provides transparency by disclosing the mission, names, and bios of staff and how they are funded.

Analysis / Bias
Truthbetold.com publishes original journalism by students of Howard University. Articles and headlines use little loaded language such as this
[Does Restricting Access To Guns Reduce Gun Violence?](https://truthbetold.news/2020/12/does-restricting-access-to-guns-reduce-gun-violence/) This story is properly sourced to [PBS News Hour](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pbs-news-hour/), [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/), and [Our World in Data.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/our-world-in-data/)
Most opinions reflect a liberal perspective such as this
[‘Defund The Police’ Doesn’t Mean Dystopia In America](https://truthbetold.news/2020/11/defund-the-police-doesnt-mean-dystopia-in-america/). Truth Be told, reports news factually and with a left-leaning editorial bias.
