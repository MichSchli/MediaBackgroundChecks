History
TCPalm – Treasure Coast News serves Port St. Lucie, Florida, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. staugustine.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper. TCPalm is the digital news site for Treasure Coast Newspapers. Treasure Coast Newspapers publishes three daily print newspapers: The Stuart News, St. Lucie News Tribune, and the Indian River Press Journal, as well as the weekly Jupiter Courier.

Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns the TCPalm – Treasure Coast News. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the TCPalm – Treasure Coast News covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Port St. Lucie, Florida area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Boys & Girls Clubs launches $14 million campaign to build East Stuart branch, opening 2024](https://www.tcpalm.com/story/news/local/martin-county/2022/02/24/boys-girls-clubs-martin-county-building-stuart-branch-coming-2024-indiantown-palm-city-hobe-sound/6782500001/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, the TCPalm – Treasure Coast News does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented such as this that favors the left:
[DeSantis and Mast should lead, not demagogue, on Everglades funding issue | Our View](https://www.tcpalm.com/story/opinion/2022/02/04/desantis-mast-should-work-dems-everglades-project-our-view/6638538001/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
