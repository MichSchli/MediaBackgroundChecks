History
Yeshiva World News (YWN) is an Orthodox Jewish online news publication. It started in 2003 as a news aggregation blog founded by Yoshi and Sorbs on Forbs. Over the years, it has evolved into an independent news source with freelance reporters and photographers while continuing its role as a news aggregator. There is no evidence of an about page; however, the website covers news for the Orthodox Jewish communities in the USA and Israel.

Analysis / Bias
Yeshiva World News covers news of interest to the Orthodox Jewish communities in the USA and Israel. Articles and headlines often are minimally biased and accurately describe the contents of articles like this
[IDF Describes And Publishes Footage Of Intense Battles In Northern Gaza’s Jabaliya Camp](https://www.theyeshivaworld.com/news/israel-news/2246866/idf-describes-and-publishes-footage-of-intense-battles-in-northern-gazas-jabaliya-camp.html). This story is sourced from a video of the event. Another story also relies on photos and videos as a source of information: [FDNY Battles Two Alarm Fire In Flatbush Home [VIDEOS & PHOTOS]](https://www.theyeshivaworld.com/news/general/2246910/fdny-battles-two-alarm-fire-in-flatbush-home-videos-photos.html)
Editorially, they strongly support Israel and right-leaning Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu with stories like this
[KOWTOWING TO DEMS? Biden Slams Israel’s “Indiscriminate Bombing” Of Gaza](https://www.theyeshivaworld.com/news/israel-news/2246472/kowtowing-to-dems-biden-slams-israels-indiscriminate-bombing-of-gaza.html). This same story is also critical of Joe Biden’s approach to the Israeli-Palestinian conflict. When reporting on former President Trump, they tend to hold slightly negative views, such as in this story: [Journalists Wonder: What Might A Second Trump Presidency Look Like?](https://www.theyeshivaworld.com/news/general/2246166/journalists-wonder-what-might-a-second-trump-presidency-look-like.html) In general, most information is fact-based and hold right-leaning editorial perspectives.
