History
Pique NewsMagazine is an English-language newspaper in Whistler, British Columbia, Canada. piquenewsmagazine.ca is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Pique NewsMagazine. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Pique NewsMagazine publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Whistler, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Two thirds of Canadian employees are quiet quitting, study finds](https://www.piquenewsmagazine.com/human-resources-education/two-thirds-of-canadian-employees-are-quiet-quitting-study-finds-7221186). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Pique NewsMagazine’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Monique Keiran: Canada Day was ‘Humiliation Day’ for Chinese immigrants](https://www.piquenewsmagazine.com/opinion/monique-keiran-canada-day-was-humiliation-day-for-chinese-immigrants-7226248). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
