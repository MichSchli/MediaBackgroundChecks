History
Founded in 1948, WTVR – Richmond News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Richmond, Virginia, United States. wtvr.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTVR – Richmond News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WTVR – Richmond News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Richmond, Virginia, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Student detained after bringing weapon to West End middle school, Henrico officials confirm](https://www.wtvr.com/news/local-news/holman-middle-weapon-january-20-2023). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Congressman seriously injured after falling off ladder](https://www.wtvr.com/news/national/congressman-seriously-injured-after-falling-off-ladder). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
