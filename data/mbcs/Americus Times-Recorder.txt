History
The Americus Times-Recorder is a newspaper published in Americus, Georgia. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Americus Times-Recorder is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Americus Times-Recorder primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Sumter County BOC held its monthly Work Session on Election Night, November 8](https://www.americustimesrecorder.com/2022/11/10/sumter-county-boc-held-its-monthly-work-session-on-election-night-november-8/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
