History
Founded in 1969, KION – Monterey News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)/ [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Monterey, California. The associated website is kion546.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns KION – Monterey News and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KION – Monterey News website publishes news for the greater Monterey, California area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:
[Gilroy drinking water declared safe after additional testing](https://kion546.com/news/local-news/top-stories/2022/06/18/gilroy-drinking-water-declared-safe-after-additional-testing/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [CBS News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/), [CNN Newswire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[Jimmy Panetta announces re-run for Congress](https://kion546.com/news/2021/12/28/jimmy-panetta-announces-re-run-for-congress/). Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoiding using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
