History
The Oak Bay News is a newspaper published in Oak Bay, British Columbia, Canada. The website oakbaynews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Oak Bay area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Oak Bay News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
