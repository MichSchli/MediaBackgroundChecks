History
Founded in 2011, Climate Home News is a London, UK-based news website that reports on climate change and environmental issues. According to their
[about page](https://www.climatechangenews.com/about-us/), “Climate Home News aims to be the go-to media outlet for a global community of doers and thinkers seeking to understand the political, social and economic drivers of the climate crisis and climate action.” [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Climate Change News Ltd. owns and publishes Climate Home News. The website was originally funded by
[Responding to Climate Change](https://www.rtcc.org/), a UK-based nonprofit. Currently, the website is [funded by sponsors](https://www.climatechangenews.com/about-us/) such as Signify, Unilever, Climate Investment Funds, and Climate Justice Resilience Fund.
Analysis / Bias
Climate Home News produces
[original reporting](https://www.climatechangenews.com/news/) on climate change focused on politics, finance, energy, justice, land, transport, and science. They also advocate for [Net Zero](https://www.climatechangenews.com/netzero/) carbon emissions as a goal for all countries. The website features sponsored content from their donors that often promote their goods and services, such as this [SME Climate Hub launches tools to help small businesses deliver on climate goals ](https://www.climatechangenews.com/2022/02/03/sme-climate-hub-launches-tools-help-small-businesses-deliver-climate-goals%e2%80%af/).
Articles and headlines contain moderately loaded emotional language such as this
[Will Nigeria’s climate change law put the brakes on gas flaring?](https://www.climatechangenews.com/2022/03/11/will-nigerias-climate-change-law-put-the-brakes-on-gas-flaring/) When it comes to sourcing, they use credible peer-reviewed sources and environmental-government organizations.
Editorially, they promote conservation, sustainability, and concern for the impacts of climate change. Articles are critical of governments that they perceive as not doing enough
[‘Betrayal’: US approves just $1bn climate finance for developing countries in 2022.](https://www.climatechangenews.com/2022/03/11/betrayal-us-approves-just-1bn-climate-finance-for-developing-countries-in-2022/) Finally, opinion pieces favor the left with stories such as this [We must disrupt Davos culture to end decades of failure on climate](https://www.climatechangenews.com/2022/03/04/we-must-disrupt-davos-culture-to-end-decades-of-failure-on-climate/). Climate Home News generally holds a left-leaning editorial bias and reports news factually.
