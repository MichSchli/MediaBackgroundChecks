History
The Eldora Herald-Ledger is a newspaper published in Eldora, Indiana, United States. The paper covers news, sports, lifestyle, and business for Eagle Grove and the surrounding area. eldoranewspapers.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Mid America Publishing owns and publishes the Eldora Herald-Ledger. Mid America Publishing publishes
[several community newspapers](https://www.midampublishing.com/content/mid-america-publications) throughout Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Eldora Herald-Ledger reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Eldora, Indiana region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[Bus Barn Ribbon Cutting at Hubbard-Radcliffe Elementary](http://www.eldoranewspapers.com/content/bus-barn-ribbon-cutting-hubbard-radcliffe-elementary). The newspaper and website do not publish national news, focusing on local news reporting.
Editorially, the Eldora Herald-Ledger does not produce op-eds and rarely interjects opinions in news stories. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
