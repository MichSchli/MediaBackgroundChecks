History
Founded in 1856, the Leavenworth Times is a daily newspaper published in Leavenworth, Kansas. leavenworthtimes.com covers the local, national, sports weather, and crime news.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Leavenworth Times is owned by CherryRoad Media, which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has
[primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, in the past, they donated 100% to Republican candidates. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Leavenworth Times covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Leavenworth, Kansas area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Police Department working toward cybercrime detective position.](https://www.leavenworthtimes.com/news/20200923/police-department-working-toward-cybercrime-detective-position) National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
