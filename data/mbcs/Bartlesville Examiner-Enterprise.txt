History
The Bartlesville Examiner-Enterprise is a newspaper that serves Bartlesville, Oklahoma, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. examiner-enterprise.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Bartlesville Examiner-Enterprise. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Bartlesville Examiner-Enterprise covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Bartlesville, Oklahoma, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[MedWise opens new urgent care center in Bartlesville](https://www.examiner-enterprise.com/story/news/healthcare/2023/12/26/quiktrips-medwise-brings-quality-to-bartlesvilles-healthcare-scene/72013087007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Bartlesville Examiner-Enterprise does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[The MAGA Perversion of Patriotism](https://www.examiner-enterprise.com/story/opinion/2021/12/21/maga-perversion-patriotism/8971485002/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
