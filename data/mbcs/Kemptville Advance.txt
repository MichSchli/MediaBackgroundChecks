History
The Kemptville Advance is a newspaper published in Kemptville, Ontario, Canada. insideottawavalley.com/kemptville-on/ provides local, national, and world news for the greater Kemptville, Ontario region.
Read our profile on the
[Canadian government’s influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of the Kemptville Advance is Metroland Media Group Ltd., a subsidiary of Nordstar Capital LP. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In 2015 and
[2021](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2021/09/19/the-stars-editorial-board-endorses-liberals-for-2021-federal-election.html), the [Toronto Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/) (Nordstar), which owns Kemptville Advance’s publisher, Metroland Media Group, [endorsed](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2015/10/09/for-justin-trudeau-and-the-liberal-party-editorial.html) the Liberal Party under Justin Trudeau.
In review, the Kemptville Advance uses low-biased wording when reporting local news such as this:
[Disc golf is becoming a big thing in Kemptville. We asked the club’s founder why](https://www.insideottawavalley.com/news-story/10745528-disc-golf-is-becoming-a-big-thing-in-kemptville-we-asked-the-club-s-founder-why/). National news is derived from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/), while world news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, a review of op-eds slightly favors the left. In general, they are fact-based and hold a left-leaning editorial bias.
