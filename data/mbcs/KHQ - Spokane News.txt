History
KHQ – Spokane News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated TV station licensed to Spokane, Washington. The website nonstoplocal.com/spokane/ provides local news, sports, weather, crime, and business news. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KHQ – Spokane News is owned by the
[Cowles Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cowles_Company), which owns several small TV stations and manufacturing companies in the Pacific Northwest. Revenue is derived through subscriptions, advertising, and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
KHQ – Spokane News covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Three burglaries in Deer Park leave questions in the community](https://www.nonstoplocal.com/spokane/news/three-burglaries-in-deer-park-leave-questions-in-the-community/video_d9d67a47-ac16-59fb-9f39-98fe30ad5a35.html). Some stories are accompanied by videos from the TV news station. National and international news is derived from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, KHQ – Spokane News does not provide opinion content. They maintain a neutral tone when reporting on politics, such as this
[Biden Tests Negative For Covid](https://www.nonstoplocal.com/tri-cities-yakima/news/biden-tests-negative-for-covid/article_55cd5f1a-47b9-5932-80ab-4a78c1da787a.html). In general, they are low-biased and fact-based in reporting.
