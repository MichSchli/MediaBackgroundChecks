History
The New Tropic is a Miami, Florida community news and information website. They also publish a newsletter. According to their
[about page](https://thenewtropic.com/about/), they “connect Miami.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[WhereBy.Us Enterprises, Inc.](http://whereby.us/) owns and publishes The New Tropic. Paid memberships generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The New Tropic has a left-center bias through
[story selection](https://thenewtropic.com/conservatives-right-side-equality/) that mostly favors the left. Each article is well-sourced to credible media outlets and information. There is little use of loaded words in both headlines and articles. The New Tropic reports news with minimal bias; however, editorially, there is a left bias based on political affiliations supported by the left, such as [LGBTQ and race rights](https://thenewtropic.com/conservatives-right-side-equality/) and [environmental concerns](https://thenewtropic.com/miami-beach-election-question/).
