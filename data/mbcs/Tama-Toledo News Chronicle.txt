History
The Tama-Toledo News Chronicle is a Toledo, Iowa-based newspaper. The website for the newspaper is tamatoledonews.com covering local and national news, sports, crime, and business news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Newspaper is owned by
[Ogden Newspapers Inc.,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ogden_Newspapers_Inc.) which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the USA. Ogden has historically donated to Republican candidates. According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), since 1992, they have [donated 93% to Republicans](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000036358&cycle=2018). [Robert Nutting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Nutting) is the current CEO. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Tama-Toledo News Chronicle produces original reporting for Toledo, Iowa, and the surrounding area. Headlines and articles contain minimal emotional language:
[Conifer Power speaks to turbine coalition](https://www.tamatoledonews.com/news/local-news/2022/07/01/conifer-power-speaks-to-turbine-coalition/). National and international news is republished from the [Associated Press.](https://www.post-journal.com/news/local-news/2019/10/police-investigating-saturday-stabbing/)
Editorially, a review of editorial commentary shows a reasonable balance between right and left without delving into extremes; however, slightly more op-eds favor the right. The news is generally reported factually with a slight conservative bias editorially but balanced enough to be considered least biased as each side has fair representation.
