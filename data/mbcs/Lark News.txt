History
Founded in 2002, Lark News is a satire website that mocks Christians and evangelicals.
[Joel Kilpatrick](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lark_News) is the founder and chief contributor. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website is presumably owned by Joel Kilpatrick. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Lark News typically publishes satire for a liberal audience with stories such as this
[Christian couple maintains abstinence through first two years of marriage](http://www.larknews.com/archives/217). While Lark News does not openly disclaim they are satire a read of their [about page](http://www.larknews.com/about-us) would clue most people in.
