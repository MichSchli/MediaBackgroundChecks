History
[Founded in 2007](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/InsideClimate_News), InsideClimate News is a non-profit and non-partisan news organization focusing on environmental journalism. The publication [writes](https://insideclimatenews.org/about) that it “covers clean energy, carbon energy, nuclear energy, and environmental science—plus the territory in between where law, policy, and public opinion are shaped.” It won the [2013 Pulitzer Prize](http://archives.cjr.org/the_observatory/insideclimate_news_pulitzer_pr.php) for National Reporting for its coverage of the Kalamazoo River oil spill. They state their [mission](https://insideclimatenews.org/about) is to “serve as watchdogs of government, industry, and advocacy groups and hold them accountable for their policies and actions.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
InsideClimate News is owned by Lost Light Projects Inc, a not-for-profit incorporated in the state of New York and a 501 C3 tax-exempt organization operating with IRS approval as a public charity. According to their
[about page](https://insideclimatenews.org/about), they are supported primarily by grants for general support from charitable foundations and tax-deductible donations from readers. The website is fully transparent regarding funding, with a full list [here](https://insideclimatenews.org/about/our-funders). Major donors include [Ford Foundation](http://www.fordfoundation.org/), [Knight Foundation](http://www.knightfoundation.org/), [Leonardo DiCaprio Foundation](http://leonardodicaprio.org/), and [Rockefeller Family Fund](http://www.rffund.org/).
Analysis / Bias
In review, InsideClimate News uses minimal loaded words and always sources from highly credible media and science outlets. InsideClimate News could easily be placed in our
[Pro-Science](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pro-science/) category. However, they frequently cover politics such as this: [Trump (Sort of) Accepted Covid-19 Modeling. Don’t Expect the Same on Climate Change.](https://insideclimatenews.org/news/19042020/coronavirus-modeling-trump-fauci-climate-science-climate-denial)
Editorially, they frequently report negatively on President Trump and Republican positions on
[Climate Change](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/) and the environment in general.
