History
The South Missourian News is a newspaper published in Salem, Arkansas. southmissouriannews.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the southern Missouri area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The South Missourian News is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The South Missourian News covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater South Missouri area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Generous donation made to Oregon County Food Pantry in Alton](https://www.southmissouriannews.com/2023/03/20/generous-donation-made-to-oregon-county-food-pantry-in-alton/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
