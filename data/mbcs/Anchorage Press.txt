History
The Anchorage Press is a weekly alternatitve newspaper based in Anchorage, Alaska, serving the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at anchoragepress.com.
Funded by / Ownership
Wick Communications owns the Anchorage Press and several small community papers across the Pacific Northwest. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Anchorage Press publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Mayor Bronson Announces 1st Quarter Budget Revisions](https://www.anchoragepress.com/breakingnews/mayor-bronson-announces-1st-quarter-budget-revisions/article_58be7544-b789-11ec-8db9-cf6bc7a6d9b1.html). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Anchorage Press leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. The news is generally reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
