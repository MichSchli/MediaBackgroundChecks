History
[Founded in 1889](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agerpres), The National News Agency “AGERPRES” is the oldest Romanian news agency and the first autonomous agency in Romania. [Read our profile on Romanian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/romania-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Agerpres is a member of the European Alliance of News Agencies, which sets guidelines and standards to limit bias in reporting. Agerpres remains independent but is under threat of
[government takeover](https://europeanjournalists.org/blog/2017/10/06/romania-draft-law-threatens-independence-of-agerpres-news-agency/).
Analysis / Bias
Agerpres furnishes the press with domestic and foreign news. It transmits abroad daily summaries of Romanian political, economic, and cultural concerns in Russian, French, German, English, and Spanish. Articles and headlines are typically low-biased as expected from a news agency
[Parliament to consider national social security budgets at joint plenary sitting](https://www.agerpres.ro/english/2021/03/01/parliament-to-consider-national-social-security-budgets-at-joint-plenary-sitting--669731). Information is properly sourced. In general, Agrepres is low biased and is a credible new agency from Romania.
