History
Founded in 1954, WKMG – ClickOrlando is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Orlando, Florida, United States. Clickorlando.com is the associated website that reports news, crime, sports, weather, and lifestyle for the greater Orlando area. [Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WKMG – ClickOrlando is owned by
[Graham Holdings](https://www.ghco.com/), which owns seven TV stations and the website [Slate](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/slate/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WKMG – ClickOrlando provides news, sports, weather, crime reporting, and lifestyle for the greater Orlando metro area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this
[Skeletal remains found at Orlando construction site, deputies say](https://www.clickorlando.com/news/local/2022/06/15/skeletal-remains-found-at-orlando-construction-site-deputies-say/). Many stories provide videos from the KPRC TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally offers national and world news.
Editorially, WKMG – ClickOrlando does not produce Op-Eds; however, they do report on politics through the Associated Press and original reporting. Political news is generally balanced and presented with minimal bias, such as this
[Railroad talks stall, so Biden likely to pick review board](https://www.clickorlando.com/news/politics/2022/06/14/railroad-talks-stall-so-biden-likely-to-pick-review-board/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
