History
Founded in 1956 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)/ [Fox](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station, KXII News12 serves Sherman, Texas and Ada, Oklahoma, and the surrounding area. KXII.com is the associated website for the news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KXII News12 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KXII News12 provides news coverage for Dothan, Alabama, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Texas Senate advances bill allowing handguns without a license](https://www.kxii.com/2021/05/06/texas-senate-advances-bill-allowing-handguns-without-license/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KXII News12 does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
