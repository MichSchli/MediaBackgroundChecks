History
[Founded in 1783](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Herald_(Glasgow)), The Herald, based in Glasgow, Scotland is a daily broadsheet newspaper. The paper also has the distinction of being the longest-running national newspaper in the world. The current editor is Donald Martin. [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Glasgow Herald is published by Herald & Times Group, which is owned by Newsquest Media Group Ltd. Newsquest is the second-largest publisher in the united kingdom, publishing 205 brands including 165 newspaper. Further, Newsquest is owned by US-based Gannett which publishes
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/). In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Glasgow Herald publishes local news about Glasgow as well as regional, national and international news. Local news is reported with low bias such as this
[Police inquiry under way after Herald memorial garden plaque stolen](https://www.heraldscotland.com/news/19191533.police-inquiry-way-herald-memorial-garden-plaque-stolen/). National and international also is low biased in tone and derived from sources such as the [BBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bbc/) and the [Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/).
Editorially, they clearly label opinion pieces and name authors. Opinion content tends to favor the left with many stories negative toward Conservative Prime Minister Boris Johnson such as this
[Opinion Matrix: The union is sunk with ‘blundering’ Boris Johnson](https://www.heraldscotland.com/news/18890100.opinion-matrix-union-sunk-blundering-boris-johnson/). They also have a section dedicated to concerns for climate change called [Climate for Change](https://www.heraldscotland.com/business_hq/climateforchange/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a left-leaning editorial bias.
