History
The Hutchinson News is a newspaper that serves Hutchinson, Kansas, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. hutchnews.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Hutchinson News. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Hutchinson News covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Hutchinson, Kansas area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Lindsborg motorcyclist injured in a late-night collision in Lyons](https://www.hutchnews.com/story/news/2022/06/17/lindsborg-motorcyclist-injured-late-night-collision/7657928001/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Hutchinson News does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Topeka correctional officer: Moving state employees from KPERS to thrift savings plan is wrong for Kansas](https://www.hutchnews.com/story/opinion/columns/2022/04/22/topeka-correctional-officer-protect-kpers-pension-retirement-kansas-state-workers/7408775001/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
