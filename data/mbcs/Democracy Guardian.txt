History
[Founded in 2017](http://whois.domaintools.com/democracyguardian.com), Democracy Guardian is a news and opinion blog that presents news from a liberal perspective. According to their [about page](https://democracyguardian.com/about), “Americans have a responsibility to be the guardians of our democracy–not just during an election but every day – President Barack Obama.” The about page also lists authors who publish on the website.
Read our profile on
[USA government influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/).
Funded By / Ownership
Democracy Guardian does not disclose ownership, though it is hosted through
[Medium.com](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/medium/). Revenue is generated through Medium’s platform.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Democracy Guardian is a news and opinion blog with a strong left-leaning bias in reporting. All articles on this website favor the left and are opposed to the right. There is significant use of loaded words in both headlines and articles. For example,
[Narcissist Sociopath Trump: Easy to Manipulate and Crazy is Crashing](https://democracyguardian.com/narcissist-sociopath-trump-easy-to-manipulate-and-crazy-is-crashing-2b3541b092cb). Most articles are well researched and, for the most part, sourced; however, many articles are pure opinion pieces that favor the left.
The two primary themes of the website are anti-Trump and anti-Bernie Sanders articles. Throughout the website, there is a conspiratorial claim made that Sanders’s followers are a
[part of a cult](https://democracyguardian.com/the-sanders-extreme-tea-party-left-isnt-winning-they-are-just-loud-and-obnoxious-fbd6aefc1b7e). In general, the stories on Democracy Guardian are opinion based but generally sourced properly.
