Analysis / Bias
Southend News Network is a satirical website with a tagline that reads “The latest news from SouthEnd, Honest.” Although they do not officially disclaim they are a satire site it is implied with the tagline. Articles tend to mostly poke fun at elected officials and those in power, such as
[UK Government Confirms Farage Banking Scandal Renewed For Second Season](https://southendnewsnetwork.net/2023/07/26/uk-government-confirms-farage-banking-scandal-renewed-for-second-season/).
