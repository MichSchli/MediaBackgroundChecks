History
The Daily 49er is a student-run newspaper that serves California State University. According to the paper’s about page, the first issue of the
[Daily 49er](https://www.daily49er.com/about-the-paper/) was published on November 11, 1949 and they also state that in 1994, the Daily 49er published online for the first time.
Funded by / Ownership
The Daily 49er is owned by California State University and funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
The Daily 49er uses loaded language both in their headlines and articles that are strongly biased toward progressive causes through story selection, such as: “
[Checking up on Trump’s promises to the far-right.](http://www.daily49er.com/opinion/2017/09/05/checking-up-on-trumps-promises-to-the-far-right/)” Editorially, the Daily 49er has a strong left wing bias with the [majority of staff](http://www.daily49er.com/opinion/2016/11/06/our-view-how-to-approach-election-day/) voting for Democratic candidates. In their articles they tend to source to credible information through quotes, but [fail to provide links](http://www.daily49er.com/opinion/2017/09/06/trump-lets-down-daca-recipients-over-labor-day-weekend/) to their sources, which would be helpful for context. We could not find any instances of failed fact checks.
