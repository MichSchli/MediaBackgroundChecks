Factual Reporting: LOW
Country: Unknown
History
The domain anonews.co used to point to a conspiracy website allegedly published by the Anonymous collective. In 2019, it appears the website was taken over by an unknown group that publishes a variety of news stories. The website completely lacks transparency as they do not have an about page, name owners, or editors.
Funded by / Ownership
Anonymous News does not disclose ownership and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, previous conspiracy content prior to 2019 has been removed from the site. Currently, Anonymous News publishes a mix of world, political, and sensationalized news stories. Articles and headlines often use moderate loaded emotional language such as this
[If Politicians Were Put On A Minimum Wage](https://www.anonews.co/if-politicians-were-put-on-a-minimum-wage/). In general, stories are sourced to mainstream media outlets such as [The Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/) and [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/). It appears this site minimally active at this time.
