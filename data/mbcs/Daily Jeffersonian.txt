History
The Daily Jeffersonian is a newspaper that serves Cambridge, Ohio, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. daily-jeff.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Daily Jeffersonian. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Daily Jeffersonian covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Cambridge, Ohio, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language such as this
[The 59th Barnesville Pumpkin Festival returns to Belmont County on Thursday](https://www.daily-jeff.com/story/news/local/2023/09/19/59th-barnesville-pumpkin-festival-begins-on-thursday/70889029007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Daily Jeffersonian does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Nan Whaley on Ohio abortion law impact: “People that have choices are going to choose not to live here’](https://www.daily-jeff.com/videos/opinion/2022/10/24/ohio-governor-candidate-nan-whaley-discusses-abortion-ohio-editors-mike-dewine-election-heart-beat/10564616002/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
