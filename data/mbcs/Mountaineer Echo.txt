History
The Mountaineer Echo is a daily newspaper published in Flippin, Arkansas. flippinonline.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for Flippin Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Mountaineer Echo is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Mountaineer Echo covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Flippin Arkansas area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Judge Massey Charged, Booked and Released](https://www.flippinonline.com/2022/07/13/judge-massey-charged-booked-and-released/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
