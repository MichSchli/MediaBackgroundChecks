History
The Battlefords News-Optimist newspaper was founded more than a century ago in Battleford,
[Saskatchewan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saskatchewan), Canada. It is printed twice weekly.
Read our profile on
[Canadian government influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The newspaper and website is owned by
[Glacier Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Glacier_Media), which owns several small-town newspapers throughout Canada. The newspaper is funded through a subscription and advertising model.
Analysis / Bias
The Battlefords News-Optimist publishes local news through the use of journalists with minimal bias in reporting. National and International news is republished through the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). All information is properly sourced throughout. Editorially, there is a balance between left and right views with very few extremes presented.
