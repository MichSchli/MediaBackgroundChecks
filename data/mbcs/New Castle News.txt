History
[Founded in 1880](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/New_Castle_News), the New Castle News is a Western Pennsylvania daily newspaper published in the city of New Castle and covering Lawrence County. ncnewsonline.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news. [Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Community Newspaper Holdings Inc owns the New Castle News
[.](http://www.cnhi.com/) They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the New Castle News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Lawrence country region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Woman reports indecent exposure incident](https://www.ncnewsonline.com/news/local_news/woman-reports-indecent-exposure-incident/article_f3b3dc78-0cf0-555c-aef1-1e6ae64cb3eb.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, the New Castle News endorsed Democratic Presidential candidate
[Hillary Clinton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daily_newspaper_endorsements_in_the_2016_United_States_presidential_election) in 2016. A review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left, such as this [OUR VIEW: Police board right idea, but needs right people](https://www.ncnewsonline.com/opinion/editorials/our-view-police-board-right-idea-but-needs-right-people/article_f009915c-cb8a-5b6e-adc7-d677f9a02bfc.html) and [ERIC POOLE: On Labor Day, unions are the reason for the season](https://www.ncnewsonline.com/opinion/columns/eric-poole-on-labor-day-unions-are-the-reason-for-the-season/article_9b5627c6-6949-5e21-a88c-49033a71c6bc.html). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
