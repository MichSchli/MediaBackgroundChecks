History
Founded in 1901, the Cullman Times is a daily newspaper published in the city of Cullman, Alabama, and covers Cullman County. cullmantimes.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Cullman Times. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
