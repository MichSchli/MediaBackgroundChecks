History
The St. Clair News-Aegis is a weekly newspaper published in the city of Pell City, Alabama, and covers St. Clair County. newsaegfis.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the St. Clair News-Aegis. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the St. Clair News-Aegis covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Pell City, Alabama, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Leeds police charge man with distribution](https://www.newsaegis.com/news/leeds-police-charge-man-with-distribution/article_2f24e5ec-f22b-11ec-85e2-3b4686124b72.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left, such as this
[Greene may be too red](https://www.newsaegis.com/news/greene-may-be-too-red/article_e38e8d12-6f00-11eb-abcb-7bcd5f65b7fe.html). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
