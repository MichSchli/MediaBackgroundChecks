History
Founded in 2015 by Dattatreya Mandal, Realm of History is a website that publishes news and information on history and architecture. According to its
[about page](https://www.realmofhistory.com/sample-page/) “Realm of History is intended as an online compendium dedicated entirely to the fascinating study of history (and just history). Our aim is to celebrate the extraordinary human triumphs, unravel the myriad of mysteries, and scrutinize the major failures – all noted in the annals of history.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website is owned by Dattatreya Mandal with revenue derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
Realm of History is a website that publishes news and information on history. The website presents information with minimal bias, such as this
[Powerful Types Of Warships From History: 7th Century BC – 17th Century AD](https://www.realmofhistory.com/2023/08/11/historical-warships/). This story, like all others reviewed, is properly sourced from credible outlets.
Editorially, they do not report on politics and do not offer political opinion; therefore, we rate them a least biased pro-science source.
