History
Launched in 2000,
[Al Bawaba](https://www.albawaba.com/abstatic/about-us) is a news website that focuses on the Middle East and the Arab world that features news, politics, Arab entertainment, economy, culture, and more. Al Bawaba is based in Amman, Jordan and is published in Arabic and English. Al Bawaba has several web portals, one of them is SyndiGate which is a digital content syndication service. The Executive Publisher of Al Bawaba English is [John Lillywhite](https://www.albawaba.com/business/al-bawaba-news-relaunches-web-platform-nineteenth-anniversary-1281446). He is also the Digital Transition Lead. For the editorial team see [here.](https://www.albawaba.com/abstatic/editorial-team-contributors-columnists) [Read our profile on Jordanian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/jordan-government-and-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Al Bawaba Website does not disclose ownership or financing information. Our research reveals that
[Hani Jabsheh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/hani-jabsheh-25133036?trk=org-employees_profile-result-card_result-card_full-click) is the CEO and founder of the site. Also, [THCG, Inc.](http://www.sahafi.jo/arc/art1.php?id=b6062ae066f2e838f3269dc3543ff4e13bec0131) a New York-based venture capital firm, as well as [Frankfurt-based TFG Venture Capital](http://www.mafhoum.com/press/47T1.htm), initially provided funding to Al Bawaba. The name of the owner in Jordan (headquarters) is [Al Bawaba Middle East Limited](https://www.albawaba.com/abstatic/contact-us), which is registered in [Cyprus](https://cyprusregistry.com/companies/HE/91131). Revenue is derived through [advertising](https://www.arabnews.com/node/1281221/advertise-arab-news).
Analysis / Bias
In review, when reporting national news Al Bawaba uses emotionally loaded language in their headlines that is always favorable to Jordan’s Royal Family such as
[“Dubai’s Missing Princess: The Worrying Case of Sheikah Latifa”](https://www.albawaba.com/news/dubais-missing-princess-worrying-case-sheikah-latifa-1130270) and [“Princess Raya of Jordan is Engaged to a British Journalist Who Happens to be Roald Dahl’s Grandson”](https://www.albawaba.com/node/princess-raya-jordan-engaged-british-journalist-who-happens-be-roald-dahls-grandson). When covering the national news they republish articles through SyndiGate, the Jordan Times, and the Jordan News Agency, such as this: [“King Abdullah Promises Better Life for People in Jordan”](https://www.albawaba.com/news/king-abdullah-promises-better-life-people-jordan-1332876). Further, they republish celebrity gossip [here.](https://www.albawaba.com/entertainment/nicki-minaj-reveals-she-loves-being-married-kenneth-petty-1362284)
When it comes to sourcing, Al Bawaba utilizes credible sources such as
[hrw.org](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/human-rights-watch/), Deutsche Welle ( [DW](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/dw-news/)) as well as factually mixed sources such as [The Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-observer/), [Anatolian Agency](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/anadolu-agency/), and questionable sources such as [ dailymail.co.uk](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-mail/). Al Bawaba also frequently reports negatively on the UAE “ [Curious Case of Emirati Emir: Sheikh Rashid Flees to Qatar, Reveals UAE Royal Secrets!”](https://www.albawaba.com/loop/curious-case-emirati-emir-sheikh-rashid-flees-qatar-reveals-uae-royal-secrets-1159476) In general, Al Bawaba publishes heavily censored, right-leaning news that is not always factual.
