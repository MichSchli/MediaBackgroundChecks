History
The Santa Fe New Mexican is a
[daily newspaper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Santa_Fe_New_Mexican) published in Santa Fe, New Mexico. Its first issue was printed on November 28, 1849. The New Mexican was named Newspaper of the Year in 2015 by the Local Media Association, a national organization of television, newspaper, and radio companies. Funded by / Ownership
Robin McKinney Martin owns the Santa Fe New Mexican. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
