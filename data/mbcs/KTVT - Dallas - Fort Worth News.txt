History
Founded in 1955, KTVT – Dallas – Fort Worth News is a CBS-owned and operated television station licensed to Fort Worth, Texas, United States. The official website for the TV station is cbsnews.com/dfw/, which covers news, sports, weather, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Dallas-Fort Worth area.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTVT – Dallas – Fort Worth News is owned by CBS Broadcasting Inc., which operates dozens of TV stations in the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the KTVT – Dallas – Fort Worth News website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater Dallas-Fort Worth region. Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording such as this:
[Dallas reaches highest murder rate it has seen despite police efforts](https://www.cbsnews.com/dfw/news/dallas-reaches-highest-murder-rate-it-has-seen-despite-police-efforts/). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published by other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, KTVT – Dallas – Fort Worth News does not publish op-eds; however, they report on politics slightly favoring the left, such as this
[Jan. 6 Committee discussed oath keepers leader Stewart Rhodes in primetime hearing](https://www.cbsnews.com/dfw/news/jan-6-committee-discussed-oath-keepers-leader-stewart-rhodes-in-primetime-hearing/). Most importantly, the reporters deliver political news with little opinion interjected. In general, KTVT reports news factually and with a slight left-leaning bias in story selection.
