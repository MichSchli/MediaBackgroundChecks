History
[Founded in 2010](https://whois.domaintools.com/sanevax.org), according to their about page, “ [The SaneVax mission](http://sanevax.org/media-about/about/) is to promote only Safe, Affordable, Necessary & Effective vaccines and [vaccination practices](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/) through education and information. We believe in science-based medicine. Our primary goal is to provide the information necessary for you to make informed decisions regarding your health and well-being. We also provide referrals to helpful resources for those unfortunate enough to have experienced vaccine-related injuries.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
SaneVax does not clearly state ownership. Although they lack transparency, donations derive revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In reality, their mission is not science-based medicine but rather a rebuke against science-based medicine. In short, this is an anti-vaccine organization that promotes pseudoscience and pharmaceutical industry conspiracies, especially as it relates to the
[HPV vaccination](https://sanevax.org/gardasil-and-my-sons-shattered-dreams/). For an excellent description and refutation of SaneVax, I recommend reading this from the website [Science-Based Medicine](https://sciencebasedmedicine.org/beware-hpv-dna-in-gardasil/).
