History
The Rocky Mountain Outlook is an English-language newspaper in Canmore, Alberta, Canada. rmoutlook.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Rocky Mountain Outlook. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
The Rocky Mountain Outlook publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Canmore, AB region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Cougar warning issued for Kananaskis YMCA camp](https://www.rmoutlook.com/kananaskis-country/cougar-warning-issued-for-kananaskis-ymca-camp-7396717). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Rocky Mountain Outlook’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Closed door bylaws one piece of the climate puzzle](https://www.rmoutlook.com/opinion/editorial-closed-door-bylaws-one-piece-of-the-climate-puzzle-7293829). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
