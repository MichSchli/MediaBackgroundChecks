History
Founded in 1970, KSHB – Kansas City News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Kansas City, Missouri, United States. kshb.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KSHB – Kansas City News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KSHB – Kansas City News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Kansas City, Missouri, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Former Scholastic Teacher of the Year appears in court on charges of unlawful sexual relations](https://www.kshb.com/news/local-news/former-scholastic-teacher-of-the-year-appears-in-court-on-charges-of-unlawful-sexual-relations). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Gov. Kelly, Schmidt to square off in gubernatorial debate in Johnson County](https://www.kshb.com/news/political/elections-local/gov-kelly-schmidt-to-square-off-in-gubernatorial-debate-in-johnson-county). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
