History
Founded in 1949, KPRC – Click2Houston is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Houston, Texas, United States. Click2houston.com is the associated website that reports news, crime, sports, weather, and lifestyle for the greater Houston area. [Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KPRC – Click2Houston is owned by
[Graham Holdings](https://www.ghco.com/), which owns seven TV stations and the website [Slate](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/slate/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KPRC – Click2Houston provides news, sports, weather, crime reporting, and lifestyle for the greater Houston Metro area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this
[Pedestrian fatally struck by vehicle in hit-and-run in NW Harris County, HCSO says](https://www.click2houston.com/news/local/2022/06/08/pedestrian-fatally-struck-by-vehicle-in-hit-and-run-in-nw-harris-county-hcso-says/). Many stories provide videos from the KPRC TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally offers national and world news.
Editorially, KPRC – Click2Houston does not produce Op-Eds; however, they do report on politics through the Associated Press and original reporting. Political news is generally balanced and presented with minimal bias, such as this
[Justice Dept. names 9 to aid in review of Uvalde shooting](https://www.click2houston.com/news/politics/2022/06/08/justice-dept-names-9-to-aid-in-review-of-uvalde-shooting/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
