History
Founded in 1953, KXLY – Spokane News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Spokane, Washington, United States. Kxly.com is the associated news website for the TV Station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KXLY – Spokane News is owned by
[Morgan Murphy Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morgan_Murphy_Media), which owns 14 TV stations and several radio stations throughout the Midwest. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KXLY – Spokane News reports news, sports, weather, and crime for the Spokane, Washington region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Fire on I-90 near Maple and Walnut is under control](https://www.kxly.com/there-is-a-brush-fire-on-i-90-near-maple-and-walnut/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally provides national and international news.
Editorially, KXLY – Spokane News does not publish opinion pieces; however, they report on politics primarily through the Associated Press. The news is generally reported factually and with minimal bias due to a lack of editorializing.
