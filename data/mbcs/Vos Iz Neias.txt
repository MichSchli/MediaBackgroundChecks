History
[Vos Iz Neias](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vos_Iz_Neias%3F), also known as VIN, is a commentary political blog that does not list the founder or current ownership. Further, they do not have an about page to describe their purpose. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Voz Iz Neias completely lacks transparency as they do not name ownership. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Vos Iz Neias main focus is delivering news catering to American Orthodox Jews, particularly the Orthodox Jewish community in New York:
[“New York – US Orthodox Jewish Groups Condemn Violence By Religious Extremists Against Israeli Soldiers And Police Officers,”](https://www.vosizneias.com/295534/2018/04/17/new-york-us-orthodox-jewish-groups-condemn-violence-by-religious-extremists-against-israeli-soldiers-and-police-officers/) as well as mainstream news such as [“Camillus, NY – 30-year-old Man Evicted By Parents Finalizing Moving Plans.”](https://www.vosizneias.com/298580/2018/05/30/camillus-ny-30-year-old-man-evicted-by-parents-finalizing-moving-plans/)
In review, Vos Iz Neias typically republishes news from least biased credible sources, such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/), and the [Jewish Telegraphic Agency](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/jewish-telegraphic-agency-jta/). Vos Iz Neias also publishes original news content through [staff writers](https://www.vosizneias.com/authors/15927/posts). The original news stories are written with minimal loaded words and typically focus on local community news such as this: [Brooklyn, NY – Person Of Interest Questioned In Borough Park Attempted Kidnappings](https://www.vosizneias.com/297849/2018/05/17/brooklyn-ny-person-of-interest-questioned-in-borough-park-attempted-kidnappings/). In these original news stories, they typically do not hyperlink to sources but instead use quotes. However, a [factual search](https://factualsearch.news/#gsc.tab=0&gsc.q=vos%20iz%20neias&gsc.sort=&gsc.ref=fact_checking) reveals that Vos Iz Neias has never failed a fact check.
