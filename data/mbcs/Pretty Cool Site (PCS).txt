History
[Founded in 2018](http://whois.domaintools.com/prettycoolsite.com), Pretty Cool Site (PCS) is a satire website that does not have an about page. However, they do have a tagline that reads: “News for the Dumb, Joke for the Wise.” [Read our profile on Romanian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/romania-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not disclose ownership and appears to be funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Pretty Cool Site has a clear disclaimer at the
[bottom of the page](https://prettycoolsite.com/) that reads: “This is a fake news site and it has to be treated as a joke!” The satire found on this website is not very political, but rather focuses on the silly and comedic, such as this: [License plates replaced with Facebook profile names](https://prettycoolsite.com/license-plates-replaced-facebook-profile-id/).
