Analysis / Bias
In review, Eagle PAC is a conservative political action committee that funds and supports conservative candidates and causes through
[donations](https://www.opensecrets.org/pacs/lookup2.php?cycle=2018&strID=C00625285) and political activism. Eagle PAC’s current stated goals are to Drain the Swamp, Build the Wall and Save the House. Eagle PAC is not shy about their support for President Donald Trump.
The website publishes news that ranges from right biased through story selection to outright questionable such as this story:

This story is sourced back to [TownHall](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/townhall/), which has a mixed track record with facts.
