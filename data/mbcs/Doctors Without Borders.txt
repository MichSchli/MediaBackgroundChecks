History
Founded in 1971 in France by French doctors,
[Doctors Without Borders/Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/M%C3%A9decins_Sans_Fronti%C3%A8res) is an international medical humanitarian, non-governmental organization (NGO) best known for its projects in war-torn regions and developing countries affected by endemic diseases. It provides this help to all people, regardless of their race, religion, or political beliefs. [Javid Abdelmoneim](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Javid_Abdelmoneim) is the current president, and there are now 24 associations around the world.
Doctors Without Borders/ MSF indicate their
[mission statement](http://www.doctorswithoutborders.org/about-us) as “We help people worldwide where the need is greatest, delivering emergency medical aid to people affected by conflict, epidemics, disasters, or exclusion from health care.” In 1999, [Doctors without Borders](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/M%C3%A9decins_Sans_Fronti%C3%A8res) was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize. Funded by / Ownership
Doctors without Borders is a charitable corporation; they are transparent, and their financial statements can be viewed
[here](http://www.doctorswithoutborders.org/about-us/financial-information). Doctors without Borders state that they [have not received](http://www.doctorswithoutborders.org/faq-us/finances) or solicited funding from the US Government since 2002 ( [FAQ Question Nr 15](http://www.doctorswithoutborders.org/support-us/donation-faq) [)](http://www.doctorswithoutborders.org/support-us/donation-faq). Internationally, they indicate they limit government funding to less than 20 percent of total funds raised. They are a top-rated charity organization by [charity watchdogs](https://www.charitywatch.org/top-rated-charities) and has 97.23/100 rank by [Charity Navigator.](https://www.charitynavigator.org/index.cfm?bay=search.summary&orgid=3628)
Analysis / Bias
Doctors Without Borders aims to expand medical care across national boundaries and irrespective of race, religion, creed, or political affiliation. Doctors Without Borders frequently put their lives in danger by going to war-torn or severely impoverished counties and providing medical care. During the Covid-19 pandemic, they have provided medical care and support in
[70 different countries](https://www.doctorswithoutborders.org/covid19).
The website does not editorialize content and provides news about the organization such as this

All information reviewed is factual and presented with minimal bias.
