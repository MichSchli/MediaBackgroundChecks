History
The Warren Reporter is a newspaper/website based in Warren County, New Jersey. The paper and website cover local, national, business, crime, and sports news for the Warren County area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Advance Publications owns the Warren Reporter. The company dates to the early 1920s and is owned by the
[Newhouse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel_Irving_Newhouse,_Sr.) family. Donald Newhouse, together with his brother Samuel “Si” Newhouse Jr. owns Advance Publications. The [New Yorker](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-yorker/), [Vanity Fair](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/vanity-fair/), and Reddit are all produced by Advance Publications. Revenue is generated through advertising and subscription sales.
Analysis / Bias
The Warren Reporter reports local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[See all homes sold in Warren County, March 13 to March 20](https://www.nj.com/realestate-news/2023/03/see-all-homes-sold-in-warren-county-march-13-to-march-20.html). National/international news is covered via the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other Advance publications.
Editorially, The Warren Reporter does not publish opinion content online; however, story selection tends to favor the left slightly, such as this
[Hamilton school principal receives MLK community spirit award](https://www.nj.com/mercer/2023/01/hamilton-school-principal-receives-mlk-community-spirit-award.html). Generally, the news is factual and slightly left-leaning in story selection.
