History
Eagle Valley News is a newspaper published in Eagle Valley, British Columbia, Canada. The website clearwatertimes.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Eagle Valley, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Eagle Valley News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Eagle Valley News covers news for Eagle Valley and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Shuswap wildfire near Adams Lake reaches 165 hectares.](https://www.eaglevalleynews.com/news/shuswap-wildfire-near-adams-lake-reaches-165-hectares/) National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Eagle Valley News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
