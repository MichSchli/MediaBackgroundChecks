History
The Saturday Paper is a weekly newspaper published in Melbourne, Australia, in 2014. Morry Schwartz publishes the Saturday Paper, and Erik Jensen is the editor. The Saturday Paper primarily covers current affairs, culture, and Australian politics.
[Read our profile on Australian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/australia-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Schwartz Publishing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Schwartz_Publishing) owns The Saturday Paper. Subscription fees generate revenue.
