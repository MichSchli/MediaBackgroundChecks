Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Lee Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lee_Enterprises), which according to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), has donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) since 1990. The paper is funded through an online advertising and subscription model.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Daily Journal covers local news with journalists and reporters throughout the Missouri region. For national news, they use the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). All news is reported with minimal loaded words and bias. The Daily Journal always sources its information properly. [Editorially](https://dailyjournalonline.com/opinion/#tracking-source=menu-nav), there is a balance between right and left-leaning opinions with just a slight edge to the left.
