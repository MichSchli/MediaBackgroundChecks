History
Founded in 2015, The Ron Paul Liberty Report is a website and video program created by former Republican (Libertarian) Congressman Ron Paul. The Liberty Report advocates for a non-interventionist foreign policy and protecting civil liberties at home. According to their
[about page](http://www.ronpaullibertyreport.com/about.html), “The Liberty Report brings provocative opinion and analysis to the breaking issues affecting our lives and finances.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not clearly state ownership and who funds the Ron Paul Liberty Report. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Ron Paul Liberty Report features commentary and YouTube videos by Ron Paul and his staff. Articles and headlines often contain loaded emotional wording that conveys a Libertarian perspective
[Strong Dollar? Weak Dollar? Stable Dollar? — The Fed Is Ruining The Dollar!](http://www.ronpaullibertyreport.com/archives/strong-dollar-weak-dollar-stable-dollar-the-fed-is-ruining-the-dollar) While many articles deal with economics, others promote conspiracy theories and pseudoscience. For example, they have promoted [anti-mask propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-mask-misinformation/), which has resulted in false claims ratings by fact-checkers (see below).
The Liberty Report generally sources credible information. However, there are times that they source to
[far-right](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/right/), [questionable](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fake-news/), or [conspiracy](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/conspiracy/) websites. They also occasionally publish unsubstantiated claims, such as the Syria chemical attack being a [false flag operation](http://www.ronpaullibertyreport.com/archives/syria-gas-attack-assads-doingor-false-flag). In general, they promote free-market capitalism; however, they often promote far-right talking points such as this [Do We Need A ‘9/11 Commission’ To Investigate The Fake ‘Insurrection’?](http://www.ronpaullibertyreport.com/archives/do-we-need-a-911-commission-to-investigate-the-fake-insurrection) The Ron Paul Liberty Report is right biased and sometimes not factual.
