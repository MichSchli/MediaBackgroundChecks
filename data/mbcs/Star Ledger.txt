History
[Founded in 1832](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Star-Ledger), The Star-Ledger is the largest circulated broadsheet newspaper in the U.S. state of New Jersey and is based in Newark. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Advance Publications own the Star-Ledger. The company dates to the early 1920s and is owned by the
[Newhouse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samuel_Irving_Newhouse,_Sr.) family. Donald Newhouse, together with his brother Samuel “Si” Newhouse Jr. owns Advance Publications. The [New Yorker](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-yorker/), [Vanity Fair](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/vanity-fair/), and Reddit are all produced by Advance Publications. Revenue is generated through advertising and subscription sales.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Star-Ledger reports local news through reporters with minimal bias, and national/international news is covered via the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). This review is focused on content from Nj.com, which publishes online content for the Star-Ledger. NJ.Com publishes mostly editorial content from the Star-Ledger that tends to lean left, such as these: [Trump’s environmental record should be impeachable: Sierra Club](https://www.nj.com/opinion/2019/12/trumps-environmental-record-should-be-impeachable-sierra-club.html) [New Jersey’s proud role in the impeachment fight | Moran](https://www.nj.com/opinion/2019/12/new-jerseys-proud-role-in-the-impeachment-fight-moran.html) [The #MeToo movement vs. Gov. Phil Murphy | Moran](https://www.nj.com/opinion/2019/12/the-metoo-movement-vs-gov-phil-murphy-moran.html)
Finally, the Star-Ledger has
[endorsed](https://noahveltman.com/endorsements/) Democratic Presidential candidates since 1992. The Star-Ledger generally reports news factually, with a left-leaning editorial bias.
