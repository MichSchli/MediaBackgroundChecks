History
Founded in 1956 and an ABC/NBC-affiliated television station, WDAM – Hattiesburg News serves Hattiesburg, Mississippi, and the surrounding area. wdam.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.

Funded by / Ownership
WDAM – Hattiesburg News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WDAM – Hattiesburg News provides news coverage for Hattiesburg, Mississippi, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Hattiesburg City Council accepts redistricting plan](https://www.wdam.com/2022/01/19/hattiesburg-city-council-accepts-redistricting-plan/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WDAM – Hattiesburg News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
