History
CKNX News Today is a part of Blackburn Media Inc., originally known as Blackburn Radio Inc., which was founded in 1852 by
[Josiah Blackburn](https://blackburnmedia.ca/about-us-history) as the London Free Press. Today, it operates 13 radio stations and local news sites, serving Southwestern Ontario. CKNX News Today, established to provide local news, sports, and event coverage across the London communities, is headquartered in Ontario, Canada. Funded by / Ownership
CKNX News Today is owned by
[Blackburn Media Inc](https://blackburnmedia.ca/)., a Canadian media company. The company operates several radio stations and news websites in Ontario. The revenue for CKNX News Today is generated through advertising and sponsorships.
Analysis / Bias
The news articles on CKNX News Today generally focus on local events and issues within the communities they cover, such as
[OPP increase presence in Brussels for investigation](https://cknxnewstoday.ca/midwestern/news/2023/08/05/opp-increase-presence-in-brussels-for-investigation). Generally, articles are sourced through quotes, and they do not cover national or international news.
Editorially, CKNX News Today does not have a strong editorial stance, as its primary focus is on local news and events. However, they have a left-leaning perspective on the issues they present that the progressive left favors, such as
[Climate change affects vulnerable people more](https://cknxnewstoday.ca/midwestern/news/2023/07/26/climate-change-affects-vulnerable-people-more). In general, the website is fact-based and holds a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
