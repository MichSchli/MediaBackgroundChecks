History
Founded in 1953, WLFI – Lafayette News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Lafayette, Indiana, United States. wlfi.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Lafayette area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WLFI – Lafayette News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WLFI – Lafayette News publishes news for Lafayette and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Indianapolis man arrested following deadly I-65 crash](https://www.wlfi.com/news/indianapolis-man-arrested-following-deadly-i-65-crash/article_ec034c14-eddd-11ec-bf0e-b7a4d6468a98.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
