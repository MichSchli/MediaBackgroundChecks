History
The Orillia Today is a newspaper published in Orillia, Ontario, Canada. simcoe.com/orillia-on/ provides online local, national, and world news for the greater Orillia, Ontario region.
Read our profile on the
[Canadian government’s influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of Orillia Today is Metroland Media Group Ltd., a subsidiary of Nordstar Capital LP. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In 2015 and
[2021](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2021/09/19/the-stars-editorial-board-endorses-liberals-for-2021-federal-election.html), the [Toronto Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/) (Nordstar), which owns the Orillia Today publisher Metroland Media Group, [endorsed](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2015/10/09/for-justin-trudeau-and-the-liberal-party-editorial.html) the Liberal Party under Justin Trudeau.
In review, Orillia Today uses low-biased wording when reporting local news such as this:
[Man and woman in their 30s charged in connection to drug bust at home in Orillia’s north end](https://www.simcoe.com/news-story/10849051-man-and-woman-in-their-30s-charged-in-connection-to-drug-bust-at-home-in-orillia-s-north-end/). National news is derived from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/), while world news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, a review of op-eds slightly favors the left. In general, they are fact-based and hold a left-leaning editorial bias.
