History
[Founded in 2002](https://whois.domaintools.com/gaycitynews.com), Gay City News is a New York City-based weekly newspaper serving the LGBTQ community. According to their [about page](https://gaycitynews.com/about/), the “Gay City News is the city’s only LGBTQ newspaper, covering news, politics, health, crime, activism and advocacy, leadership, arts, and events around town.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Schneps Media](https://gaycitynews.com/about/), a private New York City-based company, owns and publishes Gay City News. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Gay City News publishes news, politics, crime, art, and sports for the gay community in New York City. Articles and headlines sometimes contain moderately loaded emotional language such as this
[The Gender Issues Permeating PrEP](https://gaycitynews.com/gender-issues-permeating-prep/). When it comes to sourcing, they reference interviews, police reports, and credible media outlets such as the [Bay Area Reporter](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bay-area-reporter/).
Under the “Perspectives” category, they publish opinion pieces and editorials that favor a progressive liberal perspective, such as this
[Overdue Diversity for Council’s LGBT Caucus](https://gaycitynews.com/overdue-diversity-for-councils-lgbt-caucus/) and this [Op-Ed: We Can’t End HIV Epidemic Without Gender-Affirming Care](https://gaycitynews.com/op-ed-we-cant-end-hiv-epidemic-without-gender-affirming-care/). In general, they publish fact-based news from a liberal perspective.
