History
Founded in 1985, WBTS – NBC10 – Boston is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Nashua, New Hampshire, United States. NBCboston.com is the associated website covering news, weather, sports, crime, and lifestyle. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WBTS – NBC10 – Boston is owned by NBCUniversal a subsidiary of Comcast, which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WBTS – NBC10 – Boston covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Two Teens Shot Near McDonald’s in Framingham: Police](https://www.nbcboston.com/news/local/police-investigation-in-framingham/2771939/). Videos from the TV station accompany most stories. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other NBC-owned stations provide national and world news. All information is sourced from first-hand accounts and other credible media sources.
Editorially WBTS – NBC10 – Boston typically does not produce opinion content; however, they report on national politics with a slight left-leaning bias in wording and story selection
[Mass. Senate Passes Reproductive Rights Bill; Now Work Starts on Final Compromise](https://www.nbcboston.com/news/local/mass-senate-passes-reproductive-rights-bill-now-work-starts-on-final-compromise-with-house/2771742/). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering political issues.
