History
The Peace Arch News is a newspaper published in Peace Arch, British Columbia, Canada. The website peacearchnews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Peace Arch area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Peace Arch News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Peace Arch News covers news for Peace Arch and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Mayor-elect Brenda Locke announces housing summit for the spring as Surrey grapples with affordable housing crisis.](https://www.peacearchnews.com/news/mayor-elect-brenda-locke-announces-housing-summit-for-the-spring-as-surrey-grapples-with-affordable-housing-crisis/) National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Peace Arch News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
