History
K-Hits 106.9 – Tulsa is a radio station based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Khits.com is the website for the radio station and its news programming.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Griffin Communications, a media company based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, owns K-Hits 106.9 – Tulsa. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
