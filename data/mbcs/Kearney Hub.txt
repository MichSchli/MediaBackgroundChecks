History
Kearney Hub is a daily print newspaper published in the town of Kearney, Nebraska, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Kearney and the surrounding area. Kearneyhub.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lee Enterprises owns and publishes Kearney Hub. According to
[Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Lee Enterprises donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) from 1990 until 2016. However, during the 2020 election cycle, they donated 70% to Democratic candidates and causes. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Kearney Hub reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and entertainment for the greater Kearney, NE region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[Kearney real estate office expands, adds mortgage business](https://kearneyhub.com/news/local/kearney-real-estate-office-expands-adds-mortgage-business/article_0954ddb2-9c3c-11ec-ae19-f38d7a27361e.html#tracking-source=home-top-story). Further, the website publishes national and international news from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). Finally, all information is sourced via first-person accounts or through credible media.
Editorially, Kearney Hub reasonably represents both sides. A review of editorial articles demonstrates a reasonable low bias; however, slightly more op-eds favor the right. While more op-eds favor the right, liberal perspectives have fair representation; therefore, they earn a least biased rating on the right side of the scale. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
