History
Freedom House is a U.S.-based organization that conducts research and advocacy on democracy, political freedom, and human rights. Freedom House was founded in
[October 1941](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Freedom_House). They have been criticized for excessively criticizing states opposing US interests while unduly sympathetic to regimes supporting US interests. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Freedom House is a 501(c)(3) U.S. Government-funded non-governmental organization (NGO).
[Funding](https://freedomhouse.org/sites/default/files/FINAL_Basic_Financial_Statements_2016.pdf) is primarily derived from the US Government.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Freedom House annually produces its
[Freedom in the World](https://freedomhouse.org/report/freedom-world/2019/democracy-retreat) report, which assesses each country’s degree of political freedoms and civil liberties. It is frequently cited by political scientists, journalists, and policymakers. For example, in 2019, they reported that “US Freedom is in Decline.” Freedom House also produces reports on Authoritarian Reach, Democracies in Decline, and Equality & Human Rights. In addition, freedom House occasionally utilizes emotional wording such as: [The Crisis of Social Media](https://freedomhouse.org/report/freedom-net/2019/crisis-social-media).
Freedom House has faced criticism by authoritarian regimes and
[Libertarians](https://web.archive.org/web/20121212002804/http://paul.house.gov/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=217&Itemid=60) in the USA who find they are too pro-USA Government.
