History
The Park Record is a daily newspaper serving Park City, Utah. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Park Record is published by Swift Communications, which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the western USA. Ogden Newspapers owns Swift. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Park Record publishes news for Park City, Utah, and the surrounding area. Local news is covered through reporting with minimal bias in wording, such as
[Wanship woman arrested after three-month drug investigation](https://www.parkrecord.com/news/summit-county/wanship-woman-arrested-after-three-month-drug-investigation/). The paper and website do not cover national and international news.
A review of editorials demonstrates a reasonable balance without extremes such as this
[Record editorial: County Courthouse hiring difficulties are a warning for Park City-area businesses](https://www.parkrecord.com/opinion/record-editorial-county-courthouse-hiring-difficulties-are-a-warning-for-park-city-area-businesses/). In general, they exhibit low bias and produce fact-based news.
