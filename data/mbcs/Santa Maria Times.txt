History
The Santa Maria Times is a print newspaper from Santa Maria, California, that serves Santa Barbara County.
[Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper is owned by
[Lee Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lee_Enterprises), which according to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/) has donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) since 1990. The Santa Maria Times is funded through a subscription and advertising model.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Santa Maria Times covers local news with journalists and reporters throughout Southern California. For national news, they use the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). All news is reported with minimal loaded words and bias. The Santa Maria Times always sources its information properly. [Editorially](http://santamariatimes.com/opinion/columnists/mike-gonzalez-stop-hyperventilating-census-citizenship-question-makes-good-sense/article_3b87baf5-15de-522b-864b-fe6b36c7188c.html), there is a balance between right and left-leaning opinions with just a slight edge to the right.
