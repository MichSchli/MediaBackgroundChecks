History
Founded in 2005 and an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station, KJCT – Grand Junction News serves Grand Junction, Colorado, and the surrounding area. kjct8.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KJCT – Grand Junction News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KJCT – Grand Junction News provides news coverage for Grand Junction, Colorado, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Robbery leads to car crash, suspect detained](https://www.kjct8.com/2022/11/27/robbery-leads-car-crash-suspect-detained/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KJCT – Grand Junction News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
