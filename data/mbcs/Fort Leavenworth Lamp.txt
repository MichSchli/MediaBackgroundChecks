History
The Fort Leavenworth Lamp is a newspaper published in Fort Leavenworth, Kansas. gardnernews.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the Fort Leavenworth Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Fort Leavenworth Lamp is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Fort Leavenworth Lamp covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Fort Leavenworth, Kansas area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[School board provides updates on enrollment, budget, communication, security](https://www.ftleavenworthlamp.com/2022/08/25/school-board-provides-updates-on-enrollment-budget-communication-security/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
