History
Lacombe Express is a newspaper published in Lacombe, Alberta, Canada. The website lacombeexpress.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Lacombe, Alberta area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Lacombe Express. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Lacombe Express covers news for Lacombe and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Province holding firm on oil company tax break](https://www.lacombeexpress.com/news/province-holding-firm-on-oil-company-tax-break-7322342). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Lacombe Express does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
