History
Founded in 1952 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station, KWCH – Wichita News serves Wichita, Kansas, and the surrounding area. Kwch.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KWCH – Wichita News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KWCH – Wichita News provides news coverage for Wichita, Kansas, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Missing Marion County man found dead](https://www.kwch.com/2022/04/10/chase-county-sheriffs-office-seeks-help-locate-missing-man/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KWCH – Wichita News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
