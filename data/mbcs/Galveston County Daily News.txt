History
Founded in 1842, the Galveston County Daily News is a newspaper and website published in Galveston, Texas, covering Galveston County. The paper covers local news, sports business, crime, and opinion.

Funded by / Ownership
The Galveston County Daily News is owned by
[Southern Newspapers Inc](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_Newspapers)., which owns many small-market newspapers in Texas, Alabama, and Georgia. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Galveston County Daily News covers local news through journalists with minimal bias such as this:
[Police: Man in custody in connection to fatal Seawall hit-and-run](https://www.galvnews.com/news/police/free/article_340a9fd5-0b1b-5985-806f-9a0e6d1666f7.html). National news is delivered through the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, the Galveston County Daily News provides balance; however, more opinion pieces favor the right such as this
[Family, community are where the answer to gun violence begins](https://www.galvnews.com/opinion/editorials/article_510bc453-5773-540b-a8f7-381f36ec2650.html?block_id=531919). They generally report news factually and with a slight right-leaning editorial bias.
