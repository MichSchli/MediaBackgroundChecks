Analysis / Bias
In review, the KFOR-TV website publishes news about Oklahoma City and the surrounding areas. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias such as this:
[Oklahoma City police investigating possible homicide after person found dead in vehicle](https://kfor.com/news/local/oklahoma-city-police-investigating-possible-homicide-after-person-found-dead-in-vehicle/). National news is primarily covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). Lastly, the KFOR website does not publish op-eds. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
