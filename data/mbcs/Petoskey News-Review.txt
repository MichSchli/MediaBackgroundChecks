History
The Petoskey News-Review is a newspaper that serves Petoskey, Michigan, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. petoskeynews.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Petoskey News-Review. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Petoskey News-Review covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Petoskey, Michigan area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Two new workforce homes break ground in downtown Petoskey](https://www.petoskeynews.com/story/news/local/2022/12/03/two-new-workforce-homes-break-ground-in-downtown-petoskey/69693686007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Petoskey News-Review does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Kendall Stanley: Feeding the insanity](https://www.petoskeynews.com/story/opinion/columns/2022/12/03/kendall-stanley-feeding-the-insanity/69694643007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
