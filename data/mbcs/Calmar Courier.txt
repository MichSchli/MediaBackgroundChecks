History
The Calmar Courier is a newspaper published in Calmar, Indiana, United States. The paper covers news, sports, lifestyle, and opinion for Calmar and the surrounding area. calmarcourier.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Mid America Publishing owns and publishes the Calmar Courier. Mid America Publishing publishes
[several community newspapers](https://www.midampublishing.com/content/mid-america-publications) throughout Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Calmar Courier reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Calmar, Indiana region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[LEGO League Scores Well at State](http://www.calmarcourier.com/content/lego-league-scores-well-state). The newspaper and website do not publish national news, focusing on local news reporting.
Editorially, the Calmar Courier reasonably represents both sides. A review of op-eds demonstrates a reasonably low bias; however, slightly more op-eds favor the right. While more op-eds favor the right, liberal perspectives have fair representation; therefore, they earn a least biased rating on the right side of the scale. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
