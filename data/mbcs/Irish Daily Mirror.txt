History
Launched in 1997,
[Irish Daily Mirror](https://www.irishmirror.ie/about-us/) is an Irish national daily tabloid. It is based in Dublin, Ireland, and covers various topics such as news, sports, business, classifieds, and lifestyle. Irishmirror.ie is the online version of the newspaper. The format is similar to the British national tabloid [The Daily Mirror](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-mirror/), including celebrity gossip and [viral videos.](https://www.irishmirror.ie/news/irish-news/irish-ice-cream-shop-goes-27636442) [View our profile on Ireland’s media and government](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/ireland-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Irish Mirror’s parent company is
[Reach PLC](https://www.reachplc.com/about-us/our-brands) which owns numerous newspapers and websites across the United Kingdom, like [The Daily Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-star-uk/). Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
The Irish Daily Mirror is fully transparent by describing itself as a
[left-of-center](https://www.irishmirror.ie/about-us/) publication.
The Irish Daily Mirror covers various topics, including politics, most often with emotionally loaded language such as
[“Leo Varadkar says he hasn’t experimented with recreational drugs since being elected to Dail.”](https://www.irishmirror.ie/news/irish-news/politics/leo-varadkar-says-hasnt-experimented-28811772) [Leo Varadkar](https://www.politico.eu/article/leo-varadkar-prime-minister-ireland-elections-wins/) is the Center-Right Prime Minister of Ireland. Regarding sourcing, they often use extensive quotes and other credible sources like [The Irish Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-irish-times/), [The Irish Examiner](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-irish-examiner/), and [Irish Independent](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/irish-independent/).
On the issues such as immigration and refugees, Irish Daily Mirror reports with a positive tone,
[“Syrian refugees living in Ireland tell of the loneliness they endure in centres across the country.”](https://www.irishmirror.ie/news/irish-news/syrian-refugees-living-ireland-tell-17267218) Irishmirror.ie also publishes celebrity gossip such as [“Adele sends message to Megan Thee Stallion after Tory Lanez shooting verdict.”](https://www.irishmirror.ie/showbiz/us-gossip/adele-sends-message-megan-thee-28816415)
Editorially they generally hold a left-leaning editorial bias in support of the
[Labour party](https://www.irishmirror.ie/news/irish-news/politics/raise-roof-protest-thousands-take-28591153) and are factual in reporting.
