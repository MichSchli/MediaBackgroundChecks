History
Founded in 1986, Air & Space/Smithsonian magazine is a bimonthly magazine put out by the National Air and Space Museum.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Air & Space Magazine is owned by Smithsonian Enterprises, with revenue derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Air & Space Magazine publishes stories that involve topics related to aviation, space travel, and space-related physics. There is minimal bias, and all reports are science-related, such as
[The F-86 Sabre, Hero of the Early Jet Age](https://www.airspacemag.com/military-aviation/north-american-f-86-sabre-180975841/). In general, this is a highly factual pro-science source.
