History
The Thomasville Times-Enterprise is a daily newspaper published in the city of Thomasville, Georgia. timesenterprise.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Thomasville Times-Enterprise. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Thomasville Times-Enterprise covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Thomasville, Georgia, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Library plans for renovations](https://www.timesenterprise.com/news/local_news/library-plans-for-renovations/article_4389c3c4-0480-11ed-832e-67f6196b6b41.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left, such as this
[Remember Juneteenth as a day of liberation and freedom](https://www.timesenterprise.com/opinion/editorials/remember-juneteenth-as-a-day-of-liberation-and-freedom/article_a267a62d-c02e-5b8a-8457-db86045c62d5.html). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
