History
The Ellwood City Ledger is a newspaper that serves Ellwood City, Pennsylvania, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. ellwoodcityledger.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Ellwood City Ledger. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Ellwood City Ledger covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Ellwood City, Pennsylvania, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Lawrence County home prices rose 1.9% in December, with houses listed at a median of $139,750](https://www.ellwoodcityledger.com/story/news/local/2024/01/12/real-estate-listings-202312/72189651007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
