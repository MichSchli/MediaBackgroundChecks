History
WindsorNewsToday is a part of Blackburn Media Inc., originally known as Blackburn Radio Inc., which was founded in 1852 by
[Josiah Blackburn](https://blackburnmedia.ca/about-us-history) as the London Free Press. Today, it operates 13 radio stations and local news sites, serving Southwestern Ontario. WindsorNewsToday, established to provide local news, sports, and event coverage across the Leamington communities, is headquartered in Ontario, Canada. [Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WindsorNewsToday is owned by
[Blackburn Media Inc](https://blackburnmedia.ca/)., a Canadian media company. The company operates several radio stations and news websites in Ontario. The revenue for WindsorNewsToday is generated through advertising and sponsorships.
Analysis / Bias
The news articles on WindsorNewsToday generally focus on local events and issues within the communities they cover, such as
[Province releases plan to support growing electricity needs](https://windsornewstoday.ca/news/2023/07/10/province-releases-plan-to-support-growing-electricity-needs). Generally, articles are sourced through quotes, and they do not cover national or international news.
Editorially, WindsorNewsToday does not have a strong editorial stance, as its primary focus is on local news and events. However, they have a left-leaning perspective on the issues they present that the progressive left favors, such as
[Downtown Chatham art exhibit to focus on climate change](https://windsornewstoday.ca/news/2023/03/05/downtown-chatham-art-exhibit-focus-climate-change). In general, the website is fact-based and holds a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
