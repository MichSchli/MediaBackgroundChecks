History
Rossland News is a newspaper published in Rossland, British Columbia, Canada. The website rosslandnews.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Rossland, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Rossland News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Rossland News covers news for Rossland and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Trail RCMP ramp up well-being patrols for people and pets during cold snap](https://www.rosslandnews.com/news/trail-rcmp-ramp-up-well-being-patrols-for-people-and-pets-during-cold-snap-7296988). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Rossland News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
