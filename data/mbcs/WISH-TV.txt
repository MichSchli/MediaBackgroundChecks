History
[Founded in 1954](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WISH-TV), WISH-TV is a CW-affiliated television station licensed to Indianapolis, Indiana, United States. The associated news website is wishtv.com which covers news, sports, weather, and crime reporting for the greater Indianapolis area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Circle City Broadcasting owns WISH-TV, and revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WISH-TV covers local news through reporters with minimal bias in wording such as this
[Indiana teachers eligible for COVID vaccine, but not through state sites](https://www.wishtv.com/podcast-episode/indiana-teachers-eligible-for-covid-vaccine-but-not-through-state-sites/). National and international news is derived from various sources such as [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WISH-TV does not produce op-eds; however, they report on politics, often using CNN as the source. Based on using CNN for political reporting, we rate them left-center biased and factual in reporting.
