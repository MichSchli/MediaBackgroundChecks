History
Founded in 1957, KSAT – San Antonio News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to San Antonio, Texas, United States. Ksat.com is the associated website that reports news, crime, sports, weather, and lifestyle for the greater San Antonio area. [Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KSAT – San Antonio News is owned by
[Graham Holdings](https://www.ghco.com/), which owns seven TV stations and the website [Slate](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/slate/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KSAT – San Antonio News provides news, sports, weather, crime reporting, and lifestyle for the greater San Antonio metro area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this
[Police arrest San Antonio man who threatened to commit a mass shooting](https://www.ksat.com/news/local/2022/06/28/police-arrest-san-antonio-man-who-threatened-to-commit-a-mass-shooting/). Many stories provide videos from the KPRC TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) generally offers national and world news.
Editorially, they do not produce Op-Eds; however, they do report on politics through the Associated Press and original reporting. Political news is generally balanced and presented with minimal bias, such as this
[GOP race for Colorado elections head features indicted clerk](https://www.ksat.com/news/politics/2022/06/28/gop-race-for-colorado-elections-head-features-indicted-clerk/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
