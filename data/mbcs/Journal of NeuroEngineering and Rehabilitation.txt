History
The Journal of NeuroEngineering and Rehabilitation is a peer-reviewed open-access journal published by BioMed Central (BMC) covering NeuroEngineering and Rehabilitation. According to its
[about page](https://jneuroengrehab.biomedcentral.com/about), “Journal of NeuroEngineering and Rehabilitation provides a forum for researchers and clinicians interested in understanding the way neuroscience and biomedical engineering are continuing to reshape physical medicine and rehabilitation and human movement augmentation.”
Founded in 2000, BioMed Central, or BMC, is a United Kingdom-based, for-profit scientific open-access publisher. They publish more than 300 journals that are entirely online. According to their about page, “Our leading research journals include selective titles such as
[BMC Biology](https://bmcbiol.biomedcentral.com/), [BMC Medicine](https://bmcmedicine.biomedcentral.com/), [Genome Biology](https://genomebiology.biomedcentral.com/) and [Journal of NeuroEngineering and Rehabilitation](https://genomemedicine.biomedcentral.com/), academic journals such as [Journal of Hematology & Oncology](https://jhoonline.biomedcentral.com/), [Malaria Journal](https://malariajournal.biomedcentral.com/), and [Microbiome](https://microbiomejournal.biomedcentral.com/), and the [BMC series](https://www.biomedcentral.com/p/the-bmc-series-journals), 65 inclusive journals focused on the needs of individual research communities. We also partner with leading institutions and societies to publish journals on their behalf.” [Read our profile on the UK government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[BioMed Central](https://www.biomedcentral.com/journals) is owned by [Springer Nature](https://www.springer.com/gp/about-springer/media/press-releases/corporate/springer-nature-created-following-merger-completion/256626), an academic publishing company created by the May 2015 merger of Springer Science+Business Media and Holtzbrinck Publishing Group’s Nature Publishing Group, Palgrave Macmillan, and Macmillan Education. The company reported revenues of over 1 billion USD.
Analysis / Bias
Journal of NeuroEngineering and Rehabilitation is an open-access journal that publishes information and research related to NeuroEngineering and Rehabilitation. The journal primarily publishes research like this
[In-depth quantification of bimanual coordination using the Kinarm exoskeleton robot in children with unilateral cerebral palsy](https://jneuroengrehab.biomedcentral.com/articles/10.1186/s12984-023-01278-6).
BioMed Central (BMC) is an open-access publisher of scientific journals. There are two types of Open Access Journals, legitimate and predatory, both of which charge the author for publication.
[Predatory journals](https://predatoryjournals.com/about/) often lack peer review and are generally considered not credible. BioMed Central is considered a [legitimate, open-access publisher](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC5017830/) that is frequently indexed in [PubMed](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pubmed/). However, BMC journals have been criticized for [retracting numerous studies](https://www.the-scientist.com/the-nutshell/mass-retraction-35728) due to fake peer reviews or poor methodology.
Although BMC has retracted some studies in other publications that were found to be of poor methodology and not thoroughly peer-reviewed, the Journal of NeuroEngineering and Rehabilitation publishes credible scientific studies that are factual and appropriately peer-reviewed.
