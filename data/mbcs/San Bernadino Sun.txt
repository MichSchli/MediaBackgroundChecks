History
The San Bernardino Sun is a print newspaper serving San Bernardino County, California, and was founded in 1894.

Funded by / Ownership
The
[San Bernardino Sun](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_San_Bernardino_Sun) is a paid daily newspaper that is currently owned by [MediaNews Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MediaNews_Group). The paper is funded through a subscription and advertising model.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the San Bernardino Sun covers local and regional news through the use of reporters with minimal bias. National news is published directly from the least biased
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). All news coverage has minimal loaded words and is properly sourced. Editorially, there is a right-leaning bias with opinions that favor the conservative policy.
