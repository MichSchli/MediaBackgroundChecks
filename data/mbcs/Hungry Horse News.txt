History
The Hungry Horse News is a newspaper published in Hungry Horse, Montana, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Hungry Horse and the surrounding area. hungryhorsenews.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
