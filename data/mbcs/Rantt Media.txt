History
According to their
[about page](https://rantt.com/about) “Rantt Media is an independently owned news company that launched in October 2016. We analyze the news, shed light on injustices, and tell the stories that matter to you.” Rantt was founded by Zak Ali, Adam Al-Ali, Josh Lytle, and Ahmed Baba who is COO/Editor-in-Chief of a Rantt Media.
Funded by / Ownership
Ownership is not disclosed on the website though one would assume the four co-founders are the owners of Rantt Media Inc. The website is funded through donations, a Patreon account and online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Rantt Media is a website that features a variety of writers mostly covering political news. Most stories tend to favor the left and are critical of the right. There is the moderate use of loaded language in headlines such as:
[Fox News Is Gaslighting Their Viewers Into Authoritarianism](https://rantt.com/fox-news-is-gaslighting-their-viewers-into-authoritarianism-bc6e69c53622), however, the wording within articles is less loaded. While the stories may favor the left, there is excellent use of sourcing within each article. Rantt Media typically uses mainstream or official Government sources that are highly factual.
A
[factual search](https://factualsearch.news/#gsc.tab=0&gsc.q=rantt&gsc.sort=&gsc.ref=fact_checking) also reveals they have not failed any fact checks to date.
