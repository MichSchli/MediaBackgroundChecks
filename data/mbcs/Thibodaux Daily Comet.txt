History
The Thibodaux Daily Comet is a newspaper that serves Thibodaux, Louisana, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. dailycomet.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Thibodaux Daily Comet. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Thibodaux Daily Comet covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Thibodaux, Louisiana area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Mom and boyfriend indicted on murder charges in death of 2-year-old Ezekiel Harry of Houma. ](https://www.dailycomet.com/story/news/2022/08/16/mom-boyfriend-indicted-murder-2-year-old-ezekiel-harry-houma/10336255002/)National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Thibodaux Daily Comet does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Our opinion: Violence should not be tolerated, at the Capitol or anywhere else](https://www.dailycomet.com/story/opinion/2021/01/17/our-opinion-violence-should-not-tolerated-capitol-anywhere-else/6632172002/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
