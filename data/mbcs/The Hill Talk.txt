History
[Founded in 2013](http://whois.domaintools.com/thehilltalk.com), The Hill Talk is a website that provides political news briefs. According to their [about page](http://thehilltalk.com/about/) “The Hill Talk delivers light and entertaining briefs of the day’s top political news from the U.S. and around the world. We have all you need to talk politics and current events at the water cooler or dinner table.” A search of the website did not disclose an editor or owner. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Hill Talk does not disclose ownership and the website is funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Hill Talk publishes/summarizes political news through a variety of writers. There is the moderate use of loaded language in headlines such as this:
[Incompetence abounds as Florida recount goes to 2nd round.](http://thehilltalk.com/2018/11/16/florida-recount-2nd-round/) When it comes to sourcing, The Hill Talk does an excellent job of utilizing credible sources and hyperlinks appropriately. [Editorially](http://thehilltalk.com/category/editorial/), there is a reasonable balance of opinions that are never extreme one way or the other, however, there is more that tend to lean left. Opinion pieces are also appropriately sourced.
