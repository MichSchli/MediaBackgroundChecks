History
Founded in 1958, WKBW – Buffalo News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Buffalo, New York, United States. Wkbw.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WKBW – Buffalo News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WKBW – Buffalo News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Buffalo, New York area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Local partners launch food distribution effort to support Buffalo community impacted by mass shooting](https://www.wkbw.com/news/local-news/local-partners-launch-food-distribution-effort-to-support-buffalo-community-impacted-by-mass-shooting). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Bidens to visit Uvalde, Texas on Sunday](https://www.wkbw.com/news/national/bidens-to-visit-uvalde-texas-on-sunday). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
