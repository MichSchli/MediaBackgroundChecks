History
The Saint John Times-Globe is a newspaper published in Saint John, New Brunswick, Canada. tj.news/greater-saint-john is the online domain that covers local, national, business, and sports news.
Read our profile on the
[Canadian government’s influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The Saint John Times-Globe is owned by
[PostMedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Postmedia_Network), which owns numerous right-leaning media outlets throughout Canada. According to a report in the left-leaning [CanadaLand](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canadaland/), the PostMedia Network has [directed](https://www.canadalandshow.com/the-conservative-transformation-of-postmedia/) its publications to be more “reliably conservative.” [Chatham Asset Management,](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2018-08-30/behind-trump-s-tabloid-king-a-connected-and-flush-hedge-fund) a New Jersey-based hedge fund, holds a large equity stake in PostMedia. The newspaper generates revenue through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Saint John Times-Globe reports local and regional news through journalists with minimal loaded words like this:
[Trio Steal $3000 from Superstore in Register Scam.](https://tj.news/greater-saint-john/102143914) The national news is derived primarily from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/) and [National Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/national-post/), whereas world news comes from multiple sources such as [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/), [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/), and [Bloomberg](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bloomberg/). Generally, straight news reporting is factual and presents minimal biased wording; however, story selection tends to lean right.
Editorially, there is a clear right-leaning bias with commentaries. While The Saint John Times-Globe does offer opposing viewpoints, on the whole, more editorials favor the conservative right.
