History
Founded in 1950 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, WSMV – Nashville serves Nashville, Tennessee, and the surrounding area. Wsmv.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WSMV – Nashville is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WSMV – Nashville provides news coverage for Nashville, Tennessee, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Metro Police investigating shooting on I-24](https://www.wsmv.com/news/metro-police-investigating-shooting-on-i-24/article_48ad0662-4d9d-11ec-b6cd-af4620aa88ff.html?block_id=994460). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WSMV – Nashville does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
