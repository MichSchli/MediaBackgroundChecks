History
The Dekalb Daily Chronicle is a newspaper published in Dekalb, Illinois. shawlocal.com/daily-chronicle/ is the website covering news, crime, sports, and business for the Morrison area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Dekalb Daily Chronicle is owned by
[Shaw Media](https://shawmedia.com/our-markets/), which is an American, Dixon, Illinois-based publisher of about 80 newspapers and news websites in Illinois and Iowa. Revenue is derived from sponsored content, advertising, and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, local news is published through journalists and with minimal bias, such as this:
[Cortland man pleads not guilty to slew of felonies involving burglary, guns and drugs.](https://www.shawlocal.com/northwest-herald/news/2022/12/02/cortland-man-pleads-not-guilty-to-slew-of-felonies-involving-burglary-guns-and-drugs/) The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other wire services cover national and international news.
Editorially, they have a page dedicated to op-eds that are basically balanced in content. Generally, news reporting is factual and low-biased, while op-eds are reasonably balanced.
