Funded by / Ownership
The PA Media Group owns the Patriot-News, and Advance Publications owns the PA Media Group. Advance Publications holds Conde Naste properties such as the left-leaning
[Vanity Fair](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/vanity-fair/), [The New Yorker](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-yorker/), and Vogue. Advertising and subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Patriot-News covers news for Harrisburgh, Pennsylvania, and the surrounding regions. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Hegins Township fire claims two lives](https://www.pennlive.com/news/2021/05/hegins-township-fire-claims-two-lives.html). National and international news is primarily covered by the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Patriot-News typically endorses Democratic candidates such as Joe Biden in 2020:
[The Editorial Board endorses Joe Biden: Here are seven reasons why.](https://www.pennlive.com/opinion/2020/10/seven-reasons-for-pennlives-endorsement-of-the-next-president-of-the-united-states-pennlive-editorial.html) A review of numerous op-eds indicates a left-leaning preference.
