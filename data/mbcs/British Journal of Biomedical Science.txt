History
British Journal of Biomedical Science is a peer-reviewed open-access journal that covers the field of Blockchain. According to its about page, “The British Journal of Biomedical Science is committed to publishing high quality original research that represents a clear advance in biomedical science and reviews that summarise recent advances in this field.”

Funded by / Ownership
British Journal of Biomedical Science is owned by
[Frontiers Media SA](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals), which is a for-profit corporation. Funding comes from digital sales of content and author fees for publication.
Analysis / Bias
British Journal of Biomedical Science is an open-access journal that publishes information and research related to biomedical science. The journal primarily publishes research like this
[Embedding Scientific Communication and Digital Capabilities in the Undergraduate Biomedical Science Curriculum](https://www.frontierspartnerships.org/articles/10.3389/bjbs.2023.11284/full).
[Frontiers Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/frontiers/) has been criticized for being a predatory open-access publisher. [In short](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-019-03759-y), Predatory Open Access publishing is an exploitative open-access publishing business model that involves charging publication fees to authors without providing the editorial and publishing services associated with legitimate journals.
However, British Journal of Biomedical Science is a credible scientific journal, as we have not found any controversy or significant retractions. Although, Frontiers Media has
[retracted](https://retractionwatch.com/2014/09/26/publisher-issues-statement-of-concern-about-hiv-denial-paper-launches-investigation/) some studies in other publications that were found to be of poor methodology and not thoroughly peer-reviewed.
