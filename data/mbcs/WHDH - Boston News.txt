History
Launched in 1982, WHDH – Boston News is an independent television station licensed to Boston, Massachusetts, United States, and also serving Fort Lauderdale. WHDH.com is the website for the TV station that publishes news and investigative reporting in the region.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WHDH – Boston News is owned by the
[Sunbeam Television Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sunbeam_Television), which is a privately held broadcasting company based in Miami, Florida, that owns three television stations in the United States. Sunbeam was founded in 1962 by Billionaires Sydney and Edmund Ansin. Revenue is generated through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WHDH – Boston News publishes news focusing on the Boston area with a heavy emphasis on crime reporting. Headlines often contain minimally loaded emotional language such as this:
[Newton woman arraigned on murder and theft charges in connection with former landlord’s death.](https://whdh.com/news/newton-woman-arraigned-on-murder-and-theft-charges-in-connection-with-former-landlords-death/) This story is sourced from first-person accounts.
US and world news is republished from the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/). Under the politics tab, reporting comes from the AP, CNN, and local journalists when pertaining to local politics. Local political reporting contains little bias, such as this: [New England governors want ‘at least’ $500 million in emergency heating aid](https://whdh.com/news/new-england-governors-want-at-least-500-million-in-emergency-heating-aid/).
The website does not publish op-eds. In general, local news reporting is straightforward and factual, while syndicated news is derived from left-leaning CNN.
