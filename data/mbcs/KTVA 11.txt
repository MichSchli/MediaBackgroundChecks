History
[Founded in 1953](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KTVA), KTVA is a [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Anchorage, Alaska, United States. KTVA.com is the associated website that publishes news and programming information for the Greater Anchorage area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTVA 11 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KTVA.com publishes local news through reporters with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Alaska governor extends quarantine for out-of-state travelers](https://www.ktva.com/story/42137010/watch-alaska-governor-extends-quarantine-for-outofstate-travelers). Most local news stories also contain video footage from the TV station. KTVA minimally covers national and international news.
Editorially, they do not publish opinion pieces; therefore, factual reporting has minimal bias.
