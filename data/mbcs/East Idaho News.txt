History
[Founded in 2015](https://www.eastidahonews.com/about-us/), East Idaho News is an independent news website based in Idaho Falls, Idaho. The website covers news, weather, sports, politics, business, and entertainment. The managing editor is Nate Sunderland. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
EastIdahoNews.com, LLC owns East Idaho News. The website generates revenue through advertising and sponsored content.
