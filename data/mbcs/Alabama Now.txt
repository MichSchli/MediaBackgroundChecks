History
Alabama Now is a website that publishes news from all of Alabama’s Boone-owned community newspapers. alabamanow.com covers local, national, crime, and culture news for Alabama.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Alabama Now is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Alabama Now primarily covers local news, sports, and culture through the network of Alabama’s Boone newspapers. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Two charged with abuse of corpse after missing woman’s dead body found inside car.](https://www.alabamanow.com/2022/08/23/two-charged-with-abuse-of-corpse-after-missing-womans-dead-body-found-inside-car/) The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, Alabama Now does not produce opinion content and only publishes straight news from the following Alabama based Boone owned community newspapers:
