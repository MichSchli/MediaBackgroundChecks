History
Founded in 1948, WSB – Atlanta News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Atlanta, Georgia, United States. The associated website wsbtv.com covers news, sports, crime, and weather. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Cox Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cox_Enterprises) owns WSB – Atlanta News through their subsidiary Cox Media Group. They are a company that owns numerous newspapers, television stations, and radio stations, such as the [Atlanta Journal-Constitution](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/atlanta-journal-constitution/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WSB – Atlanta News covers news, sports, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Atlanta, Georgia area. Local news is covered with minimally loaded language, such as this
[Leaders asking metro Atlanta hospital to not shut down emergency room](https://www.wsbtv.com/news/local/south-fulton-county/leaders-asking-metro-atlanta-hospital-not-shut-down-emergency-room/KWYWJFAR3JEXJPJ64DLNLLV6IM/). Many local news stories contain videos from the TV station. The national news is primarily republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WSB – Atlanta News does not publish op-eds; however, when covering politics, they present info with minimal bias, such as this
[Trial to decide of some of state’s elections laws are unconstitutional gets underway](https://www.wsbtv.com/news/local/atlanta/trial-decide-some-states-elections-laws-are-unconstitutional-gets-underway/MXZS5ACEHZCGBNJKTNP5BDVZBE/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
