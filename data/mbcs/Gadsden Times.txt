History
[Founded in 1866](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Gadsden_Times), the Gadsden Times serves Gadsden, Alabama, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. Gadsdentimes.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns the Gadsden Times. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
