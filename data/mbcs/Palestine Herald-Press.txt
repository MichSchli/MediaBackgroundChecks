History
The Palestine Herald-Press is a newspaper published in Palestine, Texas. palestineherald.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Palestine Herald-Press. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Palestine Herald-Press covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Palestine, Texas region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Palestine receives a win against Union Pacific](https://www.palestineherald.com/news/palestine-receives-a-win-against-union-pacific/article_b5f25894-f535-11ed-9cc0-8f4c598701b5.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
