History
Founded in 2010, TooFab is a website that reports on pop culture covering movies, fashion, TV shows, and celebrity news. The website lacks transparency as they do not offer an about page or disclose ownership. However, at the bottom of the page, they state they are in a partnership with
[TMZ](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/tmz/). [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
TooFab does not openly disclose ownership; however, it indicates they are copyrighted to EHM Productions, Inc., which Warner Brothers Entertainment owns at the bottom of the pages. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, TooFab primarily reports on entertainment news. Articles and headlines typically utilize sensationalized headlines such as this:
[21-Year-old Instagrammer Boasts About Not Social Distancing, Catches Coronavirus](https://toofab.com/2020/03/27/21-year-old-instagrammer-boasts-about-not-social-distancing-catches-coronavirus/). This story is only sourced to Instagram. Another example of sensational content is this [Viral Toilet Licker Threatens to Cough on Dr. Phil, Accuses Him of Being Illuminati](https://toofab.com/2020/03/26/viral-toilet-licker-threatens-to-cough-on-dr-phil-accuses-him-of-being-illuminati/). This story, however, is adequately sourced to the [CDC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/centers-disease-control-prevention-cdc/).
