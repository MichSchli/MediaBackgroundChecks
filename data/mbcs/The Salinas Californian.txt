History
Founded in 1871, The Salinas Californian serves Salinas, California, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. Thecalifornian.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Salinas Californian. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Salinas Californian covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Salinas County, California area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[California court unanimously rejects early parole for violent felons: Not what voters intended](https://www.thecalifornian.com/story/news/local/california/2022/01/03/california-supreme-court-unanimously-rejects-early-parole-releases-for-violent-felons-crime-prop-57/9083475002/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Salinas Californian does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented such as this that favors the left:
[Lies about COVID vaccines are killing people in less educated parts of California](https://www.thecalifornian.com/story/opinion/2021/12/13/its-high-time-squelch-covid-19-anti-vax-lies/6496023001/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
