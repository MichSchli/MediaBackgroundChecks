History
Founded in 1971 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station, WGCL – Atlanta News serves Atlanta, Georgia, and the surrounding area. CBS46.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WGCL – Atlanta News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WGCL – Atlanta News provides news coverage for Atlanta, Georgia, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Atlanta City Council to decide on the fate of the detention center](https://www.cbs46.com/2022/08/14/atlanta-city-council-consider-future-detention-center/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WGCL – Atlanta News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
