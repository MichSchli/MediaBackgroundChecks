History
The Danville Commercial-News is a newspaper published in the city of Danville, Illinois. commercial-news.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Danville Commercial-News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Danville Commercial-News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Danville, Illinois, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Texas begins busing migrants to Chicago](https://www.commercial-news.com/news/texas-begins-busing-migrants-to-chicago/article_882748be-2b02-11ed-bdb0-57686f826117.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
