History
The Ashtabula Star Beacon is a newspaper published in Ashtabula, Ohio. starbeacon.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Ashtabula Star Beacon. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Ashtabula Star Beacon covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Ashtabula, Ohio, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Ashtabula County courts updating its case management system](https://www.starbeacon.com/news/ashtabula-county-courts-updating-its-case-management-system/article_ab47479e-bff4-11ee-85cb-eb10d803e43b.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
