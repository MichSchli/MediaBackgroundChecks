History
Smithers Interior News is a newspaper published in Smithers, British Columbia, Canada. The website interior-news.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Smithers, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Smithers Interior News. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Smithers Interior News covers news for Smithers and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[One maintenance worker dead, another seriously injured in helicopter incident at Smithers airport](https://www.interior-news.com/news/one-maintenance-worker-dead-another-seriously-injured-in-helicopter-incident-at-smithers-airport/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Smithers Interior News does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
