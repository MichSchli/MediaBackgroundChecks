History
Founded in 2001, WZMQ – Marquette News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Marquette, Michigan. wzmq19.com.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Marquette region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lilly Broadcasting, based in Pennsylvania, owns WZMQ. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WZMQ – Marquette News publishes news for the Erie area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[MICHIGAN STRATEGIC FUND APPROVES REZONING FOR ESCANABA PAPER MILL](https://wzmq19.com/news/304545/michigan-strategic-fund-approves-rezoning-for-escanaba-paper-mill/). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WZMQ – Marquette News does not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
