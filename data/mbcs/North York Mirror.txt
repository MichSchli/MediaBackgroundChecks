History
The North York Mirror is a newspaper published in North York, Ontario, Canada. therecord.com/local-toronto-north-york provides online local, national, and world news for the greater North York, Ontario region.
Read our profile on the
[Canadian government’s influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of the North York Mirror is Metroland Media Group Ltd., a subsidiary of Nordstar Capital LP. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In 2015 and
[2021](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2021/09/19/the-stars-editorial-board-endorses-liberals-for-2021-federal-election.html), the [Toronto Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/) (Nordstar), which owns the North York Mirror publisher, Metroland Media Group, [endorsed](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2015/10/09/for-justin-trudeau-and-the-liberal-party-editorial.html) the Liberal Party under Justin Trudeau.
In review, the North York Mirror uses low-biased wording when reporting local news such as this:
[New Baycrest dementia care pilot program in North York seeks to ‘restore functions’](https://www.therecord.com/local-toronto-north-york/news/2023/01/12/new-baycrest-dementia-care-pilot-program-in-north-york-seeks-to-restore-functions.html). National news is derived through the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/), while world news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, a review of op-eds slightly favors the left. In general, they are fact-based and hold a left-leaning editorial bias.
