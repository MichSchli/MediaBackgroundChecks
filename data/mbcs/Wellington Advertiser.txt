History
The Wellington Advertiser is a newspaper focusing on local news and events in Wellington County, Canada. The website includes various content categories such as local news, events, and more.
[Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The paper and website are independently owned and published by
[Dave Adsett](https://www.wellingtonadvertiser.com/author/dave-adsett/). Revenue is generated through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
The Wellington Advertiser covers local community news with minimal bias, such as this:
[Facing $3-million deficit, Community Living intends to close day programs, lay off staff](https://www.wellingtonadvertiser.com/facing-3-million-deficit-community-living-intends-to-close-day-programs-lay-off-staff/). This story relies on quotes rather than hyperlinked sourcing. The paper does not cover national or international news.
Editorially, the Wellington Advertiser covers both left and right-leaning perspectives, indicating a balance resulting in a least biased rating. However, the lack of hyperlinked sourcing indicates a mostly factual rating.
