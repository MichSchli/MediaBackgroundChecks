History
Founded in 1980, WQOW – Eau Claire News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Eau Claire, Wisconsin, United States. wqow.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Eau Claire area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WQOW – Eau Claire News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WQOW – Eau Claire News publishes news for Eau Claire and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Firefighters battle early-morning barn blaze in Cornell](https://www.wqow.com/news/firefighters-battle-early-morning-barn-blaze-in-cornell/article_1caed682-1708-11ed-9b18-abb7cc77ca12.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
