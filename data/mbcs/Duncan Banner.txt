History
The Duncan Banner is a newspaper published in Duncan, Oklahoma. duncanbanner.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Duncan Banner. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Duncan Banner covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Duncan, OK, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Stephens County man faces felony charges for embezzling over $1 million from local business](https://www.duncanbanner.com/news/stephens-county-man-faces-felony-charges-for-embezzling-over-1-million-from-local-business/article_c634e4f0-46b7-11ee-8c31-fb625431e262.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
