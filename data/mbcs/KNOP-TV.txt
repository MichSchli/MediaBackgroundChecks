History
Founded in 1958 and an NBC-affiliated television station, KNOP-TV serves North Platte, Nebraska, and the surrounding area. Knopnews2.com is the associated website for the news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KNOP-TV is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KNOP-TV provides news coverage for North Platte, Nebraska and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[234 pounds of marijuana found in two traffic stops on I-80.](https://www.knopnews2.com/2021/04/21/234-pounds-of-marijuana-found-in-two-traffic-stops-on-i-80/) The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KNOP-TV does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
