History
KYUK – NPR – Yukon is an NPR AM radio station (640) based in Bethel, Alaska, serving the Yukon and broadcasting in English and Yupik languages.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KYUK – NPR – Yukon is owned and broadcast by Bethel Broadcasting. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
