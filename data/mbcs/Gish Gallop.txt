History
Founded in 2018, Gish Gallop is a satire website.
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not disclose ownership and is funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
Gish Gallop clearly states on their
[about page](https://www.gishgallop.com/terms-and-conditions/) that they are a satire website: “Please do not sue us. Here’s why. The First Amendment protects parody and satire as a form of free speech and expression. The Gish Gallop uses invented names in all of its stories, except in cases where public figures are being satirized.”
Articles consist of political satire such as this:
[Trump To Shoot Somebody On Fifth Avenue To Celebrate MLK Day](https://www.gishgallop.com/trump-to-shoot-somebody-on-fifth-avenue-to-celebrate-mlk-day/) to the silly such as this: [Scientists Create World’s First GMO Reverse Dalmatian](https://www.gishgallop.com/scientists-create-worlds-first-gmo-reverse-dalmatian/).
