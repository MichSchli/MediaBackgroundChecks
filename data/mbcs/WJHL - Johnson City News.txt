History
Founded in 1953, WJHL- Johnson City News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Johnson City, Tennessee. The associated website is wjhl.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WJHL- Johnson City News is owned by
[Nexstar Media Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nexstar_Media_Group), which owns around 200 TV stations nationwide. The founder, chairman, president, and CEO of Nexstar is Perry A. Sook. [According](https://www.opensecrets.org/pacs/pacgot.php?cmte=C00567388&cycle=2018) to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Nexstar Media group gave more money to Republicans in the 2018 election cycle (45% to Democrats, 55% to Republicans). However, in 2020 they gave more to Democrats (55% Democrats, 45% to Republicans). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The WJHL- Johnson City News website publishes news for the greater Johnson City, Tennessee area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias such as this:
[Jonesville, Va. woman charged with first-degree murder](https://www.wjhl.com/news/crime/jonesville-va-woman-charged-with-first-degree-murder/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through Nexstar Media Wire, the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and other large credible media outlets.
