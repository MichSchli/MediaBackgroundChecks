History
Founded in 1954, KNTV – NBC Bay Area (San Francisco) is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to San Jose, California, United States. NBCbayarea.com is the associated website covering news, weather, sports, crime, and lifestyle. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KNTV – NBC Bay Area is owned by NBCUniversal a subsidiary of Comcast, which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KNTV – NBC Bay Area covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Heat Advisory Issued as Temperatures Soar Across Bay Area](https://www.nbcbayarea.com/weather/high-temps-return-to-bay-area-heat-advisory-issued-for-tuesday/2924513/). Most stories are accompanied by videos from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other NBC-owned stations provide national and world news. All information is sourced from first-hand accounts and other credible media sources.
Editorially, KNTV – NBC Bay Area typically does not produce opinion content; however, they report on national politics with a slight left-leaning bias in wording and story selection
[Biden Focuses on Workers as High Inflation Remains a Risk](https://www.nbcbayarea.com/news/national-international/biden-focuses-on-workers-as-high-inflation-remains-a-risk/2919352/). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering political issues.
