Reasoning: Lack of Transparency, Poor Sourcing
Country: Unknown
History
[Founded in 2016,](https://whois.domaintools.com/pmnightlynews.com) PM Nightly News is a conservative news website that promotes conspiracies and also has questionable science news. [According](https://pmnightlynews.com/#) to their about description “PM Nightly News is committed to the core principles of journalism, open debate, civil discourse, and fair and researched coverage of the news. We are opposed to intolerance, excessive government control, and attempts to monopolize opinion or suppress freedom of thought.”
The website lacks transparency as they do not have an actual about page or disclose ownership.
Funded by / Ownership
PM Nighty News does not disclose ownership and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, PM Nightly News does not report original news, but rather summarizes existing news stories with conservative opinions. Articles and headlines utilize loaded emotional wording that favors the right such as this
[Looters in Chicago attacked Ronald McDonald House with sick children inside, charity says.](https://pmnightlynews.com/looters-in-chicago-attacked-ronald-mcdonald-house-with-sick-children-inside-charity-says/) This story is properly sourced. Other stories are sourced to only Twitter and Instagram with a strong right-leaning bias. Regardless, this not a credible news source.
