History
Next Big Future is a science and technology website run by Brian Wang, a self-described futurist, and lecturer. According to their
[about page](https://www.nextbigfuture.com/about), the website “covers many disruptive technology and trends including Space, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Medicine, Anti-aging Biotechnology, and Nanotechnology.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Brian Wang Owns Next Big Future. Donations and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Next Big Future covers technology and science from a predictive futuristic perspective. While futurism is a questionable science as it relies on hypothetical predictions, Next Big Future is highly science-based. All information published is researched and cited in credible journals and studies. Although some of the projections for the future are questionable, the basis is related to current science and technology.
Editorially, there is no evidence of significant political bias. In general, they are a pro-science source.
