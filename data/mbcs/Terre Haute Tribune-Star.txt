History
The Terre Haute Tribune-Star is a newspaper published in the city of Terre Haute, Indiana. tribstar.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Terre Haute Tribune-Star. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Terre Haute Tribune-Star covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Terre Haute, Indiana, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Two well-known officers vie for Vigo sheriff](https://www.tribstar.com/news/local_news/two-well-known-officers-vie-for-vigo-sheriff/article_e69bdfe4-4bec-11ed-98b2-035ef701a520.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
