History
The Garden Island is a daily newspaper published in Kauai, Hawaii. The website thegardenisland.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Kauai area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns The Garden Island and is operated by Oahu Publications Inc. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Garden Island covers news for Kauai and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording such as this
[High Surf Warning issued for all south-facing shores](https://www.thegardenisland.com/2022/07/15/hawaii-news/high-surf-warning-issued-for-all-south-facing-shores/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, The Garden Island does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left, such as this
[Mental health is important, especially in time of pandemic](https://www.thegardenisland.com/2021/04/30/opinion/mental-health-is-important-especially-in-time-of-pandemic/). Generally, news reporting is factual with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
