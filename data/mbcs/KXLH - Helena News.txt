History
Founded in 1958, KXLH – Helena News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Helena, Montana, United States. kxlh.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KXLH – Helena News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KXLH – Helena News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Helena, Montana, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Carroll College continues to see enrolment growth since start of pandemic](https://www.kxlh.com/news/carroll-college-continues-to-see-enrolment-growth-since-start-of-pandemic). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[MT lawmakers split on special session to deal with budget surplus](https://www.kxlh.com/news/montana-politics/mt-lawmakers-split-on-special-session-to-deal-with-budget-surplus). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
