History
The Rockwall County Herald-Banner is a newspaper published in Greenville, Texas. rockwallheraldbanner.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Rockwall County Herald-Banner. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Rockwall County Herald-Banner covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Rockwall County, Texas region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
