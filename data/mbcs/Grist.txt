History
[Grist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grist_(magazine)) (originally Grist Magazine; also referred to as Grist.org) is an American non-profit online magazine that has been publishing environmental news and commentary since 1999. Grist is a [Pro-Science](http://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pro-science/) magazine that focuses on environmental issues. According to their [about page](https://grist.org/about/), “Grist is a nonprofit, independent media organization dedicated to telling stories of climate solutions and a just future.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Grist Magazine Inc owns the website. Advertising, grants, and donations fund Grist.
Analysis / Bias
Grist publishes original articles and republishes from other sources primarily covering the environment and climate change. Original articles and headlines often contain loaded emotional wording that emphasizes the consequences of climate change
[As Arctic broils, world leaders convene in Iceland to talk climate change](https://grist.org/politics/arctic-council-iceland-heat-wave-climate-change/). This article, like most, is properly sourced to credible media outlets such as [VOA News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/voice-of-america/), [CBS News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/), and the [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/).
Editorially, Grist holds left-leaning positions on the environment and equity, frequently reporting negatively on conservatives such as this
[After a Trump-length pause, the EPA is relaunching a major climate change report](https://grist.org/politics/epa-climate-change-report-trump-biden/). When reporting on Democrats, they tend to hold a favorable view [Biden wants to balance infrastructure and conservation goals. But it won’t be easy.](https://grist.org/energy/biden-wants-to-balance-infrastructure-and-conservation-goals-but-it-wont-be-easy/) In general, they report factual news and support the consensus of science while holding left-leaning viewpoints.
