History
Founded in 1959, WABG – DeltaNews is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Greenwood, Mississippi, United States. The associated website deltanews.tv.com covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the Mississippi Delta region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Cox Enterprises](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cox_Enterprises) owns WABG – DeltaNews through their subsidiary Cox Media Group. They are a company that owns numerous newspapers, television stations, and radio stations, such as the [Atlanta Journal-Constitution](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/atlanta-journal-constitution/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WABG – DeltaNews covers news, sports, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Greenwood, Mississippi area. Local news is covered with minimally loaded language, such as this
[New Mississippi laws take effect July 1](https://www.deltanews.tv/news/new-mississippi-laws-take-effect-july-1/article_a5ec9f5a-f260-11ec-97f1-6b71e69b9d1c.html). Many local news stories contain videos from the TV station. The national news is primarily republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WABG – DeltaNews does not publish op-eds; however, when covering politics, they present info with minimal bias, such as this
[Leflore County $1 Million Short on Budget](https://www.deltanews.tv/news/leflore-county-1-million-short-on-budget/article_4eb89bbe-d0bc-11ec-839c-6f545f467cd3.html). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
