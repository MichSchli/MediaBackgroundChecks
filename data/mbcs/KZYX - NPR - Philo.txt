History

Funded by / Ownership
KZYX – NPR – Philo is owned and broadcast by Mendocino County Public Broadcasting. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
