History
The Hendersonville Times-News is a newspaper that serves Hendersonville, North Carolina, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. thetimesnews.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Hendersonville Times-News. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Hendersonville Times-News covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Hendersonville, North Carolina area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Dozens of students banned from graduation](https://www.thetimesnews.com/news/crime/dozens-of-students-banned-from-graduation/article_0653fd9f-4f95-55f7-8315-cef22fb1e755.html). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Hendersonville Times-News does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Uptick in juvenile crime is reversible, but NC must act fast](https://www.thetimesnews.com/opinion/columns/uptick-in-juvenile-crime-is-reversible-but-nc-must-act-fast/article_5df52757-b23a-5b3f-afdc-c8289b782e9f.html). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
