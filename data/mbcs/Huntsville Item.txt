History
The Huntsville Item is a newspaper published in Huntsville, Texas. itemonline.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Huntsville Item. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Huntsville Item covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Huntsville, Texas, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[DPS Reminds Texans About Dangers of Fentanyl During Fentanyl Poisoning Awareness Month](https://www.itemonline.com/news/dps-reminds-texans-about-dangers-of-fentanyl-during-fentanyl-poisoning-awareness-month/article_03e787d8-6848-11ee-9e10-ff5200eaddc8.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
