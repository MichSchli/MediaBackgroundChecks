History
The LaGrange Daily News is a newspaper published in LaGrange, Georgia. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.

Funded by / Ownership
The LaGrange Daily News is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the LaGrange Daily News primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Commissioners approve hiring grants manager, two firefighters](https://www.lagrangenews.com/2022/12/09/commissioners-approve-hiring-grants-manager-two-firefighters/). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the LaGrange Daily News labels its opinion section appropriately, with more op-eds favoring the right. In general, LaGrange Daily News is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
