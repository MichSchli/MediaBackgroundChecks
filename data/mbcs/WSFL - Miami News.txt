History
Founded in 1982, WSFL – Miami News is a CW-affiliated station licensed to Miami, Florida, United States. wsfltv.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WSFL – Miami News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WSFL – Miami News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Miami, Florida area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Florida lawmakers respond to surge of migrant landings in Keys, but offer no long-term fix](https://www.wsfltv.com/news/investigations/florida-lawmakers-respond-to-surge-of-migrant-landings-in-keys-but-offer-no-long-term-fix). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Final jobs report released before midterms shows slight uptick in unemployment](https://www.wsfltv.com/news/national/final-jobs-report-released-before-midterms-show-slight-uptick-in-unemployment). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
