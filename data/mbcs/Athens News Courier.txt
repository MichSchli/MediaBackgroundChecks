History
Founded in 1892, the Athens News Courier is a daily newspaper published in the city of Athens, Alabama, and covers Limestone County. enewscourier.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns the Athens News Courier. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Athens News Courier covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Athens, Alabama, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Athens Renaissance students contribute back to the community](https://www.enewscourier.com/news/athens-renaissance-students-contribute-back-to-the-community/article_5c002556-e694-11ec-8283-5f9bfc7b7bea.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left, such as this
[Our view: Removing the stigma means first recognizing it is there](https://www.enewscourier.com/opinion/editorials/our-view-removing-the-stigma-means-first-recognizing-it-is-there/article_f6150a10-cafd-11ec-9dd4-3f764e679520.html). The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
