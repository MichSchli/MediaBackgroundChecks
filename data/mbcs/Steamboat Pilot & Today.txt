History
Steamboat Pilot & Today is a daily newspaper serving Routt County, Colorado. The website covers local news, sports, crime, and community information.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Steamboat Pilot & Today is published by Swift Communications, which publishes numerous small-market newspapers around the western USA. Ogden Newspapers owns Swift. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Steamboat Pilot & Today publishes news for Routt County, Colorado, and the surrounding area. Local news is covered through reporting with minimal bias in wording, such as this

[Our View: We must manage Steamboat as a destination city](https://www.steamboatpilot.com/opinion/our-view/our-view-we-must-manage-steamboat-as-a-destination-city/). In general, they exhibit low bias and produce fact-based news.
