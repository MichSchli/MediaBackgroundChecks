History
[Founded in 2021](https://whois.domaintools.com/thecountersignal.com) by Keean Bexte, The Counter Signal is a conservative news and opinion website based in Alberta, Canada. The website lacks transparency as they do not offer an about page, describe ownership or disclose their conservative perspective. [Keean Bexte](https://ca.linkedin.com/in/keean-bexte-74575492) is a former video journalist for the far-right and questionable [Rebel News Network](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-rebel/). Further, Keean Bexte was identified as one of the [operators](https://ricochet.media/en/2422/rebel-media-reporter-worked-for-white-supremacist-web-store) of Fireforce Ventures, an online store that sells white supremacist merchandise. [Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Counter Signal lacks transparency as they do not indicate that Keean Bexte is the owner. Membership fees and donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Counter Signal website consists of links to news stories, The Show, which lists one episode from 2020, Petitions, and a newsletter. Under the news category, original reporting is conducted by Keean Bexte that demonstrates far-right bias such as this
[WATCH: Non-white convoy supporters tell Trudeau to go f*ck himself](https://thecountersignal.com/non-white-convoy-supporters-tell-trudeau-to-go-f-himself/) and this [BREAKING: Justin Trudeau goes into hiding following negative COVID test](https://thecountersignal.com/justin-trudeau-goes-into-hiding-following-negative-covid-test/). This latter story is poorly sourced from Tweets.
From an editorial perspective, they align with the far-right as most content on the website is emotional and opinionated, favoring a conservative view. At the time of this review, The Counter Signal was strongly in favor of the Canadian Trucker Convoy protesting mask and vaccine mandates
[Trudeau Minister calls peaceful Convoy protesters rapists](https://thecountersignal.com/trudeau-minister-calls-convoy-protesters-rap/). Further, they also promote conspiracy theories and false information such as this [“SHOCK POLICY: Ottawa may euthanize truckers’ pets as punishment.”](https://thecountersignal.com/news/ottawa-police-may-euthanize-truckers-pets-as-punishment) The Counter Signal holds a far-right editorial bias and promotes misinformation.
