History
Lake Country Calendar is a newspaper published in Lake Country, British Columbia, Canada. The website lakecountrycalendar.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Lake Country, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Lake Country Calendar. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Lake Country Calendar covers news for Lake Country and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[North Shuswap on edge after alleged incident on Highway 1 amid wildfire](https://www.lakecountrycalendar.com/news/north-shuswap-on-edge-after-alleged-incident-on-highway-1-amid-wildfire/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Lake Country Calendar does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
