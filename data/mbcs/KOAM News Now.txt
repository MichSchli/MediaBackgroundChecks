History
Founded in 1953, KOAM-TV is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Pittsburg, Kansas, United States, and also serving Joplin, Missouri. KoamNewsNow.com is the associated news website for the TV Station. The website also serves as the news website for Fox/CW affiliate [KFJX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KFJX). The website publishes news, sports, weather, traffic, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KoamNewsNow.com is owned by
[Morgan Murphy Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Morgan_Murphy_Media), which owns 14 TV stations and several radio stations throughout the Midwest. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KoamNewsNow.com reports news, sports, weather, and crime for the Pittsburgh, Kansas region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Crawford County sees higher rate of COVID-19 hospitalizations](https://www.koamnewsnow.com/crawford-county-sees-higher-rate-of-covid-19-hospitalizations/). The Associated Press generally provides national and international news.
Editorially, KoamNewsNow.com does not publish opinion pieces; however, they report on politics primarily through the Associated Press. The news is generally reported factually and with minimal bias due to a lack of editorializing.
