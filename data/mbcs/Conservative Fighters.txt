History
Conservative Fighters is an anonymously published conservative news website. The website lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership or provide an about page. The website was previously published on the conservativefighters.com domain.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Conservative Fighters lack transparency as they do not disclose ownership. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Conservative Fighters does not perform original reporting, instead summarizes news stories from other sources. The website features general news, a separate category for Trump Statements, and Bible verses. Articles had headlines sometimes contain loaded language, but not all the time like this
[Federal prosecutors are reportedly weighing charging Democrat President Joe Biden’s son, Hunter Biden, with multiple crimes ranging from various aspects of his business dealings to his…](https://conservativefighters.co/news/critical-stage-feds-consider-charging-hunter-biden-with-multiple-crimes-including-gun-related-crime/) This story is sourced from a CNN video.
Most stories on the website do not provide hyperlinked sourcing. Further, more stories favor a conservative perspective. For example, they favor former President Trump through publishing his statements, including those promoting baseless election fraud claims such as this
[The Cheating and Irregularities were unbelievable](https://conservativefighters.co/trump-statements/the-cheating-and-irregularities-were-unbelievable/).
Under the conservativefighters.com domain, Conservative Fighters was a highly questionable source, frequently promoting misinformation. However, they publish mostly credible news now under their new domain with a moderate conservative bias. Therefore, with this update, they are moved back to Right biased and Mixed for factual reporting.
