History
Antibiotics Journal is published by
[MDPI (Multidisciplinary Digital Publishing Institute)](https://www.mdpi.com/about/history), formerly Molecular Diversity Preservation International, an open-access journal publisher based in Basel, Switzerland. Antibiotics is a [peer-reviewed](https://www.mdpi.com/editorial_process), open-access journal on all aspects of antibiotics, published monthly online by MDPI. MDPI publishes over [400 journals.](https://www.mdpi.com/about/journals) [Read our profile on Swiss Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/switzerland-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of Antibiotics Journal is
[MDPI AG](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mdpi-multidisciplinary-digital-publishing-institute-bias/), which Shu-Kun Lin owns. Here is the list of [the Management Team.](https://www.mdpi.com/about/team) According to [“MDPI Open Access Information and Policy.”](https://www.mdpi.com/openaccess) MDPI articles and all its journals are open access where authors pay a one-time Article Processing Charge (APC) to cover the costs of peer review administration and management; here is [“General Information on MDPI’s Article Processing Charges (APC).”](https://www.mdpi.com/apc)
Analysis / Bias
Antibiotics Journal covers the science of Antibiotics. Studies are published via open access, such as this
[Seminal Bacterioflora of Two Rooster Lines: Characterization, Antibiotic Resistance Patterns and Possible Impact on Semen Quality](https://www.mdpi.com/2079-6382/12/2/336). Like all others reviewed, this study is Pro-Science, well-researched, and low-biased.
While Antibiotics Journal has not failed a fact-check, its publisher has faced criticism as an open-access predatory journal. Read our full review on
[MDPI](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mdpi-multidisciplinary-digital-publishing-institute-bias/).
