History
Founded in 2008, PoliticusUSA is a political commentary site that provides news, analysis, and opinions from a progressive liberal perspective.
[Jason Easley](https://www.politicususa.com/about-us) is the founder and serves as the Managing Editor and Senior White House & Congressional correspondent.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
According to its
[About Page](https://www.politicususa.com/about-us), the founder of PoliticusUSA is [Jason Easley](https://www.politicususa.com/vocabulary/jason-easley), and they state that “not a drop of investment capital was used in the start-up. We do not seek grants or funding from foundations. We do not accept advertising dollars from any outlet that would have even the opportunity to dictate our coverage.” The website is funded through online 3rd party advertising.
Analysis / Bias
PoliticusUSA relies on news and reports from other sources while offering summaries and commentary from a progressive/liberal perspective. Articles and headlines contain moderately loaded wording, such as this
[JD Vance Tried To Save His Failing Senate Campaign On Fox News And It Did Not Go Well](https://www.politicususa.com/2022/08/28/jd-vance-fox-news.html). PoliticusUSA typically is fact-based and very well sourced from credible media outlets such as [New York Times](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-times/), [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/) [,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/), and [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/). They also republish news from Reuters through a partnership.
Editorially, PoliticusUSA states they hold a liberal perspective on their
[about page](https://www.politicususa.com/about-us) “PoliticusUSA offers news, commentary, and opinion from a liberal point of view.” Most news stories contain opinionated commentary that favors the left. They also appropriately label opinion content such as this [Opinion: The Dangerous Distortion of “Fairness” in the Student Loan Forgiveness Debate](https://www.politicususa.com/2022/08/29/opinion-the-dangerous-distortion-of-fairness-in-the-student-loan-forgiveness-debate.html). While story selection and commentary routinely favor the left, they also consistently denigrate the right such as this [Trump’s Team Is Terrified at Its ‘Comically Inept and Inexperienced’ Legal Representation](https://www.politicususa.com/2022/08/28/trumps-team-is-terrified-at-its-comically-inept-and-inexperienced-legal-representation.html) and [Biden Mocks Trump’s Claim That He Declassified Every Single Document](https://www.politicususa.com/2022/08/26/biden-trump-classified-docs.html). PoliticusUSA is generally fact-based as they issue [corrections](https://www.politicususa.com/corrections) and editorially hold a strong left-leaning bias.
