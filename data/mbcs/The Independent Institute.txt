History
[Founded in 1986](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Independent_Institute), The Independent Institute is a non-profit, non-partisan, scholarly research and educational organization that sponsors in-depth studies of critical social and economic issues. The founder and current CEO is David J. Theroux. The content of the website follows libertarian positions such as free-market capitalism, limited government, and climate change denial.
According to their
[about page](https://www.independent.org/aboutus/), “THE INDEPENDENT INSTITUTE is a non-profit, non-partisan, public-policy research and educational organization that shapes ideas into profound and lasting impact through publications, conferences, and effective multi-media programs. Our mission is to boldly advance peaceful, prosperous, and free societies grounded in a commitment to human worth and dignity.” [Read our country media profile of the USA.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Independent Institute is a non-profit that is funded through donations.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Independent Institute reports news from a right-leaning libertarian perspective such as this
[The Fed Becomes the U.S. Government’s Biggest Creditor](https://blog.independent.org/2020/05/04/the-fed-becomes-the-u-s-governments-biggest-creditor/). This story is properly sourced to federalreserve.gov, the [Independent](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-independent/), and [US News and World Report](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/us-news-world-report/). Story selection tends to favor the right with more stories favoring free-market capitalism such as this [Market Based Environmentalism vs. the Free Market](https://www.independent.org/news/article.asp?id=198).
When it comes to science, they do not actually deny human-influenced
[climate change](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/). Still, they do minimize its impact as opposed to the consensus of science: [Is the Man-Made Climate Change Debate Really Over](https://www.independent.org/news/article.asp?id=12975)?
