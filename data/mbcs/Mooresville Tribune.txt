History
The Mooresville Tribune is a daily print newspaper published in the town of Mooresville, North Carolina, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Mooresville and the surrounding area. Mooresvilletribune.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.

Funded by / Ownership
Lee Enterprises owns and publishes the Mooresville Tribune. According to
[Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Lee Enterprises donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) from 1990 until 2016. However, during the 2020 election cycle, they donated 70% to Democratic candidates and causes. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
