History
Founded in 1989 and an ABC-affiliated television station, WOHL – Lima News serves Lima, Ohio, and the surrounding area. hometownstations.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
Funded by / Ownership
WOHL – Lima News is owned by
[Block Communications](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Block_Communications), which also owns Pittsburgh Press and the [Pittsburgh Post-Gazette](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pittsburgh-post-gazette/). The TV Station and website are funded through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WOHL – Lima News provides news coverage for Lima, Ohio, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Ohio Brownfield Remediation Program funding coming to a few projects in our area](https://www.hometownstations.com/news/ohio-brownfield-remediation-program-funding-coming-to-a-few-projects-in-our-area/article_fb6d654a-8171-11ed-951c-8f69c04ef74b.html). The national and international news is provided through other Block Communications affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WOHL – Lima News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
