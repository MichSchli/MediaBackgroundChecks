History
Defense One (DO) is an online news site that primarily reports matters relating to national defense and security. According to their
[about page](https://www.defenseone.com/about/?oref=footer), “Defense One delivers news, breaking analysis, and ideas on the topics and trends that are defining the future of U.S. defense and national security. We give national security professionals, stakeholders, and citizens what they need to know, from senior leaders in Washington to commanders abroad and next-generation thinkers far from the political scrum.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Defense One is a property of
[Atlantic Media](https://www.defenseone.com/about/?oref=footer), which owns the left-leaning Atlantic magazine and website. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The articles are well-written, well-sourced, and highly factual. National defense and security are always complex and controversial subjects. The solid analysis and insight that DO provides can be a key path to navigating and understanding them. While some of the articles exhibit a slight left or right bias, this can be attributed primarily to the subject matter instead of the author’s bias. That aside, the overall content and editorial intent of DO earn them a Least Biased rating.
