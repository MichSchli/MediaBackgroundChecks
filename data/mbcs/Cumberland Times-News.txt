History
The Cumberland Times-News is a newspaper published in Cumberland, Maryland. times-news.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Cumberland Times-News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Cumberland Times-News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Cumberland, Maryland region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Garrett County men jailed on drug distribution charges](https://www.times-news.com/news/local_news/garrett-county-men-jailed-on-drug-distribution-charges/article_9c0cba40-a6e9-11ed-9d56-c3248be58770.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
