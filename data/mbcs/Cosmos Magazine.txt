Funded by / Ownership
The nonprofit
[Royal Institution of Australia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_Institution_of_Australia) owns and publishes Cosmos Magazine and the website. Subscription fees, donations, and a shop that sells gift subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Cosmos Magazine covers all areas of science and sources properly to scientific and academic journals. They do not utilize loaded emotional language such as this
[Did meat-eating really play a big role in human evolution?](https://cosmosmagazine.com/history/palaeontology/meat-eating-human-evolution-important/) In general, Cosmos Magazine is a low-biased, pro-science popular science magazine.
