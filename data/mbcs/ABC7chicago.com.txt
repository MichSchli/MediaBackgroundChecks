History
ABC7Chicago.com is the news website for
[WLS-TV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WLS-TV) in Chicago, Illinois. W S first aired in 1948, and the website was created in 2005. WLS-TV is an ABC owned-and-operated television station. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WLS-TV and ABC7Chicago.com are owned by
[The Walt Disney Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company), which operates seven TV stations across the USA. Revenue is derived through advertising.
