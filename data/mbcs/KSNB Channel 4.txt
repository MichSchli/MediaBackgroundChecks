History
Founded in 1953 and an NBC-affiliated television station, KSNB Channel 4 serves Lincoln, Nebraska, and the surrounding area. Ksnblocal4.com is the associated website for the news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KSNB Channel 4 is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KSNB Channel 4 provides news coverage for Lincoln, Nebraska, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[KPD make arrest following assault inside Target.](https://www.ksnblocal4.com/2021/05/05/kpd-make-arrest-following-assault-inside-target/) The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KSNB Channel 4 does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
