History
Launched in 1953, KAAL – Austin News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Austin, Minnesota, United States, serving the Austin television market. The associated website kaaltv.com publishes news and station information. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KAAL – Austin News is owned by
[Hubbard Broadcasting, Inc.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hubbard_Broadcasting), an American television and radio broadcasting corporation based in St. Paul, Minnesota. Stanley E. Hubbard founded it. Revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KAAL – Austin News covers news, sports, traffic, weather, and crime for the Austin, MN area. Articles and headlines rarely use load language such as this:
[Jailed Rochester woman resentenced to nearly 8 years for previous crimes](https://www.kaaltv.com/rochester-minnesota-news/chrysten-doney-resentencing-probation-violation-drug-sale-jail-sentence-olmsted-county/6548225/). A video accompanies many news stories from the TV station. Staff reporters and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) write national and international news.
Editorially, KAAL – Austin News does not publish opinion pieces; however, they cover politics with minimal bias, such as this:
[FBI seized top secret documents in Trump estate search](https://www.kaaltv.com/politics/the-trump-home-search-push-to-unseal-warrant-used-by-fbi/6548109/?cat=12170). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
