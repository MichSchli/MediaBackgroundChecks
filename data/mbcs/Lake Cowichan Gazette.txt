History
The Lake Cowichan Gazette is a newspaper published in Lake Cowichan, British Columbia, Canada. The website lakecowichangazette.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Lake Cowichan area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Lake Cowichan Gazette. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Lake Cowichan Gazette covers news for Lake Cowichan and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Vancouver Island women push to end court-ordered silence of sexual assault victims](https://www.lakecowichangazette.com/news/vancouver-island-women-push-to-end-the-silence-of-sexual-assault-victims/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, the Lake Cowichan Gazette does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
