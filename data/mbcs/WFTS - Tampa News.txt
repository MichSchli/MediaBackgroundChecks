History
Founded in 1981, WFTS – Tampa News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Tampa Bay, Florida, United States. abcactionnews.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WFTS – Tampa News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WFTS – Tampa News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Tampa, Florida, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[More than two dozen arrested in sweep focused on child predators: Polk County Sheriff](https://www.abcactionnews.com/news/region-polk/more-than-two-dozen-arrested-in-sweep-focused-on-child-predators-polk-county-sheriff). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Judge dismisses Andrew Warren’s federal lawsuit against DeSantis after suspension](https://www.abcactionnews.com/news/region-hillsborough/judge-dismisses-andrew-warrens-federal-lawsuit-against-desantis-after-suspension). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
