History
[Founded in 2003](https://whois.domaintools.com/thespoof.com), The Spoof is a UK-based satire website as disclosed on their [about page](https://www.thespoof.com/about/), “Providing an irreverent and satirical slant to the current big news stories throughout the world, bringing you more funny stories than you can shake sticks at.” [Read our profile on UK media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-kingdom-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Spoof Media Ltd. owns Spoof Media. Advertising is the primary source of revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Reviews of satire websites are limited. The purpose is to determine if they appropriately disclose they are satire. The Spoof makes it very clear as every page holds its tagline, “Always there with the funniest spoof headlines.” As further evidence, they have a
[disclaimer](https://www.thespoof.com/about/disclaimer.cfm) that reads, “The Spoof is a fabricated satirical newspaper and comedy website published by Spoof Media Ltd.”
