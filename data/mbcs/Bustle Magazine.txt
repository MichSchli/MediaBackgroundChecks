History
Bustle is an online US women’s magazine founded in August 2013 by
[Bryan Goldberg](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bryan_Goldberg) that covers fashion, entertainment, politics, and news. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Bustle is held by the
[Bustle Digital Group](https://bustle.company/), which is owned by Bryan Goldberg. Their About page says, “Bustle Digital Group is the largest premium publisher reaching millennial women. Every month, nearly 80 million readers turn to Bustle Digital Group publications — Romper, Elite Daily, and The Zoe Report — for impactful conversation around the interests and issues engaging women today.” [According to the Wall Street Journal](https://www.wsj.com/articles/bustle-turns-political-in-latest-12-million-funding-round-1490653795), Bustle has received 50.5 million dollars of funding from a variety of companies. Advertising, sponsored content, and product links generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Bustle publishes original and aggregated content. All articles on Bustle are properly sourced with some stories containing minimally loaded language, such as this
[Trump Opposes The Equality Act To Protect Against LGBTQ Discrimination, A New Report Says](https://www.bustle.com/p/trump-opposes-the-equality-act-to-protect-against-lgbtq-discrimination-a-new-report-says-17890050). As mentioned, this story is also very well sourced to credible media outlets.
In another story, there is the moderate use of emotional language
[This Study On Undocumented Immigrants & Crime Debunks One Of Trump’s Dangerous Claims](https://www.bustle.com/p/this-study-on-undocumented-immigrants-crime-debunks-one-of-trumps-dangerous-claims-17871075). Editorially, Bustle mostly favors the left through story selection and endorsement of political policies such as [abortion rights](https://www.bustle.com/p/womens-representation-in-state-legislatures-that-passed-abortion-bans-is-consistently-low-17893937) and [environmentalism](https://www.bustle.com/p/these-9-un-biodiversity-report-details-show-just-how-much-humans-have-messed-with-nature-17303840).
