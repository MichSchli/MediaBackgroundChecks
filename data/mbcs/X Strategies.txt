History
X Strategies LLC was
[co-founded](https://www.xstrategies.com/) by Alex Bruesewitz and Derek Utley. The firm positions itself as a consulting firm dedicated to advancing conservative ideals and promoting “America First” policies. Both founders have distinct backgrounds, with Bruesewitz entering the political consulting arena at a young age and Utley having a history in business and tech. X Strategies has [locations](https://www.xstrategies.com/contact) in Washington, DC, and Florida. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The specific details regarding the funding and ownership structure of X Strategies LLC are not clear. The founders are
[Alex Bruesewitz](https://www.advocate.com/news/gays-against-groomers-exposed) and [Derek Utley](https://biographiesareus.com/derek-utley/), with Bruesewitz serving as the CEO and Utley as the chairman of the organization.
Analysis / Bias
The website does not publish news; however, X Strategies LLC demonstrates a strong conservative bias, as evident from its mission statement: “Since day one, we have made it our mission to help advance the ideals that make this country great with an agenda and platform that puts our nation first. We only promote, advocate for, and help elect conservative fighters.”
Alex Bruesewitz, the CEO, is a prominent MAGA political consultant and supports Donald Trump. He is active in social media conflicts with figures opposing Trump or Trump-aligned policies. Bruesewitz has been
[involved in controversies](https://flvoicenews.com/florida-maga-consultant-threatens-to-sue-rep-adam-kinzinger-for-defamation-demands-retraction-and-apology/) related to the January 6th Committee and has made false claims, as seen below in failed fact checks.
According to
[The Advocate](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/advocate/) X Strategies, under Bruesewitz’s leadership, he has been involved in far-right activities, including the [promotion of the “groomer” slur against LGBTQ+ people.](https://www.advocate.com/news/gays-against-groomers-exposed)
Finally,
[Bruesewitz’s support for Donald Trump](https://flvoicenews.com/florida-maga-consultant-threatens-to-sue-rep-adam-kinzinger-for-defamation-demands-retraction-and-apology/) and the MAGA movement is well-documented. He has been involved in political campaigns and supported figures prominent in the MAGA movement. The involvement of X Strategies in right-wing activities, including supporting anti-LGBTQ+ rhetoric and movements, indicates strong connections to the Trump and MAGA ideologies. In general, the group promotes far-right and anti-LGBTQ+ propaganda and conspiracy theories.
