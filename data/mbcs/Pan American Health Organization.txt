History
The Pan American Health Organization (PAHO) is an
[international public health agency](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pan_American_Health_Organization) working to improve health and living standards of the people of the Americas. It was founded in December 1902. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
They are part of the United Nations system, serving as the Regional Office for the Americas of the
[World Health Organization](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/World_Health_Organization), and as the health organization of the Inter-American System.
