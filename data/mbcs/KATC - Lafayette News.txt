History
Founded in 1962, KATC – Lafayette News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Lafayette, Louisiana, United States. katc.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KATC – Lafayette News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KATC – Lafayette News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Lafayette, Louisiana, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[13-year-old booked into juvie after alleged bomb threat](https://www.katc.com/news/lafayette-parish/13-year-old-booked-into-juvie-after-alleged-bomb-threat). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Race for Crowley Mayor 2022](https://www.katc.com/news/acadia-parish/race-for-crowley-mayor-2022). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
