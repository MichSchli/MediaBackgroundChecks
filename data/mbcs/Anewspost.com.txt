History
The Anewspost
[About page](https://anewspost.com/about-us/) does not include when the site was founded; however, a domain search reveals it was [launched in 2019](https://whois.domaintools.com/anewspost.com). Anewspost states that [Wikileaks](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/wikileaks/) and [Anonymous](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/we-are-anonymous/) inspire them. The writers are anonymous, and they state on their website, “our writers maintain their anonymity, so they are free to speak their minds on controversial issues without censoring themselves.” Anewspost.com has a [meet the team page](https://anewspost.com/meet-the-team/) that is empty. The website lacks transparency. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Anewspost.com states they are a publication of Midialab LTD; however, there is no link to this site. Ownership is not known, and revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, anewspost.com publishes stories such as
[“Scientists Have Rediscovered A Rare Blue Bee That Was Thought To Be Extinct”](https://anewspost.com/scientists-have-rediscovered-a-rare-blue-bee-that-was-thought-to-be-extinct/) and [“FAA Responds To Numerous Reports Of A UFO Over Hawaii.”](https://anewspost.com/faa-responds-to-numerous-reports-of-a-ufo-over-hawaii/) The website focuses on sensationalized stories such as this [Photos Show Epstein Cuddling With Young Girl On Private Jet](https://anewspost.com/photos-show-epstein-cuddling-with-young-girl-private-jet/) and many stories covering UFOs [ Covid Relief Bill Orders Pentagon To Disclose UFO Details In 180 Days](https://anewspost.com/covid-relief-bill-orders-pentagon-to-disclose-ufo-details-in-180-days/).
In general, Anewspost.com publishes sensational news covering conspiracy-related topics; however, there does not appear to be any particular political bias.
