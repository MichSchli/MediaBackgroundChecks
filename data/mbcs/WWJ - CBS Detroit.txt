History
Founded in 1975, WWJ – CBS Detroit or 62 CBS is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Detriot, Michigan, United States. The associated website is detroit.cbslocal.com that features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Chicago area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WWJ – CBS Detroit is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the WWJ – CBS Detroit website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater Detroit Metro region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording: [Legislature OKs $25M To Aid Brain Rehab Clinics Facing Cuts.](https://detroit.cbslocal.com/2021/07/01/legislature-oks-25m-to-aid-brain-rehab-clinics-facing-cuts/) A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published from other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WWJ – CBS Detroit does not publish op-eds; however, they do report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[State Lawmakers Look To Fight Pollution in Michigan Auto Lots and Junkyards](https://detroit.cbslocal.com/2021/06/11/state-lawmakers-look-to-fight-pollution-in-michigan-auto-lots-and-junkyards/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
