History
According to their
[about page](https://iowastartingline.com/about-us/): “Iowa Starting Line is a news site devoted to bringing readers breaking news, in-depth analysis, and general coverage of Iowa Caucus and Iowa political news. Founded in January of 2015 by Pat Rynard, Starting Line works to provide a unique take on the Iowa political scene that others may not be covering. The Iowa Daily Democrat team was incorporated into our effort in mid-2016.”
Funded by / Ownership
The website is owned by Pat Rynard and funded through donations and online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, this is a strongly democratic leaning website. All stories favor democratic policy and are generally one-sided. There is a strong use of loaded language such as
[VP PENCE TRIES TO FOOL IOWANS ON GOP TAX SCAM](https://iowastartingline.com/2018/03/07/vp-pence-tries-fool-iowans-gop-tax-scam/). All stories are well-sourced to credible media outlets that have a high factual rating.
