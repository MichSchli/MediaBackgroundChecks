History
Founded in 1956 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, KGNS News serves Laredo, Texas, and the surrounding area. Kgns.tv is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KGNS News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KGNS News provides news coverage for Laredo, Texas, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[City confirms 77 active cases of COVID-19](https://www.kgns.tv/2021/01/06/city-confirms-27686-positive-cases-of-covid-19/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KGNS News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
