History
Founded in 1953 and an NBC-affiliated television station, KKCO-TV serves Grand Junction, Colorado, and the surrounding area. Nbc11news.com is the associated website for the news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.

Funded by / Ownership
11 News – KKCO is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, 11 News – KKCO provides news coverage for Grand Junction, Colorado, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Arson suspected in Fruita LDS Church fire, reward being offered](https://www.nbc11news.com/2021/04/30/10000-reward-offered-by-local-federal-agencies-on-information-that-leads-to-arrest-in-fruita-lds-fire/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, 11 News – KKCO does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
