History
Launched in 2013, Chalkbeat is a nonprofit news organization covering education and the public school system. According to their
[about page](https://www.chalkbeat.org/pages/about), they are “committed to covering one of America’s most important stories: the effort to improve schools for all children, especially those who have historically lacked access to a quality education.” Chalkbeat reports from eight locations: Chicago, Colorado, Detroit, Indiana, Newark, New York, Philadelphia, and Tennessee. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Chalkbeat Inc. is a nonprofit funded through donations and grants.
[Notable supporters](https://www.chalkbeat.org/pages/supporters) include Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, Bezos Family Foundation, Bloomberg Philanthropies, and the Democracy Fund. They also have corporate sponsors such as McGraw Hill Education. Advertising and donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Chalkbeat reports news on a broad range of topics covering public education politics and policy reform. They also produce in-depth reports focused on concerning issues. Articles and headlines often utilize moderate emotional language such as this
[What does $6 billion in COVID relief buy in Michigan schools?](https://detroit.chalkbeat.org/2021/8/16/22622237/what-does-6-billion-in-covid-relief-buy-in-michigan-schools?_ga=2.137589168.16194945.1629646406-754874325.1629646404) This story, like most, is properly sourced .gov websites.
Editorially, Chalkbeat favors the left through the promotion of
[diversity and equity](https://www.chalkbeat.org/diversity-equity). They frequently report negatively on conservatives such as this [New research finds link between districts that voted Trump and racist bullying post-election](https://www.chalkbeat.org/2019/1/9/21106480/new-research-finds-link-between-districts-that-voted-trump-and-racist-bullying-post-election). They also criticize conservative politicians regarding mask mandates in schools [Biden pushes back as governors, including in Tennessee, try to block school mask mandates](https://www.chalkbeat.org/2021/8/18/22631464/biden-cardona-mask-mandate-ban-civil-rights). In general, they publish factual news that holds a left-leaning editorial bias.
