History
The Mineral Wells Index is a newspaper published in Mineral Wells, Texas. weatherforddemocrat.com/mineral-wells/ is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Mineral Wells Index. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Mineral Wells Index covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Mineral Wells, Texas region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Early voting for May 6 city, school, bond decisions begins Monday](https://www.weatherforddemocrat.com/mineral-wells/early-voting-for-may-6-city-school-bond-decisions-begins-monday/article_d3663588-dd63-11ed-962c-53f88d129917.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
