History
The US Sun is an online-only version of The Sun, a daily tabloid newspaper published in the United Kingdom and Ireland. The US Sun focuses on US News and publishes sensational UK news for which The Sun is famous. According to their
[about page](https://www.the-sun.com/about-us/), “The US Sun has endorsed the pledges of parties of all political stripes (as in the UK) but generally leans to the center-right in its political outlook. The US Sun was launched in 2019 to bring our style of engaging, hard-hitting, entertaining journalism to a new US audience.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Rupert Murdoch’s
[News Corp](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/News_Corporation) owns The US Sun. News Corp owns many conservative/sensational media outlets around the world including [Fox News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/) and the [New York Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/new-york-post/). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The US Sun divides news into the following categories: News, Entertainment, Lifestyle, Money, Health, Sport, Tech, and Travel. The website publishes trashy, sensationalized stories similar to the UK newspaper, such as this
[HELL HOUSE Brian Coulter girlfriend’s starved kids begged for donuts after being left to starve next to ‘murdered’ brother’s corpse](https://www.the-sun.com/news/3948865/brian-coulter-girlfriend-kids-donuts-brother-corpse/). They also frequently cover celebrity gossip such as this [MORE BAD NEWS Alec Baldwin’s wife Hilaria’s Mom Brain podcast canceled as family grieves accidental shooting death on film set](https://www.the-sun.com/entertainment/3945342/alec-baldwin-wife-hilaria-podcast-canceled-accidental-shooting-death/). UK news is republished from The Sun Newspaper.
Editorially, The US Sun consistently reports negatively on the Biden administration, such as this NO HOLIDAY SPIRIT
[Biden CANCELS Halloween at the White House where trick-or-treaters dress up and get candy from presidents each year](https://www.the-sun.com/news/3946645/white-house-halloween-celebration-is-canceled/). Further, they refer to Dr. Anthony Fauci as a “Demon Doctor” SCANDAL-RIDDEN DOC [Calls to fire ‘demon doctor’ Fauci grow after ‘experiments saw puppies eaten alive by sandflies](https://www.the-sun.com/news/3944076/anthony-fauci-demon-doctor-puppy-experiments-sandflies/).’ Finally, during the Covid-19 pandemic, they published misinformation such as this [‘THE DRUG SAVED MY LIFE’ Coronavirus sufferer says Trump-backed drug saved his life](https://www.the-sun.com/news/577176/coronavirus-trump-drug-saved-life-florida/). There is [no evidence](https://www.nejm.org/doi/full/10.1056/NEJMoa2019014) to support hydroxychloroquine as an effective treatment for Covid-19. In general, The US Sun publishes sensationalized news stories from a conservative perspective and sometimes promotes misinformation.
