History
[Founded in 1884](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_News-Press), The News-Press is a daily broadsheet newspaper located in Fort Myers, Florida, serving primarily Lee County, as well as parts of Hendry, Collier, and Charlotte Counties. [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Fort Myers News-Press. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Fort Myers News-Press covers local news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Lee, Charlotte counties lead Florida in recycling rates](https://www.news-press.com/story/tech/science/environment/2019/08/01/lee-charlotte-counties-lead-florida-recycling-rates/1819942001/). National and international news is covered via the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [USA Today Network](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/).
Editorially, the paper used to endorse
[Democratic candidates](https://www.news-press.com/story/opinion/2014/10/19/news-press-endorsements/17532757/) but no longer offers endorsements. Further, the editorials tend to favor left-leaning positions such as this: [Trump stoked racial division in 2016 and again now: Your say in News-Press Thursday, July 25, 2019](https://www.news-press.com/story/opinion/readers/2019/07/25/trump-stoked-racial-tension-2016-and-again-now-your-say-news-press/1821595001/). Generally, news reporting is balanced and sourced properly, while editorials tend to favor the left moderately.
