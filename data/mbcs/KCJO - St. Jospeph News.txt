History
Founded in 2014, KCJO – St. Joseph News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to St. Joseph, Missouri. The associated website is newspressnow.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The News-Press & Gazette Company (NPG) owns KCJO – St. Joseph News and the associated website. NPG owns and publishes several TV and Radio stations and newspapers, primarily in the midwest USA. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The KCJO – St. Joseph News website publishes news for the greater St. Joseph, Missouri area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias, such as this:
[Man charged with murder in shooting on Kickapoo Reservation](https://www.newspressnow.com/news/local_news/courts/man-charged-with-murder-in-shooting-on-kickapoo-reservation/article_48ff92de-06e2-11ed-ba88-c7021bda01ed.html). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [CBS News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/), [CNN Newswire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/left/cnn-bias/), and other large, credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[An encouraging trend in highway safety](https://www.newspressnow.com/opinion/editorials/an-encouraging-trend-in-highway-safety/article_9ebc758e-036d-11ed-bf3e-5b971981688b.html). Political reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides and avoiding using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
