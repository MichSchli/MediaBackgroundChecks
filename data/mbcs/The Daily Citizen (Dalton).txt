History
The Daily Citizen-News is a daily broadsheet newspaper published in Dalton, Georgia.
[Founded in 1847](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Daily_Citizen_(Dalton,_Georgia)), the paper circulates throughout North Georgia. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Daily Citizen-News is owned by
[Community Newspaper Holdings, Inc.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNHI), an American publisher of newspapers and advertising-related publications throughout the United States. Subscription fees and advertising generate revenue for the paper.
Analysis / Bias
Staff writers cover local news with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Whitfield County commissioners to consider property tax cut Monday](https://www.dailycitizen.news/news/local_news/whitfield-county-commissioners-to-consider-property-tax-cut-monday/article_1c9896bf-38b1-53f9-aa8b-ba1a379ac110.html). Coverage of national news is provided by the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other CNHI publications.
A review of opinion pieces reveals a leftward tilt with stories such as
[One more obstacle removed for those hesitant to get vaccinated against COVID](https://www.dailycitizen.news/opinion/editorials/editorial-one-more-obstacle-removed-for-those-hesitant-to-get-vaccinated-against-covid/article_f2924b9d-3b8a-5298-9a27-1eb425ae0396.html). Although The Daily Citizen-News does offer some right-leaning opinions, the majority lean left in bias. In general, they report the news factually and with a left-leaning editorial bias.
