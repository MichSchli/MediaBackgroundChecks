History
[Demos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demos_(U.S._think_tank)) is a United States-based think tank, research, and policy center founded in 2000 that presents a liberal viewpoint on economic issues. Their focus includes election reform, economic security, sustainability, and alternative measures of economic progress.
