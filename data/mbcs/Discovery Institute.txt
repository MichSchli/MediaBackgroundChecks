History
[Founded in 1990](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Discovery_Institute), The Discovery Institute is a non-profit Christian public policy think tank based in Seattle, Washington, best known for its advocacy of the pseudoscientific principle of intelligent design. The current President is Steven J. Buri. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Discovery Institute is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit primarily funded through donations. They also publish the website
[Evolution News.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/evolution-news-and-views/)
