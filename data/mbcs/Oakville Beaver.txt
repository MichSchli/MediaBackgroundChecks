History
The Oakville Beaver is a newspaper published in Oakville, Ontario, Canada. insidehalton.com/oakville-on/ provides online local, national, and world news for the greater Oakville, Ontario region.

The publisher of the Oakville Beaver is Metroland Media Group Ltd., a subsidiary of Nordstar Capital LP. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In 2015 and
[2021](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2021/09/19/the-stars-editorial-board-endorses-liberals-for-2021-federal-election.html), the [Toronto Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/) (Nordstar), which owns the Oakville Beaver publisher Metroland Media Group, [endorsed](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2015/10/09/for-justin-trudeau-and-the-liberal-party-editorial.html) the Liberal Party under Justin Trudeau.
In review, the Oakville Beaver uses low-biased wording when reporting local news such as this:
[Town of Oakville looking into pilot project that lets youth and seniors ride transit free](https://www.insidehalton.com/news-story/10843287-town-of-oakville-looking-into-pilot-project-that-lets-youth-and-seniors-ride-transit-free/). National news is derived from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/), while world news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, a review of op-eds slightly favors the left. In general, they are fact-based and hold a left-leaning editorial bias.
