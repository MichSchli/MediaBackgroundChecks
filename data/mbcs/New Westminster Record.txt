History
New Westminster Record is an English-language newspaper in New Westminster, BC, Canada. newwestrecord.ca is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the New Westminster Record. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, New Westminster Record publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater New Westminster, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[New West finance director taking on a senior role at Metro Vancouver](https://www.newwestrecord.ca/local-news/new-west-finance-director-taking-on-a-senior-role-at-metro-vancouver-7073772). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, New Westminster Record’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[We should seek First Nations advice on New West place names.](https://www.newwestrecord.ca/opinion/letter-we-should-seek-first-nations-advice-on-new-west-place-names-7065415) The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
