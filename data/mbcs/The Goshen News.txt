History
The Goshen News is a newspaper published in the city of Goshen, Indiana. goshennews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Goshen News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Goshen News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Goshen, Indiana, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Three people injured in Elkhart shooting](https://www.goshennews.com/news/three-people-injured-in-elkhart-shooting/article_ee972588-3c1e-11ed-b070-6319ff6b4e7c.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
