History
The Montgomery Herald is a newspaper published in Montgomery, West Virginia. weatherforddemocrat.com/mineral-wells/ is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Montgomery Herald. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Montgomery Herald covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Montgomery, West Virginia region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Amid urban outbreaks, signs point to HIV spread in rural West Virginia](https://www.montgomery-herald.com/news/amid-urban-outbreaks-signs-point-to-hiv-spread-in-rural-west-virginia/article_724b83e2-c721-11ec-8545-332c70793bd4.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
