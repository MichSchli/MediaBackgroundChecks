History
Formerly the Muncie Voice,
[Middletown Media](http://middletownmedia.com/) is a progressive website featuring news and commentary. The website lacks transparency as they do not have an about page. The Facebook page states, “Alternative media whose primary focus is transformative – hold existing corporate media, government, and private sectors accountable.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Todd Smekens owns and publishes Middletown Media. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The website appears to serve as a blog for Todd Smekens, publishing articles referencing other sources with Smekens opinion added. The website also features guest writers who hold left-leaning biases such as this
[The FBI is Harassing the Antiwar Movement](https://muncievoice.com/25140/the-fbi-is-harassing-the-antiwar-movement/). This story is appropriately sourced.
Most stories and opinions favor the progressive left with support for anti-war,
[Pro-Climate action](https://muncievoice.com/25153/research-totalenergies-has-been-delaying-the-climate-transition-for-50-years/), and support of [LGBTQ rights](https://muncievoice.com/8076/gay-rights-victory-scotus-strikes-down-doma/). In general, they hold a progressive left perspective and report factually, though one-sided in nature.
