History
The Meadville Tribune is a newspaper published in Meadville, Pennsylvania. meadvilletribune.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Meadville Tribune. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Meadville Tribune covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Meadville, Pennsylvania, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Local unemployment rate drops in September](https://www.meadvilletribune.com/news/local-unemployment-rate-drops-in-september/article_0026e5be-7818-11ee-be89-0341aa142fdc.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
