History
According to their
[about page](http://www.homelandsecuritynewswire.com/about-us), “The Homeland Security News Wire is the homeland security industry’s largest online daily news publication, authoritative, in-depth analysis and coverage of the day’s most important homeland security stories.” The website does not list who and when the website was founded, but a [Whois search](http://whois.domaintools.com/homelandsecuritynewswire.com) reveals the domain was purchased in 2008. The editor-in-chief is Ben Frankel. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
News Wire Publications, LLC, owns Homeland Security News Wire. Funding is not disclosed. However, they do provide information on how to
[purchase advertising space](http://www.homelandsecuritynewswire.com/advertising-options).
Analysis / Bias
Homeland Security News Wire provides news and information regarding homeland security issues. There is minimal use of loaded words in headlines and articles such as this:
[Netanyahu sets out new Syria Policy.](http://www.homelandsecuritynewswire.com/dr20180713-netanyahu-sets-out-new-syria-policy) All information contained in articles is well-sourced to either credible mainstream media outlets or .GOV sites. A [factual search](https://factualsearch.news/#gsc.tab=0&gsc.q=homeland%20security%20news%20wire&gsc.sort=&gsc.ref=fact_checking) reveals they have not failed a fact check.
