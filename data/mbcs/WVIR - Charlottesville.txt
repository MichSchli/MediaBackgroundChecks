History
Founded in 1973 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, WVIR – Charlottesville serves Charlottesville, Virginia, and the surrounding area. nbc29.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WVIR – Charlottesville is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WVIR – Charlottesville provides news coverage for Charlottesville, Virginia, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[RVA Vaccination Hunters working to get Virginia vaccinated against COVID-19](https://www.nbc12.com/2021/10/14/rva-vaccination-hunters-working-get-virginia-vaccinated-against-covid-19/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WVIR – Charlottesville does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
