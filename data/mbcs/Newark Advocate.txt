History
The Newark Advocate is a newspaper that serves Newark, Ohio, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. newarkjadvocate.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Newark Advocate. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Newark Advocate covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Newark, Ohio, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Applicants sought for open Licking Park District board seat](https://www.newarkadvocate.com/story/news/local/2023/11/21/applicants-sought-for-open-licking-park-district-board-seat/71613787007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Newark Advocate does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[A century ago, many Newark, Licking County candidates were KKK-endorsed](https://www.newarkadvocate.com/story/opinion/columnists/2023/11/03/faith-works-many-1923-newark-candidates-were-kkk-endorsed/71393260007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
