History
Founded in 1953 and an ABC-affiliated television station, WHSV-TV serves Harrisonburg, Virginia, and the surrounding area. Whsv.com is the associated website for the news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WHSV-TV is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WHSV.com provides news coverage for Harrisonburg, Virginia, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Forest fire in Rockbridge County at 10% containment as crews monitor fluctuating conditions](https://www.whsv.com/2021/05/01/crews-respond-to-forest-fire-in-rockbridge-county/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, WHSV does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
