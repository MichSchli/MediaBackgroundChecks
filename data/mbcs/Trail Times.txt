History
Trail Times is a newspaper published in Trail, British Columbia, Canada. The website trailtimes.ca covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Trail, BC, area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Trail Times. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Trail Times covers news for Trail and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[B.C. boy, 13, brings loaded gun to school, gets 6 months ‘custodial’ sentence](https://www.trailtimes.ca/news/bc-boy-13-brings-loaded-gun-to-school-gets-6-months-custodial-sentence-7301625). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Trail Times does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
