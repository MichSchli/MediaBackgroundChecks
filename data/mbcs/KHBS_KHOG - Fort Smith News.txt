History
Founded in 1953, KHBS/KHOG – Fort Smith News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Fort Smith and Fayetteville, Arkansas, United States. 4029tv.com is the associated website that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Fort Smith and Fayetteville areas. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KHBS/KHOG – Fort Smith News is owned by Hearst Communications, which owns newspapers, magazines, television channels, and television stations, including the
[San Francisco Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-francisco-chronicle/), the [Houston Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/houston-chronicle/), and [Esquire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/esquire-magazine/). According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Hearst donated [70% to Republican candidates](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/recipients?id=D000034391) and causes in 2020. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KHBS/KHOG – Fort Smith News reports on news, crime, sports, weather, and entertainment for the greater Fort Smith and Fayetteville, AK areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Benton County has $20 million in emergency rental assistance available for people impacted by COVID-19](https://www.4029tv.com/article/benton-county-has-dollar20-million-in-emergency-rental-assistance-available-for-people-impacted-by-covid-19/39767055#). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other Hearst affiliates typically cover the national news.
Editorially, KHBS/KHOG – Fort Smith News does not publish editorial pieces, and when reporting on national politics, there is minimal bias as the primary source is usually the Associated Press. In general, they present fact-based, low-biased news.
