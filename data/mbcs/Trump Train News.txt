History
[Founded in 2016](http://whois.domaintools.com/trumptrainnews.com), Trump Train News is a hyper-partisan conservative, Pro-Trump website. The website lacks transparency as they offer an [about page](https://trumptrainnews.com/about) with minimal information, rarely names authors and ownership is not disclosed. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Trump Train News does not disclose ownership; revenue is derived through advertising and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Trump Train News typically quotes a news story from another source and then adds a few sentences or a paragraph of commentary. Some sources used are
[Fox News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news/), [Daily Wire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-daily-wire/), [Daily Caller](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-caller/), and [The Hill](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-hill/). Story selection always favors the right, and former President Trump, such as this [California Governor Gavin Newsom, Praises Trump’s Response to Virus](https://trumptrainnews.com/articles/california-governor-gavin-newsom-praises-trump-s-response-to-virus). Headlines are moderately sensationalized, such as this: [Koch-Funded Think Tank Leader Wishes Coronavirus Death on Trump](https://trumptrainnews.com/articles/koch-funded-think-tank-leader-wishes-coronavirus-death-on-trump).
The website has published conspiracy theories regarding Election Fraud
[Trump to Hold Campaign-Style Rallies in Fight Against Election Fraud](https://trumptrainnews.com/2020/11/09/trump-to-hold-campaign-style-rallies-in-fight-against-election-fraud/). They have also promoted misinformation on [Covid-19](https://trumptrainnews.com/2020/03/24/paper-of-record-sneers-at-possible-covid-19-cure-as-its-subscribers-drop-dead/) and debunked conspiracy theories such as [Seth Rich](https://trumptrainnews.com/2018/03/02/new-evidence-emerges-in-mysterious-death-of-dnc-staffer/).
Generally, all stories favor the Right and are mixed in factual reporting.
