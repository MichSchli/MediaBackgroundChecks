History
The Kingston Daily Freeman is a newspaper published in Kingston, New York, United States. dailyfreeman.com is the website for the paper covering local news, sports, crime, lifestyle, and opinion.
Read our profile on the
[USA government’s influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The Kingston Daily Freeman is owned by
[MediaNews Group](https://www.medianewsgroup.com/our-brands/) and published by Digital First Media, which publishes 190 newspapers across the USA. Digital First Media is owned by [Alden Global Capital LLC](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alden_Global_Capital), a New York-Based Hedge Fund described as “ [Vulture Capitalists](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/04/07/business/media/denver-post-opinion-owner.html)” by former [Denver Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/denver-post/) writers for the cuts made to newsrooms. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Kingston Daily Freeman publishes local and regional news through journalists with minimal bias, such as this:
[Kingston railroad crossing at Foxhall Avenue closed until 4 p.m., city says](https://www.medianewsgroup.com/communities/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Washington Post](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/).
Editorially, The Kingston Daily Freeman publishes an appropriate mix of right and left-leaning opinions. In general, they report factually and with credible sources, while op-eds are mostly balanced with differing viewpoints.
