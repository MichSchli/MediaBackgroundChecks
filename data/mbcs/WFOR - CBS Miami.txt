History
Founded in 1964, WFOR – CBS Miami is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Miami, Florida, United States. The associated website is miami.cbslocal.com that features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Chicago area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WFOR – CBS Miami is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the WFOR – CBS Miami website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater Miami and Fort. Lauderdale Metro region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording: [Miami-Dade County Public Schools Mask Policy Expected Wednesday](https://miami.cbslocal.com/2021/08/18/miami-dade-public-schools-mask-policy/). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published from other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WFOR – CBS Miami does not publish op-eds; however, they do report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[‘Death By DeSantis’: Rep. Debbie Wasserman Schultz Blames Governor For State’s COVID-19 Surge](https://miami.cbslocal.com/2021/07/19/wasserman-schultz-blames-ron-desantis-florida-covid-surge/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
