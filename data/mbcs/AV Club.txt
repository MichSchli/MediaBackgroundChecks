History
[Founded in 1993](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_A.V._Club), The A.V. Club is a website featuring reviews, interviews, and other articles that examine films, music, television, books, games, and pop culture. The current editor-in-chief is Laura M. Browning. [Read our country media profile of the USA.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The AV Club is owned by
[G/O Media Inc](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/G/O_Media)., a media company that runs [Gizmodo](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/gizmodo/), [Kotaku](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/kotaku/), [Jezebel](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/jezebel/), [The Onion](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-onion/), and other sister websites. G/O was formed in early 2019 when Great Hill Partners, a private equity firm, purchased the websites from [Univision](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Univision). Revenue is derived from advertising, sponsored content, and sales of merchandise.
Analysis / Bias
In review, AV Club reports news on entertainment, conducts interviews, and offers reviews. Entertainment news is published with minimal bias, such as this:
[Mike Schur on making The Judge’s dreams come true with a surprise Good Place cameo](https://tv.avclub.com/mike-schur-on-making-the-judge-s-dreams-come-true-with-1840910984). The AV Club infrequently reports on politics, but when they do, it is usually in the context of entertainment and frequently negative toward former President Trump: [Geopolitical checkmate: Iran might have a bloodthirsty communist dolphin squadron at their disposal](https://news.avclub.com/geopolitical-checkmate-iran-might-have-a-bloodthirsty-1840927504).
In another story, they portray right-wing media outlet
[Fox News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news/) negatively, such as this: [Megyn Kelly, Fox News accusers react to Bombshell: “It was worse than that.”](https://news.avclub.com/megyn-kelly-fox-news-accusers-react-to-bombshell-it-1840931270) In general, AV Club publishes information related to their genre, and they present with a left-leaning bias when reporting on politics.
