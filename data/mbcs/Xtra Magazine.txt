History
[Xtra Magazine](https://xtramagazine.com/about), a non-profit online magazine launched in 1984, caters to the LGBTQ2S+ communities in Toronto, Vancouver, and Ottawa. It covers politics, culture, health, sex, and relationships, promoting LGBTQ2S+ freedom and diversity. The magazine is divided into Culture, Politics, and Sister Sites sections, covering a range of topics from Drag Race to Activism. [Read our profile on Canadian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Xtra Magazine](https://xtramagazine.com/standards#ownership) is owned by [Pink Triangle Press](https://ca.linkedin.com/company/pink-triangle-press), a Canadian non-profit organization founded in 1971. The company also operates Squirt.org and Guy Spy, which are gay dating sites, and has produced TV shows such as Bump! and The Gayest Show Ever. These, along with advertising, contribute to its revenue. Pink Triangle Press is a volunteer-run company without owners or shareholders.
