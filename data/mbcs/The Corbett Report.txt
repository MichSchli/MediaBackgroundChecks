History
Founded in 2007 by James Corbett, The Corbett Report is an independent, listener-supported alternative news source. According to their
[about page](https://www.corbettreport.com/about/), “The Corbett Report is edited, webmastered, written, produced and hosted by James Corbett. James Corbett has been living and working in Japan since 2004.” They further state, “It operates on the principle of open-source intelligence and provides podcasts, interviews, articles, and videos about breaking news and important issues from 9/11 Truth and false flag terror to the Big Brother police state, eugenics, geopolitics, the central banking fraud and more.” [Read our profiles on Japan’s media and government](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/japan-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
James Corbett owns the Corbett Report, and funding is derived through
[subscription fees](https://www.corbettreport.com/members/) and [DVD sales](https://www.corbettreport.com/shop/).
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Corbett Report publishes Youtube videos, podcasts, and articles from a right-wing conspiracy perspective. The website touches on many different conspiracies and pseudoscience propaganda. Below is a shortlist of topics.
[9/11 Whistleblowers (Full Documentary)](https://www.corbettreport.com/9-11-whistleblowers-full-documentary/)(promotes alternative theories about 9/11) [How To Engineer A Crisis](https://www.corbettreport.com/how-to-engineer-a-crisis/)(False Flag Operations) [Chemtrails Exposed: The past, present, and future of the New Manhattan Project](https://www.corbettreport.com/chemtrails-exposed-the-past-present-and-future-of-the-new-manhattan-project/)( [Chemtrail](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-chemtrails/)conspiracies and pseudoscience) [Never Forget: What the Deep State Wants You to Remember About 9/11](https://www.corbettreport.com/never-forget-what-the-deep-state-wants-you-to-remember-about-9-11/)(Deep State) [The IPCC Prepares to Release More Hot Air](https://www.corbettreport.com/the-ipcc-prepares-to-release-more-hot-air/)(Human Influenced [Climate Change Denial](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-climate-change-denialism/)) [Interview 1370 – James Corbett on the New World Order](https://www.corbettreport.com/interview-1370-james-corbett-on-the-new-world-order/)(New World Order)
Politically, the Corbett Report promotes human-influenced climate change denialism, and
[Socialism fears promotion](https://www.corbettreport.com/earth-to-trump-america-is-already-a-socialist-country/).
