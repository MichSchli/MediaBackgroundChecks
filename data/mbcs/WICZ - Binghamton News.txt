History
Founded in 1957, WICZ – Binghamton News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station licensed to Binghamton, New York, United States. The associated website wicz.com covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the Binghamton region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Stainless Media owns WICZ – Binghamton News. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WICZ – Binghamton News covers news, sports, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Binghamton, New York area. Local news is covered with minimally loaded language, such as this
[Binghamton Man Found Guilty of DWI](https://www.wicz.com/story/46982989/binghamton-man-found-guilty-of-dwi). Many local news stories contain videos from the TV station. The national news is primarily republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WICZ – Binghamton News does not publish op-eds; however, when covering politics, they present info with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden introduces key members of White House science team](https://www.wicz.com/story/43183835/biden-introduces-key-members-of-white-house-science-team). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
