History
The Carroll County News is a daily newspaper published in Carroll County, Arkansas. carrollconews.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for Carroll County.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Carroll County News is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Carroll County News covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Carroll County, Arkansas area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[District 28 State Senate race County pushes King over the top in runoff.](https://www.carrollconews.com/2022/06/28/district-state-senate-race/) National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
