History
The Newburyport Daily News is a newspaper published in Newburyport, Massachusetts. newburyportnews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Newburyport Daily News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Newburyport Daily News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Newburyport, MA, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Multi-vehicle crash snarls traffic at Route 1 rotary](https://www.newburyportnews.com/multimedia/photos/multi-vehicle-crash-snarls-traffic-at-route-1-rotary/image_410e1130-401c-11ee-99d1-33db28899262.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
