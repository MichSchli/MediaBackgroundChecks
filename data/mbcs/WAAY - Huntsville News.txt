History
Founded in 1959, WAAY – Huntsville News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Huntsville, Alabama, United States. waaytv.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Huntsville area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WAAY – Huntsville News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WAAY – Huntsville News publishes news for Huntsville and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Huntsville magistrate allowed to keep working despite arrest following altercation involving gun](https://www.waaytv.com/news/huntsville-magistrate-allowed-to-keep-working-despite-arrest-following-altercation-involving-gun/article_727bc256-fefc-11ec-9c39-23d9c212c19f.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
