History
Founded in 1956, KTNV – Las Vegas News is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. ktnv.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTNV – Las Vegas News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KTNV – Las Vegas News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Las Vegas, Nevada area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Wynn Resorts announces ‘unprecedented’ million-dollar Formula 1 Las Vegas Grand Prix package](https://www.ktnv.com/sports/wynn-resorts-announces-unprecedented-million-dollar-formula-1-las-vegas-grand-prix-package). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Clark County Election Department contacts thousands of voters who need their ballot ‘cured’ before 5 p.m.](https://www.ktnv.com/news/clark-county-election-department-contacts-thousands-of-voters-who-need-their-ballot-cured-before-5-p-m) In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
