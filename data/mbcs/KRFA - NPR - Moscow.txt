History
KRFA – NPR – Moscow is an NPR FM radio station (90.7 FM) based in Moscow, Idaho, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KRFA – NPR – Moscow is owned and broadcast by Washington State University through Northwest Public Broadcasting. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
