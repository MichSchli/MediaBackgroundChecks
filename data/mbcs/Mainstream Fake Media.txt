History
According to a
[domain search](https://whois.domaintools.com/mainstreamfakemedia.com), the domain was purchased in 2017. Mainstream Fake Media is a conservative news curation website that lacks transparency as it does not offer an about page, name editors or describes ownership. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Mainstream Fake Media lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Mainstream Fake Media does not produce original content but rather provides links to external news sources. Typically, they utilize Right-biased, questionable, or conspiracy-related sources. The most common source utilized are
[Newsmax](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/newsmax-bias-credibilty-reliability/) (Far Right-Questionable), [RedState](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/red-state/) (Right-Mixed), [PJ Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pj-media/) (Questionable), [Breitbart](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/breitbart/) (Questionable), [The Blaze](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-blaze/) (Questionable), and the [Daily Caller](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-caller/) (Right-Mixed). In general, Mainstream Fake Media demonstrates a far-right bias based on story selection that always favors the right and low factual reporting due to poor sources who frequently fail fact checks.
