History
Founded in 1947, WTMJ – Milwaukee News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to Milwaukee, Wisconsin, United States. tmj4.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WTMJ – Milwaukee News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WTMJ – Milwaukee News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Milwaukee, Wisconsin area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[16-year-old killed in 95th and Silver Spring shooting](https://www.tmj4.com/news/local-news/16-year-old-killed-in-95th-and-silver-spring-shooting). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden open to talks with Putin if he’s ready to end invasion of Ukraine](https://www.tmj4.com/news/national/biden-says-hed-speak-with-putin-if-he-wants-to-end-invasion-of-ukraine). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
