History
Burnaby Now is an English-language newspaper in Bowen Island, British Columbia, Canada. burnabynow.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns Burnaby Now. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Burnaby Now publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Burnaby, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Judge awards $570K to man fired from Burnaby company – by his brothers](https://www.burnabynow.com/local-news/judge-awards-570k-to-man-fired-from-burnaby-company-by-his-brothers-6590043). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Burnaby Now’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Opinion: Eby has an opportunity to drastically address B.C.’s mental health and addictions crisis](https://www.burnabynow.com/economy-law-politics/opinion-eby-has-an-opportunity-to-drastically-address-bcs-mental-health-and-addictions-crisis-6575653). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
