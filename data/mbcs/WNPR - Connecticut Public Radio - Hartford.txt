History
WNPR – Connecticut Public Radio – Hartford is an NPR FM radio station (90.5 FM) based in Hartford, Connecticut, serving the surrounding area.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WNPR – Connecticut Public Radio – Hartford is owned and broadcast by Connecticut Public Broadcasting. Revenue is derived through institutional grants, individual contributions, and fees paid by users of the Public Radio Satellite System. NPR stations receive
[less than 1%](https://gigafact.org/fact-briefs/does-npr-receive-less-1-its-funding-directly-federal-government) of their funding from the federal government.
