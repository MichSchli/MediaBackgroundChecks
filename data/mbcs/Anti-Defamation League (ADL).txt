History
[Founded in 1913](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anti-Defamation_League), The Anti-Defamation League is an international Jewish non-governmental organization based in the United States. Describing itself as “the nation’s premier civil rights/human relations agency,” the ADL states that it “fights antisemitism and all forms of bigotry, defends democratic ideals and protects civil rights for all,” doing so through “information, education, legislation, and advocacy.” The ADL states [their mission](https://www.adl.org/who-we-are/our-mission) as “To stop the defamation of the Jewish people, and to secure justice and fair treatment to all…” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Anti-Defamation League is a

Analysis / Bias
In review, the Anti-Defamation League is an organization that advocates against bigotry and for civil rights. While the focus is on antisemitism, they cover all religious, racial, and ethnic hate. The website produces a blog and articles that utilize minimal loaded words such as this:
[How Facebook and Twitter Help Amplify Fringe Websites](https://www.adl.org/news/article/how-facebook-and-twitter-help-amplify-fringe-websites). This article is correctly sourced to credible media outlets such as the [BBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/bbc/) and [The Guardian](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-guardian/). The blog also reports news related to the ADL’s mission, such as this: [FBI Arrests White Supremacist Conor Climo, Who Reportedly Targeted Jews, LGBTQ Community](https://www.adl.org/blog/fbi-arrests-white-supremacist-conor-climo-who-reportedly-targeted-jews-lgbtq-community).
The ADL has a section called “Media Watch” in which they respond to media organizations who report false or questionable information such as this:
[Letter to the New York Times Regarding Editorial on Silencing Hate Speech](https://www.adl.org/news/media-watch/letter-to-the-new-york-times-regarding-editorial-on-silencing-hate-speech). Finally, the ADL publishes op-eds that appear in newspapers and media. Editorially, there is a left-leaning bias and unfavorable reporting on former President Donald Trump, such as this: [White Supremacist Terror is Rising, and Trump’s Policies Kneecap Our Ability to Fight Back](https://www.adl.org/news/op-ed/white-supremacist-terror-is-rising-and-trumps-policies-kneecap-our-ability-to-fight-back).
In general, ADL reports news with low bias; however, editorial content does lean left. Further, the ADL advocates for policies favored by the liberal left, such as a two-state solution for Palestine and Israel and supporting same-sex
[ marriage](https://www.adl.org/news/op-ed/white-supremacist-terror-is-rising-and-trumps-policies-kneecap-our-ability-to-fight-back) and the DREAM Act.
