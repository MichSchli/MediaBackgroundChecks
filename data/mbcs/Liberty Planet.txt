History
[Founded in 2020](https://whois.domaintools.com/libertyplanets.com), Liberty Planet is an anonymously published conservative conspiracy website. According to their [about page](https://www.libertyplanets.com/about-us/), “Maintaining liberty is the core of the American mission. Here at Liberty Planet, we feel the best way we can lend our support is through information. This is where we fight back against the liberal media and expose the truth
about those waging war on our freedoms.”
The website lacks transparency as it does not name authors, editors, or owners.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Liberty Planet does not disclose ownership. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Liberty Planet publishes summarized news and opinion stories from other sources. Articles and headlines often contain highly emotional wording such as this
[JANUARY 6TH COMMITTEE PLAYBOOK EXPOSED: Patriot Act Being Used AGAINST Protesters (1st Amendment Protections NO LONGER APPLY), Plan on ROUNDING UP Christians and Conservatives](https://www.libertyplanets.com/articles/january-6th-committee-playbook-exposed-patriot-act-being-used-against-protesters-1st-amendment-protections-no-longer-apply-plan-on-rounding-up-christians-and-conservatives/). The articles reviewed do not contain hyperlinked sourcing; however, they mention the source. Many of the sources they utilize are extreme right conspiracy websites that routinely publish false information, such as [Infowars](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/infowars-alex-jones/) and [Natural News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/natural-news/).
Editorially, they offer sycophantic level support for former President Donald Trump and promote over-the-top conspiracy theories such as this
[‘HOLOCAUST Level MASS DEATH Is Coming’: CCP Virus’ Vaccine’ Spike Proteins Are SHEDDING, Giving People HEART ATTACKS, STROKES and MORE; CDC Confirms MASSIVE 40% SPIKE in DEATH RATE, Pfizer TARGETING Anti-Depopulation/Big Pharma Greed JOURNALISTS](https://www.libertyplanets.com/articles/holocaust-level-mass-death-is-coming-ccp-virus-vaccine-spike-proteins-are-shedding-giving-people-heart-attacks-strokes-and-more-cdc-confirms-massive-40-spike-in-death-rate-pfizer-targetin/). Essentially, everything in this article is false. See failed fact checks.
