History
The Arenac County Independent is a newspaper published in Standish, Michigan. arenacindependent.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the Munising Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Aranec County Independent is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Arenac County Independent covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Arenac County, Michigan, area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[County ranks 49th in state deer harvests](https://www.arenacindependent.com/articles/county-ranks-49th-in-state-deer-harvests/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
