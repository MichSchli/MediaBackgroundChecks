Analysis / Bias
Humanium’s objectives span three complementary approaches:
- raising awareness of children’s rights to more than five million people each year worldwide
- legal assistance for victims of children’s rights violations
- supporting local partners with projects to help children
In review, Humanium assists children around the globe, especially in poverty and war situations. There is minimal use of loaded emotional language with the use of proper sourcing to credible media and organizations. Politically, Humanium supports progressive left causes.
