History
Canora Courier is an English-language newspaper in Canora, Saskatchewan, Canada. sasktoday.ca/central/canora-courier is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Canora Courier. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Canora Courier publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Canora, Saskatchewan region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[RCMP Report: Dangerous prohibited driver speeds through Canora](https://www.sasktoday.ca/central/canora-courier/rcmp-report-dangerous-prohibited-driver-speeds-through-canora-6622359). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Canora Courier’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Column: Holidays don’t have to hurt the environment](https://www.sasktoday.ca/south/opinion/column-holidays-dont-have-to-hurt-the-environment-6275752). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
