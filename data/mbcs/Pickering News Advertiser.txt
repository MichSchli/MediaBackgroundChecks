History
The Pickering News Advertiser is a newspaper published in Pickering, Ontario, Canada. durhamregion.com/pickering-on/ provides local, national, and world news for the greater Pickering, Ontario region.
Read our profile on the
[Canadian government’s influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of The Pickering News Advertiser is Metroland Media Group Ltd., a subsidiary of Nordstar Capital LP. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In 2015 and
[2021](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2021/09/19/the-stars-editorial-board-endorses-liberals-for-2021-federal-election.html), the [Toronto Star](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/toronto-star/) (Nordstar), which owns The Pickering News Advertiser’s publisher, Metroland Media Group, [endorsed](https://www.thestar.com/opinion/editorials/2015/10/09/for-justin-trudeau-and-the-liberal-party-editorial.html) the Liberal Party under Justin Trudeau.
In review, The Pickering News Advertiser uses low-biased wording when reporting local news such as this:
[Police investigate two pharmacy robberies in Pickering](https://www.durhamregion.com/news-story/10813653-police-investigate-two-pharmacy-robberies-in-pickering/). National news is derived from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/), while world news comes from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, a review of op-eds slightly favors the left. In general, they are fact-based and hold a left-leaning editorial bias.
