History
The Somerset Commonwealth-Journal is a newspaper published in the city of Somerset, Kentucky. somerset-kentucky.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Somerset Commonwealth-Journal. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Somerset Commonwealth-Journal covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Somerset, Kentucky region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[PCDC Overcrowding sees first reduction in months](https://www.somerset-kentucky.com/news/pcdc-overcrowding-sees-first-reduction-in-months/article_8af83f94-8e17-11ed-8fb7-6b52e142b00e.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
