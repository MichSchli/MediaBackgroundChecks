History
The Corbin Times-Tribune is a newspaper published in Corbin, Kentucky. thetimestribune.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Corbin Times-Tribune. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Corbin Times-Tribune covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Corbin, KY, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Development projects in Knox, Laurel counties move ahead in Round 2 of Kentucky Product Development Initiative](https://www.thetimestribune.com/community/development-projects-in-knox-laurel-counties-move-ahead-in-round-2-of-kentucky-product-development/article_aeaf4ad8-d2c0-11ee-8bd8-0314bd45648d.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
