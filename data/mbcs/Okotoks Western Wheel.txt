History
Okotoks Western Wheel is an English-language newspaper in Okotoks, Alberta, Canada. westernwheel.ca is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Okotoks Western Wheel. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Okotoks Western Wheel publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Okotoks, Alberta region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Highway 7 reopened after crash west of Okotoks](https://www.westernwheel.ca/local-news/update-highway-7-reopened-after-crash-west-of-okotoks-7184110). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Okotoks Western Wheel’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Remembering a First Nation friend](https://www.westernwheel.ca/opinion/column-remembering-a-first-nation-friend-7171112). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
