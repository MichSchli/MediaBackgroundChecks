History
Founded in 1954, KTRK – ABC13 – Houston is an ABC television network licensed to Houston, Texas, United States. ABC13.com is the associated website that provides news, weather, and sports for the Houston Metropolitan region.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KTRK – ABC13 – Houston is owned by the ABC Owned Television Stations subsidiary of
[The Walt Disney Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Walt_Disney_Company). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the KTRK – ABC13 – Houston website covers news, sports, crime, and weather for the Houston Metro area. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Amber Alert canceled for missing 4-year-old who was found safe in Houston](https://abc13.com/missing-person-persons-report-what-to-do-when-a-loved-one-is-canceled-amber-alert/11951079/). Many local stories contain embedded videos from the TV station. National and world news is covered by [ABC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/), [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), [ESPN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/espn/), and other ABC stations.
Editorially, KTRK – ABC13 – Houston does not provide opinion pieces; however, they report on politics. They provide original reporting with a slight left-leaning bias, such as this
[March For Our Lives: ‘Enough is enough’ say thousands demanding new gun measures](https://abc13.com/march-for-our-lives-uvalde-texas-buffalo/11948257/). They also rely on political news from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
