History
Founded in 1954 and an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station, KBJR – Duluth News serves Duluth, Minnesota, and the surrounding area. Kbjr6.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KBJR – Duluth News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KBJR – Duluth News provides news coverage for Duluth, Minnesota, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Roads close due to St. Louis River overflowing its banks](https://www.kbjr6.com/2022/05/16/roads-close-due-st-louis-river-overflowing-its-banks/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KBJR – Duluth News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
