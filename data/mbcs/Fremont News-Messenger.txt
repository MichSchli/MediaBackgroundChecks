History
The Fremont News-Messenger is a newspaper that serves Fremont, Ohio, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. thenews-messenger.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Fremont News-Messenger. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Fremont News-Messenger covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Fremont, Ohio, area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Local boards of election officials say people, not equipment, are key to accurate vote](https://www.thenews-messenger.com/story/news/local/2023/10/19/boards-of-elections-use-different-approaches-similar-voting-equipment-sandusky-ottawa-counties/71212464007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Fremont News-Messenger does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[Reader explains reasons to vote against Issue 1](https://www.thenews-messenger.com/story/opinion/readers/2023/08/06/letter-to-the-editor-reader-urges-no-vote-on-issue-1/70504253007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
