History
Founded in 2021 by
[L Todd Wood](https://ltoddwood.com/), the Georgia Record is a conservative news and opinion website with a history of publishing misinformation. According to its [about page](https://www.georgiarecord.com/about/), “The Georgia Record was relaunched in June of 2021 and has been extremely successful fighting corruption in the state named after King George of England. The original paper was started in 1899 and published into the early 20th century.” [Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Creative Destruction Media, owned by L Todd Wood, publishes the Georgia Record.
[CDM publishes several websites](https://creativedestructionmedia.com/cdm-sites/) using the names of old defunct newspapers. Advertising and ad-free subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Georgia Record is a conservative imposter website that is designed to look like a legitimate local newspaper. All of the websites use the same design template using an old newspaper font for their logos.
The website publishes local news related to Georgia that routinely favors the right, such as this
[Freedom Caucus Press Conference and Rally Brings Groups Together From Across Georgia And Beyond](https://www.georgiarecord.com/politics/2023/09/07/freedom-caucus-press-conference-and-rally-brings-groups-together-from-georgia-and-beyond/). This article does not provide hyperlinked sourcing but relies on video clips from the press conference.
Editorially, all stories favor the right, and particularly former
[President Trump](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/donald-trump-republican-presidential-candidate-bias-profile/), such as [Trump’s Caravan Greeted By Atlanta Residents Along His Trip Through The City](https://www.georgiarecord.com/all-posts/2023/08/25/trumps-caravan-greeted-by-atlanta-residents-along-his-trip-through-the-city/) while denigrating the left with conspiracy theories like this [Bidens regime is negotiating to relinquish U.S. ability to govern ourselves when it comes to pandemic policies.](https://www.georgiarecord.com/health/2023/02/20/biden-regime-negotiates-legally-binding-deal-to-give-chinese-backed-world-health-organization-full-authority-over-us-pandemic-policies-no-senate-approval-needed/) Based on their support for Trump, they have endorsed his lies regarding election fraud, such as this [“Explosive Revelation: Fani Willis Linked To Massive Election Fraud And Money Laundering RICO Enterprise!”](https://www.georgiarecord.com/election-integrity/2023/08/31/explosive-revelation-fani-willis-linked-to-massive-election-fraud-and-money-laundering-rico-enterprise/) This claim is [false](https://leadstories.com/hoax-alert/2023/09/222-contributions-with-zero-donor-information-do-not-prove-there-is-fraud-in-the-campaig-of-fulton-county-district-attorney-fani-willis.html), and there is no evidence of significant election fraud in the 2020 or 2022 elections.
The Georgia Record also promotes
[Covid-19 disinformation](https://www.georgiarecord.com/health/2023/09/07/the-world-health-organization-now-wants-you-to-believe-covid-is-here-to-stay-and-you-need-to-mask-up-have-they-not-read-the-study-indicating-masks-are-useless/). In general, this is an imposter website that promotes extreme right-wing conspiracy theories and pseudoscience.
