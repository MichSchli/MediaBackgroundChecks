History
Founded in 1981, WXMI – Grand Rapids News is a
[Fox](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated station licensed to Grand Rapids, Michigan, United States. fox17online.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WXMI – Grand Rapids News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WXMI – Grand Rapids News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Grand Rapids, Michigan, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[13-year-old girl dies in Montcalm County house fire](https://www.fox17online.com/news/local-news/michigan/montcalm/13-year-old-girl-dies-in-montcalm-county-house-fire). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Michigan Board of Canvassers split on ballot petition putting abortion rights in front of voters: What’s next?](https://www.fox17online.com/michigan-board-of-canvassers-split-on-ballot-petition-putting-abortion-rights-in-front-of-voters-whats-next) In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
