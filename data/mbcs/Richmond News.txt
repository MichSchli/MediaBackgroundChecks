History
The Richmond News is an English-language newspaper in Richmond, British Columbia, Canada. richmond-news.com is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Richmond News. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
The Richmond News publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Richmond, BC region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Richmond stone business closing down after 24 years](https://www.richmond-news.com/local-news/richmond-stone-business-closing-down-after-24-years-7370868). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Richmond News’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Monique Keiran: ‘Singing’ fish drowned out by human-made ocean noise](https://www.richmond-news.com/opinion/monique-keiran-singing-fish-drowned-out-by-human-made-ocean-noise-7313282). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
