History
The Mankato Free Press is a newspaper published in Mankato, Minnesota. mankatofreepress.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Mankato Free Press. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Mankato Free Press covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Mankato, Minnesota region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[HyLife pork plant likely to close, laying off 1,000](https://www.mankatofreepress.com/news/local_news/hylife-pork-plant-likely-to-close-laying-off-1-000/article_58c25ba0-d93e-11ed-a142-4b389ea6bff4.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
