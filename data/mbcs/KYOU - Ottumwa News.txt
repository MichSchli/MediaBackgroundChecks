History
Founded in 1985 and a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station, KYOU – Ottumwa News serves Ottumwa, Iowa, and the surrounding area. Kyoutv.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KYOU – Ottumwa News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KYOU – Ottumwa News provides news coverage for Ottumwa, Iowa, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Man dies from injuries suffered in Springfield motorcycle crash](https://www.ky3.com/2022/09/11/man-dies-injuries-suffered-springfield-motorcycle-crash/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KYOU – Ottumwa News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
