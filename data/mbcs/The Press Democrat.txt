History
[Founded in 1897](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Press_Democrat), The Press Democrat, with the largest circulation in the California North Bay Area, is a daily newspaper published in Santa Rosa, California. They won the [2004 George Polk Award](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Polk_Awards) for Regional Reporting, given annually by Long Island University to honor journalistic integrity and investigative reporting contributions. They have also won the Pulitzer Prize in 2018 for Breaking News Reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Sonoma Media Investments, LLC, owns and publishes The Press Democrat. Sonoma Media publishes several papers in the Sonoma region. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Press Democrat reports local and regional news through the use of journalists and other local media sources with minimal bias in wording
[Santa Rosa police chief announces retirement](https://www.pressdemocrat.com/article/news/santa-rosa-police-chief-announces-retirement/). National and international news is primarily derived from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Santa Rosa Press Democrat endorsed Hillary Clinton for President in 2016, and editorial positions slightly favor the left. All information is well-sourced and factual.
