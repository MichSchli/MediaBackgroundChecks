History
Founded in 1955 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station, KMVT – Twin Falls News serves Twin Falls, Idaho, and the surrounding area. kmvt.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KMVT – Twin Falls News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KMVT – Twin Falls News provides news coverage for Twin Falls, Idaho, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered by reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Forest Service warns of adverse conditions on roadways in South Hills](https://www.kmvt.com/2022/11/18/forest-service-warns-adverse-conditions-roadways-south-hills/). The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KMVT – Twin Falls News does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
