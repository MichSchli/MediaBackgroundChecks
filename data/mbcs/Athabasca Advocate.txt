History
Athabasca Advocate is an English-language newspaper in Athabasca, Alberta, Canada.
[townandcountrytoday.com/athabasca-news](https://www.townandcountrytoday.com/athabasca-news) is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle. [Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Athabasca Advocate. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Athabasca Advocate publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Athabasca, Alberta region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Sentencing delayed for man caught with sawed-off shotgun](https://www.townandcountrytoday.com/athabasca-news/sentencing-delayed-for-man-caught-with-sawed-off-shotgun-6406885). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Athabasca Advocate’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Stop playing Russian roulette](https://www.townandcountrytoday.com/opinion/stop-playing-russian-roulette-2265224). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
