History
[Founded in 1871](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Columbus_Dispatch), the Columbus Dispatch is a daily newspaper from Columbus, Ohio. The paper and website cover news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Columbus area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Columbus Dispatch is owned by Gannett (Gatehouse Media), which publishes
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/). In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. The newspaper is funded through advertising and paid subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Columbus Dispatch reports local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Coronavirus: What the move to online classes means for students, faculty, schools](https://www.dispatch.com/news/20200311/coronavirus-what-move-to-online-classes-means-for-students-faculty-schools). National and international news is mainly derived from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/)
Editorially, the Columbus Dispatch has always supported Republican candidates. However, in 2016 they broke this tradition and
[endorsed Democratic Candidate Hillary Clinton](https://www.10tv.com/article/columbus-dispatch-endorses-hillary-clinton-president) and Joe Biden in 2020 [Editorial: Biden can reunite, restart America](https://www.dispatch.com/story/opinion/editorials/2020/10/14/editorial-biden-can-reunite-restart-america/5977082002/). Today, the Op-Ed pages tend to slightly favor the left by only a slight margin. Although reasonably balanced, some editorials strongly favor the left, such as this [Editorial: On COVID and climate, denialism is deadly](https://www.dispatch.com/story/opinion/editorials/2021/01/29/covid-climate-change-denialism-deadly-joe-biden-knows-editorials-debates/4290330001/). In general, the Columbus Dispatch reports news factually and with reasonable balance.
