History
[Founded in 1947](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KNBC), NBC Los Angles – KNBC is an [NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/) owned-and-operated television station licensed to Los Angeles, California, United States, serving as the network’s West Coast flagship outlet. NBCLosAngeles.com is the associated website covering news, weather, sports, crime, and lifestyle for the Southern California region. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KNBC-TV is owned by NBCUniversal a subsidiary of Comcast, which owns multiple TV stations around the USA. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
