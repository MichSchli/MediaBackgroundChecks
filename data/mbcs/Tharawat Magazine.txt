History
Founded in 2008,
[Tharawat Magazine](https://www.tharawat-magazine.com/about-us/) is a family business magazine which is based in [Dubai-UAE](https://local.infobel.ae/AE100334811/tharawat_media_fz_llc-dubai.html). Tharawat Magazine focuses on family business-related topics around the world. [Ramia El Agamy Khan](https://www.facebook.com/190670083584/posts/we-are-delighted-to-welcome-ms-ramia-el-agamy-khan-editor-in-chief-tharawat-maga/10157928715273585/) is the Editor-in-Chief. [Read our profile on United Arab Emirates Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-arab-emirates-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Tharawat Magazine is owned by
[Tharawat Media FZ-LLC](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/tharawat-magazine-the-arabian-publication-for-family-businesses-launches-tablet-application-in-uae-stores-206771331.html), which is owned by [Dr. Hischam El-Agamy.](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dr-hischam-el-agamy-8312652a/?originalSubdomain=ch) He is also the co-founder of Tharawat Magazine. Revenue is derived through advertising and [subscription fees](https://www.tharawat-magazine.com/subscription/) for full access.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Tharawat Magazine provides news, profiles, expert advice, insights, and interviews on family businesses such as
[“Suvla Wines: Putting Turkey on the Viticulture Map.”](https://www.tharawat-magazine.com/stories/suvla-wines-putting-turkey-on-the-viticulture-map/) They also provide [best-practice](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/tharawat-magazine-the-arabian-publication-for-family-businesses-launches-tablet-application-in-uae-stores-206771331.html) articles such as [“What Makes Family Businesses Last?”](https://www.tharawat-magazine.com/stories/what-makes-family-businesses-last/) Lastly, they provide informational articles on the economic impact on family businesses [What is the Economic Impact of Family Businesses?](https://www.tharawat-magazine.com/economic-impact-family-businesses/) and factual lists such as this [The Ten Largest Publishing Companies in the World](https://www.tharawat-magazine.com/facts/ten-largest-publishing-companies/). All stories reviewed were properly sourced.
Editorially, they do not publish op-eds, however, they are pro-business and
[supportive](https://www.tharawat-magazine.com/craftsmanship-42/also-in-this-issue-42/launching-into-a-new-market/) of free-market capitalism. In general, they report factually and with a slight right-center bias.
