History
The Redwood Falls Gazette is a newspaper published in Redwood Falls, Minnesota. redwoodfallsgazette.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the Red Falls Area.
[Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Redwood Falls Gazette is owned by
[CherryRoad Media](https://www.cherryroad.com/cherryroad-media/), which publishes 20 newspapers in the midwest of the USA. Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Redwood Falls Gazette covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Redwood Falls, Minnesota, area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[City waives first and last rights of refusal to purchase golf course](https://www.redwoodfallsgazette.com/2022/12/22/city-waives-first-and-last-rights-of-refusal-to-purchase-golf-course/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
