History
The Point Pleasant Register is a newspaper based in Point Pleasant, West Virginia, serving Mason County and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at mydailyregister.com.com.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Point Pleasant Register and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Point Pleasant Register publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Columbus man facing drug charges](https://www.mydailyregister.com/news/71824/columbus-man-facing-drug-charges). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Point Pleasant Register leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
