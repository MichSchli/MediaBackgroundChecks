History
Founded in 2004, Wikinews is a free-content news wiki and a project of the Wikimedia Foundation. Wikinews is news written by people who have Wiki accounts.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Wikimedia Foundation owns and publishes Wikinews. Donations generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The news reported on Wikinews is typically fact-based and always properly sourced. Like all Wikis, other people can edit, correct, and refine stories. This ensures that most news stories reported have low bias and are evidence-based. In reviewing the articles, all were free of loaded words and demonstrated minimal political bias, such as this
[Younger brother of North Korean founding leader dies aged 101](https://en.wikinews.org/wiki/Younger_brother_of_North_Korean_founding_leader_dies_aged_101).
