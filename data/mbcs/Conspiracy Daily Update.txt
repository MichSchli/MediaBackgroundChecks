History
Founded in 2016, Conspiracy Daily Update is an extreme right-wing Conspiracy and Pseudoscience website. The website lacks transparency as they do not provide an about page or disclose editors.
[Read our profile on Australian media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/australia-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Conspiracy Daily Update does not disclose ownership. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Essentially, they republish snippets of news from conspiracy websites such as
[Alex Jones](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/infowars-alex-jones/) and the [Common Sense Show](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-common-sense-show/). Further, they promote [anti-vaccine propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-anti-vaccination-anti-vax/). Throughout Covid-19 they promoted misinformation that always aligned with right-leaning-Qanon-type conspiracies.
