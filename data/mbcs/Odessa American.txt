History
The Odessa American is a newspaper based in Odessa, Texas, serving Odessa and
[Ector County](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ector_County,_Texas). The website for the paper is located at [oaoa.com](http://www.oaoa.com).
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Odessa American and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
