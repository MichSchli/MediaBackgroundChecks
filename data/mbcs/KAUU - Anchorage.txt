History
Founded in 1990 and a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station, KAUU – Anchorage serves Anchorage, Alaska, and the surrounding area. Alaskasnewssource.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KAUU – Anchorage is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KAUU – Anchorage provides news coverage for Anchorage, Alaska, and the surrounding areas. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias and often has video provided by the TV station:
[Wasilla woman sets house on fire before being arrested, troopers say.](https://www.alaskasnewssource.com/2021/09/17/wasilla-woman-sets-house-fire-before-being-arrested-troopers-say/) The national news is provided through other Gray Television affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). International news generally comes from [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/) and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, KAUU – Anchorage does not offer an opinion page. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
