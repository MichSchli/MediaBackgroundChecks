History
Founded in 1948, WJZ – CBS Baltimore is a CBS-owned-and-operated television station licensed to Baltimore, Maryland, United States. The associated website is baltimore.cbslocal.com that features local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Baltimore Metro area.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WJZ – CBS Baltimore is a subsidiary of
[ViacomCBS](https://www.viacbs.com/), which National Amusements, Inc owns [.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/national-amusements) They are operated by the third-generation leadership of the Redstone family. The President of [National Amusements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/National_Amusements) and chairwoman of the company is [Shari Ellin Redstone.](https://www.forbes.com/sites/dennisjaffe/2019/08/27/the-labors-of-shari-redstone-reclaiming-a-family-legacy/#55acc0a951bf) Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the WJZ – CBS Baltimore website publishes sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and local news for the greater Baltimore Metro region through
[CBSN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/). Local news articles and headlines use minimal emotionally loaded wording: [Students Stranded On First Day Of School After 30 Baltimore City Bus Drivers Call Out](https://baltimore.cbslocal.com/2021/08/30/students-stranded-on-first-day-of-school-after-30-baltimore-city-bus-drivers-call-out/). A video accompanies most stories from the TV station. National news stories are published from other [CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/) affiliates as well as the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, WJZ – CBS Baltimore does not publish op-eds; however, they do report on politics with slightly more stories favoring the left, such as this
[Biden Readies Sweeping Policy Shifts On COVID, Economy, Immigration And More](https://baltimore.cbslocal.com/2020/11/14/biden-readies-sweeping-policy-shifts-on-covid-economy-immigration-and-more/). In general, the news is reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias when covering politics.
