History
KNDU – Richland News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated TV station licensed to Richland, Washington. The website nonstoplocal.com/tri-cities-yakima/ provides local news, sports, weather, crime, and business news. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KNDU – Richland News is owned by the
[Cowles Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cowles_Company), which owns several small TV stations and manufacturing companies in the Pacific Northwest. Revenue is derived through subscriptions, advertising, and sponsored content.
Analysis / Bias
KNDU – Richland News covers local news through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Washington Legislature considers recognizing Lunar New Year as paid state holiday](https://www.nonstoplocal.com/tri-cities-yakima/news/washington-legislature-considers-recognizing-lunar-new-year-as-paid-state-holiday/article_06d93da9-31c3-5774-923a-51704c857de4.html). Some stories are accompanied by videos from the TV news station. National and international news is derived from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, KNDU – Richland News does not provide opinion content. They maintain a neutral tone when reporting on politics, such as this
[Biden Tests Negative For Covid](https://www.nonstoplocal.com/tri-cities-yakima/news/biden-tests-negative-for-covid/article_55cd5f1a-47b9-5932-80ab-4a78c1da787a.html). In general, they are low-biased and fact-based in reporting.
