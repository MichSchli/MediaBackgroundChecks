History
Founded in 1994, WFFF – Burlington News is a
[FOX](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/)-affiliated television station licensed to Burlington, Vermont. The associated website is mychamplainvalley.com which provides news, sports, weather, politics, and crime reporting. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WFFF – Burlington News is owned by
[Nexstar Media Group](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nexstar_Media_Group), which owns around 200 TV stations nationwide. The founder, chairman, president, and CEO of Nexstar is Perry A. Sook. [According](https://www.opensecrets.org/pacs/pacgot.php?cmte=C00567388&cycle=2018) to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Nexstar Media group gave more money to Republicans in the 2018 election cycle (45% to Democrats, 55% to Republicans). However, in 2020 they gave more to Democrats (55% Democrats, 45% to Republicans). Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The WFFF – Burlington News website publishes news for the greater Burlington, Vermont area. Local news features original reporting with minimal bias such as this:
[Vermont ski resorts see more business this season](https://www.mychamplainvalley.com/news/local-news/vermont-ski-resorts-see-more-business-this-season/). Many stories also contain videos from the TV Station. National and international news is covered through Nexstar Media Wire, the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and other large credible media outlets.
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report political news with minimal bias, such as this
[Biden announces ban on unlicensed ghost gun kits](https://www.mychamplainvalley.com/news/national-news/biden-announces-ban-on-unlicensed-ghost-gun-kits/). Reporting is reasonably balanced, covering both sides, and avoids using emotional language. In general, they report news factually and with minimal bias.
