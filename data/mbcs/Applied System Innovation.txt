History
Applied System Innovation is published by
[MDPI (Multidisciplinary Digital Publishing Institute)](https://www.mdpi.com/about/history), formerly Molecular Diversity Preservation International, an open-access journal publisher based in Basel, Switzerland. Applied System Innovation is an international, [peer-reviewed](https://www.mdpi.com/editorial_process), open access journal on integrated engineering and technology. It is the journal of the [IIKII (International Institute of Knowledge Innovation and Invention)](http://www.iikii.org/) and is published online bimonthly by MDPI. MDPI publishes over [400 journals.](https://www.mdpi.com/about/journals) [Read our profile on Swiss Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/switzerland-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The publisher of Applied System Innovation is
[MDPI AG](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mdpi-multidisciplinary-digital-publishing-institute-bias/), which Shu-Kun Lin owns. Here is the list of [the Management Team.](https://www.mdpi.com/about/team) According to [“MDPI Open Access Information and Policy.”](https://www.mdpi.com/openaccess) MDPI articles and all its journals are open access where authors pay a one-time Article Processing Charge (APC) to cover the costs of peer review administration and management; here is [“General Information on MDPI’s Article Processing Charges (APC).”](https://www.mdpi.com/apc)
Analysis / Bias
Applied System Innovation covers the study of Applied System Innovations. Studies are published via open access, such as this
[Data Verification in the Agent, Combining Blockchain and Quantum Keys by Means of Multiple-Valued Logic](https://www.mdpi.com/2571-5577/6/2/51). Like all others reviewed, this study is Pro-Science, well-researched, and low-biased.
While Applied System Innovation has not failed a fact-check, its publisher has faced criticism as an open-access predatory journal. Read our full review on
[MDPI](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/mdpi-multidisciplinary-digital-publishing-institute-bias/).
