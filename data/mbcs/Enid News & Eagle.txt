History
The Enid News & Eagle is a newspaper published in Enid, Oklahoma. enidnews.com.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Enid News & Eagle. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Enid News & Eagle covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Enid, Oklahoma region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[House GOP plan would boost teacher salaries, create tax credit program](https://www.enidnews.com/news/house-gop-plan-would-boost-teacher-salaries-create-tax-credit-program/article_66732ac6-ae51-11ed-85f6-cbcb26d84eb6.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
