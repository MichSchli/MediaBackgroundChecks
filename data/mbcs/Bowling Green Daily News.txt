History
The Bowling Green Daily News is a newspaper published in Bowling Green, Kentucky. The paper covers local, national, crime, and sports news.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Bowling Green Daily News is owned by
[Boone Newspapers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Boone_Newspapers), which owns dozens of community newspapers and magazines. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscription fees.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Bowling Green Daily News primarily covers local news, sports, weather, and entertainment through the use of local journalists. Articles and headlines use minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Winter storm rages as baby arrives in bus](https://www.bgdailynews.com/news/winter-storm-rages-as-baby-arrives-in-bus/article_4248fb6a-c082-5a62-be14-4c5b3e280947.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) typically serves national and international news.
Editorially, the Bowling Green Daily News labels its opinion section appropriately, with more op-eds favoring the right. In general, the Bowling Green Daily News is factual with a right-leaning editorial bias.
