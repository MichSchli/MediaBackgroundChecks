History
[Founded in 1897](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palm_Beach_Daily_News), The Palm Beach Daily News is a daily newspaper in Florida, serving the town of Palm Beach in Palm Beach County in South Florida. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
In 2018, The Palm Beach Daily News was bought by Gatehouse Media for
[49.25 million dollars](https://www.palmbeachpost.com/news/palm-beach-post-daily-news-sold-fast-growing-gatehouse/w3vAL3HVogbSDBY342rPoI/). In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. The newspaper is funded through advertising and paid subscriptions.
