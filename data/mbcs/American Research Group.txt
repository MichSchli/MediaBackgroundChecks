History
American Research Group is based in Manchester, New Hampshire, and specializes in market research. They were founded in 1985 by Lafell Dickinson Bennett.
[Read our profile on the United States media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
American Research Group is a private company. The sale of surveys and market research generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
American Research Group conducts market research and polling via various mediums. The website does not generally report on politics.
[FiveThirtyEight](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fivethirtyeight/), an expert on measuring and rating pollster performance, has [evaluated 279 polls](https://projects.fivethirtyeight.com/pollster-ratings/american-research-group/) by ARG, indicating they are accurate 75% of the time or Mostly Factual. They also conclude that their polling favors the left with a score of +0.1, which equates to Least Biased polling. In general, ARG is a reasonably accurate pollster that demonstrates minimal bias in polling.
