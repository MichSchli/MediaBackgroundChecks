History
Founded in 1956, KRIS 6 News – Corpus Christi is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Corpus Christi, Texas, United States. kristv.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KRIS 6 News – Corpus Christi is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
KRIS 6 News – Corpus Christi covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Corpus Christi, Texas area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Pastor arrested on allegations of continuous sexual assault of a minor](https://www.kristv.com/pastor-arrested-on-allegations-of-continuous-sexual-assault-of-a-minor). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Text from Supreme Court ruling overturning Roe v. Wade](https://www.kristv.com/media/v/content/e38479715c763a4972a85cc4003d73f9). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
