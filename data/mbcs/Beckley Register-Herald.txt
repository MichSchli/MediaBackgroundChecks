History
The Beckley Register-Herald is a newspaper published in Beckley, West Virginia. register-herald.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Beckley Register-Herald. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Beckley Register-Herald covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Beckley, West Virginia, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[Beckley Council to vote on city manager government](https://www.register-herald.com/news/beckley-council-to-vote-on-city-manager-government/article_71c53352-ac1a-11ee-a1ca-e34e0d795ffc.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
