History
Founded in 1947, WRC-TV is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/) television station licensed to the American capital city of Washington, District of Columbia. The associated news website is nbcwashington.com, covering local news, sports, weather, and politics. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
NBCWashington.com and WRC-TV are owned by NBC Universal, which is in turn owned by Comcast Corporation. Advertising generates revenue.
