History
The Fulton County Expositor is a newspaper based in Wauseon, Ohio, serving Fulton County and the surrounding area. The website for the paper is located at fcnews.org.
Funded by / Ownership
[AIM Media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/AIM_Media_Texas) owns the Fulton County Expositor and several small newspapers in Texas, Ohio, and Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Fulton County Expositor publishes local news through the use of local journalists. Local news uses minimally loaded wording like this
[Red Cross issues call for health and mental health volunteers](https://www.fcnews.org/news/35506/red-cross-issues-call-for-health-and-mental-health-volunteers). The website does not cover national news.
Editorially, the Fulton County Expositor leans right in its views as more op-eds slightly favor the right. In general, the news is reported factually and with a right-leaning editorial bias.
