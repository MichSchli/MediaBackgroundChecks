History
Keremeos Review is a newspaper published in Keremeos, British Columbia, Canada. The website keremeosreview.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Keremeos, BC area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns Keremeos Review. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
Keremeos Review covers news for Keremeos and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[All evacuation alerts lifted for Eagle Bluff Wildfire in Osoyoos](https://www.keremeosreview.com/news/all-evacuation-alerts-lifted-for-eagle-bluff-wildfire-in-osoyoos/). National and international news is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-canadian-press/).
Editorially, Keremeos Review does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left. Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
