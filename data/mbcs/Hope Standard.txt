History
The Hope Standard is a newspaper published in Hope, British Columbia, Canada. The website hopestandard.com covers news, sports, crime, and business for the greater Cloverdale area.
[Read our profile on the Canadian government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Black Press Media](https://www.blackpress.ca/about-us/) owns the Hope Standard. Black Press, based in British Columbia, Canada, publishes over 170 titles across Canada and the western United States. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Hope Standard covers news for Hope and the surrounding area with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Chilliwack avian flu outbreak may be linked to wild bird migration](https://www.hopestandard.com/news/chilliwack-avian-flu-outbreak-may-be-linked-to-wild-bird-migration/). National and international is published through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, the Hope Standard does not regularly produce op-eds. However, political news coverage tends to lean left, such as this
[Chances of climate catastrophe are ignored, scientists say.](https://www.hopestandard.com/news/chances-of-climate-catastrophe-are-ignored-scientists-say/) Generally, news reporting is factual, with a left-leaning bias in story selection.
