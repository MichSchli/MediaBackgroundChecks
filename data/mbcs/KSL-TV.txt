History
[Founded in 1949](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KSL-TV), KSL-TV is an NBC-affiliated television station licensed to Salt Lake City, Utah, United States. KSL.com is the associated website covering news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KSL-TV is owned by
[Bonneville International Corporation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bonneville_International), an arm of [The Church of](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corporation_of_the_President_of_The_Church_of_Jesus_Christ_of_Latter-day_Saints) [ J](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corporation_of_the_President_of_The_Church_of_Jesus_Christ_of_Latter-day_Saints) [esus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corporation_of_the_President_of_The_Church_of_Jesus_Christ_of_Latter-day_Saints) [ Christ of Latter-day Saints](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Corporation_of_the_President_of_The_Church_of_Jesus_Christ_of_Latter-day_Saints), making it one of only a few for-profit TV stations run by a religious organization. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KSL.com covers local news, sports, weather, and crime for the greater Salt Lake City region. Local news is reporting with minimal bias such as this
[Gov. Cox: Utahns 65 and older can get COVID-19 vaccine in March; 1,273 new cases, 14 deaths reported Thursday](https://www.ksl.com/article/50101165/gov-cox-utahns-65-and-older-can-get-covid-19-vaccine-in-march-1273-new-cases-14-deaths-reported-thursday). National and international news is republished through [Deseret News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/deseret-news/), [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/), and the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, they do not produce op-eds; however, they report on local politics through
[Deseret News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/deseret-news/) and national politics through Reuters and the Associated Press. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
