History
Established in 1961, GMA Network is a Philippine television and radio network based in Quezon City, Philippines.
[Felipe L. Gozon](https://www.gmanetwork.com/corporate/boardofdirectors/) is the Chairman of the Board of Directors and Chief Executive Officer of GMA Network, Inc. Launched in 2007, GMA News Online is the official site of GMA News. Its main focus is news, business, sports, technology, and entertainment. [Read our profile on the Philippines government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/philippines-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
GMA News Online is owned by
[GMA Network, Inc.](https://www.gmanetwork.com/corporate/), [owned](https://philippines.mom-rsf.org/en/owners/individual-owners/detail/owner/owner/show/the-jimenez-family/) by Menardo Jimenez, Felipe L. Gozon, and Gilberto M. Duavit. Sr. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
After President Duterte’s administration’s pressures on main rival
[ABS-CBN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abs-cbn-news/) Corp, GMA [ Network Inc.](https://business.inquirer.net/323273/gozon-touts-win-over-abs-cbn-as-investors-flock-to-gma) became the dominant network. President Rodrigo Duterte is the chairman of the left-wing [PDP-Laban party](https://www.gmanetwork.com/news/news/nation/787330/pdp-laban-exec-defends-pacquiao-from-members-criticizing-his-remarks-on-duterte-s-wps-stance/story/).
GMA News publishes articles with a favorable tone towards President Rodrigo Duterte
[“Duterte approves purchase of 15 Black Hawk helicopters for military.”](https://www.gmanetwork.com/news/news/nation/774555/duterte-approves-purchase-of-15-black-hawk-helicopters-for-military/story/) Further, when reporting on the opposition party, 1Sambayan, they report less favorably, such as this [Duterte hits 1Sambayan, Carpio: What have you done for Filipinos?](https://www.gmanetwork.com/news/news/nation/784365/duterte-hits-1sambayan-carpio-what-have-you-done-for-filipinos/story/). When covering USA politics, they usually rely on the [Reuters](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reuters/) wire service. Finally, they also republish [articles](https://www.gmanetwork.com/news/news/world/782231/britain-adds-philippines-three-other-countries-to-travel-red-list/story/) from [The Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). In general, GMA reports news that is mostly factual and with a left-leaning pro-government perspective.
