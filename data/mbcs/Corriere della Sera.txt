History
Founded in 1876 by Eugenio Torelli Viollier,
[Corriere della Sera](https://www.rcsmediagroup.it/en/brand/corriere-della-sera/) is an Italian daily newspaper published and based in Italy. [Luigi Albertini](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Luigi_Albertini) served as an editor during the 1910s when the paper became the most widely read daily newspaper in Europe. The newspaper focuses on news, politics, sports, entertainment, culture, business, and technology. [Luciano Fontana ](https://www.rcsmediagroup.it/en/press-release/press-release-rcs-mediagroup-board-of-director-30042015/)has been the editor-in-chief since 2015. Corriere della Sera also has an English section. [Read our profile on Italy’s government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/italy-media-profile/)

Analysis / Bias
In 2022,
[Reporters Without Borders](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/reporters-without-borders/) ranked Italy 58/180 in their [Press Freedom Index.](https://rsf.org/en/italy) They report that journalists are “still under police protection.” Currently, the Prime Minister of Italy is Mario Draghi, who leans [center-left](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2019/sep/10/italy-pm-conte-left-leaning-coalition-vote-of-confidence) due to a coalition government.
In review, Corriere della Sera reports news, national and international politics, sports, lifestyle, economics, and entertainment. Articles and headlines typically do not use loaded language such as this:
[Salvini expels the imam who attacked Daniela Santanchè: “He incited Islamic terrorism](https://www.corriere.it/politica/18_ottobre_10/salvini-espelle-imam-che-aggredi-daniela-santanche-incitava-terrorismo-islamico-ca2a598c-cc96-11e8-a06b-75759bb4ca39.shtml).” Further, news stories are properly sourced from first-hand accounts, quotations, videos, or external hyperlinking.
When reporting on the USA, they typically report neutrally and use quotations from political leaders and experts such as this:
[Coronavirus in the USA, Fauci: «With the reopening, there is the risk of unnecessary deaths](http://www.corriere.it/esteri/20_maggio_12/coronavirus-usa-fauci-con-riapertura-si-rischiano-morti-inutili-1c261704-941d-11ea-9026-0ed4807e0a45.shtml).» Editorially the paper presents both sides without the use of loaded emotional language. In general, this is a factual news source that is least biased.
