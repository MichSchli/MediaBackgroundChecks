History
The Eagle Grove Eagle is a newspaper published in Eagle Grove, Indiana, United States. The paper covers news, sports, lifestyle, and business for Eagle Grove and the surrounding area. theeaglegroveeagle.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Mid America Publishing owns and publishes the Eagle Grove Eagle. Mid America Publishing publishes
[several community newspapers](https://www.midampublishing.com/content/mid-america-publications) throughout Indiana. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
The Eagle Grove Eagle reports on news, sports, weather, crime, and lifestyle for the greater Eagle Grove, Indiana region. Reporters demonstrate minimal bias when reporting local news, such as this
[Strub recognized for 10 years of service](http://www.theeaglegroveeagle.com/content/strub-recognized-10-years-service). The newspaper and website do not publish national news, focusing on local news reporting.
Editorially, the Eagle Grove Eagle does not produce op-eds and rarely interjects opinions in news stories. In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
