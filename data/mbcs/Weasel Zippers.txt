History
Founded in 2010, Weasel Zippers is a conservative news website that does not have an about page. A search of
[Whois](http://whois.domaintools.com/weaselzippers.us) and trademarking indicates the Weasel Zippers name is registered and trademarked to [Andrew Fell](https://trademarks.justia.com/857/00/weasel-85700947.html). Weasel Zippers offers a tagline that reads “scouring the bowels of the internet.” [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not disclose ownership, and funding appears to come primarily from online advertising and donations.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Weasel Zippers produces very little original content. They typically provide a summary or a few paragraphs from a news article and then encourage the reader to read the full article offsite. All news articles reviewed favor the right and denigrate the left.
Weasel Zippers links to various sources, with most falling on the right side of the spectrum. They also publish news from factually Mixed sources such as the
[Daily Wire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/the-daily-wire/), [Daily Caller](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-caller/), [Daily Mail](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/daily-mail/), and questionable sources such as [Breitbart](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/breitbart/). Since Weasel Zippers is not writing the content, loaded emotional language comes from the source they are linking to.
