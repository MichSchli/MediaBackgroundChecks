History
The Lebanon Reporter is a newspaper published in the city of Lebanon, Indiana. reporter.net is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc owns The Lebanon Reporter. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Lebanon Reporter covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Lebanon, Indiana, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Suspect in school threat must wear GPS monitoring](https://www.reporter.net/across_indiana/suspect-in-school-threat-must-wear-gps-monitoring/article_a30696d0-53de-11ed-9cff-77bc2aa5b57f.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
