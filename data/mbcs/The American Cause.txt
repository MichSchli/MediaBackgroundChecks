History
[Founded by Pat Buchanan in 1993](https://theamericancause.org/about-tac/), The American Cause is an educational foundation whose principle mission is to advance and promote foreign and domestic policies that put America and Americans first. Other than this statement from their About page, there is minimal transparency. Analysis / Bias
The American Cause republishes conservative news and opinion stories from other sources as well as original writing from Pat Buchanan. Articles often focused on a perceived culture war, Big-Tech censorship of conservatives, and America first policies. Articles and headlines contain loaded emotional wording such as this
[China Is ‘Laughing’ As US Culture War Erodes Combat Readiness, Says Former Defense Secretary](https://theamericancause.org/uncategorized/china-is-laughing-as-us-culture-war-erodes-combat-readiness-says-former-defense-secretary/). This story comes from Questionable [Fox News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/fox-news-bias/).
Editorially, all stories favor a conservative perspective, such as this
[My Cancel-Culture Nightmare Is Over](https://theamericancause.org/article/my-cancel-culture-nightmare-is-over/).
