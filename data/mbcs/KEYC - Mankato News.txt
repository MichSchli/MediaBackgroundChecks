History
Founded in 1960 and a CBS-affiliated television station, KEYC – Mankato News serves Mankato, Minnesota, and the surrounding area. keyc.com is the associated website for the TV news station that provides local and national news and sports, weather, traffic, and entertainment.

Funded by / Ownership
KEYC – Mankato News is owned by
[Gray Television](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gray_Television), which operates 145 stations across the USA. Sponsored content and advertising generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
