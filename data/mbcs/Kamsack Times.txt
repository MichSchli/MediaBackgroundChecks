History
Kamsack Times is an English-language newspaper in Kamsack, Saskatchewan, Canada. sasktoday.ca/central/kamsack-times is the online domain covering news, sports, business, and lifestyle.
[Read our profile on Canada’s media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Glacier Media Group](https://www.glaciermedia.ca/brands/media-directory#Community+Media) owns the Kamsack Times. Revenue is derived through advertising and subscriptions.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Kamsack Times publishes sports, weather, national, world, and local news for the greater Kamsack, Saskatchewan region. Local news is reported factually and with minimal bias, such as this
[Crop report: producers making strides: 21% seeded in area](https://www.sasktoday.ca/central/kamsack-times/crop-report-producers-making-strides-21-seeded-in-area-7019153). The national news is typically republished from the [Canadian Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2018/06/15/fact-check-of-trumps-canadian-trade-stats/), while world news is from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/).
Editorially, Kamsack Times’s Op-eds favor the left with stories such as this
[Opinion: Geopolitical catastrophes in the making](https://www.sasktoday.ca/opinion/geopolitical-catastrophes-in-the-making-6984879). The paper generally offers a moderately liberal perspective and reports news factually.
