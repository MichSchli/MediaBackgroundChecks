History
[Founded in 1957](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/KETV), KETV is an [ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Omaha, Nebraska, United States, and serving Council Bluffs, Iowa. KETV.com is the associated news and information website for the TV station. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
KETV is owned by Hearst Communications, which owns newspapers, magazines, television channels, and television stations, including the
[San Francisco Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/san-francisco-chronicle/), the [Houston Chronicle](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/houston-chronicle/), and [Esquire](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/esquire-magazine/). According to [Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Hearst donated [70% to Republican candidates](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/recipients?id=D000034391) and causes in 2020. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, KETV.com reports on news, crime, sports, weather, and entertainment for the greater Omaha, Nebraska area. Local news is covered through reporters with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Omaha police identify officers involved in deadly police shooting near 27th & Harrison](https://www.ketv.com/article/1-in-critical-condition-after-officer-involved-shooting-thursday-evening/34732243). Most stories are accompanied by a video from the TV station. [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/), [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/), and other Hearst affiliates typically cover the national news.
Editorially, KETV has a section dedicated to editorials that are not overtly biased. They also report on national politics with minimal bias as the primary source is usually the Associated Press. In general, the news is reported factually with minimal editorializing.
