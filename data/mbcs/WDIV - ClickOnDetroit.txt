History
Founded in 1946, WDIV-TV is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Detroit, Michigan, United States. Clickondetroit.com is the associated website that reports news, crime, sports, weather, and lifestyle for the greater Detroit area. [Read our profile on USA media and government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WDIV – ClickOnDetriot is owned by
[Graham Holdings](https://www.ghco.com/), which owns seven TV stations and the website [Slate](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/slate/). Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, ClickOnDetriot.com provides news, sports, weather, crime reporting, and lifestyle for the greater Detroit Metro area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this
[Redford Township police looking for missing 73-year-old woman who is ‘at risk’.](https://www.clickondetroit.com/news/local/2020/09/24/redford-township-police-looking-for-missing-73-year-old-woman-who-is-at-risk/) Many stories provide videos from the WDIV TV station. The Associated Press generally offers national and world news.
Editorially, ClickOnDetroit does not produce Op-Eds; however, they do report on politics through the Associated Press and original reporting. Political news is generally balanced and presented with minimal bias, such as this
[Attention Michigan voters: Ballots mailed out today](https://www.clickondetroit.com/decision-2020/2020/09/24/attention-michigan-voters-ballots-mailed-out-today/). In general, the news is reported factually and with minimal bias.
