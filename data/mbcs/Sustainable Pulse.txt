History
[Founded in 2012](https://whois.domaintools.com/sustainablepulse.com), Sustainable Pulse provides the general public with the latest global news on GMOs, Sustainable Food and Sustainable Agriculture from their network of worldwide sources. [Read our profile on United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Sustainable Pulse lacks transparency as they do not disclose ownership. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the purpose of this website is to cast GMOs in a negative light with articles such as this
[Let Them Eat GM Cottonseed!](https://sustainablepulse.com/2019/10/18/let-them-eat-gm-cottonseed/#.XzXdjOhKiUk) This story is linked to [GM Watch](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/gmwatch/) which MBFC has categorized as a quackery level pseudoscience website. In general, Sustainable Pulse routinely reports [anti-GMO Propaganda](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/pseudoscience-dictionary-gmo-dangers/) without a peer-reviewed source. In general, all stories report negatively on GMOs.
