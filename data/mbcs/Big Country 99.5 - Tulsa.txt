History
Big Country 99.5 – Tulsa is a radio station based in Tulsa, Oklahoma. bigcountry995.com is the website for the radio station and its news programming.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Griffin Communications, a media company based in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, owns Big Country 99.5 – Tulsa. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Big Country 99.5 – Tulsa primarily covers local news, crime, entertainment, and sports for the Tulsa, Oklahoma, area. Local news is covered by the Least Biased
[KOTV – News On 6](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/kotv-newson6-bias/) TV station with minimal bias through reporters like this [2 Adults, 3 Children Injured In Crash At Tulsa Intersection ](https://www.bigcountry995.com/story/63320c3c1f75060725003d96/2-adults-3-children-injured-in-crash-at-tulsa-intersection)The Associated Press provides national and international news.
Big Country 99.5 – Tulsa does not offer editorials and rarely covers political news. Most political news is derived from third-party sources such as the
[Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/). In general, news reporting is factual and displays minimal bias.
