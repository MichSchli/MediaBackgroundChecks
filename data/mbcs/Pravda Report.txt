Funded by / Ownership
Pravda is owned by Pravda.ru Holding, headed by
[Vadim Gorshenin](https://www.pravdareport.com/author/2-1/). Mr. Gorshenin is a supporter of Russian President Vladimir Putin. Revenue is derived through advertising.
Analysis / Bias
The Pravda Report covers Russian news, politics, and opinion from a pro-government perspective. They frequently promote propaganda in support of President Putin while constantly denigrating the west and former President Trump:
[The Trump regime is insane](https://www.pravdareport.com/news/opinion/140732-trump_regime/). This opinion piece is not sourced at all.
Besides promoting nationalist propaganda, Pravda Report frequently promotes conspiracy theories. It makes false claims such as promoting that the chemical attack in Syria was a
[false flag operation](http://archive.fo/Op8EF) or that [Aliens are visiting earth](http://archive.fo/Eu9Cn#selection-565.1-565.45) and having sex with people. In general, almost all content on this website is either misleading or false.
