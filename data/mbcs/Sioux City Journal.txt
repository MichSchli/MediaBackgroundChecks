History
[Founded in 1864,](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sioux_City_Journal) The Sioux City Journal is a daily newspaper published in Sioux City, Iowa, United States. The paper serves northwestern Iowa and portions of Nebraska and South Dakota. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Lee Enterprises own the Sioux City Journal. According to
[Open Secrets](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/center-for-responsive-politics-open-secrets/), Lee Enterprises has donated 88% of its money to [Republican politicians](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/totals.php?id=D000052990&cycle=2014) since 1990. Advertising and subscriptions generate revenue.
