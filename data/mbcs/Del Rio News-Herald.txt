History
[Founded in 1884](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Del_Rio_News-Herald), The Del Rio News-Herald is a broadsheet newspaper published in Del Rio, Texas, covering Val Verde County. It is published on Tuesday through Friday afternoons and on Sunday morning. The current editor is Ruben Cantu.

Funded by / Ownership
The Del Rio News-Herald is owned by
[Southern Newspapers Inc](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Southern_Newspapers)., which owns many small market newspapers in Texas, Alabama and Georgia. The newspaper and website is funded through advertising and subscription fees.
