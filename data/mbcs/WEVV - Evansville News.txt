History
Founded in 1983, WEVV – Evansville News is a
[CBS](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cbs-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Evansville, Indiana, United States. wevv.com is the associated website that provides news on crime, sports, and lifestyle for the greater Evansville area. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Allen Media LLC, a subsidiary of Entertainment Studios, owns WEVV – Evansville News. The owner of Entertainment Studios, Byron Allen, is a frequent
[donor to Democratic](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/byron-allen-on-sinclair-politics-130236929.html) candidates and causes. Advertising and sponsored content generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, WEVV – Evansville News publishes news for Evansville and the surrounding area through reporters and with minimal bias, such as this
[Owensboro non-profit hosts fundraiser for homeless teens](https://www.wevv.com/news/owensboro-non-profit-hosts-fundraiser-for-homeless-teens/article_f99c6496-05f7-11ed-8084-1f91addbecd5.html). Videos from the TV Station accompany many local news stories. National and international news is typically republished through the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and [CNN](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cnn/).
Editorially, they do not publish Op-eds, and story selection is balanced.
