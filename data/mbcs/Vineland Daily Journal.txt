History
The Vineland Daily Journal is a newspaper that serves Vineland, New Jersey, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. thedailyjournal.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper.
[Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns The Vineland Daily Journal. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Vineland Daily Journal covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Vineland, New Jersey area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[ This South Jersey city wants some city workers to pay if they quit](https://www.thedailyjournal.com/story/news/local/2023/01/27/vineland-nj-city-employees-payment-due-invoices-quitting/69842486007/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, The Vineland Daily Journal does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented, such as this that favors the left:
[We can’t let the GOP undermine Jan. 6 panel’s findings — or undermine cops | Pascrell](https://www.thedailyjournal.com/story/opinion/2023/01/19/gop-and-jan-6-we-cant-let-republicans-undermine-panels-findings/69818923007/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
