History
Founded in 2017, InfoScum is a news aggregation website portal to sell products. InfoScum does not have an about page, nor any information about ownership, or really anything that points toward a living human being. According to
[Whois,](http://whois.domaintools.com/infoscum.com) the domain is registered to someone in Toronto, Canada.
Read our profile on the
[Canadian government’s influence on media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/canada-media-profile/).
Funded by / Ownership
The website does not offer any ownership information, and they are funded through advertising. They have an
[entire page](http://www.infoscum.com/deals) dedicated to “Deals.”
Analysis / Bias
In review, InfoScum is a news aggregator that breaks the news into the categories of all news, science, video, and podcasts. All links lead to the source websites when clicked. The all-news section comprises mostly left-leaning sources such as
[HuffPo](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/huffington-post/), [Yahoo News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/yahoo-news/), and [Axios](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/axios/). Science news points to Pro-Science sources such as [Phys.org](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/phys-org/), [Science Daily](http://www.infoscum.com/deals), and [Scientific American](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/scientific-american/). The podcast section links mostly left-leaning podcasts such as Adam Carrolla.
The main purpose of this website is to encourage clicks on ads.
