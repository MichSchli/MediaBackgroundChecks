History
The Sharon Herald is a newspaper published in Sharon, Pennsylvania. sharonherald.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Sharon Herald. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Sharon Herald covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Sharon, PA, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this
[Hermitage introduces zoning map that includes Wheatland](https://www.sharonherald.com/news/hermitage-introduces-zoning-map-that-includes-wheatland/article_7b49213c-5e53-11ee-be03-170b053ac633.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
