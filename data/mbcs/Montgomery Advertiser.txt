History
[Founded in 1829](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Montgomery_Advertiser), the Montgomery Advertiser serves Montgomery, Alabama, and the surrounding area. The paper covers local, national, and international news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion. Montgomeryadvertiser.com serves as the online domain for the newspaper. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
Gannett, the publisher of
[USA Today](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/usa-today-2/), owns the Montgomery Advertiser. In August 2019, [Gatehouse Media](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/2019/08/06/gatehouse-gannett-to-merge-for-1-4b-build-newspaper-giant/) purchased and merged with Gannett becoming the largest newspaper chain in the USA. After the merger, the companies retained the Gannett name. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Montgomery Advertiser covers news, sports, lifestyle, and entertainment for the greater Montgomery, Alabama area. They report local news with minimal loaded emotional language
[Student detained after a gun found at Lee High School](https://www.montgomeryadvertiser.com/story/news/2021/12/09/high-school-student-detained-after-gun-found-lee-high-school/6453983001/). National and international news comes from other Gannett publications and the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, the Montgomery Advertiser does not make Presidential endorsements; however, a review of op-eds shows that both sides are represented such as this that favors the left:
[Billionaires like Branson should stop investing in Alabama until death penalty is gone](https://www.montgomeryadvertiser.com/story/opinion/2021/11/14/billionaires-like-branson-should-stop-investing-alabama-until-death-penalty-gone/8614748002/). And this favoring the right [President Biden offers an anti-family agenda](https://www.montgomeryadvertiser.com/story/opinion/2021/11/29/president-biden-offers-anti-family-agenda/8802184002/). After reviewing dozens of op-eds, we found slightly more favor a liberal perspective. Finally, the news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning editorial bias.
