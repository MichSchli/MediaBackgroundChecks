History
The Daily Star is a daily broadsheet newspaper published in Oneonta, New York.
[Founded in 1980](https://www.thedailystar.com/site/about-us.html), the paper circulates in a four-county area in central New York state. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The Daily Star is owned by
[Community Newspaper Holdings, Inc.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/CNHI), an American publisher of newspapers and advertising-related publications throughout the United States. Subscription fees and advertising generate revenue for the paper.
Analysis / Bias
Staff writers cover local news with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[DEC seeks hosts for invasive species awareness events](https://www.thedailystar.com/news/local_news/dec-seeks-hosts-for-invasive-species-awareness-events/article_7be84687-19dc-5c26-b8d0-eeed65f48e4c.html). Coverage of national news is provided by the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and other CNHI publications.
A review of opinion pieces reveals a leftward tilt with stories such as
[In Our Opinion: Be a good neighbor and get vaccinated,](https://www.thedailystar.com/opinion/editorials/in-our-opinion-be-a-good-neighbor-and-get-vaccinated/article_b397b5ee-1ce6-528c-80e6-52a425a5ce13.html) and [In Our Opinion: Pot legalization was the right thing to do](https://www.thedailystar.com/opinion/editorials/in-our-opinion-pot-legalization-was-the-right-thing-to-do/article_4b619d69-3727-5871-96bd-368cda124443.html). Although The Daily Star does offer some right-leaning opinions, the majority lean left in bias. In general, they report the news factually and with a left-leaning editorial bias.
