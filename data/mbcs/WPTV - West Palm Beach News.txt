History
Founded in 1954, WPTV – West Palm Beach News is an
[NBC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/)-affiliated station licensed to West Palm Beach, Florida, United States. wptv.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WPTV – West Palm Beach News is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://scripps.com/our-brands/local-media/), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WPTV – West Palm Beach News covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater West Palm Beach, Florida, area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[800-acre brush fire in Martin County 100% contained, Florida Forest Service says](https://www.wptv.com/news/treasure-coast/region-martin-county/several-mobile-homes-at-risk-as-large-brush-fire-spreads-in-martin-county-fire-rescue-says). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Manhattan district attorney postpones Trump grand jury session](https://www.wptv.com/news/national-politics/manhattan-district-attorney-postpones-trump-grand-jury-session). In general, they present low-biased, fact-based reporting.
