History
[Founded in 1883](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Idaho_Press-Tribune), The Kuna Melba News is a weekly newspaper published in Kuna, Idaho, United States, that serves Kuna and the surrounding area. The website is located on IdahoPress.com/kuna/ that also hosts other newspapers, including [Boise Weekly](https://www.idahopress.com/boiseweekly/). [Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
The newspaper is owned by
[Adams Publishing Group](https://www.poynter.org/business-work/2017/who-are-the-adams-family-and-why-are-they-buying-newspapers-by-the-dozen/), which owns over one hundred papers across the USA. Stephen Adams is the owner and [donates to Republicans](https://web.archive.org/web/20080724023422/http://www.madcowprod.com/billboardenvy.htm), raising 1 million dollars for George W. Bush in 2000. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, the Kuna Melba News covers local news through journalists with minimal bias in wording, such as this
[Avian flu moving through Idaho, kills more than 50 birds at Kuna animal sanctuary](https://www.idahopress.com/kuna/news/avian-flu-moving-through-idaho-kills-more-than-50-birds-at-kuna-animal-sanctuary/article_ca16cf7d-c21b-5f98-8130-ab0773925169.html). Local information is sourced through quotes, interviews, and accounts, while National and world news comes directly from the [Associated Press](https://www.bozemandailychronicle.com/news/city/bozeman-continues-to-foster-midtown-development/article_51464c29-97fa-57b9-9535-05375f0853c8.html).
Editorially, the Kuna Melba News provides a balance in opinions, with slightly more favoring the left. They generally report the news factually and with a slight Left-leaning bias.
