History
Founded in 1946, WEWS – News 5 – Cleveland is an
[ABC](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/abc-news/)-affiliated television station licensed to Cleveland, Ohio, United States. News5cleveland.com is the associated website for the TV station that publishes news, sports, weather, and crime. [Read our profile on the United States government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
WEWS – News 5 – Cleveland is owned by
[The E. W. Scripps Company](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/E._W._Scripps_Company), an American broadcasting company founded in 1878 by Edward Willis “E. W.” Scripps. Advertising generates revenue.
Analysis / Bias
WEWS – News 5 – Cleveland covers local news, sports, weather, entertainment, and crime for the greater Cleveland, Ohio area. Local news is reported with minimal bias, such as this:
[Cleveland firefighters: Careless smoking likely caused series of mulch bed fires in the Flats](https://www.news5cleveland.com/news/local-news/cleveland-firefighters-careless-smoking-likely-caused-series-of-mulch-bed-fires-in-the-flats). Like most stories, there is an embedded video from the TV station. The national news is derived from various sources, most coming from the [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and Scripps National.
Editorially, they do not offer op-eds; however, they cover political news through the Associated Press, and local journalists with minimal bias, such as this
[Former Atlanta mayor joins Biden administration](https://www.news5cleveland.com/news/national/former-atlanta-mayor-joins-biden-administration). In general, they present low biased, fact-based reporting.
