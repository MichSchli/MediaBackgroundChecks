History
Aspenia Online is a forum and online journal of Aspen Institute Italia. Aspen Institute Italia is a partner of the
[Aspen Institute](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/aspen-institute/) think tank based in Washington DC. According to Aspenia Online’s [about page,](https://aspeniaonline.it/aspenia-classic/) Aspenia Online is a quarterly journal of Aspen Institute Italia, focusing on international affairs, addressing “issues of transatlantic interest, offering significant political commentary on US-European relations and their impact on the world order.” Aspen Institute Italia is based in Rome-Italy. [Roberto Menotti](https://aspeniaonline.it/about-us/) is Editor-in-Chief of Aspenia Online, and Marta Dassù is Editor-in-Chief of the print edition. [Read our profile on Italy’s government and media.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/italy-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[The Aspen Institute Italia](https://www.aspeninstitute.it/en/institute/identity-and-mission) does not clearly state its ownership; however, they partner with the USA-based Aspen Institute. The Aspen Institute Italia is chaired by former Minister of Economy [Giulio Tremonti](https://www.weforum.org/people/giulio-tremonti), who served under the Right-Center Silvio Berlusconi Government. For more information about Aspen Institute’s funding, please see our review of [Aspen Institute](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/aspen-institute/) USA.
Analysis / Bias
In review, Aspenia Online features articles that promote a left-leaning bias on environmental issues such as
[“Why we need a green social contract to make decarbonisation just.” ](https://aspeniaonline.it/we-need-a-green-social-contract-to-make-decarbonisation-just/)and on immigration, [“The politics of future migration: hoaxes, nightmares and few certainties.”](https://aspeniaonline.it/the-politics-of-future-migration-hoaxes-nightmares-and-few-certainties/) However, they also publish articles critical of the Biden administration, such as [“Why Biden’s magical thinking is already failing in the Middle East.”](https://aspeniaonline.it/why-bidens-magical-thinking-is-already-failing-in-the-middle-east/) Aspenia Online utilizes various credible sources such as [The Washington Post,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/washington-post/) [NBC News](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/nbc-news/), [Cato.org](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/cato-institute/), [Financial Times,](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/financial-times/) and [Foreign Affairs](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/foreign-affairs/).
Editorially, Aspenia Online promotes left-leaning causes such as environmentalism and report negatively on former President Donald Trump,
[The tragic parable of the Trump presidency](https://aspeniaonline.it/la-tragica-parabola-della-presidenza-trump/). In general, they publish factual information that is slightly left-center biased.
