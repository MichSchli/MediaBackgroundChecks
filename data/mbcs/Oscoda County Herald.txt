History
The Oscoda County Herald is a newspaper published in Mio, Michigan. oscodawherald.com covers local, national, sports, weather, and crime news for the Oscoda Area.

Funded by / Ownership
The Aranec County Independent is owned by

Over the last few elections, CherryRoad has [primarily](https://www.opensecrets.org/orgs/summary?id=D000024733) donated to Democrats; however, they donated 100% to Republican candidates in the past. Advertising and paid subscriptions generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Oscoda County Herald covers news, sports, community, and crime for the greater Ogemaw County, Michigan, area. Local news is reported with minimal loaded emotional language, such as this
[Mio and Fairview team up](https://www.oscodaherald.com/articles/mio-and-fairview-team-up/). National and international news comes from the [Associated Press.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/)
Editorially, a review of op-eds indicates a reasonable balance between left and right. The paper reports factually and with minimal bias.
