History
The Salem News is a newspaper published in Salem, Massachusetts. salemnews.com is the associated website covering local, national, crime, and lifestyle news.
[Read our profile on USA Media and Government.](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/united-states-media-profile/)
Funded by / Ownership
[Community Newspaper Holdings](http://www.cnhi.com/) Inc. owns The Salem News. They presently publish more than 100 newspapers in the USA. Advertising and subscription fees generate revenue.
Analysis / Bias
In review, The Salem News covers crime, sports, entertainment, and politics for the Salem, Massachusetts, region. Local news is reported through reporters with minimal bias, such as this:
[North Shore voters weigh the issues ahead of likely Biden, Trump rematch](https://www.salemnews.com/election/north-shore-voters-weigh-the-issues-ahead-of-likely-biden-trump-rematch/article_25688170-af04-11ee-bd74-3f92ee48181f.html). The [Associated Press](https://mediabiasfactcheck.com/associated-press/) and the CHNI news service cover national and international news.
Editorially, a review of Op-Eds reveals a reasonable balance with more favoring the left. The news is generally reported factually and with a slight left-leaning bias through endorsements and op-eds.
