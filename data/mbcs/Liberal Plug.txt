Reasoning: Extreme Left, Propaganda, Fake News
Country: USA
World Press Freedom Rank: USA 45/180
History
Liberal Plug is an extreme left-wing news and opinion blog that does not have an about page. A Whois search reveals the domain was registered privately in 2016.
Funded by / Ownership
Liberal Plug does not list an owner, however, articles are posted under the name
[Justin Soloman](http://liberalplug.com/author/justin-soloman/). The website is funded through online advertising.
Analysis / Bias
The Liberal Plug is a news and opinion website with extreme left-wing bias. Articles utilize strong emotional loaded words that always favor the left such as this:
[Forget Just Resigning, Rep. Cummings Has Brutal Plan For Jeff Sessions, He’s In Huge Trouble.](http://liberalplug.com/2017/03/03/forget-just-resigning-rep-cummings-brutal-plan-jeff-sessions-hes-huge-trouble/) Some stories are outright [fake news](http://www.politifact.com/texas/statements/2017/feb/15/liberal-blogger/fake-news-texas-congressman-pecos-wishes-another-9/) and most others are not properly sourced and amount to left-wing propaganda. Liberal Plug is also on [Politifact’s Fake News List](http://www.politifact.com/punditfact/article/2017/apr/20/politifacts-guide-fake-news-websites-and-what-they/?platform=hootsuite).
