History
The Shoshone News-Press is a newspaper published in Shoshone, Idaho, United States. The paper covers news, sports, entertainment, lifestyle, and opinion for Shoshone and the surrounding area. shoshonenewspress.com serves as the domain for the online version of the newspaper.
